
							About This Content
							Garruk Wildspeaker’s fully unlocked “Hunter’s Strength” deck. Garruk is a hunter to the core, a planeswalking predator whose hunting ground is the entire Multiverse. Now, you can see your enemies through Garruk’s eyes, sensing their weaknesses and moving in for the kill. Garruk can call on all kinds of savage beastsbut he is also a formidable spellcaster in his own right, allowing him to pick his fights and use his creatures to maximum effect. With Garruk’s knowledge of the hunt, there’s nowhere in the Multiverse that your opponent can hide! Additional content for Magic 2014.
