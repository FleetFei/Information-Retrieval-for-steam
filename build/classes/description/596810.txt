
							About This Game
							Save Their Souls is a mix of platformer and memory game to play solo or with a friend (local co-op mode).Players must be fast and memorize the rescue plan of each level, then, run to save the inhabitants of the occupied apartment. Use minimap to recognize stairs location and make the faster way. Share the responsability with your partner in co-op mode.The destiny of these poor souls lies in your hands!!Linear level unlock.Retro style with password saves.Full of humor.Up to 2 players, splitscreen.Keyboard and controller compatible.
