
							About This Content
							The Zombien invasion started in the Rooster Teeth studio, but it's spread... TO THE BUNGALOW. Fend off ravaging waves of Zombiens in a more open environment with less room to cower in terror within. Still, build your defenses, scavenge for weapons, and fight for your life in REMEMBER THE BUNGALOW for Rooster Teeth vs. Zombiens!Avoid the dangers of Zombien attacks amidst the wide-open parking lot, filled with cars abandoned by the — likely devoured — souls that once drove them. Scour office buildings looking for supplies and weapons. Barricade yourself up in the Rooster Teeth bungalow. Do whatever it takes to survive because, after all, you must survive. You will survive.You will probably survive.** Probably will not survive.
