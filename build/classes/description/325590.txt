
							About This Content
							This DLC includes The Art of Outland and the Outland OST. The Art of Outland (PDF) is a 90-pages long journey that covers Housemarque's critically acclaimed platformer from inception to release. From the initial publisher pitches – when it was looking like some sort of "Indiana Jones meets Pitfall" – to the its final, elegant form, the Art of Outland reveals, inspirations, challenges and struggles that made Outland shine in the eyes of so many gamers. [To access the Artbook from Steam: select 'Outland' -> right click -> 'View Downloadable content' -> 'Local Files' -> 'Browse local files' -> 'Artbook and OST'-> 'Art' -> 'art_of_outland.pdf']The Outland OST, composed and performed by Ari Pulkkinen (Resogun, Dead Nation and many more...), is a collection of 29 tracks for a total of 1.5h+ of music.
