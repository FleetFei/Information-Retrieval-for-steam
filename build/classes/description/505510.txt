
							About This Content
							AAW Map Pack (Volume #4) contains 56 maps of various types and sizes.Inside you will find:- Maps of various residential buildings from rich palace to poor tent- Five wild forest maps and several forest shrines- Maps of different cavern lairs.- Three city maps.- Eight overland maps.- Two maps for city encounters.- Linked miniatures for all map types.Works with all rulesets.Requires: A Fantasy Grounds full or ultimate license or an active subscription and any ruleset.
