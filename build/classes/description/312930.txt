
							About This Game
							Rugby League Live 3 - The FanHub BETA provides an unparalleled ability to create and share your favorite Rugby League Stars.

The FanHub BETA offers player and club creation and sharing. The ability to create Referees and modify your own Leagues. You can also learn the skills of the game by going into the Drills to compete against friends and the world with Goal Kicking and Passing Drills complete with leaderboards.

The player creation suite offers the ability to alter players appearance, height and weight. Add tattoos, strapping and headgear. Even change players boots!

The club creation suite offers the ability to alter clubs playing rosters, identity, home grounds. You can even customise your clubs jerseys!

Featuring an online community with over 70,000 creations!

Get Creating and Get Sharing!
