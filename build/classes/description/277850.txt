
							About This Game
							Sportsfriends is a compendium of award-winning local multiplayer games for living rooms, parties, and even backyards. Featuring:  BaraBariBall - retro-styled fighting game Super Pole Riders - QWOP-like pole vault dueling game Hokra - high-velocity sports game Johann Sebastian Joust - face-to-face physical party game (Mac and Linux only)Highlights Four award-winning games, together in one tight-knit package Simple, accessible, multiplayer fun Strategically deep and highly replayable Perfect for events and partiesNoteJ.S. Joust requires a Bluetooth connection and at least two Playstation Move controllers (sold separately), and is only supported on Mac and Linux. BaraBariBall, Super Pole Riders, and Hokra require two USB game controllers with twin analog sticks.
