
							About This Game
							Take on the role of Lorelai, a young woman of humble means who learns that her true identity is the heir to a powerful empire! Her quest will take her around the land of Dondoran. On her journey, Lorelai recruits the help of a charming cast of characters, as she seeks to unlock the secrets of her exile, destroy the seven deadly sins, and fulfill her destiny as the true princess!Game Features:Lovable, addicting charactersUnique Steampunk settingClassic look, modern feelStunning original soundtrack
