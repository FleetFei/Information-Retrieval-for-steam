
							About This Game
							Dungeon Crawlers is a game designed to explore the fun and funny concept of what "Ghostbusters" might've looked like if it were set in a dungeon.  It's a turn-based strategy game that is easily accessible.  Don't expect to dive too deep into statistics and weapons types here.  The interface is highly streamlined.  This game is all about launching and playing, but still being demanding and challenging to even the most seasoned of turn-based vets.Key Features:Fun, streamlined, turn-based gameplayHilarious story, it will keep you interested throughout!Incredible visuals for a game of this scope6-8 Hours of core gameplay, and several hours of additional gameplay constantly added in the form of challenge maps.
