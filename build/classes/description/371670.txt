
							About This Game
							Inspired by games such as Sonic The Hedgehog and Super Monkey Ball, Rolling Sun takes advantage of the latest version of CRYENGINE, with best in class physics, lighting and water effects.  Discover ancient magic as you guide your Sun Stone through lost Incan and Mayan civilizations.Take control of a defeated Sun God trapped on Earth.  Use the last of your powers to guide a stone across and ancient civilization, gaining power as you get closer and closer to your rival, the Moon God.   Skills to unlock :As you gain power, you'll gain such skills as double jump, mid-air dash, and more.   Physics-based gameplay.  Take advantage of a living environment, powered by CryEngine physics, to reach your goals.
