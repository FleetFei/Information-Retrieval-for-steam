
							About This Content
							Dies Irae adds increased depth and scope to playing as Germany through World War 2. Special events, mechanics and graphics have been added and designed to make the Hearts of Iron experience even more engrossing. The emphasis has been placed on the aggressive nation of the war. This allows players to guide and control the war’s outcome.
						Key Features:
						
						A new face of war: A revamped graphical interface has been designed to fit the German mood and focus
						Lead the most famous Wehrmacht divisions to victory!
						Unleash the Wolves of the Atlantic with historically accurate U-boat mechanics
						Manage your internal government, deal with the German ministers as you need resources to fight the war
						All new terrain-based unit system, where operational planning will be the key to success.
						Dies Irae: Götterdämmerung will be compatible with both HoI3 expansions, Semper Fi and For the Motherland
						Inspired by fans for the fans!
