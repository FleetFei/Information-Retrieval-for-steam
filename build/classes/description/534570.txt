
							About This Game
							Survival Kingdom is a turn-based, retro strategy game.You are the ruler of a kingdom in a medieval fantasy-pixel world. Embark on an adventure and test your ruling abilities.Will your kingdom escape death and achieve glory? Will you be able to survive?It all depends on you!FeaturesPopularity System: The people are resentful, so treat them wellUpgrade Buildings: You can improve your buildingsManage your Kingdom: Taxes, Laws and Prison Penalties28 Endings: Composed of 3 different modulesHonor the gods with statues and sacrificesInteractive EventsPermadeath
