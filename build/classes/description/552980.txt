
							About This Game
							Escape the Island is an experimental/survival game where you wake up on the lost, forgotten island. Look for whatever you can find and try escaping the island. Seek for help and try to survive the dangerous sea-world! Scavenge. Discover. Survive.Key bindings:WASD - Walk/SwimSpace - JumpF - FlashlightQ - Underwater MaskE - Enter BoatEscape - Pause Menu
