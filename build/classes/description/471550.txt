
							About This Game
							Nine Parchments is a co-operative blast'em up game of magic mayhem from Frozenbyte! A group of drop-out wizard apprentices decide to skip their training for a quick path to the Nine Parchments.As the wannabe wizards rapidly discover powerful new spells and ignore all safety aspects, it's natural that their hasty progress results in lots of unwanted deaths...Features Top-down chaos action with a crazy amount of different spells 1-4 Players, online and local co-op Roguelike progression, randomly generated monsters Unlockable characters and items Frighteningly powerful spells:  Projectile, DoT, AOE, Beam,  Spray and Physical spell types Five spell elements: Fire, Ice, Life, Death, ElectricAvailable in 2017!
