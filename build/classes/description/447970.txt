
							About This Game
							Vaporize the enemy in this intense vertically scrolling space shooter! The only things you'll need to bring to this battle are a quick wrist and an insatiable appetite for action, so suit up and strap in for the battle of your life!

Vaporize the enemy in this intense vertically scrolling space shooter! Pilot a heavily armed ship into hostile territory to complete missions and introduce your adversaries to cold vacuum of space. Unleash a barrage of primary and secondary weapons on the invading hordes, collect powerups that keep you alive and avoid enemy projectiles so you can live to fight another day. The only things you'll need to bring to this battle are a quick wrist and an insatiable appetite for action, so suit up and strap in for the battle of your life!
