
							About This Game
							Hyperspace Invaders II: Pixel Edition is a hardcore, uncompromising vertical scrolling bullet hell/invader hell shooter (shmup) that doubles as a techno-based lightsynth, triggering bursts of funky invaders at the pulse of a relentless beat.HIII:PE also features an extra track/level by French flashcore artist HFK, pushing the engine to a gameplay tempo of 360BPM.- Hardcore, uncompromising vertical scrolling bullet hell/invader hell action.- Tempo-synced Gameplay (invader movement, bulletpatterns and more get triggered at the pulse of a beat)- Adaptive Difficulty (harder as you play better)- Generative Gameplay (never the same level twice)- Dominate the Steam Leaderboards and unlock all the Achievements!- Full Controller Support (+ Vibration) - An OST of 10 tracks by renowned artists in the world of electronic music, such as: Carl Finlow / Ronny Ragtroll / Karl Lihagen / Atomhead / Subjex / Zuvuya / Mergel / NoProblemCore / Psykovsky & Arcek / HFK and WK(ES).
