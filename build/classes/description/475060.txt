
							About This Game
							Have you ever dreamed yourself as a zombie killer?In Electric Zombies! these deadly creatures will try to teach you a lesson! But what could be more brutal and fun than watch them all crash, die and explode?More than just another zombie gameElectric Zombies! is a casual zombie survival shooter with cartoon/comic-like style. Kill the waves of zombies to unlock a vendetta with Zombie Boss!Use all your skills to survive!Most weapons in the game have alternative and primal fire. Use abilities and stage abilities to make zombies fall underground, fry or just do a mass kill!Art is a bang!Electric Zombies brings you a beautiful stylish world that makes killing zombies a sense of art. Each level of the game has its own unique concept, design, gameplay and of course style!
