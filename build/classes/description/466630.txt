
							About This Game
							The Pedestrian is a 2.5D side-scrolling puzzle platformer that takes an interesting twist on the genre. It's a build-your-own-solution game with a unique art style centered around public signs, e.g., male & female bathroom symbols, elevator symbol, etc.Key FeaturesPuzzlesPlanning, moving, connecting, adjusting, tweaking, winning! Puzzles are simple - get from the start to the end, but as they say, “It’s not the destination, it’s the journey.”EnvironmentsThe adventure takes The Pedestrian through signs located in a variety of environments-Modern office building lobby-Dark and musty warehouse-Sprawling subway system-Bright expansive city-Sparkling art gallery -Damp sewer-Stormy construction siteWordless ExperienceNo more walls of text! All puzzle mechanics are taught using symbols and animations, while story elements are portrayed around gameplay.
