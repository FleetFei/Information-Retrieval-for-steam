
							About This Game
							Archaica: The Path Of Light is a visually stunning puzzle game with lasers and mirrors, that enraptures gamers not only with finely designed levels, but also with an interesting storyline. In this game we become the Chosen One from the nation that believes walking the legendary Path of Light will save the world from impending disaster. We revive ancient mechanisms, explore secrets of an ancient civilization, and discover the true destiny of The Path along the way through extraordinary realms. KEY FEATURES:An extraordinary atmosphereWe’ve put a lot of work to make Archaica’s world a beautiful and remarkable place. Every single level has it’s unique atmosphere and encourages further exploration. Interesting puzzlesWe’ve prototyped hundreds of puzzles but selected only some of them. This ensured puzzles are unique and each of them is based on a separate idea. Additionally there are several mysterious devices occurring only in particular places making them even more unique. Ancient artifacts and secretsArchaica’s world is full of secrets ready to be revealed: artifacts and devices that help you develop your Totem, mysterious ancient machines and their purpose awaiting to be discovered, and entire secret rounds where hint is not available.
