
							About This Content
							This free extension of Farming Simulator 2013 offers a selection of authentic vehicles and equipment letting you to reconnect with the early years of modern agriculture!
You will be pleased to find classic vehicles such as Deutz D80, Güldner G40, Hatz H340, Hürlimann D110, Lindner BF 450 A, Schlüter Super 1500 TVL Special and many more!
