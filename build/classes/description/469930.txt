
							About This Game
							Pandum is 3D crossplatform mmorpg with arcadified gameplay.Some cool features are : In game party voice message on devices equipped with a microphone In game custom avatar can be uploaded from web site Arena team battle with ranking (see web site) PVP everywhere (except trade zone and dungeon instances) Free character skill path Free to play and free to win (shop doesn't provide items unbalancing combat)
