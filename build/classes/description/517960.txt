
							About This Game
							Be the last punk standing: Grab some friends and get in on some chaotic couch play with this clever game of crate action chess!Each arena is made of 100% crates, which means anything you can stand on can be used as a weapon.Crate Punks is best with 2 to 8 players, but you can hack the text files to add more. Geez, how many USB controllers do you have? How are you going to plug them all in?Shibuya, the shining downtown ward of Tokyo: now one of the cities gone lightless due to "The Cratening." After the death of the C.E.Overlord, the factories kept making crates, the drones kept delivering them, and no one could shut it down. The supply chain has uncoiled: now merchandise outpopulates the people.The city's piled with crates. And those few who live here, foraging on crate food,well...they don't get along too well.FeaturesShort, violent bursts of crate-thrashing gameplayHandcrafted and customizable MapsUnrecommended capacity to play 10+ player chaos matchesUSB controller support (please use controllers!)The juiciest crates in yearsControls and Human RequirementsYou will need at least one human friend with you to play!Up to two players can use the keyboardPlayer 1: A/D to move, J to Jump, K to "Crate"Player 2: Arrows to move, Num 2 to Jump, 3 to "Crate"
