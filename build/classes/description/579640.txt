
							About This Software
							Liquivid Video Fisheye Removal is an easy-to-use app that removes fisheye distortion from videos taken with action cameras or other extreme wide angle lenses.

The app contains lens profiles for the following cameras:
- DJI Phantom 3, Zenmuse X3 (Osmo, Inspire 1)
- Drift HD Ghost
- Garmin VIRB
- GitUp Git2
- GoPro Hero3, Hero3+, Hero4 Back & Session, Hero5 Black & Session
- Mobius ActionCam (lenses A, C2)
- Rollei 6S, 410
- SJCAM SJ4000, SJ5000
- Sony HDR-AS100V, HDR-AS50, HDR-CX220, FDR-X1000V
- Zoom Q8

Imports all common video formats and codecs in any resolution (including HD, 2K, 4K).
Exports videos as MP4 files with H.264 codec.
