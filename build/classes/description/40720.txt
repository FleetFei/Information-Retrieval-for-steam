
							About This Game
							Samorost2, the sequel to free browser based game Samorost1 is a short point-and-click adventure in which you must help out a little space gnome as he searches for his dog, which has been taken away by aliens.						Adventure full of surprising creatures and locations.						Beautiful ambient music by Tomas "Floex" Dvorak.						Relaxing and peaceful game with intuitive gameplay accesible even for non-experienced players of all ages.
