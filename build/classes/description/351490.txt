
							About This Game
							Blast your way through space in this innovative 3D bubble puzzler! Bounce, bobble, swing, and explode bubbles across the universe. Intergalactic Bubbles advances the classic match-3 genre into the 21st century with stellar graphics, 3D action, and upgrades."It's a great game!" - James Bartholomeou, Initiative CheckSectors full of levels provide hours of bubble blasting challenges.Upgrade your abilities to enhance your play style.Use power bubbles alone or combine them to unleash devastation!
