
							About This Game
							OverviewExperience the thrill of VR sailing in this breathtaking experience using your HTC Vive!DescriptionThis FREE experience lets you test your sailing skills on a realistic water simulation and in 3 different weather conditions. This VR simulation is really easy on motion sickness due to special camera implementation and "cockpit effect". You can use your Vive controllers to interact with the ship like a real sailor! There is not any purchases inside the game, this is 100% Free!FeaturesOutstanding water effect and simulation. Realistic wind, sails and boat movement. 3 different weather conditions: day, night & storm. Motion sickness free! (If you can handle the storm :) )
