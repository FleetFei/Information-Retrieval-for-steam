
							About This Game
							Tcheco in the Castle of Lucio is an exciting, fast-paced action game.Help Tcheco the Adventure Boy to leave a mysterious castle full of deadly creatures! Be ready for a big challenge, full of nonsense humor and retro goodness.FEATURES:2D platform game, perfect for classic Famicom/NES game fans! Graphics and sounds inspired by 8-bit aesthetics. Compatible with Xbox gamepad and keyboard. 65 rooms of progressive difficulty. Easy to learn, simple gameplay.
