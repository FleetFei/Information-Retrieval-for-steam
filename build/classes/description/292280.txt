
							About This Game
							Grab you helmet and help SPY Fox catch Russian Blue and return the prized Limburger Cheese!That pretty blue kitty has heisted the famous fromage and it's up to kids to catch her! SPY scooters, jet-skis and ultralights are just a few of the racey rides kids need to outrun Blue's goon squad. Kids also put their creativity to the test as they construct challenging new levels using Professor Quack's Custom Level Builder!Challenging, arcade-style games for kids ages 5 to 10 (and children of all ages)Brand-new action with all the quality you'd expect from Humongous Entertainment!Save your game after each level and track the scores of up to 75 players!Junior Arcade games are jam packed with fast-and-furious game-play that satisfies kids' hunger for action and surprises!
