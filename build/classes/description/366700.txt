
							About This Game
							"Breakout Invaders" is a fun fusion between two classic game genres: "Breakout" and "Space Invaders". Play through 100 variety levels, with a lot of original game styles. Defeat the space invasion destroying enemies and blocks structures like castles, temples, towers, cities, cemeteries, cathedrals,... You can customize and improve your ships, collect the enhancers items, fight again final bosses and much more.
