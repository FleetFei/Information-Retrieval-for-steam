
							About This Game
							Stick 'Em Up 2: Paper Adventures is a run'n'gun/platform game of stick figure characters fighting through beautiful paper and card levels.With this Starter Edition you can play the first level and fly across city rooftops firing rockets from a helicopter. If your friend has the full edition of the game you can also use this version to join them over your local network and play the whole game: leaping between pirate ships with a sword, racing buggies on the moon, and more!Play with between 1 and 4 players with extra controllers or by connecting over your local networkPlay together in campaign mode, or fight against each other in VS and Team VS modes when playing over network
