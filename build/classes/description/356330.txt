
							About This Game
							Prepare for the hunt! Dragomon Hunter is a thrilling, vibrant anime-style MMORPG for adventure seekers everywhere! Gather your friends and travel across a huge world to track down and defeat over 100 exclusive Dragomon. Master hunters will be rewarded with glory and power: conquer Dragomon and add them to your stable of mounts, or harvest materials from them to upgrade your gear.A true hunter utilizes strategy as well as strength. Observe your prey, determine their weaknesses, and use the most effective weapons and traps to bring them down. Play as one of four classes and customize your abilities for the greatest chance of success. Join a party of fellow hunters to vanquish the most dangerous Dragomon!FEATURES: Hunt over 100 species of Dragomon Ride defeated Dragomon as mounts Upgrade your gear with materials gathered from defeated prey Choose from four playable classes: Scout, Mercenary, Mage, and Cleric Team up with friends to tackle the strongest enemies in Co-Op mode Play with the original Japanese voice-over featuring actors from world-famous anime series Play with a controller as an alternative to keyboard and mouse controls Enjoy a beautiful world brought to life with a vivid anime art style
