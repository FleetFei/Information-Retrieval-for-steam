
							About This Game
							You're one of the top pilots on Uranus, the temporary flagship of IV Allied Fleet in battle against aggressive race of aliens. The year is 2514. Sit in the cockpit of your space fighter and destroy enemy patrols lurking in the area.
Your mission is to protect the flagship at any cost, leaving no enemy to report your position.
The stake of the battle is the survival of human race and you're on the frontline.

Hostile Dimension is a dynamic space shooter that features:
- 3 types of spaceships
- 3 playable races
- 3 game modes
- 10 types of weapons
