
							About This Content
							The residences will become available for selection in Empire Mode, and the scenarios will become available for selection.

* How to use
The residence can be selected by going to "Settings" -> "Select Residence" under the "Info Menu".
The scenario can be selected by going to "Downloadable Content" under "Select Scenario".
