
							About This Content
							The Maker's Eden is a first-person hypertext adventure game presented in the style of a motion comic. You play a character trying to discover what their predefined purpose is, in a science fiction/noir inspired world filled with androids, flying cars and an early 20th century vibe.The game has been built around the music from the beginning and the tracks are a combination of smooth jazzy sounds with electronica.Tracks - Act 1:01 - Iron Skies (Introduction Theme)02 - A Lost Soul (Title Music)03 - Delicate Machinery (Atmospheric Gameplay)04 - An Unlikely Sleuth (Exploration Gameplay)05 - Boilerplate Prison (Cinematic Gameplay)06 - Surveillance Drone07 - Stasis Pod DreamsTracks - Act 2:01 - More is Revealed02 - In the Darkened Rain (Title Theme) 03 - Treading Lightly (Atmospheric Gameplay) 04 - An Unlikely Alliance (Exploration Gameplay)05 - Giving Chase (Cinematic Gameplay)06 - Power Station07 - A Perfect Future
