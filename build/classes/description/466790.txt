
							About This Game
							PixBit  is a small game in which your objective is to survive running into an enemy, collect as many crystals as possible and to keep the enemies from reaching one of the points. When a crystal is picked up a new crystal randomly appears somewhere on the map and one of the weapons appears in your hands.In the game there are
15 kinds of weapons15 different maps7 enemies10 charactersYou can unlock them all by achieving certain goals in the game.
