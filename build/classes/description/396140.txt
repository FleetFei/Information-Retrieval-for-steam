
							About This Video
							Mel Gibson and Tina Turner star in the third film of the Mad Max series.Max stumbles onto a desert city in the post-apocalyptic
world that is built on the principle of unbridled, cutthroat capitalism where anything--even a human life--can be bartered. Caught
in a power struggle between the city's two rulers, Max breaks the one inviolable rule of a city with no other rules when he refuses
to kill.
