							Europa Universalis IV: Wealth of Nations E-bookTo celebrate the release of the Wealth of Nations expansion for Europa Universalis IV, Paradox Books releases this classic work of the same name, in ebook format.The Europa Universalis series, the heart of Paradox Interactive, describes the transformative period starting with the Renaissance leading up to the industrialization and the violent transformations of Napoleon Bonaparte. The Wealth of Nations by Adam Smith is one of the major classics works that have come define and symbolize this change.This work is in the public domain. It includes a new preface by Fredrik Wester, the CEO of Paradox Interactive.Instructions are as follows: ========================== Upon purchase, this book will be downloaded to your computer in two formats: epub and mobi (Kindle). To access your ebook, navigate to the ebook folder in your Europa Universalis IV game folder.* Right click Europa Universalis IV in your Steam Library * Select Properties * Go to the Local Files tab * Click Browse Local Files * Open the ebook folder* To read the book on your mobile device, transfer the epub or mobi file to the device and open the file in your ebook reader app. Enjoy!
