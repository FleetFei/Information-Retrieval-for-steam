
							About This Game
							Paintey is a freeform do whatever you want painting simulator!You can paint everything from your face to the entire environment.Key features:Paint all the things!Paint balloons!Googly eyes to stick onto anything!An innovative (Optional) climbing locomotion system!Birds!Twitch chat integration! - Optionally spawns users from your twitch chat into the game.Coming soon:More levelsMore interactivity overallMultiplayer!Time trial mode?Paintey does not have any sort of predefined goals, you just have fun and mess around!
