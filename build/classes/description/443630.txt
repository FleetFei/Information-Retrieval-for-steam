
							About This Game
							Chicku is a nail bitingly hard platformer in which the player has to escape the Barrington farm. You’ll be forced to dodge saws, spikes and many other deadly traps in order to save your brethren and rise against the evil Dr. Barrington. Play alone or with friends in this epic adventure and slaughter save millions of chickens along the way.When you’re done saving your species, challenge your friends’ flying skills or their ability to handle a weapon in the bloodthirsty cockfight arena.Also, why not wear a fancy hat while you do it?Playing with a controller/gamepad is highly recommended Features:     Explore six major environments with well over 200 maps.      Local multiplayer, play together with friends. Just as easy as picking up your controller and pushing a button.      Master the maps to gather coins to unlock cosmetics.     Learn to fly as a chicken! A unique way of controlling the character which is easy to learn, but hard to master!     Replay your favorite environments in our new brawl mode with crazy modifiers to alter the gameplay.
