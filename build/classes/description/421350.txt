
							About This Video
							Independent writer and director Kevin Smith and Jason Mewes return as best buddies Jay and Silent Bob in JAY AND SILENT BOB STRIKE BACK. When they discover that a major motion picture is being based on their likenesses, the dopey duo head for Hollywood to claim the movie money they deserve. Packed with stars, including Ben Affleck, Will Ferrell, Tracy Morgan, Jon Stewart and many more.
