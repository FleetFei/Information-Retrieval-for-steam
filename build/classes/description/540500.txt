
							About This Game
							Free multiplayer is available in the demo!Strap into the cockpit of your Arduxim fighter and take on enemy starships in this first-person tactical space shooter. Navigate through dangerous ice crystal fields, take hyper gates to other systems, take on enormous capital ships and more.Includes:A twelve mission story based campaign covering your first tour of duty on the front linesMultiplayer to test your skills against other pilots and AI botsSurvival/horde challenge mode with online leaderboardTutorialPractice modeStarfighter Arduxim requires a gamepad, joystick or HOTAS. Controls are fully customisable.
