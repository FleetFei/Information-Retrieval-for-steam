
							About This Game
							Hero Quest is a Tower-battle in the most literal sense! Defeat waves of enemies as they attempt to overthrow your keep! An extension on the freeware flash game- this is your chance to own your very own copy of this highly addictive game for your Steam library! Main Features:Use spells ranging from freeze, fire and thunder to gain the edge in battle! Gain the upper-hand and destroy your enemy's castle! Upgrade your units and spells to become the mightiest general!
