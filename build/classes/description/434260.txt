
							About This Game
							If you are looking for a next generation gameplay experience and an engrossing open world environment...!!! Well, this is not that game... This is the touching story about a Mayo Jar. This is a clicking adventure that simulates the real-life tapping of a Mayo Jar! You will unveil its story and (maybe) you will learn from it, while you unlock 50 achievements!!Key Features    Unlock Random Facts!    Discover the 4 different stories!    Unlock 50 unique achievements!    Collect the hand-made Steam Trading Cards!    Compete with your friends and the world with the online leaderboard!
