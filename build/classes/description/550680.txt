
							About This Content
							A 5E Mini-Dungeon for 4–6 Characters Level 6–7Torren and Jelax are brothers who came to town about 6 months ago after adventuring abroad to open an inn. The brothers mostly keep to themselves and seem cordial, but rumors have started to spread throughout the town of adventurers disappearing that use the brother’s services. The local magistrate has discreetly hired the adventuring group to investigate these disappearances.5E Mini-Dungeons are small adventures for 5th Edition which are setting agnostic and are easily inserted anywhere in your campaign.Requires: A Fantasy Grounds full or ultimate license or an active subscription and the included 5E Ruleset.
