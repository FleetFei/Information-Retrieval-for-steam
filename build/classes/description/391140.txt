
							About This Game
							DescriptionBlast-off is a hardcore jumper, where your alter ego will be a ball of anti-matter seeking to leave our Universe. Anti-matter is extremely difficult to exist in our universe, because when it  contact with matter it annihilates. This forces the hero to reach super-fast speed to break through the space-time and break through the limit, where he will find peace in the void.Jump and shoot a lot. Enemies must be avoided to touch, pick up shields use the incredible power and reach more speed. With the first levels, the difficulty will only increase, however, the ability of anti-matter will increase too. The player himself will be able to pick out the right combo of all available active and passive abilities. In fact, only with them, you can defeat the epic bosses to overcome the gravity of black holes and to reach a fantastic life-saving speed.Features3 completely different locationsSpecial challenge zones9 incredible skills10 Steam achievements and legendary in-game achievementsSpecial boss enemyState of the art 2D graphicsOver 30 minutes of original soundtrackEndless competitive mode of the game
