
							About This Content
							The Onikira Bonus Contents Pack includes 4 tracks from the Onikira Soundtrack, to be expanded upon throughout Early Access.

Contents from this pack can be found in your Steam game directory under Soundtrack.

Tracks include:
Track 1 - Intro - Simon O'Connor
Track 2 - Onikira - Trailer - Simon O'Connor
Track 3 - Onikira - New Honour (level 1) - Simon O'Connor
Track 4 - Onikira - Imafuku (level 2) - Simon O'Connor
