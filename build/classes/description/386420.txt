
							About This Game
							Do you like Platformer Games?Help Job the Leprechaun to rescue his friend Eri on this retro style one.Collect all clovers on stage and find the door to go to the next level.A horde of goblins, zombies, caterpillars and many more monsters will try to stop you, use your Magic Hat to kill them all.You are not alone in this adventure, Catalina Cow and and The Elf will help you with their abilities.Will peace and quiet come back to Leprechaun Village?Features:38 levels full of monsters and puzzles.Retro Pixel-Art and Chiptune Sound.Full Controller Support.
