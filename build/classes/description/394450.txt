Smash the alien threat in a bespoke campaign against the Tyranids.COLLECT: Collect unique Deathwatch Space Marines. Customise them with a huge assortment of different weapons and wargear.LEVEL UP: Gain experience points on missions and spend them on your Space Marines to unlock powerful skills and abilities.
