
							About This Content
							Original soundtrack to the outstanding bullet hell game "eXceed 3rd - Jade Penetrate Black Package", created by Tennen-sozai and localized into English and published by Nyu Media Ltd. 

Includes 12 remixes of tracks from the original release "eXceed 3rd – Jade Penetrate", PLUS 4 new tracks, including “Providence”, the theme for Black Package’s ultimate boss, Undead Lord Remedy.
