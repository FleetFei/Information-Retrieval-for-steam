
							About This Game
							Glory Kingdom is an advanced tower defenced wrapped in a classic cube style, you have to battle it out against many enemies and fight to protect your kingdom. There are multiple levels and vast enviroments, and strategically spawn enemies you are forced to think carefully about your every move. There is a wide range of towers to choose, each with their own andvantages to support you with your noble quest. So prepare for battle your majesty and get ready to protect your glorious kingdom.
