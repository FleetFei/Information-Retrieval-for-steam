
							About This Game
							Brother Wings is a shoot up jet fighter game with vertical scrolling. In this game the player can choose between controlling Marko or Guida, brothers and pilots. The game has two forms, infinity and time attack, both with several ships and types of villains to face. 



Brother Wings é um game de nave estilo shot em up com rolagem vertical. Neste game o player pode escolher entre controlar Marko ou Guida, dois irmãos pilotos. 
Há 2 modos de jogo: modo infinito e o time attack, com varias naves e vários boss para enfrentar.
