
							About This Game
							If you love pixelated retro games, this is for you and get ready for some intense retro space shooting! 
Fight again waves of invaders in this arcade space shooter and collect power-ups to increase your fire power. Try to beat your high-score in Classic, Time Attack or Hardcore mode or challenge your friend in Multiplayer mode. If you like classic arcade shooters then you will love Super Destronaut.
