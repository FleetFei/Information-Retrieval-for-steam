
							About This Content
							The StR Drama music pack is a collection of 30 musical cues that adds great emotional feelings to your games. Produced by SoundtRec Boston and its incredible, multi-talented composer team, the StR Drama is an essential tool for you to move people.This pack contains: 30 musical cues, produced by using various high-quality, industry-standard sample libraries and real instruments, which are royalty free music to use in your commercial and non-commercial RPG/IG Maker projects. This can be used in SONAR Steam Edition or any compatible digital audio workstation with support for Wave files.
