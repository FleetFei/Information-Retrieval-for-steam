
							About This Content
							** Items from this pack will be delivered IN GAME to a Gift Chest in your personal Vault **

Gear to get you started in the Realm:
1x Backpack - This lets you hold DOUBLE the loot on your character!
2x XP Booster - Level up extra fast on two characters with this XP booster!
1x Uncommon Mystery Egg - This will give you 1 random pet to help you on your adventures!
1x Great Taco - Feed this to your pet to level them up!

Use a key and open a dungeon portal to try for better loot:
1x Forest Maze Key - This intro dungeon will help you get your feet wet and earn some gear!
1x Totem Key - Use this to access the Forbidden Jungle, which is slightly more advanced than the Forest Maze
1x Snake Pit Key - Enter the Snake pit and face off with a Medusa as you hone your skills!
1x Sprite Key - Use this to open a portal to the Sprite World, a unique and challenging dungeon!
