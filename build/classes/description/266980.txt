
							About This Game
							The legendary King Minos has commissioned a labyrinth bridging time and space. Once completed, this labyrinth, with doorways into all eras of history, will allow Minos to rule the Earth.You have been chosen as hero. Abruptly lifted from your routine existence, you have been dropped into a realm where time and space hang in check. Through exploration and puzzle solving, you must overcome the awesome powers of Minos and destroy the labyrinth by manipulating the very fabric of history.Beware - the future may not unfold in quite the way you have planned...FeaturesOver 1800 high resolution images representing over 275 locations.Over 25 megabytes of music and sound effects.Stunning 3D rendered, 256-colour ray-traced graphics.Hours of exploration within a photo-realistic world.
