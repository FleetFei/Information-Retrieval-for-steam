
							About This Game
							Leap through a charming and minimalist floating world in this first-person platformer. Face-off against the environment and fanciful adversaries using casual gameplay mechanics.Enjoy an inspiring soundtrack throughout all of the action.Solve and overcome challenges in a variety of levels. Survive an onslaught of outlandish enemies in the increasingly difficult Ascend mode.Master agility, perfect timing, exploit physics and collect rewards.
