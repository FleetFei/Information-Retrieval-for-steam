
							About This Game
							Sword of Fireheart - The Awakening Element is an action RPG in the classic '80s style.
In it you embody a young swordsman who must save his village from a curse that froze everything.

During the adventure a few inhabitants can help you giving tips.
Walk through a vast world facing a varied horde of creatures that need to be exterminated and puzzles that need to be solved, then bring peace back to your village.
