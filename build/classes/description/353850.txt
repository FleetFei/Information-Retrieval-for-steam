
							About This Content
							These tracks are the culmination of Peter's 2 year involvement with Reassembly. They are meant to depict the evolving atmospheres in the game, with tones spanning from danger and loneliness to discovery and wonder. The majority of the collection is ambient, punctuated with high energy tracks.
