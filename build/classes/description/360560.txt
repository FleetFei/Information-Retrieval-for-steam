
							About This Game
							Being able to control fate would be a blessing for many. To Miyon, it is her greatest curse. East and West Taria are in conflict, and Miyon's power will be the most valuable weapon. Meanwhile, Gai has been sold by his family into Miyon's temple. Will the two save or destroy one another?Cursed Sight is a visual novel about people's struggle against fate. The ~45,000 word story features four endings that are unlocked based on the player's choices. This demo contains a linear portion of the full game.Thank you!
