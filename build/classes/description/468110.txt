
							About This Game
							There’s a fire at the children’s hospital! The animal shelter’s been flooded! The hot air balloon's suffered a disaster! Save defenseless creatures and rescue the helpless in Rescuties VR!Designed for the HTC Vive and the Oculus Rift, Rescuties VR will have you dashing around your living room catching babies and tossing them into rescue vehicles in your mission to save the day. Put on that headset, pick up those controllers and get rescuin’... in Rescuties VR! More than ten crazy scenarios, ranging from a burning building to the International Space Station! 50+ unlockable cuties!  Cats!  Hedgehogs!  Babies of every sort, from punks to wizards! Supports HTC Vive, Oculus Rift with Touch, and you can even try it out with mouse and keyboard without a headset. 10+ edible powerups!  Munch a pizza slice to freeze time -- pop a cupcake for extra points -- or eat a cookie for rocket gloves! More than 25 crazyfiers -- mods that can be mixed and matched to make gameplay even crazier!  Play as a giant in a windstorm!  Or try scoring with phase babies in zero-g!
