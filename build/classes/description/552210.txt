
							About This Game
							Let's light a candle... it's the beginning of Sparky's great adventure!The Sparky's battlefield is the only 6 x 12 square lattices.Make an efficient route for Sparky in this new challenging puzzle.This game is lighting a candle by leading Sparky. All you can do is planting a flag on the route as a destination.Sparky will find its way to shortest distance, but you should know Sparky's impulse very much... Try to find the best route for each stage!Route Candle has more than 100 stages and the various obstacles don't get you boring.Find your own enjoys!Twitter @peakvox / @peakvox_en
