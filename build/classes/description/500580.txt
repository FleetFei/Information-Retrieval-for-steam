
							About This Game
							Kitty Cat: Jigsaw Puzzles is the puurfect game to cat lovers and jigsaw puzzle game enthusiasts. The game includes 84 challenging jigsaw puzzles with beautiful pictures of sweet, irresistible and adorable cats and kittens. There are 4 levels of difficulty adding to the fun. Puzzles will increase in size with additional pieces to place, and the quicker you solve each puzzle, the better star rating you will get.
