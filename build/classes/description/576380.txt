
							About This Game
							Play sequel of "Abrix the robot" game - new adventures of Abrix. Now with longer levels where several rooms are connected into one long maze. Thanks to this, it reminds the old retro games from the 80s and 90s.During the cleanup work Abrix robot got stuck in a room full of containers, crates and other objects. Help him find a way out of the room.Game features: 100+ different rooms. 10 long levels. Editor of the rooms allows you to create your own puzzles. Saves in the Cloud Steam, achievements and Steam trading cards. The unique soundtrack of electronic music composed by Ree-D.The game provides many hours of intellectual entertainment ranging from fairly simple puzzles, to the extremely difficult, forcing the player to maximum effort. You can also create your own rooms with the editor.
