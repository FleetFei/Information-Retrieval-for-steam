
							About This Content
							Toposim Middle East for FSX: Steam Edition provides LOD 11 / 19m Multi-LOD terrain mesh for the whole Middle East region, improving the accuracy of the default terrain mesh and offering a marked improvement to the landscape profile. VFR flying over the Middle East has never been more realistic with lakes, mountains and ground undulations all where they should be. Please note this pack corrects the default terrain mesh and does not include any textures.This pack includes terrain mesh for the following countries: Turkey	Iran	Iraq	Syria	Lebanon	Israel	Jordan	Saudi Arabia	Kuwait	Bahrain	Qatar	Oman	United Arab Emirates	Yemen	Cyprus
