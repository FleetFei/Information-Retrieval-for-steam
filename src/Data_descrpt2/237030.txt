
							About This Content
							Play as the mysterious new character TSYGAN, a rogue insurgent that joins the Core Guardians in defending the only enclave of humanity left on the planet LOEK III. Road to Elysion features a new area to explore with four new maps, new unlockable towers, weapons and perks! This DLC is the first out of four contained in the Sanctum 2 Season Pass.Note: Road to Elysion is included in the Sanctum 2 Season Pass and is part 1/4 in a series of DLC for Sanctum 2Key Features:New Playable Character - TSYGAN, the “Rogue Insurgent”4 New Unique Maps – adding refreshing new tactics and replayability.2 Weapons - Rapid-firing Gatling Laser & TSYGANs own handheld Ballista!2 Towers - Range Spire that dramatically increases the range of nearby offensive towers and the Slow Field Dispenser letting you strengthen your choke points further!Enhanced Enemies - From new support monsters that heal and mutate the attacking horde to a brand new aerial enemy boss, players should be prepared for this heightened challenge.Additional Perks – Customize your character even more with six new perks, including a perk that gives you an in-game pet robot that will fight for you!
