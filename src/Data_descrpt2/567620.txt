
							About This Game
							The Strayed - adventure platformer gameOne hot July day, the young adventurer Mr J, and his girlfriend decided to fly out to a small island in the middle of the ocean for some rest. This small uninhabited island was their special please where they spent their time away from the rest of the world. During this particular trip, strange things started to happen during the flight, the controls of the plane became unresponsive. The windows of the small plane began to freeze, and suddenly the plane was enveloped in a thick black smoke.  Mr J started to lose consciousness , but before he fell into the darkness he saw strange demons grab his girlfriend, and disappear into a strange vortex which had appeared out of nowhere. When our hero woke up, he was lying near his crashed plane in a unfamiliar place.Mr J. must find his way through the island, encountering dark caves, a temple, and some of the ugliest critters on the face of the Earth. There are many secrets and mysteries to be solved, and many coins and ancient artifacts to discover.The Strayed is a classic old school side scrolling platformer with an original storyline, containing over 30 levels. This game is still in early access.
