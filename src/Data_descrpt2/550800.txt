
							About This Content
							The official simulation of the popular Brazilian Formula Truck series - the most competitive truck racing series in the world - has now arrived for Automobilista. Experience the thrill of racing aboard the 5-ton, 1200 HP turbocharged super-trucks which compete in the series!

Originally released as the standalone Formula Truck 2013, this DLC brings along several graphical and audio improvements, new series features such as the 160 km/h Speed Trap rule, as well as all the advancements from Automobilista itself.

Formula Truck DLC is free for those who own Formula Truck 2013 on Steam, as well as owners of the Automobilista Season Pass.
