
							About This Content
							The Expansion One Deck is an expansion to SUPERFIGHT that adds over 80 new characters and attributes to the core deck.  This expansion contains characters and attributes that everyone will love, with the same broad appeal as the original SUPERFIGHT core deck!The Expansion One Deck comes complete with new Achievements, Titles, and Art to unlock!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Expansion One Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.
