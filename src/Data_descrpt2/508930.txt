
							About This Game
							Engage in a fantastic mini race in unexpected places. Choose your favorite ZRacer and fight your way through 16 tracks in 4 awesome worlds. Grab ZBags and unleash power-ups on your foes! Be in the podium to earn Zcoins and improve your racer with several new equipment! Master 3 different game modes and explore levels to discover the full history of each racer.
- Deal with Meatballs!
- Scape from dirty rats!
- Slide trough wet marble!
- Fight giant AntZ!
- Much more!

FEATURES:
- Classic top down gameplay blending micromachines and mario kart!
- Intuitive Gameplay
- 16 creative tracks
- 10 awesome ZDrivers with history and individual endings
- 13 unique powerups!
- 18 items to improve your Zkart!
- 10 Trophies
