
							About This Game
							MagixHome VR is an experimental home design game where you can use your VR headset to explore and design your own virtual room. You can add new furniture, change wall and furniture material, teleport across the room, or even toss things around. Use MagixHome VR as a solitary escape, an interior design experiment or a place to juggle sofas! Features - Design your own room easily and see the magic transforms it to a realistic photos - Browse unique furniture and see if you love them before any purchase - Play around with multiple interesting floorplans from around the globe - Give a virtual tour of your dream home to your friends remotely
