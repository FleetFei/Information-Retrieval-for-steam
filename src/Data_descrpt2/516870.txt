
							About This Game
							Isomorph is an atmospheric 3D puzzle-platformer. Solve dozens of challenging puzzles by changing the direction of gravity and manipulating physics, while being immersed in a calming ambient environment.Features: 24 puzzles, yielding 6-8 hours of gameplay A gun that can collect and place cubes Gravity, Antigravity, and Nongravity cubes Interactive lasers, pressure plates, and timers A calming ambient soundtrack which evolves as the game progresses Steam Cloud, Achievements, and Trading Cards Windows, Mac, and Linux Support
