
							About This Game
							The retro magic of the NES-era has returned! This game perfectly recreates the feelings, moods, and memories of those magical 8 bit days. Two modes available: Try your skill on 1 player mode, Or, try 2 player mode! Supports both online, offline play. Compete for a new high score with family and with your friends, or try new stages. 99 tough stages will recall the feeling of childhood days.Be careful when playing Virus Crashers, and don't get too addicted!
