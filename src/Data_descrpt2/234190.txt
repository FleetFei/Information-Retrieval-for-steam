
							About This Game
							Receiver was created for the 7-day FPS challenge to explore gun handling mechanics, randomized levels, and unordered storytelling. Armed only with a handgun and an audio cassette player, you must uncover the secrets of the Mindkill in a building complex infested with automated turrets and hovering shock drones.Watch the videos to learn more about our design experiments in this game!Key FeaturesDetailed simulations of a Colt 1911 pistol, S&W Model 10 "Victory" revolver, and Glock 17 with full-auto modTaser robots and machine gun turrets with physically-based damage modelsBallistic modeling of ricochets and bullet drop11 audio tapes to prepare you to defend yourself against the inevitable Mindkill eventBecome literate in how guns actually operate
