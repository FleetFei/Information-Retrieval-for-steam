
							About This Game
							Crypt of the Serpent King is a medieval first-person hack'n'slash dungeon crawler with roguelike elements. Explore large, randomly generated dungeons.  Fight goblins, orcs, skeletons, giant spiders and all sorts of underworld creatures. Wield an array of melee and ranged weapons.  Jump over lava pits and spike traps.  Level up your character's abilities. Choose between 3 levels of difficulty, from casual to hardcore.
