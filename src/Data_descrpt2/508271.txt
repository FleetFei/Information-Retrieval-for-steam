
							About This Content
							Long for the bygone days of crinkly old game manuals?
Perhaps you'd simply like to learn more about the world and Ada.

Yearn no more with the Explorer's guide to Pan-Pan.
The guide is a detailed listing of the world, foes and hints to some of the trickier puzzles you're likely to encounter across your journey.

So gear up, get your nuggen primed and answer the call for adventure.

-----------------------------------------------------
The manual is located in the sub-folder called ”Manual” (directly under the Pan-Pan root folder)
