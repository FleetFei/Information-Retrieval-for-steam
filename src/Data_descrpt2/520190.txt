
							About This Content
							The original soundtrack for Sisyphus Reborn. Contains 7 tracks composed by Edwin Montgomery.

Tracklisting:

1. Intro
2. The Cave
3. The Desert
4. The Hermit
5. The Nothing
6. Sisyphus Reborn
7. This Path Was My Own (Bonus Track)

The tracks are in mp3 format and will be downloaded within the game folder in your Steam directory, for example:
C:\Program Files\Steam\SteamApps\common\Sisyphus Reborn\Soundtrack
