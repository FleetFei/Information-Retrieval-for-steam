
							About This Game
							Shapes of Gray is a stripped-down action-arcade game about rapidly descending through a series of seconds-long levels, each one containing a unique combat-puzzle micro-challenge. Turn your enemies against each other to get the high score and unlock the secrets of a tiny enigmatic world!FeaturingHundreds of randomized levels!Epic boss battles!An unlockable arcade mode with a sky-high skill ceiling!A rad micro-soundtrack by Chicago's indie-punktronic music producer Andrew Nee!High score challenges!Gamepad support!An amazing sense of discovery!Deep hidden meaning!?And so much more!
