
							About This Game
							Nancy Drew®: The Haunted Carousel is a first-person perspective, point-and-click adventure game.  The player is Nancy Drew and has to solve a mystery. Explore rich environments for clues, interrogate suspects, and solve puzzles and mini-games.					You, as Nancy Drew, have just entered a tightening circle of fear and crime as you explore a seaside amusement park that’s swirling with secrets.  An antique carousel horse as been stolen!  Now, late at night, the merry-go-round takes off on bizarre riderless journeys to nowhere.  Are the painted ponies searching for their missing lead horse, or is there another explanation for the midnight rides?  Take a spin with danger to unravel the mystery of a ghostly carousel!
