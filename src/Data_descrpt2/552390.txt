
							About This Game
							The main hero of the game is a druid who is looking for a teacher to gain wisdom, but his way lays through the magic forest that contains a lot of obstacles and riddles.In the game "Druid" you have to collect magic runes that gives you different abilities, solve puzzles and help forest dwellers to find your teacher.Features Magic forest atmosphere; Stylized graphics; Big set of different abilities; Large number of interesting puzzles.
