
							About This Game
							Hyperun is an interplanetary speed and score event set in the future. It is regarded as the most dangerous sport in the galaxy.

The game is a mix of driving skills, endurance, style and gravity defying roads.

Drive as fast as possible on an infinite and procedural road. You’ll need the best reflex to beat the many 90° turns and spectacular road patterns. Be a superstar taking risks by making awesome acrobatics tricks. Surpass yourself and challenge the world on online dedicated speed and score leaderboards.

Vehicle customization? Missions? Achievements? All included in Hyperun!
