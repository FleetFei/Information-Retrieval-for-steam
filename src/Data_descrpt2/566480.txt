							Deadlands Reloaded: Abracadabra and an Arab CadaverAdventureGather 'round, pardners, for a li'l tale of one man's quest to reshape the Weird West, another man's mission to resurrect evil in a foreign land, and how the posse gets caught in between. Oh, you're one of the posse? Whatcha doin' here then? You shouldn't be peekin' at this!Anyway, this is an adventure full of intrigue and weird magic, barnstormin' and minglin', usual and unusual characters. The locales are made generic for ya, Marshal, so you can drop them into your campaign, and the premise is flexible enough to build a long saga on. So hold on to your boots and away we go!Fantasy Grounds Conversion: Ben TurnerRequires: An active subscription or a one time purchase of a Fantasy Grounds Full or Ultimate license, a one time purchase of the Savage Worlds Ruleset, a one time purchase of the Deadlands Reloaded Player's Guide module and the Deadlands Reloaded Marshalls module.
