
							About This Game
							Breaking Bones is a survival game using the technology of photogrammetry (3d scan). It's a First Person Melee game with some shooting component (bow & catapult).Travel the world through quarries, islands, forests, mountains, deserts and more. Fight skeletons, dragons and dinosaurs. At your disposal a large number of deadly weapons, catapults and bows. You can pick up weapons from your enemies after their deaths. Each map contains hidden weapons, life potions and explosive barrels.In each level, there is a wolf in her cage. when you find the key and free him, he will fight at your side.- 6 maps- 18 enemies- 6 boss and 6 big boss- 20+ weapons- Catapult- Wolf
