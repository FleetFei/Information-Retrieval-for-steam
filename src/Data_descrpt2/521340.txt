
							About This Game
							From the creator of Pythagoria & Aurora NightsTake a quiz to test your intuition and knowledge - and learn some interesting and specific facts along the way. Your goal is simple: decide whether the statements are true or false. Some of them may seem pretty crazy, but they are true; while other "facts" are actually false. Is there a chocolate-scented flower? You will soon know the answer!Key FeaturesOver 200 surprising and entertaining facts. Calm and relaxing OST. DRM-free. You can play the game anywhere anytime!
