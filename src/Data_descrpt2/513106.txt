
							About This Content
							There were 1400 vans produced to the basic design of VAA-VDA during the 1970s. These wagons were designed with continental practices having a curve up to a central roof panel. There have been many uses of these wagons from wagon load freight to fixed work such as transporting bottled water from Inverness.The VGA/VKA Wagon Pack for TS Marketplace includes the following wagon Liveries:GreySpeedLinkWeatheredYellow EndsMaroon (VKA)
