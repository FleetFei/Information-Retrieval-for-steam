
							About This Content
							The Millionth Mile is the first free DLC for Wargame: Red Dragon.Focusing on the Eastern Bloc coalition, it will feature more than 60 new units for Poland, Czechoslovakia & East-Germany, bringing them in the 90’s and on an equal footing with W:RD’s main nations. The whole range of units will be represented: infantry, tanks, planes, troop transports, helicopters, …But The Millionth Mile will also introduce a brand new kind of unit, in the form of AShM (anti-ship missile) truck carrier, which are missile artillery units capable of engaging ships over a long distance, from land. 6 of those will be included, split as 3 per alliance.
