
							About This Content
							Created by Riley D. Owens (aka Darkwoulfe), this product consists of 25 top-down uniquely designed, 3d rendered tokens, depicting heroes, villains and monsters. You can use these to replace the letter tokens or built-in tokens in many popular adventure modules. Just unlock the encounters and drag these over top or drag them over top of the listing in your combat tracker.This Token Pack includes:    Black Bear the Barbarian    Caped Archer    Conan the Conqueror    Cyclops Attacker    Gorgon    Hannibal    Hoplite    Medjai archer    Medjai Spearman 2    Medjai Swordsman    Medusa    Medusan with Spear    Prospero    Proto-human Shaman with Staff    Proto-human with Bone Axe    Proto-human with Club    Proto-human with Stone Axe    Tigra    Troglodyte Axeman    Troglodyte Bladesman    Troglodyte Savage    Troglodyte Shaman    Troglodyte Spearman    Warrior with Chakram    Warrior with GladiusRequires: A Fantasy Grounds full or ultimate license or an active subscription and any Ruleset.
