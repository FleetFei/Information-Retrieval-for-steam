
							About This Game
							ZeGame is a beautiful puzzle game with complex but rewarding puzzles in three dimensions. Discover the new surroundings in every world, each having their own unique look and feel. Slide your blocks in the right directions in order to progress to the next level and discover new block types in every world. Create your own levels and share them with your friends, or play levels made by the community.Features8 worlds and 160 levels Stunning backgrounds for each world A level editor Browse levels made by the community Achievements and cardsSteam Cloud supportLeaderboards2 game modes Controller support
