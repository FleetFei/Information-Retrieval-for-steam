
							About This Content
							A great evil lurks in the dark and enchanted depths of this castle...A great villain must have an evil lair, and what better location than an evil castle? Evil Castle Tiles will help you develop a perfect backdrop for that final boss encounter.With Evil Castle Tiles, you can create a dark magic castle, a derelict crypt or a dungeon of torture. Unique details and decorative elements will bring each map to a new level, while the dark and rich colors will enhance the atmosphere of unease and fear.Features:A smooth, painterly style like the one you'll find in Royal Tiles and Dungeon & Volcanoes packs.Auto-tiles for walls, water and floors.Decorative elements such as statues, magical paraphernalia, and caskets.Castle staples, including heavy gates, torches, trap elements and more.
