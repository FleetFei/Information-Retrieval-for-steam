
							About This Game
							Parkitect is a business simulation game where you must build a successful theme park.Build the theme park of your dreams, and keep it running! Meticulously design that newest roller coaster, place the rides where you want them to be, and construct scenic structures and objects to make the park beautiful. Let your creativity shine, and when you're finished share it with the world!FeaturesTools to build the theme park of your dreamsA robust, piece-based roller coaster designer to let you build the greatest roller coaster you can think ofDetailed guest behaviors. Watch them take the train to that new section of the park, or read their map to find a rideBuild efficient transport infrastructures to keep your park stockedMaintain the illusion by hiding utility structures and the inner workings of the park from guestsExtensive support for mods and custom content
