
							About This Game
							The Legend Of Tango is a short comedy JRPG that tells the story of how Tango Fiesta was born.Early one summer's morning, the renowned swordsman known only as Smith is reflecting on his future when who should appear but himself... from the future! Warning himself of the darkness that is coming Old Smith nearly gets a migraine before disappearing but Young Smith sets out on an epic quest to save the world! He must gather his friends around him, battle his way through a devious tournament of martial skills that the devious Judges have set up to find the world's greatest mercenaries, and finally face his darkest fears......so what are you waiting for? Grab your sword, gather your team, and travel to the royal city of Mirmingham with Smith and his crew - enter the Yewrogamma Tournament and save the world from its dark future, if you can!Tango Fiesta can be purchased on Steam right here: http://store.steampowered.com/app/276730/
