
							About This Content
							For 4-6 Characters of Levels 7-8Venture into this pleasure den where nymphs await the arrival of your party. You will be well taken care of and become so relaxed you just may drift off to a peaceful slumber.... ZZZZZMini-Dungeons are small adventures for Pathfinder which are setting agnostic and are easily inserted anywhere in your campaign.Released on February 10, 2017. Designed for Fantasy Grounds version 3.2.2 and higher.Requires: An active subscription or a one time purchase of a Fantasy Grounds Full or Ultimate license and the included Pathfinder Ruleset.
