
							About This Content
							The fully unlocked “Lords of Darkness” deck. Embrace your inner darkness and know what it feels like to wield the power of a demon lord. Summon all manner of fiends from the fires below as you terrify puny mortals into making unreasonable bargains. If you enjoy consuming delicious souls and removing pesky fleshlings from the battlefield, then this is the deck for you! Send in your demonic minions to claw and rend your opponent while you work on darker magic to bring forth true terrors from the darkest depths. Additional content for Magic 2014.  Requires Magic 2014 Expansion.
