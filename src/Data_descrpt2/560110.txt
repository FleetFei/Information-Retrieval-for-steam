
							About This Content
							This module makes it easy to bring camp sites, mountain and forest trails into any campaign or ruleset.Ever find yourself in a game without a map for your players when playing online? A random encounter in the mountains, or an ambush on a forest trail? With Map Adventures you can have such maps at your fingertips!Included in Map Adventures #1 are low- and high-res map of the following:Forest CampsiteForest TrailMountain CampsiteMountain TrailA campsite and a watchtower to layer upon any map in this pack.Cartography by: Mysie Sabin, Anthony Catana, and Mark MandersConverted by: Alan Neilson
