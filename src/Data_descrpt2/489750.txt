
							About This Content
							This purchase allows you to boost a single new character straight to level 80. After completing character creation you will be given the option to make your newly created character at level 80.

Your boosted character will also receive the following: 

* Max Rank Combos and Spells
* Full Set of Raid Gear (Tier 3)
* Max Inventory Bag (216 slots)
* Max Resource Bag (102 slots)
* Complete Mount Training
* Purebred Tarpani Stallion
* 4 Royal Treasure Chests
* Additional Specialization
* 100 Expertise Points
* 20 Teleport Coins

Note: All items are spawned on your newly created level 80 character.
