
							About This Game
							Fat Mask is a 1-4 players arcade puzzle brawler game where you acrobatically create giant combos by matching small blocks together. BEWARE! Other players will try to steal your combos and you must defend them at all cost if you ever wish to win! Thanks to its unique mix between multiplayer platformers (Bubble Bobble, Towerfall) and competitive puzzle games like Tetris, Fat Mask is a frenetic experience that is sure to ruin some friendships. Steal your friends' hard earned combos in this crazy action-packed game!
