
							About This Content
							Bulleid coaches were introduced by Southern in the 1940s by the Chief Mechanical Engineer O.V.S Bulleid. Bulleid coaches pass a lot of their design on the British Rail Mk1 coaches build during the 1950s but are recognisable by the small window above the droplight in the door.The Bulleid Coach Pack 01 for TS Marketplace includes the following coach types in Southern Green, BR Crimson and Cream and BR Coaching stock Green livery:Brake CompositeCorridor CompositeCorridor FirstCorridor ThirdOpen ThirdSemi-open Brake Third
