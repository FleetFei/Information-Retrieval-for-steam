
							About This Game
							In Freedom Poopie you are a little and lost Poopie who was born in a dark and oppressive world and wants to look for his freedom arriving to the sea.
But that will not be so easy, there will be a lot of deadly traps, gadgets and enemies that will kill you in an instant.
Help Poopie with all your dexterity due his freedom become true using the shinny keys through this abrupt world.
