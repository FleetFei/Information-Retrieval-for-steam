
							About This Game
							Omega One is an amazing take on the shoot em up genre. The game will have a number of features: In game features:- Xp Leveling system - rank up your profile to unlock new ships and weapons. - Wave system with boss battles to keep things interesting. - Online Scoreboards. - 360 movement - Turn/Strafe/reverse and boost your way in and out of battle. - Split screen Vs mode. - Offline practice mode. - Online competitive mode. - Many enemies and enemy types.- Many different power-ups to keep you alive in battle. - MAYHEM! Other features:- Full controller support - Steam achievements - Steam trading cards- Current Trailer song by: youtube user/Lyrics22ful/-
