
							About This Content
							The Anime Deck is an expansion to SUPERFIGHT that adds over 80 cards inspired by your favorite anime.  From pocket monsters, to titans, to sailor scouts, this deck is ready to raise your fights to a new level of insanity, the kind you can only find in Japan.The Anime Deck comes complete with new Achievements, Titles, and Art to unlock!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Anime Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.
