
							About This Content
							This piece of DLC is a set of 25 Wallpapers, which showcases the talents of Enrique Belatre showing characters from the games in a variety of fun, silly, and possibly compromising situations.

The contents of which will be viewable as a new 'wallpapers' folder in the games main directory once the dlc is purchased and downloaded.

The pack currently includes 25 Wallpapers.
