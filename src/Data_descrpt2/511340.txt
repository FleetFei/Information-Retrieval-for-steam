
							About This Game
							DescriptionExistentia - post-apocalyptic adveture puzzle game with dark existential atmosphere. As one of the few survivors after nuclear disaster you will explore ruins of the post-Soviet republic. While gathering supplies during your sortie from the shelter you will find out a stories about the world which will never be the same. The lonely wanderer decides to go on his way after few months of a pause. He was hidden too long but now he is going home.
