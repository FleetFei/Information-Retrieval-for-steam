
							About This Game
							The critically acclaimed mobile game comes to Steam!INCREDIBLE ADVENTURECrowman & Wolfboy are an unlikely pair of shadow creatures on a bizarre journey to escape their own nature and find the mysterious "humans". Help them flee the planet of shadows and the life-devouring Darkness following their every step. Will the path to “humans” lead Crowman & Wolfboy to happiness? Will they ever escape the persistent Darkness? Could a real life crow lift a real life wolf over pits of mutant garbage cans? ... What?!GAMEPLAY Crowman & Wolfboy is a story driven, level based, action-platformer/runner hybrid. The characters progress through a series of increasingly difficult levels as they attempt to flee their dark past. Throughout the levels, they'll collect orbs of light to push back the ever pursuing Darkness and learn abilities to better traverse the environments. KEY FEATURES• Over 30 levels of challenging action!• 5 unique environments• Revisit levels with new abilities to find hidden items• Three bonus objectives per level• Find hidden items, unlock ancient knowledge and save the babies!• Captivating soundtrack • Travel through space and time in unique play modes!
