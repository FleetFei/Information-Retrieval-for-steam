
							About This Game
							DED - a game in the shoot'em up genre. Your granddaughter has been kidnapped, and your daughter and son-in-law murdered. Having just bits and pieces of information about the abductors, you will have to save your granddaughter and find the masterminds behind these crimes.A moderately hardcore game with dynamic gameplay that will help you while away a few evenings.Survival ModeFAQ.
