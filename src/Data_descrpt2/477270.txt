
							About This Game
							Lightblade VR is a virtual reality training simulation for self-illumintated plasma blades. This is a childhood dream come true! Get trained by your personal robot and deflect all incoming laser beams!

Top features:
• Fight with one or two blades
• Master four different training modes and extra challenges
• Unlock five blade colors
• Unlock four hilt variations
• Stunning 3D visuals
• Immersive fighting sounds
• Beautiful minimalistic user interface
• The latest series of training robot
• Fight another blade master in the Desert Temple mission

This is a fan-made game without any in-app purchases or advertising.
