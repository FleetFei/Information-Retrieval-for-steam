
							About This Game
							Landmine Larry is explosive action... Literally.The Enchanted Forest has been overrun with an obscene amount of Landmines (and other explosives). Larry must clear (or avoid) landmines scattered throughout; While dodging traps, monsters and other surprises. Get Larry to the goal to clear the level... While trying to keep your keyboard in one piece.  The game's concept is simple, but the levels will have twists and turns to keep you on your toes.Features Cartoon style environment and characters. Challenging Levels... Good Luck.Easy to use control interface (WASD setup, with mouse axis movement) Numerous power-ups and weapons (Exploding carrots, cluster bombs & exploding sheep to name a few!)
