
							About This Game
							Adventuring Gentleman is a steampunk roguelite platform shooter where you can go on a number ofadventures offered by your clubhouse members. Each adventure consists of procedurally generated floor with enemy wave sequences unique tothe chosen adventure. Each adventure has three difficulty levels and lengths.

After beating each floor you are offered a choise from two powerups which will make you stronger, change playstyle and alter your dashing looks.

Depending on the length of an adventure you will face up to three boss battles with varied and very nasty adversaries with changing behaviour.
