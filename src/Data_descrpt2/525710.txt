
							About This Video
							Pelle is enjoying life with his girlfriend Ida, when a new superhero appears and the problems start piling up. Gæmelkrå is released from Dragensborg and rehired by the new CEO of Exofarm, Alicia Dufort. The summer takes an unexpected turn, and Pelle faces his biggest and final battle as Antboy.
