
							About This Game
							When deciding to learn card magic in a school of an old wizard you could not imagine that you would be chosen by fate as a savior of an entire world. Emerland is an ancient world of magic, where the fragile balance is threatened by the evil sorcerer Sat. Consolidate around you the races populating this fairy land: Elves, Humans, Dwarves and Mermaids. Only together you can challenge him!
 - Awesome and stunning graphics.
 - Mix of a number of Solitaire styles and Hidden objects
 - Fascinating scenario.
 - Two difficulty levels.
