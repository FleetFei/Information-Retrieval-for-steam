
							About This Game
							Eyestorm is a helicopter simulator game which takes place on a vast archipelago through forty exhilarating missions. The player must undertake rescue operations for survivors of accidents and shipwrecks, find missing expeditionists or transport important cargos, all within a limited time frame.

- Play through 40 exhilarating missions where you undertake rescue operations for survivors of accidents and shipwrecks, find missing people or transport important cargos.
- Choose between 3 different helicopters with its very unique own capacities, speeds and degrees of autonomy.
- Simulation or Arcade pilot mode.
- 5 exclusive helicopter skins.
- 9 outstanding islands.
