
							About This Game
							This is a Virtual Reality survival horror game.Do not buy it if you can't handle it.You open your eyes to find yourself alone beneath an unfamiliar night sky.  The wind howls around you, carrying the sound of insects chirping, rustling grass, and . . . something breathing.  Looking around for weapons you find only a flashlight.  Nearby, a radio cackles to life and a voice greets you, "Welcome to Raptor Valley!"Survive the night.
