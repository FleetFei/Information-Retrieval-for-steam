
							About This Content
							Garruk Wildspeaker’s fully unlocked “Pack Instinct” green deck. Garruk Wildspeaker is a hunter, deeply attuned to creature magic. Garruk is always on a quest for bigger prey, and his travels have taught him well. Garruk has tamed ferocious beasts from across the Multiverse. Use these beasts to overrun the opposition and watch them cower in fear as you unleash the primal fury! Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013.
