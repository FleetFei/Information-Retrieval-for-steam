
							About This Game
							C-RUSH is an old school Shooter, with HD graphics.A new threat is coming from the space; the plasma insects. Exterminate the vermin!!Beat your own score and compare it with the other players !Key Features:- One ship with different positions to use- Different Gameplay according to the positions and shoots- A Schmup where you need reflection as well- Works with the XBOX 360 Pad - 2 Difficulty modes- Compatible with Steam leaderboards- Achievements to unlock!HD video here : http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6kZeFqj0gNgC-Rush was made by 2 people only, and contains one world divided in 4 stages. This is a score game, the main goal is to improve your score each time you play.
