
							About This Game
							Galactic Conquerors is a simultaneous turn-based sci-fi strategy game with tactical gameplay and fast  paced, action filled battles. You need to fight to control planets and resources by mastering different kinds of commanders, units and their skills in multiple on-going battles in order to overcome your enemies. Orbiting planets and other objects create an ever changing playground for epic and massive battles up to eigth players.
