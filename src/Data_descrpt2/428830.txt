
							About This Game
							Save the Dodos! We’re all familiar with the sad tale of the charming, yet unintelligent Dodo. But now you can prevent their heart-breaking tale of extinction! Guide the dodos through 100 challenging levels and save this avian dullard from extinction.Since Dodos are not all too clever, you won’t be able to directly command them. Our feathery friends would just get confused and they have enough on their plate as is! You’ll be guiding the Dodos by reshaping the levels. By changing the platforms and obstacles you can guide the Dodos to safety and avoid mass extinction.FEATURES4 worlds to guide your dodos through; Prehistorical, Ancient Egypt, Antiquity and the harsh north of the Vikings4 special Dodo’s 4 game modes (Classic, Survival, Rescue, Collection)6 Steam Trading cards
