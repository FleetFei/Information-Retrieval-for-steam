
							About This Game
							Hilarious adventure game designed in a TV cartoonish style.This is the story of Johnny Bonasera, a kid that was beaten and humiliated by a gang punk.Fueled by an insatiable desire for revenge, Johnny Bonasera promises revenge against each of the punks who humbled him. One by one. Unscrupulous. Mercilessly.Features 2D HD graphics in TV cartoon style.  An adventure full of hilarious dialogues and puzzles. Outlandish characters to talk, interact, humble, beat, insult...
