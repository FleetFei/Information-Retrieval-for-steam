
							About This Game
							GameplayCaretaker Retribution is a six degrees of freedom first person shooter set inside a massive alien machine.You can play on PC and Mac. You can also play in VR using an HTC Vive or Oculus Rift. The VR is completely optional.If you have an HTC Vive Caretaker Retribution makes full use of room-scale tracked motion controllers. For all other VR platforms a Joypad is needed.The storyLong ago two brave pioneers set out into the expanse to find out why the stars were going out in the universe, apart from a single cryptic message that led to the discovery of the Alpha Device, they were never heard of again. Years later what happened to them was finally discovered. After finding their way into a Star Killer, Commander Andy Carolan watched as his friend and colleague Commander Alan Purdy was killed trying to disable it.
