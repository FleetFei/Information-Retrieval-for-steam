Can you find the missing dame with the velvet voice, or will the mystery remain as murky as the bayous and darkness that shroud the Big Easy? And if the goin’ gets tough, is the promise of a big bar tab enough to keep you goin’?The Old Absinthe House Blues is the first adventure for Deadlands NoirRequires: A Fantasy Grounds full or ultimate license, the Savage Worlds Ruleset and the Deadlands Noir Setting for Fantasy GroundsFantasy Grounds Conversion: Ben Turner
