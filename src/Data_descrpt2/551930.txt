
							About This Game
							A skateboarding game for tony hawk series fans!
Over 70 various goals to beat in over 8 big levels! This game has all it needs for a complete skateboarding experience:
- Over 30 tricks to beat
- get special and make some special tricks
- manuals and grinds with balance meter
- secret gaps to find
- combine tricks in big combos
- do spine transfers
PRO TIP: By default you have to press A+D same time to do a spine transfer. If you want to use only one button to do a spine transfer(get out of a half pipe) change the controls to use A+A for example.

Hours of fun gameplay in Tony Hawk's games style.
