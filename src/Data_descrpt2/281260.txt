
							About This Game
							The rules of engagement are simple; if they are armed and they’re shooting at you, they are the “bad” guys and you have unrestricted license to let them have it… The only thing keeping you and your buds from harm is your weapon, you’ve got plenty of ammo and you don’t have to pay for it.

Features:

Hard-core, non-stop arcade action

Unlimited firepower and unlimited ammo

20 multifaceted levels to beat

Powerful weapons to choose from
