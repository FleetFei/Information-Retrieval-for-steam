
							About This Game
							Take control of five cute, mystical creatures of miniature proportions called CreaVures.  CreaVures always travel in pairs, and they must use their special abilities to help each other solve interesting platforming puzzles.  Set in a world with beautiful phosphorescent colors, these tiny creatures journey to save their homeland and restore light to their dying forest. 						Key Features:Guide 5 CreaVures, two at a time, through beautiful and magical habitats & discover their unique abilities						Jump, glide, swing, roll, and bounce through 18 levels of interactive, physics-based puzzle platforming as well as epic boss battles						Use CreaVures together to complete puzzles in different ways 						Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of CreaVures						Atmospheric music						Lovable characters in whimsical cutscenes						Creavures now speak 6 different languages!  English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and Dutch
