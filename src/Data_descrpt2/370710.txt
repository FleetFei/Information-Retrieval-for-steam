
							About This Game
							PresentationLast Heroes is an RPG developed by Warfare Studios, the team already behind Vagrant Hearts, Ashes of Immortality, Valiant: Resurrection and Midnight's Blessing. PlotViktor is the new king of Abakam, but powerful forces are moving to destroy him and his kingdom. At the forefront of the resistance is Viktor's own brother, Viper. Viper wants Viktor's crown and he will do anything to get it. To stop his brother, Viktor must embark on a quest. Along the way Viktor will make new friends, experience dreams, and discover his destiny.
