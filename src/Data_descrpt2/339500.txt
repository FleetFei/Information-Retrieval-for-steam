
							About This Game
							SummaryBlasted Fortress is a physics-based castle assault game with voodoo magic and resource management. When you're not playing, seasons change, and you may come back to be greeted by disasters that happened while you were away or gifts to bolster your resources. It's a blend of physics decimation, hoarding resources, and RPG-style enhancements.If you're more creatively inclined and want to add more content to the game, you can even create your own buildings to siege using the built-in Building Editor.Throughout the course of the game you'll unlock new ammo types that can each fill a niche in your strategy; from brutish explosive cannonballs that stick to blocks and blow up violently, to cannonballs gushing acid that impart a vicious debuff.We're also dedicated to providing 100% free updates, so join up with us and discuss your desires as we roll out new content.Game FeaturesCrush physics-enabled castles with the aid of a powerful cannon that can fire an assortment of strange ammunition.Over 70 random buildings will stand in your way during a siege, and more can be added to the random rotation by saving custom levels in the Building Editor.Choose a difficulty level to increase or decrease the time limit, and select from a Small, Medium, or Large building to really tune in on the difficulty you desire.Purchase one-shot voodoo spells or gain powerful passive abilities by completing sieges and leveling up.Fill your Vault with stolen gold and watch it rain!One minigame that allows you to earn quick gold, with two more on the way.Any and all future updates, even ones that include content, are 100% free.New Steam FeaturesBlasted Fortress first came out in 2013, but many changes and additions were made new and exclusive to Steam owners!New maps have been built-in, bringing the total amount to over 100.Balance and playability have been tweaked in dozens of areas very carefully to make for faster, more rewarding gameplay.Brand new Plunger and Detonation Blocks have been added; several old maps and most of the new maps make use of these awesome additions.20 Steam achievements add even more replay value.Global statistics in-game for many things, such as total blocks broken by the community, gold vaulted by the community, etc.Bigger plans ahead!
