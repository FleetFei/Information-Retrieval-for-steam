
							About This Video
							Comedy Central honored him on its list of the Top 100 Greatest Stand-Up Comedians of All Time! Now Eddie Griffin showcases his awesome comedic talents in this stellar performance filmed before a live audience in Atlanta, GA. Known for his outrageous personality, keen wit, and in-your-face style, Griffin combines personal experiences with his edgy take on everything imaginable to bring down the house in this hysterical, politically incorrect concert film.
