
							About This Game
							$1 Ride is a casual game with a fast paced, timing and reflex testing game-play style. Travel through space at warp speed! Collect power ups to increase your speed or risk your fuel to collect money, missiles and more.$1 Ride Features- Collect power ups and loot as you fly through space. - Do battle with your friends on the leaderboards. - Unlock new ships. - A fantastic soundtrack by Eric Matyas.
