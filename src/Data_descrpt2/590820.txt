
							About This Game
							Pixels Mystery Dungeon is a roguelike game with pixel art graphics, inspired by the old Pokemon Mystery Dungeon with a larger RPG component, with the ability to buy and sell weapons / items in the stores of the citadel, to capture the enemies to make them your allies, and much more!In the citadel where you live a tournament was held to decree a new Hero; in order to enter the final battle against the current Hero and defender of the city, you must defeat all of the Dungeon bosses and prove to be a worthy successor.- 200+ Dungeon - 8 Different settings - 4 Different classes- 25+ Type of Enemies - 8 Boss- 50+ Skill - 300+ Weapons & Armours- 50+ Items - Subquests- Skill editor- Catch the enemies and make them your alliesWe are two guys from Milan, founder & indie developer of the PowerGaelic.The passion for video games has led us to start this project, despite our theoretical and practical inexperience in the field, we wanted to test ourselves in this endeavor and we managed, with great difficulty, to carry it forward and to finally land on Steam.
