
							About This Game
							Build, Battle and Brawl your way out of trouble!			Play through all three classic Indiana Jones™ movies and relive your favorite Indy adventures in the tongue-in-cheek worlds of LEGO®.			Explore and Discover - Battle enemies, solve puzzles, and seek out the world's greatest treasures.			More Than 60 Playable Characters - Take control of Indy, his friends, and even his enemies. Team up with a friend and together search for fortune and glory.			Whip Into Action! - Attack and disarm enemies, swing over perilous pits, and interact with LEGO® objects and puzzles!
