
							About This Game
							Are you skilled enough to guide Force in his quest seeking emotional stableness? Gravity Error is an indie 2D puzzle/platformer about changing gravity. Guide Force in his quest seeking emotional stableness in a set of levels and avoid being crushed. Solve the puzzles in multiple creative ways.FeaturesHelp Force in his quest seeking emotional stableness. 2D platforming using 4 gravitational directions. Creatively solve levels the way you want. Beautifully composed soundtrack by Agent Whiskers. Challenging levels to make you suffer. Supports 9 languages including: English, Arabic, French, German, Italian, Russian, and more.
