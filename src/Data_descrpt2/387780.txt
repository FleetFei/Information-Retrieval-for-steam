What Is Microsoft Access for Office 2013 & Office 365?
Microsoft Access is a database management system and is a part of the Microsoft Office Suite. Access for Office 2013 & Office 365 is the latest version of the powerful program that allows users to store and track large amounts of data that can be analyzed for different needs.
