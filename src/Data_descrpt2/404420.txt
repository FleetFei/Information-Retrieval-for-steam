
							About This Game
							Sun Dogs is about exploring our inner solar system, altering your body, and embracing death. In a future where humanity alters itself without a second thought, you must do the same. Skim along the Sun's corona, float in the Venusian clouds, travel the Martian plains.When your body dies, your mind will be given a new one, and you will keep going.FeaturingExpansive and unique science fiction, across planets, moons, and space stationsDynamic text reacts to your character and actions Full modding system allowing additions or rewrites to the entire game
