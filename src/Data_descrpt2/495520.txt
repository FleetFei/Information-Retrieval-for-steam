
							About This Game
							PING PONG LEAGUE is a VR table tennis game where you can enjoy a real match experience.You can enjoy the feeling of an actual table tennis by tossing and serving the ball, and hitting back to a different direction by adjusting the angle of the racket.You also can walk freely in the stadium and enjoy playing basketball free throw and more(while the menu window is off).Multi-playerCompete with other players from all around the world, interact with voice chat and hand gesture. You also can enjoy competing for weekly ranking.Single-playerHave quick fun with arcade mode in Easy, Normal, and Hard modes. You also can enjoy competing for high score.Invite FriendWith Steam friends, compete for table tennis.
