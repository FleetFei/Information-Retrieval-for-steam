
							About This Content
							Join the Guardians of the Galaxy as they attempt to to defeat a cosmic force of epic proportions. Led by the interstellar adventurer Star-Lord, the ragtag band of outsiders, misfits and swashbucklers must defeat Ronan the Accuser, and secure the mysterious Orb in their possession.

Table  features:
- Iconic characters from the film featured in full 3D, including Star-Lord, Drax the Destroyer, Ronan the Accuser and Rocket.
- Battle against Ronan's fleet of Necrocraft and engage in other key events from the film.
- Additional mini-playfield featuring the fight between Gamora and Nebula.
- A totally awesome soundtrack created especially for the pinball game.
