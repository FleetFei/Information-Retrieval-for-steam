
							About This Content
							The ~4 hour soundtrack to Even the Ocean, including over an hour of unused or old music. Composed by Sean Han-Tani-Chen-Hogan mainly from 2013-2014, with some additional work in 2015-2016. Also features liner notes on each track.This soundtrack is VB0 .mp3 format. It is downloaded into Even the Ocean's installation directory under Soundtrack/ . The soundtrack is not available in other file formats on Steam (see external links for where to buy as .wav/.flac/etc)Contents- Brief liner notes about each track, featuring the composer's thoughts on
