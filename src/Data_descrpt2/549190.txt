
							About This Game
							How far would you go to save your best friend? The core of Fetch is the sensational and fantastic journey of a boy attempting to rescue for his best friend: his dog, Bear.   It is a romp through the marvel of childhood, equal parts adventure and arcade. Fetch replaces the traditional brain-bending puzzles of the adventure game genre with high quality arcade experiences.- 50 in-game and Steam Achievements- Built in Strategy Guide- Concept Art- Re-playable Arcade mini-gamesOriginally released only to iPhone/iPad, now coming to PC/Mac and Android devices.We are grateful to all the loyal fans who pushed for the port to other devices.  We love the game, and hope you do too.
