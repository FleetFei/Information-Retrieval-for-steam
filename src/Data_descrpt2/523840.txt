
							About This Content
							The RunGunJumpGun Original Digital Soundtrack comes with all of the thumping tracks featured in the game, and includes numerous "Cutting Room Floor" tracks. All tracks are available in High-Res MP3. Additionally this DLC acts as an upgrade path to the game's Special Atomik Edition, and thus also includes a Pack of Exclusive High-Resolution Wallpapers, including Original Cover Art created by famed artist El Huervo.
