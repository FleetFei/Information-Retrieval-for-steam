
							About This Game
							Wand Wars is a fast-paced magical sports game. Players ride brooms, cast spells and turn opponents into adorable chickens!Magical SportsThe game's epic stages, colourful characters and quirky powerups generate competitive gameplay that is incredibly fun to participate in and spectate.Game ModesLearn the game in  Story Mode, compete for a high score in The Trials. Challenge your friends or the computer in Versus Mode.The SphereTry to control a powerful magical sphere that bounces around the arena, increasing in speed and size over time.Key FeaturesLocal multiplayer (up to 4 players).2 singleplayer modes - story mode and the trials5 play modes: Arcane Arena, Team Arena, Merlinball, Hexout, Team Hexout.Game changing arenas, powerups and spellsUnlock content with XPBeautiful pixel art graphics with a modern twist.Original soundtrack full of magic and wub.
