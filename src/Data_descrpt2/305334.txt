With the release of World Grooves Vol 1, The Loop Loft brings Mino's legendary rhythms directly to your DAW.Recorded in pristine detail at NYC's famed Bunker Studios, The Loop Loft tracked Cinélu performing on his massive collection of drums and percussion, resulting in a collection of loops that is a melting-pot of grooves from around the world.
