
							About This Content
							This is the Android version of 'Unhappy Ever After'  - available as a free DLC to anyone who purchased the game on Steam. Please be aware it is in a lower resolution and lacks some effects to make it mobile compatible. Saves are also non transferable.

In order to play the APK file you will need to install it on your android device. There are many ways to do this: in the announcement for this DLC there is a link that outlines a few methods.
