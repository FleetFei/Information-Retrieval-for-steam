
							About This Game
							It is a time of war in Europe. Driven by new ideas of Enlightenment, the hearts of men and women across the continent are inflamed with dreams of freedom and glory. Antagonism between the Empires is at an all time high and war seems inevitable. Millions of men prepare for battle, while the finest strategists draw up their plans for the domination of Europe. This is the moment for a military genius to forge himself a name to be remembered throughout all eternity. This is your moment.Key FeaturesSpectacular real-time 3D land and naval battlesComplex battlefields where units can gain higher ground, hide behind cover or use formations to their advantageA comprehensive turn-based management model with over 50 provinces in Europe and North Africa
