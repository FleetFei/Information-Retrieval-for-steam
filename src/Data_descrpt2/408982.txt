
							About This Content
							The StR Action music pack is a collection of 30 epic musical cues you can use to maximize the impact of your games. The exciting, heavy rock/metal influenced songs will get your heart pumping like no other! Produced by SoundtRec Boston and its incredible, multi-talented composer team, the StR Action is a variable musical item in intensifying your battle scenes.This pack contains: 30 musical cues, produced by using various high-quality, industry-standard sample libraries and real instruments, which are royalty free music to use in your commercial and non-commercial RPG/IG Maker projects. This can be used in SONAR Steam Edition or any compatible digital audio workstation with support for Wave files.
