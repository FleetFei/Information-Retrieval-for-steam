
							About This Game
							IN A GOOLAXY FAR AWAYBillions of year ago in a faraway goolaxy living organisms were combined with the goo nebula formulae. And the Algoorithm was created. Time has passed, and the Algoorithm remained incomplete...You are given a chance to solve the last 50 equations! But be careful, even a small mistake could end everything!Give it all you got and complete the Algoorithm once and for all! Become the Goomaster and be ready to face the final equation - the MASTEREQUATION... In all the GooUniverse, you were the choozen one...
