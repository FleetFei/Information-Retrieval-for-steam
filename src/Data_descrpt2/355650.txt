
							About This Game
							By the same studio of The Bluecoats: North vs SouthThe pirates have embarked on a treasure hunt, and the corsairs have embarked on a pirate hunt. Things won't end well! Davy Jones' gold surfaces on its own, throwing the pirate-infested waters into turmoil. Capture, pillage, rob and crush your opponents in this naval strategy game. Play alone or with multiple players. All aboard!Key Features:A large marine map. A strategic war game. Action phases to resolve combats. Local multiplayer or pass-n-play.
