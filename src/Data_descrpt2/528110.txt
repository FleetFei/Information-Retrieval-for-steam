
							About This Game
							Become A Tennis Coach !In this tennis manager game, you take control of a tennis coach and his player, and your goal is to reach the Number One ranking in the world, to stay there as long as possible and win as many Grand Slam titles as possible.Features:Men or Women Tour3500 players evolving other 38 years, from 1990 to 2027More than 300 tournamentsWorld Team Cup and Masters CupSingles & doublesUp to 4 coached players5 difficulty levelsFull ranking system (like the entry one from ATP)Important Note:You do not play tennis in Tennis Elbow Manager : it's a manager game., matches are seen only by their scores and events.
