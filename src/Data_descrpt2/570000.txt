
							About This Game
							With the award winning, critically acclaimed experience Old Friend, visionary VR creator Tyler Hurd delivers a fun animated reactive VR music video for the track “Old Friend” by the band Future Islands. Lose yourself in an overwhelmingly joyful world where only dance matters.  Gaze upon the elegant dance routines and enjoy the blissful absurdity with your jiggling cartoon body. Unhinge yourself, feel the ridiculousness, and dance like everyone is watching.
