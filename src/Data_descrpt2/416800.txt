Your disabled ship is running on limited auxiliary solar power that charges during the daytime. Can you survive seven nights to ESCAPE PLANET POULTROID? 

Gameplay: 
- Monitor cameras to keep tabs on the advancing poultroid. 
- Listen for signs of unseen intruders. 
- Activate bright lights to ward-off the nocturnal creatures. 
- Activate infrared night vision to stealthily spot critters lurking in and around your ship. 
- Seal cockpit doors and shield as a last line of defense. 

This is a Five Nights at Freddy's style parody game with some new mechanics, fun artwork, humor and VO based on characters from the upcoming adventure game, SpaceVenture, being released by the Two Guys From Andromeda(original creators of the Space Quest series)

Genre: 
Survival horror with a funny, kid-friendly(playtest with ages 10 and up), sci-fi theme.
