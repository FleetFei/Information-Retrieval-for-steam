
							About This Game
							Inspired by games like Ultima, Eye of the Beholder, and others, Tormented12 is a historical fantasy game based on the biblical account of the twelve Hebrew tribes. Encounters, riddles, and magic will be integrated according to that history, with a bit of old-fashioned RPG game play weaved in. Players select a Judge (a heroic title from the biblical era) from 11 different characters, each having their own set of skills and traits with which to deal with the threats/situations presented in the game.You Begin Your Adventure In Biblical Times. Around 740BC an orcish invasion (Imitation of the Assyrian documented invasion) is threatening the kingdom. You start your game inside the temple mount trying to push back the Orcish raid. Fight your way through slavery and Goblinian (Babylonian) exile and restore hope for your people. Discover holy relics and lost treasures. Forge alliances and rebuild the Temple. Fight biblical demons and become the most powerful Judge history has ever known.
