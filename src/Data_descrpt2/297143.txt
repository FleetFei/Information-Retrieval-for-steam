
							About This Content
							The RPG Costume Pack adds fantasy adventure to ComiPo!Thanks to this pack, your ComiPo characters are ready for swashbuckling adventures in fantasy worlds! The set includes a variety of characters wielding swords and shields in different combat poses, as well as villager characters to wander the towns of your fantasy world. In addition to characters, the RPG Costume Pack comes with a trove of backgrounds and still images to use for message windows. This material has uses outside the world of ComiPro and can also enhance your apps and games!This pack contains:10 male fantasy characters.10 female fantasy characters.39 backgrounds and images.
