							MEGA PACK 2This bumper pack contains over 600 game ready entities! The pack includes assets that can be used to construct game scenes based in castles, dungeons, caves, church interiors and temples. There are two winged demon enemies that will add some great combat to your games. The pack also includes a range of useful decal animations, these are great for adding special effect touches to your games. 20 Castle parts 187 Catacombs items 73 Cave items Two Winged Demon Enemies 77 Church items 21 Emission decals 14 Explosion decals 7 Impact decals 5 Light decals 25 Dungeon parts 72 Gothic Fixtures 28 Furniture items 72 Gothic scenery partsThe pack will effortlessly install direct into your GameGuru library. When you run GameGuru and click on New Entity in the Library you will see a new section called "Mega Pack 02". Click on it to start exploring your new library resource of game assets.All the assets in this Mega Pack are royalty free, meaning any game you make with them can be legally sold by you.
