
							About This Content
							Space... the final frontier. The time has come where Earth is explored and charted and mankind must turn its collective attention upward, to the stars.This resource pack gives you the power to create your own sci-fi maps and games with a focus on a wide-range of interiors for ships, space stations, bases, buildings, and many other futuristic scenarios you can imagine.Features:16 Tilesheets (A1, A4, A5, and 13 B-E style sheets).21 Animated items ranging from computer glows and lights/buttons, to sliding doors and view-screen monitors.3 Parallax backgrounds plus bonus Title image.4 fully parallaxed sample maps with separated 'floor' and 'ceiling' areas as seen in the game Sector 12.66 pre-made Sci-Fi themed character spritesheets.
