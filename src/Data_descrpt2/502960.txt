
							About This Content
							Containing over 120 creatures, Malevolent & Benign is a must-have monster source for populating any Pathfinder game.    139 Creatures    Organized alphabetically, by CR, by terrain, and by type.    Includes 4 versions of Faceless Ones.    Includes creature types: Abberations, Animals, Constructs, Fey, Humanoid, Magical Beasts, Monstrous Humanoids, Outsiders, Plants, Undead, and Vermin.    Includes 10 types of Outsiders.Requires: A Fantasy Grounds full or ultimate license or an active subscription and the included Pathfinder ruleset (compatible with the 3.5E ruleset).
