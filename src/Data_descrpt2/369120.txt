
							About This Game
							Note: this is the standalone version of Invisible Apartment Zero that was released in 2015; however, we encourage you to download the same story as a DLC (downloadable content) from the main application: http://store.steampowered.com/app/351790/Teenage girl hacker stumbles upon a secret that changes her life forever. This is a cyberpunk visual novel about the growing up of a girl from the suburbs who dreams of a life in the big city. It's also about a conspiracy, hacking, surveillance, but most importantly about friendship. Invisible Apartment Zero is part of a series and is a prequel to Invisible Apartment which you can also find on Steam.
