
							About This Game
							A ninja survival game where you wage combat in an expansive diorama landscape. Defeat hordes of enemies using shuriken throwing stars!

Increase your score by effectively utilizing hanabi (homing explosives), katon (fire breath), and explosive fuda (land mines).
Become more powerful by defeating enemies and ranking up.
Restore life with sushi.
Ramp up the fight with two powerful items: bunshin-no-jutsu and gama.
Play with others online.

With the ability to choose from five graphic settings, you can even play on your laptop.

*1 map only (1 map and 19stages)
