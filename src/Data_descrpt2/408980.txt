
							About This Content
							The Cyborg Collection features a wide assortment of Sci-Fi sound effects ranging from small user interfaces to massive robots. Everything from simple droids to powerful plasma rifles and futuristic transformations are included in this 3.2GB library. Features a variety of robots, interfaces, weaponry, vehicles and spacecraft included in the collection. Includes 3,000+ sounds that are the perfect companion for SONAR Steam Edition or any compatible digital audio workstation with support for Wave files.
