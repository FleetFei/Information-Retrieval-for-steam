
							About This Game
							Dungeon of Zolthan is a casual, arcade-style metroidvania platformer where your goal is to defeat Zolthan; a powerful mage that lives inside the dungeon.Using an array of powerups and your own wit and guile, guide your character through Zolthan's Dungeon! Dodge obstacles and projectiles, solve puzzles, and lay waste to your foes on your way to the final confrontation!Features:Good old-fashioned retro CHALLENGE!Classic, time-tested platformer mechanics!Metroidvania explorative elementsPuzzles6 Powerups40 Dungeon 'rooms'4 Bosses
