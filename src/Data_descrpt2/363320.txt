
							About This Content
							Void Destroyer - Mini Sandbox is a proof of concept - of the planned sequel to Void Destroyer - released for fans to enjoy, give feedback and generate ideas.While the "mini sandbox" has a lot of common sandbox features - it lacks the intended depth/polish of a full release and many planned features of Void Destroyer 2.For these and other reasons this DLC is free to owners of Void Destroyer.Features: Start with a small fighter - work your way up in the universe Expand your fleet with wingmen Complete missions for factions Collect bounties on pirates Purchase resources for trade - or steal them from cargo ships Persistent and living (though minimal) open sandbox Estimated 5 hours of play timeI hope you enjoy getting a sneak peek at a sandbox experience in the Void Destroyer universe.
