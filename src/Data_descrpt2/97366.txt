
							About This Content
							Jace Beleren’s fully unlocked “Dream Puppets” blue deck. Jace Beleren specializes in mind-magic, wielding illusion and deception, always keeping foes off-balance. Jace’s powerful magic can turn opposing creatures against their masters. With all of these tools at your disposal, you’ll be able to dominate your foes on both the physical and mental planes.
