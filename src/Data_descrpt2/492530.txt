
							About This Game
							OverviewThe Dark Legions is a classical real-time strategy game in the medieval ages. Command your young hero Theodoric, whose family was slaughtered by the evil King Frank Kalap. Build up your empire through story driven missions. Train and lead your warriors to battle and defeat your ancient enemy, or prove your strength against computer players on randomly generated missions.Game MechanicsHarvest the landBuild up an EmpireExplore on land and on SeaResearch new TechnologiesTrain your massive armyDefend yourself and vanquish your foes
