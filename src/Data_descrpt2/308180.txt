
							About This Game
							Jacob Jones is back in Episode 2 of his Bigfoot Mystery.

Following the events of Episode 1, Jacob and Biggie set off to the spooky Crackskull Mountain to try uncover the secret of Biggies childhood. 

Join the new best buddies on a brand new adventure and unravel more of the mystery in a series of brain twisting puzzles. 

More crazy characters to meet, from statically charged beavers to the charming Miller and his not so charming wife.
