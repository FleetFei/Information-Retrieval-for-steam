
							About This Game
							Adjust your angles! 
Time your touches!
Solve each shape!

Hyspherical 2 is an acutely tricky puzzle game where you must time dropping perfect circles onto different shapes.  Sounds easy, but timing is everything and Geometry has never been simple to master!

Make sure you complete a full rotation for each shape in order to proceed to the next level.  A beautifully simple but aesthetically elegant title.  Hyspherical 2 is sure to lure you in calmly but have you screaming as it gets more difficult.

• 150 pleasantly designed geometric challenges
• Try your hand juggling upto 8 different spheres on 8 different shapes! 
• A relaxing musical score
• Frantic and frustrating gameplay keeps you entertained for hours
• See how your friends are progressing in the map!
