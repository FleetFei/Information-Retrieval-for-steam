
							About This Video
							On the surface, Simon is a dedicated teacher, living a normal life. But he has a dark secret - Beneath the surface, he is a ravenous vampire, compelled to drink human blood in order to survive. Using online chat rooms as his hunting grounds, he searches for suicidal young women who satisfy his hunger and ensure his survival.
