
							About This Content
							4-6 Characters of Levels 8-93,500 years have passed since the vampire Daenyr and his followers terrorized the countryside from his dark temple. His name now myth, his temple in ruins save for a few remaining chambers; no one recalls how or why this came to be. A mysterious cult has taken up residence in the ruins, and rumors of blood rituals and a crimson god are spreading across the countryside, while many disappearances go unanswered.Mini-Dungeons are small adventures for Pathfinder which are setting agnostic and are easily inserted anywhere in your campaign.Released on January 03, 2017. Designed for Fantasy Grounds version 3.2.1 and higher.Requires: An active subscription or a one time purchase of a Fantasy Grounds Full or Ultimate license and the included Pathfinder Ruleset.
