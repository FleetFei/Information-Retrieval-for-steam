
							About This Game
							Tactical stealth action in a post-apocalyptic settings!Fight against the robots and reclaim the land! Attempt to survive in a post-apocalyptic wasteland, where it seems like everything wants you dead! Features: Stealth Action!  Intelligent enemy AI includes visibility and audibility parameters  The use of many of the game's vehicles  Futuristic weapons and items - Frickin' Lasers, invisibility cloaking devices and holograms!When, finally, the dust of the battle settles, who will stand victorious? Complex intelligence, durability and brute force, or human ingenuity? 

The choice is yours.
