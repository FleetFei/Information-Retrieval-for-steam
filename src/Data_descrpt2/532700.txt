
							About This Game
							Finque is stuck in a dream and trying to get out of her dream-world. 'The Eye' says he is the only one who can get her out. Finque has no choice and needs to follow his steps.Welcome to Finque's Dream-WorldThis world has its own laws of physics. "As long as you step on something, you'll never fall." says The Eye. Everything in this world is actually normal in this world but it doesn't look normal to Finque. She doesn't even know what 'normal' is. She will ask so many questions while trying to get out of this world.Finque's SkillsThe Eye gives her 4 skills. Finque will use them to find her way-out but she often gets confused. Finque finds this world hard to understand.
