
							About This Game
							The story of funny little friends who got into trouble and found themselves far away from home. They need your help to get back home! Your journey will be peppered with wonderful adventures and incredible tests. Fun, challenging levels combined with superb music and unique game mechanics will insure you’ll have a tough time tearing yourself away from a tale that’ll plunge you in marvelous world of adventures! When it was being developed, it received the award from PlayFirst Developer Dash and reached the finals of Casual Game Assotiation Innovate 2007.Feature ListNew Twist to Match 3 FunUnique Game MechanicsWonderful Adventures and Incredible TestsFun, Challenging LevelsSuperb Music
