
							About This Game
							Prompt is a hardcore platformer.  It is minimalist, it is retro, and it is very, very hard.Everything from the visual style to the design of every level has been coded with love, just for you.  Each level will give you a new challenge, and it will require you to complete it in a new way.Prompt has a full level editor.  Create and share levels with your friends!Must-have Features: Double jumping! Simulated gravity! Moving platform physics! Full 1-bit color depth! Extra widescreen 5:4 aspect ratio!The excellent music in Prompt was produced by Matt Simmonds.  Check out his other work at http://4mat.bandcamp.com!
