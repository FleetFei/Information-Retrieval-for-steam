
							About This Content
							For 4–6 Characters Level 6–7“When revenge calls, act as the tiger... with hard-favor’d rage!”Torren and Jelax are brothers who came to town about 6 months ago after adventuring abroad to open an inn. The brothers mostly keep to themselves and seem cordial, but rumors have started to spread throughout the town of adventurers disappearing that use the brother’s services. The local magistrate has discreetly hired the adventuring group to investigate these disappearancesMini-Dungeons are small adventures for Pathfinder which are setting agnostic and are easily inserted anywhere in your campaign.Requires: An active subscription or a one time purchase of a Fantasy Grounds Full or Ultimate license and the included Pathfinder Ruleset.
