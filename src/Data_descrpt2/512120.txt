
							About This Game
							It's the year 2120 where living on an orbital station around the earth has become a reality, home to more than 200 people with regular travel between planet earth and the station itself, HORIZON has become bigger and more popular then anyone had ever planned.

In Nakiti Generations you play as a heroine who has been caught in a huge disaster aboard the station that takes you crashing on to an uncharted world, are you the only survivor?
