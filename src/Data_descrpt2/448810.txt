
							About This Game
							Torgar is 4th in line to rule the Splitbeard clan of dwarves. He is also kind of crazy. These are the reasons, he decided to pack a lunch and nothing else, before heading into the long abandoned Deepgold Mine, looking for the shards of an artifact that will give Torgar ultimate power - The Mastery Crystal!Turn-based adventuring!Find 7 crystal shards to WIN!Permadeath. No SAVE! Procedural dungeons. Every level is different.Upgrade weapons and armor as you go.Uncover lore.Find food and potions to stay alive.Break things and find secret treasure!Learn special abilities, as Torgar levels up.Too easy? Try hard or nightmare setting.
