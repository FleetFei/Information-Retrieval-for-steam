
							About This Game
							Smithy is an underground rodent that happens to be an excellent weapons smith. 

- Blast your way through procedural generated caverns using unique weapons. 
- Face new and interesting enemies as you burrow your way deeper. 
- Find hidden treasures to purchase new weapons and upgrades. 
- Destroy everything standing in your way!


* To change controls or resolution, hold Shift when launching the game.
