
							About This Game
							FINAL FANTASY® XIV: A Realm Reborn™ is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) that invites you to explore the realm of Eorzea with friends from around the world.In the updated Free Trial version of the FINAL FANTASY XIV, you’ll now be able to play as your favorite FINAL FANTASY class up to level 35.Form a party and adventure with a multitude of players from across the globe.Forge friendships, mount chocobos, and board airships as you and your companions create your own unique stories in Eorzea.Set out alone or enlist the aid of friends as you undertake epic quests, battle familiar FINAL FANTASY monsters, and acquire unique abilities and equipment.All the classic elements from the series that you have come to know and love are waiting!Embark on the adventure of a lifetime to help reforge the fate of Eorzea.
