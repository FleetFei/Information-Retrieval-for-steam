
							About This Content
							These 7 basic Assist Gems are sure to be a big hit with novices! These Assist Gems can be used by all characters. Gain some great new abilities, such as easier special moves when used together!

Note: If you would like to see the effects of the Gems individually before purchase, you can view them in-game from the Gem List within the Store, or from Gem Unit in the Customize menu. The effects of the Gems may be subject to change.
