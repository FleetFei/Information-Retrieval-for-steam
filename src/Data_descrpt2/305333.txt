							All content included is royalty-freeAs a member of The Legendary Roots Crew (The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon), Mark Kelley's infectious bass grooves are heard by millions on TV every night.  With his first Loop Loft release, "Mark Kelley - Bass Loops Vol 1", we bring his signature style of deep pocket, fat tone and funky grooves directly to your DAW.Recorded in NYC with a combination of mic'd (Ampeg amp) and direct signals (Avalon preamps), Mark tracked nine complete sessions covering a wide range of styles including hip hop, reggae, rock, pop and world.  Each bass loop was meticulously hand-edited and sliced, allowing you to drag and drop the grooves straight into your production, giving you instant access to one of the funkiest bass players on the planet.
