
							About This Content
							Norse Dragons is the third free DLC for Wargame: Red Dragon.Dedicated to the Scandinavian coalition, it features more than 60 new units for Sweden, Denmark & Norway. Effectively propelling this coalition as well as the individual countries into the early 90’s era, it will bring them onto an equal footing with the other nations from Wargame: Red Dragon. Every type of unit is represented (planes, infantry, tanks, IFVs, …) including some iconic and long-expected “Northern” units: the Saab Gripen, Frømandskorpset, NASAMS, …And as a bonus, ANZAC will also get an extra unit, in the form of the Vickers Mk.11.
