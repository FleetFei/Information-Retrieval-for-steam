
							About This Content
							In the 4th release of Season 1, this pack includes 10 Boost Gems exclusively for Street Fighter characters. Equip these Gems and fulfill certain conditions for increased attack damage.


Note: If you would like to see the effects of the Gems individually before purchase, you can view them in-game from the Gem List within the Store, or from Gem Unit in the Customize menu.


The Effects Of the Gems May Be Subject to Change.
