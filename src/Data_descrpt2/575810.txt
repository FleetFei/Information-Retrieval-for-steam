
							About This Game
							Add action game and mahjong game, then do not divide by 2!
A game that forcibly merged action game and mahjong appeared!
Of course, since it is Mahjong, up to 4 players are allowed.
Hands that cannot be made normally can dramatically be made, every player can become a winner, and gameplay is excellent too!
A lot of new strategies that were not in Mahjong are included, so a new page...even a new chapter is added to the history of Mahjong!
