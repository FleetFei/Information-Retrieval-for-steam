
							About This Content
							This product is an expansion pack for Session Drummer 3, which is compatible with all versions of SONAR X3.Cakewalk has teamed up with ChocolateAudio to bring you the Chocolate Cake Drums: Jazz Kit Sticks for Session Drummer 3. If you have a sweet tooth for high-quality drum sounds then Chocolate Cake Drums are sure to satisfy. This kit was meticulously sampled by the artisans at ChocolateAudio to create some of the most authentic virtual drums available. Highlights:A Ludwig kit with Ludwig snare: 20" kick, 10", 13", 15" Toms, 14"x5.5" Snare, Paiste Cymbals.Recorded ay 96KHz/24bit at ChocolateAudio Studio, delivered at 44.1KHz/24bit.Recorded and Produced by Simone Coen for ChocolateAudio. Editing and programming by Simone Coen and Sara Rusignuolo.Kick: 15 velocities - 2 variations (round robins) - Total: 30 samples.Snare: Side 16 velocities, Standard Hit 24 velocities - 2 variations (round robins), Rim Hit 16 velocities - 2 variations (round robins) - Total: 96 samples.Hat 14": Pedal 16 velocities, Tip with 10/16 velocities - 2 variations - 5 positions (closed to open) (the 3 extra positions apart from closed and fully open are mapped as Percussions) - Total: 160 samples.Ride: 8/16 velocities - 3 positions (Bell, Mid and Edge) - Total: 32 samples Toms: 3 Toms (Mid and 2 Floor) 10 velocities - 2 variations (round robins) each - Total: 60 samples.Cymbals: Crash 17", Crash 18" Edge + Mid with 8/16 velocities each - Total: 48 samples.TOTAL: 426 samples.
