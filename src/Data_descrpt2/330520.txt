
							About This Game
							Tail Drift is a fast action racing game where you zoom through fantastic environments. Compete while flying around floating islands, ancient temples and desert canyons, with a fleet of unique planes to take through a variety of game modes. Purchase and upgrade your fleet of planes to gain the edge as you play through 18 unique events.Features 2 tours featuring 18 unique events to compete in 7 beautifully hand-crafted tracks to compete on 3 different event types including Classic Race, Time Trial, and Time Extension 4 unique planes to purchase and upgrade Upgrade each plane's speed, handling, acceleration and boost 3 unique power ups to give you the competitive edge including turbo boost, deadly mines and stun cannons
