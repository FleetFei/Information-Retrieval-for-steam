
							About This Game
							Repulsanoid is a unique but simple 3D Puzzle game, that features a one-of-a-kind control method and provides a wide variety of challenges based around it.

It tasks the player with controlling the bounds of the stage, rather than a character.
It's a stress-free, but engaging test of your reflexes and 3D thinking.

Guide the projectile around the stage, to mark every target within.
With a variety of controllable shapes, types of targets and obstacles, you'll find a wide range of increasingly complex tasks.
