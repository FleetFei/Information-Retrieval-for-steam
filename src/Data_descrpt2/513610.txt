
							About This Game
							MadRobot X is an old school shoot 'em up game. You control a robot trying to escape from the laboratory of a mad scientist, and to accomplish this, you'll need the help of GoboGobo, another robot who also wants to escape. The task is not easy; the mad scientist will do anything to prevent anyone from escaping.Features Pixel art style Progressive challenge Power ups Special skills 12 Bosses Bullets, bullets, bullets! Nightmare Mode Gameservices Scoreboard Gamepad support with 2 modes: * Normal: LStick moves the robot, RStick shoots and maneuvers the beam * Classic: LStick moves the robot and maneuvers the beam, buttons shoot Gameplay adjustments (hitbox, touchscreen, hp recovery, etc...) The ability to mute, not only the music, but sound effects as well And much more (like bullets!)
