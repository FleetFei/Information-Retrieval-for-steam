
							About This Content
							Mixed Booster Pack includes 11 new game cards (3 rare, 4 uncommon, 4 common) designed by Junpyon and Hono of Orange_Juice. The cards of the set can be purchased from the in-game shop and used in both single and multiplayer. All cards in a multiplayer game can be used by all players via the center deck, even if they do not own the DLC.
