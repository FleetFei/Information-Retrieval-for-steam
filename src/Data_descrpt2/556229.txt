
							About This Content
							Toposim US Mountain West for FSX: Steam Edition provides LOD 12 / 10m Multi-LOD terrain mesh for this topographically varied region of the United States, improving the accuracy of the default terrain mesh and offering a marked improvement to the landscape profile. VFR flying over this region of the United States has never been more realistic with lakes, mountains and ground undulations all where they should be. Please note this pack corrects the default terrain mesh and does not include any textures.This pack includes terrain mesh for the following US States:	Colorado	Idaho	Montana	Utah	Wyoming
