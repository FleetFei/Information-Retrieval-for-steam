
							About This Game
							The year is 1967 and you are a TV repair person. Get to work. In TV Trouble, you are presented with an endless conveyor belt of faulty TVs. Each TV is a micro-puzzle comprised of dials, button panels and antennae. Tune in and repair as many televisions as you can against the clock. Earn bonus points for speedy and efficient work. Gamepad and Keyboard supported.
