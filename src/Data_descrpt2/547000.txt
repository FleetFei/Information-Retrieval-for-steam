
							About This Game
							After encountering an alien creature in a bathroom stall, one man is propelled into a never-ending quest of sewer dwelling carnage! Septic Savages is a 2D, 1 or 2-player, twin-stick shooting roguelike with loads of randomized elements and hours upon hours of gameplay.Thanks to the power of HTML5 and Construct 2 you can try a demo of Septic Savages right now in your browser! Please see the above announcements to give the game a whirl and decide whether or nor it's for you.Game Features4,320 different powerup combinations!27 possible difficulty settings, from simple fun to insane brutality!2 modes of play: an 8 level campaign and endless arcade mode!Play alone or with a friend! Local co-op support for both modes of play.Complete controller support!Bosses, potions, enemies, biomes, random environments, catchy tunes, and more!
