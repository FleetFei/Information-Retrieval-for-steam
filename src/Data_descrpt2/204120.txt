
							About This Game
							Based on the last three Harry Potter books and final four films, LEGO Harry Potter:  Years 5-7 takes players through Harry Potter's heroic adventures in the Muggle and wizarding worlds.  From Privet Drive in Little Whinging to Diagon Alley, Hogsmeade and Hogwarts, players will encounter new faces, new challenges and new magic, preparing them for the ultimate face-off against Lord Voldemort!						Key Features:						Learn and use new charms and spells, including the Unforgivable Curses						Master advanced duelling skills and battle Voldemort's Death Eaters						Unlock over 100 characters, including Fenrir Greyback, Bellatrix Lestrange &   Professor Slughorn						Explore new locations, including Grimmauld Place, the Ministry of Magic, and Godric's Hollow						Play with friends and family using easy drop-in/drop-out co-op play with dynamic split screen
