
							About This Game
							Use your control of time to dismantle and destroy waves of enemies! Slow down time to dodge enemy fire and aim your shots! AmaranTime is a Wave Shooter that challenges players to survive using their guns, their wit, and their control over time.Special Features: Players can choose when to slow down time Players can Run in Place to move around the game along with move in the Roomscale are Players can pick up guns and throw them!
