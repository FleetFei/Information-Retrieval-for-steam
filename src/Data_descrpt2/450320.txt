
							About This Video
							Simon Leach is a very sick man. Thriving on despair, pain, and panic, he unites a group of broken and desperate teens and showers them with the love and affection they're so desperate for. But Simon has a much darker plan for his followers. When a young woman embeds herself in the cult looking for answers to her sister's disappearance, she discovers their dark secret and an even darker secret within herself.
