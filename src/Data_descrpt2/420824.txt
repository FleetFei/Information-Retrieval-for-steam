
							About This Content
							The FaceRig support for Intel® RealSense™ DLC enables you to add this sensor to the FaceRig multiple sensors update. 
You can combine the Intel® RealSense™ Camera (for hands, cheeks and tongue) with another regular camera (for face tracking).

 The FaceRig support for Intel® RealSense™ DLC only works if you have the  Intel® RealSense™ Camera.
