
							About This Game
							In this room escaping VR Game you will become an astronaut who have to escape from a monstrous spaceship. The puzzles within every scene are extremely challenging, be aware of those cabinets and draws, some of them may be hiding secrets. Pay attention to the minor details of every object as there may be important clues in there, those clues can be crucial for you to un-puzzle the mysteries. The Game can be  played in any space, but you can get the maximum expierence in a room size lager than 3m(length)x2.5m(width)x2.4m (height) .Instructions:Press Trigger to grab objects.
