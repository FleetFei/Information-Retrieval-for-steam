
							About This Game
							This application can be used by a child without the help of an adult, but remember that the interaction you have while playing with them is the best educational gift a child can receive.Discovering Colors - Animals features:- 12 drawings which come alive when the child colors them in!- 4 additional drawings to unlock.- A free painting mode.Ideas and tips:- Parents should join in on the first few plays.- Learn color names by saying them aloud!- Talk about the kinds of animals the child is bringing to life.
