
							About This Game
							The mysterious Symmetry Scrolls will bring you in the world of Fearful Symmetry, a dynamic 2D pixelated puzzle game in which the player controls two characters at the same time.It’s a tough game, with puzzles somewhat reminiscent of old Zelda games. It’s conceived to challenge a variety of abilities including reflection and abstraction skills, coordination, memory, timing, and others.The game will include:-20 levels set in 3 different environments-12 unique and fully animated traps-Emergent gameplay allowing creative problem-solving-Progress auto-saving feature
