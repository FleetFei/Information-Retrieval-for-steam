Basic goal is to restore 4 elemental magic books, earth, fire, wind and water. To unlock a book you need a key, which you will find amongst an array of objects in a HOS. Once that is completed you are treated to 4 three match games followed by a relatively easy spot the difference this will give you a short read about the magic of a mythical character such as a faerie, a water turtle, a  unicorn or something of the like. Making a 5 match adjacent to ice will cause an explosion that will move that obstacle and as for the cement blocks you will have to locate an explosive spear and let the liquid meet that for the spear to shoot off and make contact with another spear or the cement block and obliterate that obstacle. The journey can be quite long as you make the liquid traverse through tight narrow passages and patterned chasms. You have some power ups also at your disposal depending on which colour gems you match up. A spade will unlock one block of dirt (green gem), a bomb can take out the ice and rocks (red gem), a switch will change the position of a pair of gems of your choosing (yellow gem) and random will randomise the gem layout again when you get limited in good options (blue gem).<br><br>The game is unique not only for its mechanics, it's board fancy grandiose lay out, but also thanks to its background animations, such as an assortment of fish swimming around in a gigantic aqua world or the waterfalls that emit from the mouth of the altar when your fluid reaches them.
