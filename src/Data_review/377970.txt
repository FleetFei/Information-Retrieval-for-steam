This is an adventure game,  and it indulges in some of the worst elements of the genre; i.e. the puzzles tend to involve combing over every single screen and interacting with everything in every possible way until something actually happens. I loved existing in this world; I loved playing as Fyn, who is one of those cyberpunk protagonists who's always starving and strung out and lurking around in grimy clubs and working dead-end jobs just to get by.
