It still is a very good purchase.<br><br>Crown princess Isabella returns to Castle Greystone for his marriage to Prince Adam, only to find the castle in ruins, all rooms cursed and the royal family trapped in mirrors! All witnesses speak of a vile witch visiting the castle and putting a curse over the land, yet torn pages from our mother's diary indicate another forgotten secret to be uncovered. Will you be able to face the witch and save your family in the end?<br><br>Plot explanation aside, this game is a classic for the HOG genre. Sadly, the Steam overlay doesn't work, and the game has no achievements or such due to its age.<br><br>Gameplay is basic HOG; collecting items and solving puzzles. The ending scene is unique in the HOG genre, by presenting a boss battle - a timed HOG challenge between you and the AI - which was never used in any other game aside the series in the genre. Map has a teleport function and you are declared when you complete all there is to do in a room, marking it as cleared. By the end, the game presents you 2-3 hours of clean cut fun.<br><br>Princess Isabella - aside being a bit fluffy on the tone - is a good purchase for all casual gamers and a must have for all HOG lovers.
