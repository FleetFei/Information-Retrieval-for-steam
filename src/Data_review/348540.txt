After disrupting a battle between two mysterious demi-goddesses, Aaki learns that he is the reincarnation of the god Zeus and they insist that he must marry one of them and take his rightful place in the Pantheon as ruler of the heavens. If that wasn't enough it seems that several women he knows have also decided he must go out with them and all the while he has his college exams to deal with too. The story has some talented voice acting with lots of tongue in cheek humor as well as an adbundance of choices to make as the storyline branches. There's also a number of romance options available to Aaki as he attempts to choose his future wife be they goddess or mortal.<br><br>Graphically this novel looks really great. The artwork is spectacular and vivid. One favorite thing to note is that in many of the scenes the characters are somewhat animated in that they have various facial expressions when speaking, they blush or frown and their eyes blink as they stand silently listening to others during conversation. This is a very refreshing take on the typical static nature of the characters in many visual novels that I've previously read and it's a quality of presentation that I could easily get used to seeing more of.
