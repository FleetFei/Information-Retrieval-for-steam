The game itself actually runs nearly flawlessly and it's still so early in dev.<br><br>Deckbound Heroes is a turn-based, strategic TCG where you use abilities from several cards to destroy your opponent. Unlike games such as MTG, you can see your opponent's cards and there is no deck to randomly draw from. Everything is laid out, so you have to plan accordingly. You start at 15 life and get to use two abilities per turn. Since you have a maximum of seven cards in your deck, rather than 60 for MTG for example, you might think it could end quicker or have less things to do, but that isn't the case. I won't go into detail, but this game uses the Bitcoin blockchain to generate unique cards, while also using it and their own system to keep track of who owns which cards. At the time of writing, I have 399 cards and not one of them is the same. There are thousands of different possible names, and thousands of abilities that can be assigned to the cards, depending on where in their 3D map it spawned from the blockchain. Also, they'll be able to be traded with other players and put into the nomad pool where players can use those cards to play without having to buy booster packs. Your unused cards will be leveled up by other players! Those features will come later. I'll revise this review when they are live.<br><br>There will be four different playable card types: hero, minion, structure, and rune. Currently you can only use hero cards, but the other three will give different abilities and effects. Minion cards will act as buffs, structures will be passive abilities, and rune cards will be stronger versions of heroes. Curiosity cards are another type and are collectible but aren't playable and come from curiosities, which you get in packs and will give you something random.<br><br>I mention all this before it's in the game because I want to point out how fun this game is, even without all those features implemented yet! If you are a fan of strategic TCGs at all, you should check it out. <br><br>Everything I have mentioned are pros for this game.
