What I like to see further into development is more resolution options, few more track and combos maybe, rebindble keys not only for keyboard, but also for controller. I try both methods, but controller is more fun. Since I don't have Xbox 360 controller (which by the way is supported), I had to use third party software. It's promising title, not very expensive and updates come fairly frequently. Just because it's in Early Access my advise is to wait and see how much content there will be.
