Aside from some, and certainly not all, stealth mechanics it's a shooter, with enough gore to satisfy those who crave it. It's all about slaughtering stick figures who apparently are ideologically incorrect and copies and clones and bosses and mafia and crimes oh my. I probably have issues but remember, video games don't make us violent - lag does.
