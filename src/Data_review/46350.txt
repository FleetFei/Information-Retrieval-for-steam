Engine and visuals are decent, even if a bit dated. AI is quite nice, acting and reacting on your every move very adequately. Overall gameplay, however, somewhat lacking. I'm not a big fan of Total War series in general, yet I see some reasons behind downvoting reviews for this &quot;Real Warfare&quot; title.<br><br>All that you have to do in the game is about commanding 5-10 units in some battles, roughly based on historical events.
