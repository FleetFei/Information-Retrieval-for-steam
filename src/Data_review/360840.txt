You need to try can keep at least 1 of each of the types of birds alive within your swarm so you can use the abilities. <br><br>Throughout exploring you will find certain collectables and other pickups. The game doesn't explain what the other collectables are for so I have no idea. I found one that wasn't activtaed so I couldn't pick it up. But I had no idea how to activate it. <br><br>In the basis of the game there is prety much no challenge. But if you do want a challenge there are some very difficult achievements. These include things like keeping all of the birds alive, not killing any enemy and getting all energy. They will add a tonne of time to the playthrough if you tried to get them all. <br><br>Overall the experience was extremly enjoyable and relaxing.
