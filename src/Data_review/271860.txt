Personal opinion I guess.<br>Lastly you have a survival mode, which has no math, you have one life and max multiplier.<br>The game modes are well varried, and you can play around with them and find your favorite.<br><br>So I'm not sure weather to put this under Gameplay or Design but  think it may go best here.
