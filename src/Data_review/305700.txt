class=\"content\">\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo far, I'm really enjoying this game. I've always wanted to SCUBA dive, but I've not been able to. This game is realistic enough to give me a small taste of what it might be like. It simulates important effects of depth such as nitrogen narcosis and the need for a safety stop after a deep dive.<br><br>So far, the game includes two maps: Chuuk Lagoon and Belize. (The Belize map is 500 clams, but you start with that much at the beginning.) Chuuk Lagoon includes the wreck of the Gosei Maru, a Japanese ship that sank there in World War Two. Belize includes the Mesoamerican Reef. The player is given a list of &quot;Things to Do&quot; at each site, such as swimming to special areas, finding underwater caches, spotting different kinds of wildlife, finding artifacts, and, at Belize, cleaning up trash. I thought the activities were interesting, though some of the collections are very hard to complete.<br><br>The swimming feels natural, though it took me some time to get used to using the camera independently of the swim direction. The player can hit &quot;N&quot; to orient oneself, but I wish that there was also a button to just reset the camera. Also, the &quot;H&quot; key gives help for the keys but doesn't mention the ballast vest controls, CTL and SPC. A player would have a tougher time playing without that information.<br><br>A couple of nitpicks: The Belize map has water currents, but they seem to flow randomly and without a pattern. I could be mistaken, though. Also, a sudden surface from a very deep dive still counts as a &quot;Successful dive&quot; even though in real life it would cause decompression sickness.<br><br>The game does have microtransactions, but except for extra maps (in the future) it all seems to be cosmetic. There doesn't seem to be a noticeable difference in how the extra gear works. While some people hate microtransactions on principle, I personally have no problem with buying extra content.<br><br>For the price, I would recommend this.<br><br>Edit: My remarks to another review were deleted by the reviewer, so to answer those points again here:<br><br>&quot;30 second unskippable warning screen every time during launch&quot; and &quot;Extremely long loading times accompanied by obnoxious music, even on an SSD.&quot; This is only the first time you play the game. The long loading time is because the maps are downloaded from the Internet when you first load it. The other reviewer must have just started the game a single time and then immediately deleted it.<br><br>&quot;Only ONE map is included in the game price.&quot; It comes with one map and 500 &quot;clams,&quot; and then you can get the second one for 500 clams.
