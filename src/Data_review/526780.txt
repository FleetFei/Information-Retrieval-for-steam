Why play these games? Screw those guys---I like running and jumping!<br><br>-Some modest amount of puzzle-solving, but with a focus on skilled execution<br><br>-A level editor<br><br>-A plot which is silly, vague, and impressionistic, yet surprisingly evocative at points, with elements of Hotline Miami-esque psychological horror coming to the fore in a very well-planned scene that I won't spoil.<br><br>-Short length and moderate difficulty (for the genre), which could be a plus or minus. (He's a square-shaped, unsightly drunkard with an orange nose.  Note, I am not complaining that he's a square, that's a good shape for this type of game!)<br><br>But you know, I paid two bucks for the game, and I'm quite satisfied.
