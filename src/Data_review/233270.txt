The main character \u201cRex\u201d is voiced by Michael Biehn, who was a protagonist in the first terminator movie and was commonly seen in 80's action films. You'll fight armies of cyborg soldiers and encounter some fierce wildlife, including mutant dinosaurs that can shoot laser beams from their eyes! Rex being a high tech cyborg himself can sprint at high speeds without getting tired, stay underwater without losing his breath, and land on his feet unscathed from a 1000 meter drop. Being a game that was worth $15 upon its release, that's a good amount of play time. I just wish this game had more story missions because I actually enjoyed the story more than I expected.<br><br>Although FC3 Blood Dragon is an enjoyable game, I still have a few complaints. There is no coop support, which is unfortunate because it would have been fun to play through the game with a friend. Rex can only carry four guns at a time, which is sufficient, but having unlimited carrying space would have been more convenient and more fitting for this type of FPS. The game has a fuzzy screen overlay that cannot be turned off, unless you modify a couple of files manually. My biggest complaint though, is you need to run uplay and have a uplay account to play this game. There's really no need to play this game through two gaming networks at once. I bought this game on steam, so I should only have to play it on steam.  <br><br>Complaints aside, I loved almost every minute of FC3 Blood Dragon.
