Artworks are marvelous, and site transitions are wondrously smooth. You keep playing the game without a notch of display quality dropping down. I have to add that the soundtrack was also the best in the series since the first one. Kudos!<br><br>This is a text-book classical P&amp;C adventure if I've ever seen any. It is a must for the fans, and a more than decent addition to all adventure gamers' library.
