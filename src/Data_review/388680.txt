The puzzle solving, while simple, is unique and entertaining, but not enough to keep someone captivated. However, the game makes up for its simplicity with its story. The narrator, and evidently the man who drew the stick figure in the first place, can be heard dictating different key moments in his life as an artist. The artist, struggling to look for work, faces the same struggles his stick figure creation does in the sense that neither of them are complete. As someone with experience in the field of art, the story struck a very emotional chord with me and was that much more meaningful. If you are looking for solid gameplay and replayability, this is not the game for you.
