You won\u2019t see him again in at least half a minute (or more for large maps). And it helps to avoid the situation where if you spawn on SL, you're likely to immediately die, but if you don\u2019t, you\u2019re not in the capzone, you\u2019re not helping your team cap, you\u2019re not protecting your SL. Survival in situations like these depends largely on luck: if you happen to spawn behind cover, you\u2019re good, but if you spawn in the open where one of the enemy machine gunners can see you, you\u2019re boned and will have to try again. Basically, an MDV is an APC with a spawn point inside. You can park it anywhere, but it\u2019s bulky and noisy \u2014 hiding it behind enemy lines can be tricky. When you spawn in it, you can have a look around and hop out when you think it\u2019s safe-ish. Or, if it clearly isn\u2019t, you can try moving it to a better position. It\u2019s vulnerable to satchels, panzershrecks and whatnot, so the chance to die immediately after spawning is still there, but at least it\u2019s not random and it\u2019s very rare.<br><br><\/li><li><i>No stat grinding or unlocks<\/i>. M, SdKfz 234\/2 \u201cPuma\u201d, BMW R71 motorcycle (with or without sidecar), ZiS-3 truck, Willys and Volkswagen K\u00fcbelwagen, STZ-5 (although it\u2019s used as an infantry transport) and even a farming tractor. Not to mention a good number of Italian and Hungarian tanks (even World of Tanks doesn\u2019t have these). There are AT and FlaK guns, boats, planes you can paradrop from, mortars you carry around and setup wherever you like. You can also use tanks to transport infantry.<br><br><\/li><li><i>Tank crewmen can get out of their vehicles and get back in<\/i>. It adds immersion, allows you to park your tank in a safe place, get out and gather intel with your binoculars (silhouette of a person with binocs is nowhere near as noticeable as a tank on the horizon). Tank crew frantically getting out of a burning tank is something you\u2019ll never see in RO2. <br><br><\/li><li><i>Team-tanking is fun and useful<\/i>, whereas in HoS you\u2019re often better off with bots even if you use voip. It\u2019s just faster to press W and have your tank move forward immediately. In RO:Ost, it is faster to switch to driver and press W too, but you won\u2019t be able to reload the gun at the same time. Or keep an eye on the enemy.
