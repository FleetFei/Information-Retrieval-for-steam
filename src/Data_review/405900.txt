This way you can build strong characters with lots of abilities.<br><br>   <\/li><li><b>Geo Panels<\/b> - GPs are colored areas in the field, which have different effects on characters standing on them<i>(like 50% bonus exp.)<\/i> You can change the effect or even remove the Geo Panel completely by picking up and throwing the crystal causing an effect to another place, or by destroying it.
