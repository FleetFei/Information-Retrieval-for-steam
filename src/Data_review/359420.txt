While it can be argued that the constant backtracking through multiple time zones to retrieve an item you need in another, or to use a newly acquired item in a previously visited location can get tiresome (since given you're returning to the same exact point in time, all the previous progress through the location is undone), it adds to the setting and idea of jumping back and forth thru time trying to fix everything.
