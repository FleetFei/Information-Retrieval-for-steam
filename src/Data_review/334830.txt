At the beginning of the game, you descend from the heavens. It literally appears as so as you play as a pink, luminous orb that gently floats and guides you to your objective. It\u2019s your job to jump from platform to platform without fail; even if mistakes were made, the game introduces a rewind mechanism which is activated by holding down the right mouse button.
