It's not actually easy for another person to kill you. Oh sure they can drive over you, but that's not as easy as it may sound. They can't actually just shoot you either. Each person has a 100% Karma level, only if your Karma level goes down can they start firing at you. Your Karma level will start to drop as soon as you start attacking someone's wall or base, then it's suddenly opening firing range. This means you can defend your base quite easily if your base is being attacked, but they can't shoot you as you haven't been destroying anyone's walls.<br><br>Unlike other survival games which demand constant logging in daily, this one has a mechanic which allows a person to leave the game for a long period without the need of again having to start the whole thing from scratch. As long as you keep count of the secret place you have buried your stash, unless discovered by someone by accident it should remain where you buried it, allowing you again to start off with the items you have thus far accumulated. Even with the developers constant upgrades to the game your hidden stash remains safe.
