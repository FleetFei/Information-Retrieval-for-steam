I love the comic book style. Even though it looks hand drawn or painted, the imagery is very vivid. I could only play an episode at a time, because the final images of each episode would stay with me, and I had to get up and take a break. The music just adds to the tense and thrilling feel of the game. As I write this review, I am listening to the soundtrack, because it is just that good. <br><br>I am starting to notice a serious trend in adventure games feeling less ridiculous and more common sense. Not paying attention to details can cost you a lot in the game, from losing trust with co-workers, to flat our losing partners to a killer. There are timed scenarios.
