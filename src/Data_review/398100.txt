The story revolves around Shoji's relationship with two girls his age, as well as the principal if you make the right choices that will include her in the story line. His relationship with the two main girls, Selina and Hoshi, is based on friendship for the majority of the game and his romantic interest seems to rise only when the girls themselves bring it to the table.<br><br>There is definitely a pacing issue in this game and the end of the game comes really suddenly, making it feel like the devs rushed it or simply gave up on the project.
