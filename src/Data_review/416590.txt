You keep it even if continuing after death.<br>Audio recorders can also be listened to and will provide backstory to the game.<br><br><br>2. The game is broken into 7 parts\/hours. After a certain amount of ingame\/real time passes the clock will chime and move into the next phase of the game. When you continue, the game will resume at the previous hour you reached. <br>     11PM-12AM is the intro to the game where you get to meet the dolls and explore the house.<br>     12AM-1AM is playing a game with Kiki (the female doll in a black dress)<br>     1AM-2AM is playing two games with Kiki and Mr. Tatters (clown)<br>     2AM-3AM is playing two games with Mr. Tatters and Chester (male doll in suit)<br>     3AM-4AM is playing three games with all three dolls     <br>     4AM-5AM is playing with Emily alone<br>     5AM-6AM is playing with Emily and all three dolls<br>     <br>3. During the first hour of the game, the game itself is leading you to a &quot;secret&quot; by opening doors towards the parent's bedroom closet. In the bedroom itself is a note that will explain how to exploit the &quot;secret&quot; that will lead to the basement. Without opening the secret passage, it is nearly impossible to beat the game. Find the correct wall lamp in the room to touch.<br><br>4. The white board in the kitchen gives hints on how to play with each dolls, the first three hours are a lie (due to winking face) so you want to do the opposite of what it says. When you hear a particular sound cue in a room, the game is telling you that one of the dolls has entered and you must deal with him\/her.  Kiki has a childish chuckle, Mr. Taters has a low sounding chuckle, and Chester has a higher pitch laugh. <br><br>5. These are the games each dolls\/Emily wants to play and how to deal with them.  Kiki wants to play red light\/green light with you being the light post so once you hear her chuckle look for her immediately and stare at her until she vanishes. If she is not in your field of vision she will make it to the light post (you) eventually. You can also run from her into nearby rooms which she may or may not follow.  Mr. Tatters is playing the same game but in reverse roles. Once you hear his sound cue, stop all movement immediately and wait until he goes through another two sound cues with the second sounding like a sigh. Once he leaves the room you may begin moving again. Chester is playing tag and he is &quot;it&quot;.  Once you hear his sound cue, run away into another room as fast as you can or he will tag you.  Emily wants to play hide and seek in the dark so once she shows up at 4AM, you are given a timer to find her in the house but luckily it's just the two of you. If you turn on a lightswitch, she will come after you for not playing her game correctly, so the flashlight is needed to proceed. If the timer drops to zero you lose and she will find you instead. Once you do find her and tag (touch) her, she will hide again in the house and the timer will reset. Each room has one possible  hiding spot so continue playing with her until the next hour comes and memorize the locations to save time seaching each room. If you don't find the entrance to the basement you may never get past this part since she sometimes hides down there. Also sometimes she is tricky but hiding in the same spot twice with each timer reset so check it again if you can't find her. Lastly, for the final hour Emily wants everyone to play so now you must continue to look for Emily through the house while dealing with the three dolls.  Run from Kiki (stare her down if you have the time though) and Chester but stop for Mr. Tatters.<br>Make it through this and you finish the game.
