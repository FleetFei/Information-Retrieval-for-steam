Sound, pacing and music all give these scenes a cinematic feel, even if the characters move a bit stiffly with limited facial expression.  How you present the material makes all the difference in the world.   <br><br>The best gameplay and graphics mean little without a compelling narrative to pull you in, and this is where True Fear shines. This is one of the few times I've felt the bonuses are indeed worth looking through. <br><br>Have I interested you in the game yet? If you were ever mildly interested in hidden object games, this is a great title to wet your feet in.  As for HOG and adventure game veterans, it's a MUST play!
