January 20\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\r\n\r\n\t\t\t\t\t\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\t\t\t\t<div class=\"content\">\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have a Bias, I love RPG maker, But now that all of the DLC has been released for this &quot;Season pass&quot; I feel I can make a review. The 'remastered RM2k tracks are nice. But I do not find them to be great for this, and it's only ten tracks, not enough to really use, but for side listening I like them. They give me that nostalgia feel that I happen to enjoy. The secon Part, the RM2k Hero's For those of us that Have used RPG Maker for YEARS, it's nice to SEE these characters redrawn to sizes we could use in the newest editor, But the only real use I can see them in, is the only thing Ive seen them in. Campy Tutorials in Youtube videos where game footage is used to explain things. Yanfly does this sort of thing a lot as there is no audio [aside from music] in his videos.<br><br>Continueing on, we get our first original content, Train stuff! Now this LOOKS promising. But those tracks... why so tiny? Those are great for mine carts, but a full train on them just looks so out of place and wrong. It's almost aggrivating, because otherwise the whole tileset LOOKS great.<br><br>Finally, we get some generator parts, to build kids. I understand that gender differentiation is more pronounced in older indeviduals. But, I feel that we could use some more face shapes, and features to really nail it home that these characters are different, as having the same basic shape for each face on the kids, really makes everything feel samey.<br><br>So really, it's a mixed bag. I feel that more thought should have been put into the package as a whole, especially with how many purchased the pack with their innitial purchase of MV, but things such as they are, where the 'wednesday freebies' that we used to get, being some of the RM2k character sprites, and 10 remastered tracks ect. Between those freebies and this DLC pack, if they were all one or the other, it would have been better I think. The kids generator parts, would make a fine DLC on their own provided they had more options, hell more options across the board would be nice for the generator. I for one would like to see Busts added to the generator.<br><br>Final thought: This package doesnt feel like it really is following through with its goal. It feels incomplete and despite the time it took to get all of it, feels rushed. Originally looking at it, I expected original content, things that would make me want to use it. In the end I dont know weather I will or not, but I know the Only thing I can reccomend is, pick this up if you want the stuff available now, otherwise wait for the indevidual releases. The BGM and 2k Heros have been released separately. Only a matter of time before they release the generator parts and the train set.<br><br>I do not reccomend this package for general users, Only for veterens who years past have used the RPG Makers of Yesteryear and like a little of that nostalgia.
