You play as a mysterious boy or girl, wandering around the Boon Hill cemetery reading as many epitaphs (messages on the gravestones) as you please, pondering some and dismissing others. Some may come off as funny, others meaningful, and some as downright depressing. There are hundreds gravestones to read and each message is different with its own meaning. This experience is yours: you may leave when you please and stay for however long you want.
