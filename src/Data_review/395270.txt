To begin with your mother and surroundings seem very peaceful, but this soon takes a dark turn. The puzzles do require some thought though; you shouldn\u2019t be put off by the starting area as that is the one I found the most difficult. I\u2019m not ashamed to say I spent at least ten minutes trying to get out of the shed only for the answer to be extremely obvious.
