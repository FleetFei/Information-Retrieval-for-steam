With a 6 round mag, a decent fire rate and good skills, the R93 can be a close ornlong range sniper, doing both pretty effectively. With this, it can two shot Dozers on DW. This is the sniper for one down. With this, you're best as a specials killer, considering you don't have a lot of ammo to work with. This asset mostly goes unused during your heisting.<br><br>4 new masks, patterns and materials:<br>Quickly brushing over these.
