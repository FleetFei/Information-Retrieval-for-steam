I hadn't played a P n' C Adventure this good since the Chzo Mythos collection by Ben &quot;Yahtzee&quot; Croshaw. Surely there are other great recent games like the Secret of Monkey Island HD remakes, Sam &amp; Max: The Devil's Playhouse or Hector: Badge of Carnage but they're nothing new. If I had to describe it I'd say it is a mixture between The Secret of Monkey Island (the boat segment is clearly inspired by the Pirate LeChuck), Maniac Mansion (the supernatural part of the game), Broken Sword (the ambience) and Beneath a Steel Sky (the gadgets Rosa uses, the disco scenario).<br>The characters are well built and the side dialogues make you know them better if you give the effort.
