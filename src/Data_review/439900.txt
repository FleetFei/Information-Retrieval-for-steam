Basically check the map and watch your footing, or you may end up paying for it.<br><br>There are a handful of new enemies too, some that are just outright nasty if you go in unprepared. ]Weavers, a spider like mutant deserve a special mention as they can inflict a new ailment, for which the only cure is time. Planning and preparation are more essential as there is less to loot and more things out for your head. <br><br>You may notice some items are missing from the main game. Some time has passed in the world, so great level supplies are rare, and can only be found in the field. Finding one will feel more rewarding, and some of the new weapons are just like that, including a new trophy that, if you get it, will feel a bit overpowered. Good luck getting it though.<br><br>The new character, Screw, has been through rough times even before the bombs. That is the only spoiler for the character you are going to get from me, but I can say that his new trait 'Lucky' Is probably one of the most powerful traits in either game. You are going to need it, as I remember testing it when it wasn't implimented. Made fights alot more challenging than even I was comfortable with early on. But if you want some sadistic pleasure from the DLC Check the bottom skill form the passive skill choices when upgrading. It is ther perfect thing if you like efficency or just want to laugh at your enemies misfortune.<br><br>Deliverance is a fun little addon, with a few new twists and threats to keep you on your toes.
