2016\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\r\n\r\n\t\t\t\t\t\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\t\t\t\t<div class=\"content\">\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe mechanics in this DLC are significantly different from the core Party Hard game, and with little in the way of a tutorial or explanation of what needs to be done differently I think a lot of people are getting confused and frustrated.<br><br>You start out as a Van Helsing-like undead hunter. Your weapon can now take out multiple enemies at the same time, but when you kill an enemy they don't stay down permanently. You have to finish them off with &quot;Holly Water&quot; [sic], which involves standing over the body and using your holy water ability that takes about 5 seconds to finish activating. This creates a completely different strategy for the game, and slows down the action of an already slowwww game. You'll want to kill a few enemies, pile up their bodies and do your holy water ritual to take care of several at a time. But do it quickly, because if they come back to life they come back <i>angry<\/i>.<br><br>That's the second major difference in this DLC; when someone sees you kill they turn aggro, with a pair of glowing red eyes above their head. In the core Party Hard game if someone turns aggro they can kick you to the ground, but in this DLC they kill you outright. This is somewhat unbalanced, since aggro enemies move faster than your character (who for some reason feels even slower than the playable characters in the core game). You get some help from your demon dog companion who will rush out and kill aggro enemies if they're close enough to him, but he disappears immediately after and reappears after a 10 second or so cooldown. What all of this adds up to is a very slow strategy, a lot of waiting for enemies to group together or wander off on their own. Otherwise you die. A lot. And given the very slow progress of the level, it's incredibly frustrating to spend 10 minutes to clear out 50% of the enemies only to die from an aggro NPC killing you from outside of the reach of your weapon.<br><br>At the time of writing there are a lot of bugs and rough edges in the DLC. About 20% of the time when you kill an enemy their sprite will remain in the upright\/alive position, which is kind of confusing. There are apparently traps in the level but I wasn't able to find them or activate them. There is a part of the castle wall where enemies can go behind it and you have no idea that they're standing there. I've died several times to that because an enemy that was two steps away from me was hidden from sight and turned aggro when I murdered someone else and they saw it.<br><br>Overall I think this is a really good concept but the game play needs serious polish. The range of your weapon should be increased as well as the movement speed of your character. The dog cooldown could be reduced as well as the movement speed of aggro enemies. If you're going to slow down the gameplay with the &quot;kill them twice&quot; mechanic then you need to adjust for that in other areas, especially given the size of the level and the time it takes just to walk from one side of the  map to the other.<br><br>I like it and it's free. Needs a little more refinement, though.
