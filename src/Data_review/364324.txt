the world of complex and realistic FSX airliners. It offers the completely different gaming experience in comparison to stock FSX airliners. The simulated aircrafts are equipped with complex and detailed FMS (flight management system) instead of stock FSX GPS unit, very advanced autopilot with autoland capability, TCAS, 2nd pilot callouts and so on.<br><br>But for those who wants simply set the GPS course, advance the throttle and fly somewhere this addon may be too steep. To get familiar with this aircraft you MUST study the 126-page manual. Luckily, it contains the step-by-step guide to your first flight and quite detailed description of FMS. I'd recommend loading the manual to tablet PC for reading without interruption your flight. Also it may be very useful to print the most necessary pages - I count 19. <br><br>Before each flight you need to input numerous values into the FMS: your route (loading the native FSX flight plan is supported), departure and arrival routes (optional), cruise altitude and speed values, aircraft weight (from Configuration Tool), fuel weight and reserve, calculated V-speeds (using the tables in manual) and so on. It's highly realistic, but it takes MUCH more efforts than getting the stock FSX airliner into air. <br><br>So, if you are not planning to LEARN this aircraft (or you aren't an experienced hardcore simmer) - do not buy this addon. Otherwise, it would be a nice first realistic airliner in your collection.<br><br>Several notes:<br>1. There is no stock FSX GPS module. The GPS is embedded into FMC, but its interface is far from being user-friendly. You may find good videos of setup process on Youtube. Forget about simple pressing the GPS and NAV buttons to begin the course following.<br>2. The PDF manuals are located somewhere in your FSX\/DLC folder.<br>3. The aircraft configuration utility shall be downloaded separately from Library\/Tools menu in your Steam client. It allows setting the payload, getting the zero fuel weight (necessary for FMS setup before flight) and tune various properties of aircraft.<br><br>ADDED:<br>Unfortunately, the included manual doesn't cover all aspects of simulated aircraft systems. You'll have to deal with it on your own by using forums, official FMS manuals and your own mind. For example, the go-around and many other standard procedures are missing from included documents. Also sometimes the plane shows quite strange behaviour, like AP disconnect on loading the game or loss of power on APU off in flight.<br><br>ADDED:<br>To be precise, from &quot;dark and cold&quot; condition you can get the plane into air in ~16 minutes, if playing all necessary callouts.
