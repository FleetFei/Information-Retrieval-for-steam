There is an aspect of randomness by dice having to be rolled for battles and luck situations. Some battles can be avoided even by making clever choices. The book is a timeless classic, one that can be picked up and played - it is, as I said, for everyone. Maybe because I'm older, maybe because sitting with a book and paper, trying to draw an own map while going through the book, feels different.
