However, the design of these pathways are sometimes altered by the game\u2019s events, and even when they aren\u2019t, traversal in any way different from the standard is a challenge in and of itself, as the developers clearly did not design these paths to be easily followed backwards (or forwards, for that matter), as you will have to do. Warrior Within is a truly brutal and often unfair achievement in sadistic game design. It\u2019s also one of my most satisfying completions in the entirety of my gaming career. Nearly every instance of combat in this title will chew on your face and kick you in your \u2665\u2665\u2665 until you <i>really<\/i> learn to play it.
