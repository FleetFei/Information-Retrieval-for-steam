Best example would be opening. I know I have been praising since the beggining of this review, but god. I love it so much. And that is not the only song I would listen to on repeat if I could (I couldn't find it anywhere ;-;). Really, out of all the tracks, maybe 3 are songs I would rather not listen to. Rest of the soundtrack is really good.<br><br>I have not talked about art enough. This game looks amazing. From character sprites to CG pictures to backgrounds.
