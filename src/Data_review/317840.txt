And if you like puzzle platformers you should get Dokuro, as it's a great game in that genre too.<br><br>The story is set in a non-descript fantasy kingdom, where the Dark Lord has just kidnapped the local Princess. With no hero around to stop him, it seems she's destined to a forced marriage - until the titular Dokuro, a lowly skeletal mook, decides she's a bit easier on the eyes than his current boss. Almost all the levels feel well-designed, and while there are some hard ones it never gets to the point of being frustrating. Dokuro controls well, and it doesn't require pixel perfect movement.<br><br>The game looks quite nice, and the story, while simple, makes you care about since Dokuro is a believable underdog. It's also decently long: I think I'm only about halfway through it at the time of this review.
