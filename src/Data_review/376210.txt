I wasn't playing The Isle when it came out, so I don't remember eating trees or any other map except for v3, so you may take what I have to say with a grain of salt. However, I have had some amazing moments in this game. I have also had some extremely boring moments in this game. I reached Shantungosaurus after a few days of gameplay and honestly, right now, there isn't much reason to play massive herbivores. Then there was another...and another. I knew I had to get out of the area fast. So I stood up and took off. This led to an insane chase which felt like minutes of heart pounding terror. There is no endgame right now. The endgame of The Isle is Chat Simulator, Lawnmowing Simulator, or Walking Simulator. The real fun lies in the mid-tier dinosaurs where the danger is palpable and the fear is real. I would recommend it right now as I feel I have had enough fun to justify $20. Would I pay more than $20 knowing what is inside the game? Nope. Not at all. As of right now, this is a $20 game that I would recommend.
