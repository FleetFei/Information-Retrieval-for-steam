For me, tagged enemies did not appear through the walls, and the cameras were too poorly placed for the NPCs to be spotted.  For some reason, the developers felt that it would be more important to place cameras around the empty sparesely-patrolled hallway, instead of ones in the rooms of the building, where the majority of the hostile NPCs are spawned in. Epsilon approaches this subject with its tiptoes, so to speak. Why are the pistols more inaccurate than my shotgun?<br><br>Personally, I believe the game shows so much potential, but I'm worried that the developers will forget about these major issues with the game. A tactical approach must be used and it definitely shows that this fps is meant to be played slowly and strategically.
