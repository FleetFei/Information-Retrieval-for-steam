The more you progress, the more of Cole's evil past, Courtney Sheldon's story, and the character development of Cole you will see. These strings will cross paths at the end of the game, and you will get the full picture (no spoilers intended). <br><br>If you haven't tried this game out just yet, then you have to check it out right away!
