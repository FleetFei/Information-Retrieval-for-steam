I actually have not been able to find out if the harpoons deal damage to yourself if you are within the blast radius or not, but I'll assume they do. Not a practical weapon but it is pretty entertaining to watch the chaos these things are capable of producing given the right placement.<br><br> Overall none of these weapons are all that dependable, however they can be fun to use and add to your selection of things to eliminate zeds with.
