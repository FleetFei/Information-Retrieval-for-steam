I would rather just have health drop although with this game your health recovers so maybe that is why the dev used the bloody face. Your face should have blood drops on it then not a red hue. After all it is dark as it is. The guns sound weak and there is no vibration with each shot. The kick back of the hand gun is extreme and makes the next bullet fly higher until your last shot is so off target you are missing altogether. You literally have to turn the gun further downward with each shot. That is not fun for someone like me. I don't need that much realism. I just want to jump in and defend myself and have fun. The reason I complain about this is because you have to shoot fast in this game to survive when you start to get mobbed. If this was a slower paced shooter than the kick back would be ok and tolerable. Another thing that could make this game much better if it was free-roaming within a map or cycling of maps (more my preference). This way you can still have fun and use evasion to lengthen your life and still have to dodge firey skulls (like in hordes) and the dev could have the ogre type guys throw their clubs\/axes at you too so that if you are farther away from using evasion you still have to worry about things flying at you. The game as it is does nothing to keep me wanting to play it. If the game play is changed drastically and free-roam is added then maybe. But as a wave shooter I am not really interested at all.
