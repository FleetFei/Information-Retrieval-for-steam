Someone with good problem solving skills can get there more quickly and easily.<br><br>That said, allow me to critique the problems in this game: Take a look at Problem 1.16 in the screenshots. A lot of puzzles in the game involving rectangles are like this, though you still have to be canny and figure out which areas and what ratios.
