If you enjoy tower defense games then you\u2019ll have no problems, but it also doesn\u2019t add anything new or inventive. What it does offer are three campaigns to go on and a total of fifty levels to keep you busy.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Gameplay<\/div>Your goal is to defend the castle from waves of enemies. There are different types of enemies, some fly, some are fast or have high defense. The strategy of the game is knowing which type of defense to put up when you need it.
