When an enemy of a different color is hit by the chain reaction, it will freeze and become indestructible, forcing you to navigate around it. These standard enemies <i>never<\/i> do direct damage; the challenge of the puzzle phase comes from having to navigate through the horde of enemies without getting pushed to the bottom of the screen.
