(achievement)<br><br>To talk about the investigation scene: you see the current scene where there are many clues more or less obvious. (If you play on an easier mode your cursor will glow when you are close to a clue) Then you need to place the evidence in the right order: that\u2019s a linear puzzle where you need to order each tile to have the correct shape.
