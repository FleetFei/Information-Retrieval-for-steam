And within 20 years, <i>Tcheco<\/i> will be still remembered thanks to the love and perfectionism put in every single detail. With every single screen being an almost different universe, with different animations, enemies and background layouts. An impressive artistic work that is full of ambition for a solo game developer.<br><br>Note that you should expect a recreation of the NES period games also in its mechanics.
