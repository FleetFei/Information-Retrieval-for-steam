Necrologue isn't about revelation, well..though of course it eventually does, it's about an escape, from the Shelter, past mistakes, and regrets. I love how they nail Clarence's voice and personality perfectly.   <br><br><br><b> Gameplay (8.5\/10)<\/b><br><br>Again, it's Penumbra on it's true form. The gameplay is true-to-root Penumbra. I mean, what's an adventure game without a few hair-tearingly annoying bits where you either feel like giving up altogether, or at the very least succumbing to the evil urge to look at someone else's walkthrough.  Answer: Well, an EASY adventure game, I suppose.  Oh, and the &quot;I hate linear games&quot; people are gonna LOVE this one, by the way.
