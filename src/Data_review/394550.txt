Playing through the different characters will add around 2 hours each, so this game has a 6hr+ gameplay, which is impressive for such an inexpensive title.<br><br>- The Music is fantastic, just like the first game, really adds to the experience and enjoyability of playing this game.<br><br>- In this game there are now some &quot;third person&quot; style stages...in which you are behind the character instead of the standard 2D side scroll action.  I'd compare one of those stages to a Star Fox style.  Definitely an interesting addition to the game.<br><br>- The BOSS Battles are beautiful to look at and very fair to the player.  None of the Boss Battle I came across in this game were overly difficult.<br><br>- The PRICE is outstanding.  I play a lot of games and very few at this price point have been anywhere near as solid as this game is.  I truly hope the Dev pursues a Gunman Clive 3. <br><br>-------------------------------------------<br><br>-CONS-<br><br>- As with the first game, the levels with the &quot;gravitational pull&quot; arrows are back. This lead to some anger moments since I had to redo the stage from the start upon each death.  <br><br>- The jumping is a bit sluggish\/floaty, but you get used to it fairly quickly.  If you played the first game, you know what to expect.<br><br>- The controls to &quot;aim&quot; in the &quot;Flying&quot; areas are a bit weird, so I would of liked those to be a bit tighter.<br><br>- There were not as many weapon choices in this game as there were in the first.  I would of liked a bit more assortment, but the weapons included get the job done.<br><br>- You can only shoot straight in front of you, no angle shots.  However, I understand why the Dev did this...it adds a bit to the difficulty.  I will say he did keep the &quot;Contra&quot; gun in this game, so if you get that you can shoot in three directions (top\/middle\/bottom)<br><br>----------------------------------------------------<br><br>That concludes this Review, I honestly really enjoyed playing both of the Gunman Clive games.
