Although this is obviously aimed at High Chaos playstyles, it can be a situational way to regain health when you encounter some of the more &quot;corrupt&quot; guards in Low Chaos, or even assassination targets if you get the opportunity to take one out from above. Strong in combination with <b>Agility<\/b>, especially at higher difficulties.<br>- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - <br><br><b><u>ARCANE ASSASSIN PACK<\/u><\/b><br><b>VOID CHANNEL<\/b> - &quot;Powers effects slightly improved.&quot;<br><b>OPINION:<\/b> 5\/5. Easily one of the best charms in the game as it makes all of your active powers a little bit better. Just the extra range it gives to Blink is worth slotting this for alone.<br><br><b>WHITE RAT FRIEND<\/b> - &quot;White rats won't attack you.&quot;<br><b>OPINION:<\/b> 0\/5. A more accurate description would be &quot;You take slightly less damage from rat swarms&quot;, which doesn't sound as good (because it isn't). Even if it was the case that a swarm of rats could be comprised of mostly white rats, the brown ones would still attack because their AI is programmed to behave as though they are in a full, hostile swarm, regardless of how many brown rats there are or whether or not the white rats will actually help them attack. Hell, even if this was &quot;Rat swarms will not attack you&quot;, it would still only be situationally good as rat swarms are not hard to avoid in a game where you can always teleport away (admittedly that would at least have a tangible, considerable effect).<br><br><b>GUTTER FEAST<\/b> - &quot;White rats can be consumed for mana.&quot;<br><b>OPINION:<\/b> 5\/5. Works well in combination with Devouring Swarm, allowing you to restore most of the mana you spent on it. This is also a good bone charm for making use of hostile rat swarms, to replenish a little bit of mana and\/or to destroy the swarm quicker. Occasionally you'll also find lone white rats scurrying about the place, ripe for the picking. This charm nudges them over to the later, but when enemies are that far away, they're not going to come to investigate regardless of whether or not they hear it, so long as it's only once. Still, occassionally this will work to its full potential and enemies won't hear anything at all, meaning they won't stop or even look in your direction. Even in the Flooded District, the area with the most abundant water, this just doesn't do much.
