It is not perfect. There are a lot of bugs and issues that need to be fixed still. A lot of that was due to lazy coding or even inexperienced coding and whatnot from the old team. The new team seems leagues above experience wise and it is their engine so I have faith a lot of these issues will be fixed. Before I touch into that lets touch base on a couple things to clear up some stuff with a bunch of the negative reviews and posts.<br><br>Was there some drama? Yup. There was. I think a lot of it was glossed over to save face and what really happened wasnt what was really told. That is my honest belief. And then the old team sold the game to the new team IF. That caused a lot of stink because of the stuff we had been told before...which again, I dont believe was the whole truth. There was too much backpeddling and too much &quot;dont lay blame...mistakes were made all the way around&quot; etc. <br><br>I dont think the new team deserves the flak they have been given. From what I have seen so far they are very excited to own the game and fix it up the way it deserves to be fixed up. They are already off to a great start tho everything needs time. It wont be instant. There was a lot of laziness and screw ups on the old teams end. The game has a lot of issues because of it. But the new team is already hard at work and is being made aware of the problems from the smallest ones to the biggest ones by dedicated players here.<br><br>And we DO have dedicated players. You wont find a better community anywhere. The community here is very helpful and amazing.<br><br>All that said the game:<br><br>This is a game where you can truly jump right in and do what you want to do. Custom tailor your char to be exactly what YOU want. Skills are lvld up by use. That is a great mechanic that gives you total control over you char. No needing to play some random FOTM and no needing to fit in some random mold. You can do some things...you can do all things. Time is your only enemy. :P<br><br>The crating system....this is what people stay for my friends. You cant compare it to any other game nor can you beat it. It is so complex and deep that at first glance you might want to curl up in the fetal position and cry a little and that is perfectly acceptable. :D But once you jump in and get your hands dirty? OMG...it is so darn addicting and your mind will be blown.<br><br>The pet system is above that of any other game too. Could there be more pets? Sure...but again this is alpha and hopefully those will come in the future. But there are still a LOT avail now and 3 types of pets: Robots, Genetically engineered, and tamed. Pet lovers can rejoice...you can do all 3. :P Although you can only use 1 type at a time.<br><br>The combat system needs a bit of work but there are a lot of different wpn types and choices.  Many different &quot;side&quot; skills to lvl up as well such a dirty fighting, doge, parry, counter attack, and so on. The skill list in this game is quite in depth. Not all are 100% or perfect but it is still early stage alpha.<br><br>The housing system needs work too IMO but has more options that most other games as well. There are nation cities, open world housing plots, and instanced housing. They were well on their way with a bunch of deco items and building types already in. My main comments would just be that it could all be a little more user friendly but still great options in the housing system.<br><br>PvP. This is a risk vs reward game. There are contests {pvp} zones where if you go there you can get killed. Should that make you run scared from the game? Hell no! Dont let that scare you in the least. I have yet to be killed in all the hours I have played due to a PvP kill and I harvest all the time in a contested zone. You CAN do what you want to do and usually do it safely. Is some of it luck? Maybe. Some of it because Im in a beast of a nation? Maybe but TBH I think I have seen a red dot on my map once or twice ever that was a player who passed me by. So you are by no means FORCED to pvp and you dont have to necessarily cower in a corner.<br><br>That said there are large safe zones for each faction. You can stay there forever and ever and still be able to do anything to your hearts content. Harvestables in the contested zones can be better sometimes but that isnt to say you cant find A grade nodes in the safe zones either. They just might be fewer and pop up less often. But everything is doable without ever having to leave a safe zone.<br><br>One of the best parts of this game IMO is that everything is pretty much viable. You want to just harvest all day every day? You can and can become hella rich doing so. Want to just make components forever and supply higher lvl crafters? You can and can still get rich from it. Want to be a higher lvl crafter? You can and can put up workorders to buy stuff if you never want to harvest. It is ALL viable. Want to do combat all the time? Harvest what you kill and sell it. Someone somewhere needs pretty much every item in game. There isnt much in the way of junk.<br><br>Not sure what else to touch base on....just that I love this game. I know it isnt perfect yet. But I have faith. The new team owns the engine it is built on and they know how to push all boundaries with it. I have faith they will make it amazing and be able to take it to a lvl the old team never would have been able to. Add in the experience of the new team over the old one....and you cant go anywhere but up TBH. <br><br>And dont be quick to judge just because random people whine or run at the mouth about stuff they dont know about or understand.
