Fortunately, dated graphics don't hold back this gem of a puzzle adventure.  <br><br>In what essentially amounts to a series of riddles strung together between threads of storyline, the Witch's Prison's gameplay consists mostly of gathering clues before attempting to solve the numerous puzzles spread throughout.
