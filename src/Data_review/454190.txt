Once all the coloured squares are cleared you must get to the exit square. As you pass each square it falls away so you can't pass it again and the whole board gradually disappears. There is only one possible route and at the end of the level there is little, if anything, left of the board.<br><br>It can be played with one hand, using only up\/down\/left\/right keys, Del to reset and Enter to move to the next level. You look at the board and plan your route before you start moving. If you hit a fail point you reset and try a different route. The difficulty increases with larger boards and more coloured squares. There are no new mechanics introduced at all, it's the same all the way through.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Complaints<\/div><br>For such a simple game, both programming and gameplay-wise, I would expect every aspect of it to be polished and perfect but instead there's a litany of minor glitches and irritations. It doesn't work unless you go into the settings and change the key binding. If you want to raise your game to the lofty heights of the $2 category you need to have something extra over and above the thousands of other good $1 games and this has absolutely nothing to give it that edge.<br><br>Call me a skinflint if you like, but if I had $2 in my pocket I'd buy 2 other good $1 games, not this one.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Steam<\/div><br>3 achievements, one for each section.
