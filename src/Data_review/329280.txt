This causes very long battles since only the side with more units will win... Right? Well, to a point, yes. You just need to have some more ranged units such as artillery and archers. You can zoom in very far to see the mass destruction you have caused. There's many upgrades, something I love in any game. Progression is highly demanding but highly rewarding. Currently, there's tons of buildings to build and then upgrade. The units are many in number and all have a different usage. Graphically, the game is not impressive even for a 2012 game. Optimization isn't really great at all. The AI needs to be improved. The game is too easy even in 3V3 all Hard\/Nightmare bots (then again, I've recently played some older commander-strategy games so it might just be experience), I'd like to see some more difficulties. Mass upgrading takes a long while, maybe add a button to select multiple building units and upgrade them all at once? Maybe even automatically when resources are plentiful. Also, there's one one map at this time.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\"> <b> Gameplay <\/b> <\/div>Buggy but fun. That's the simplest description I could write about this game. Watch units pass right through your walls as if they never existed and kill your swordsmen. As buggy is it fun, though. It's difficult to explain. Only people who have played Total War games will understand. When you lose a group of units, the frustration is real. If you triumph over the dead bodies of your enemies, it's a true reward.
