It\u2019s all up to you how you want to interpret the story, even if some of it is crystal clear.<br><br>Behind the gorgeous graphics lies a game that reminded me a bit of WarioWare Inc. Again, there are times when you\u2019ll know exactly what to do, and other times it will take you several minutes before you realize exactly what the game\u2019s asking from you. It\u2019s all pretty fun. That is, until you hit a level where you\u2019re having a hard time using the physics to your advantage. There was one level where it was perfectly clear what I had to do (save a girl from drowning) and yet, because of the physics, it became a pain in the \u2665\u2665\u2665 to accomplish. The game could be a little more forgiving in this department. I also reached a point in the game - just a few levels later - where the game just infinitely rotated every object I picked up. There was no way to finish the level so I had to restart the game (after which the problem was solved). I might be nitpicking here, but a game this small shouldn\u2019t have too many bugs or glitches I think.<br>Now, it seems I didn\u2019t enjoy it because so far pretty much all I\u2019ve been doing is complaining, but trust me, I really do like the game. It\u2019s certainly different than many other games, and I mean that in a good way. It\u2019s unique and beautiful and people should take note, which brings me to the following: the graphics.<br><br>And God, these graphics are absolutely gorgeous. It has an Arabian, dreamy, atmosphere where everything in the foreground is silhouetted with only a few parts here and there that are shown as what looks like stained glass. It\u2019s an amazing effect and it looks just beautiful in motion. The background is shown in full color, with gradients and it makes the whole game look perfect. Amazing work was done here, the developers can be really proud of this aspect.<br>The animations could use a little work. If you can look past that then there\u2019s a charming game to be found here. In that case this game is an absolute must.
