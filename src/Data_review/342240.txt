This blue-energy is humankind's last hope against this alien threat.  <br><br>There are thirty-six story missions that span across nine planets. Alternatively, the soundtrack edition of the game sells for $5.99.<br><br>While SCHAR: Blue Shield Alliance is rough around the edges, there is some fun to be had in this title.
