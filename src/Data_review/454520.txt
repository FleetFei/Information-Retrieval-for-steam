Later you can unlock more weapons such as flamethrower, rocket launcher, machine gun etc., using gems which you can get from playing the levels. The first level\u2019s enemies are termites, the later levels start throwing more enemies at you like the annoying wasps in second level, beetles. Etc. There\u2019s a lot of variety of enemies. There\u2019s a background layer as well where enemies can spawn and shoot bullets at you in the foreground. To kill these enemies, you must aim directly at them and once you lock on and shoot them, your bullets should go towards the background instead of straight 2D in line. The boss battles can be pretty nice as bosses don\u2019t die that fast and are sort of bullet spongy. <br><br>You have health bar with little dividers in between. So if your health is higher than any of these dividers, it\u2019ll regenerate health till that little part is full. You get these health drops from enemies and the big health packs are also in specific spots, like before boss battles etc. Checkpoints are plenty and generous.<br><br>The game has its fair share of problems too. I noticed the controls are not that great. They are just not responsive.
