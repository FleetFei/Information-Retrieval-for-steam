It's a feature that I've liked since I've played it but it's just a shame that it's not used more in the game.<br><br>Add to that the hunt for hidden snowflakes and origamis and you'll have a complete game (apart that it's not a CE but I'm not missing this specifity). It made me sweat a little when I couldn't see any of them (some origamis are hard to spot), so you bet that I was relieved when I saw the achievements for finding all of them unlock.<br><br>The graphics are also very nice, as usual. Contrasting to the murder context, everything is very bright and colorful. The soundtrack is also great, very fitting. And as usual, the voice acting is quite nice.<br><br>In other words, I loved Crime Secrets: Crimson Lily. But I wished that it was a little more hard and maybe to have a counter for origamis and snowflakes. But the rest was well-polished.
