Three of the DLCs currently available on Steam are &quot;Experiences&quot; - full seasons with tracks and cars - DTM 2013 and 2014, ADAC 2014. It actually makes more sense to buy these as DLCs rather than buying a whole new game every year, as with the F1 games. The fourth DLC is just a car pack, not an &quot;Experience&quot;.<br><br>Here's where the problems begin...<br><br>The three Experience DLCs contain a lot of duplicate content. If you buy all the packs I think you get a discount of around 30%<br><br>To give you an idea of cost, I spent less than \u00a330 to get all the tracks and about a third of the cars.
