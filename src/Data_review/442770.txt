There are times he'll wait for you to check out an area of interest and return to him afterwards. I particularly enjoyed the camerawork used as Mii traverses a very long, very high bridge. It's quite cinematic.<br><br>The Minims can be played either windowed or in full-screen.<br><br>The game offers no user-initiated saves.
