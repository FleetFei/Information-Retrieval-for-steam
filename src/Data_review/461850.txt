Each level must be completed within a time limit as Abrix will fall to pieces if you fail.<br><br>Boxes aren't your only challenge in Abrix the Robot, you'll also encounter crates and barrels which can only be moved around or destroyed using a laser, fireball, bomb or dynamite. EXP is spent unlocking new levels so it's better to try and get as much EXP on the lower levels as you can to allow you to skip the harder ones if you're truly stuck.<br>There is also the editor which will allow you to create your own level that you can share with others via Steam Workshop. The editor is easy to use and I created my own little easy chamber in ten minutes which was fun and it was my first ever upload to the Steam Workshop.
