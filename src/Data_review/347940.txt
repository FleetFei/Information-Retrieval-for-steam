Being an Early Access title, it is safe to assume that the music is just not ready to be implemented yet, and I'm sure it will be in the future. For now, this is a great game to play with your own playlist blasting in the background!<br><br><br><b>Performance<\/b><br>==========<br>In the dozen hours I played I ran into no issues with the game.
