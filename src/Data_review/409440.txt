Your apple makes some cute, worried sounds when the snakes get too close, and there's a Pacman-like 'wubwubwubwub' when you fail a level, which seems fitting.<br><br>Gameplay - SnakEscape is played through a series of isolated levels in which you must manoeuvre your apple towards the glowing golden exit square, avoiding snakes, dodging around obstacles, and (hopefully) collecting three stars along your way.<br><br>You control your apple either with the WASD or arrow keys, or by using the mouse to rotate the screen until a little transparent apple marker appears on the square next to you in the direction you want to move, and then left clicking. It's a completely unoriginal progression system, but that's fine; it's easy to understand and it allows you to move on to new levels even when you're having trouble getting a perfect three-star score, although new levels require enough stars to unlock that it's easy to become stuck on a handful of levels quite early on.<br><br>Oh, and I've given an extra plus because each level has a name, which I like.
