This really helps shrug off that icky MMOish feel that people like me have grown tired of, and really immerse you into the game. You level up much more slowly in this game than most RPGs. Time and time again I have joined random parties and time and time again everyone cooperates to get the job done. There are some toxic players out there, but they\u2019re few and far between. <br><br>\tAll in all DDO is very unique MMO with extensive class building and filled with immersive adventures.
