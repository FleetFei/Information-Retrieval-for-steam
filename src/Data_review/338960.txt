The premise is simple, disaster has struck three small islands and you must send workers to rescue stranded residents and repair the damage.  Sounds good to me.<br><br>Time management games generally aren't my forte, but this little gem is a lot more fun and engaging than I was expecting.  Gameplay consists of completing various objectives (rescue residents, rebuild homes, etc.) before time runs out.  You're only given so much time and resources to complete each level and earning a gold trophy often takes you right up to the wire. <br><br>Despite the casual tag, earning gold is anything but casual and will require pushing your multi-tasking skills to the limit. Once you get the hang of things, though, it's just a matter of execution.<br><br>With 60 progressively harder levels, there's plenty to keep you busy.
