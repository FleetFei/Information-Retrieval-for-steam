Heaven is the fourth DLC for Tropico 4. This DLC contains one outfit for your avatar, one new building, one new extra mission, one new trait, and a new island.<br><br>The extra mission is &quot;Pirate Heaven&quot;. The mission takes place in Isla de Bucanero where it was supposedly to have been inhabited by pirates in the past. There will be a pirate index and heritage index. If the pirate index reaches 100 you will lose, and if the heritage index reaches 100, you will win. The pirate index will increase over time. To get heritage indexes, you must complete objectives. Some side objectives will give you some annual cash. And some objectives will reset your pirate index to 0. You will also be embargoed by every country so the smugglers hut will be useful. It's a moderate mission, and should take you about an hour or so to finish. It's a pretty fun mission in my opinion.<br><br>The new building is the smugglers hut. It allows you to import resources at a lower price and allows imports during an embargo. Each smugglers hut can only import one type of resource, and only 5 can be placed on the island. It costs $3000 and it's quite small. I only know how great it was after playing the Pirate Heaven mission as the price of imports are noticeably cheaper. I find the new sailor trait to be underwhelming. The effects are lowering the time between ships arriving by -25% and at 5 stars, it will reduce the construction cost for docks, tourist docks, the luxury liner, marinas, and ship-o-rants by -25%. The outfit is only for female avatars as there is already a pirate themed outfit for the male avatars.
