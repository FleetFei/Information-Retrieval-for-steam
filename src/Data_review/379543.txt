It's not easy to fly by any means, but once you get the hang of it, it's a very fun and challenging plane to fly. Before you even start down the runway set pitch trim to +10 - +12 degrees, it helps alot with take off stability and its about how you'll have it set for cruising. The most notable characteristic is that the plane is under powered, very prone to departure stalls and slow to climb, it's also easy to gain too much speed in a decent.
