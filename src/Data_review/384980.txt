There is NOTHING you can buy that you cannot earn. All units and &quot;items&quot; are unlocked just by playing. EVERY level you play will earn you coins. So, if you have time and patience and upgrade the right units as you go, you have to spend NOTHING to progress. I even saw some people claiming that upgrading your items with coins was &quot;hidden&quot; to get you to spend money. FYI, there is only one way to upgrade anything: coins. The troops are just melee or ranged and there is no variation on how they work. The cleverest part of the mechanic is that each unit has a % associated with it. Each level has a certain number you have to fill to play it. So, the stronger the unit, the lower the % and the more different units you have to have. The more different units you have the more symbol types you have to match and the harder it is to make matches.<br><br>In the end, it is an easy to play and a bit grindy free system that is worth playing for a bit. Currently, the achievements are semi-broken and there are some rough edges, but it is basically solid.
