There are numerous ways to kill yourself that are quite amusing; oftentimes you won\u2019t realize it until it\u2019s too late. The object descriptions are all very humorous in a condescending manner, further solidifying your subconscious theory that the protagonist is an idiot.<br><br>This game is really, <i>really<\/i> tough.
