Every time you come to a moment where a choice must be made, the game freezes and lets you pick from two choices. Most of the choices are pretty much risk or be cautious and see how your choice plays out once chosen.<br><br>When you pick a risk choice you might get an item which comes into play at a later time in the game. This gives you another set of choices or a new scene all together. The game only had few of these, and the choice segments are what you encounter most while playing the game.<br><br>Graphics<br><br>A Trip to Yugoslavia: Director's Cut is pretty much a home made movie, but with a VHS filter on top of it. While I like when developers use the VHS filter on horror based games (as it helps it feel more genuine and terrifying), I felt this game didn't need it. The footage they shot looks way better than something a real VHS camera could film.<br><br><br>Audio<br><br>You rarely can hear anything other than the music that plays over the main game.
