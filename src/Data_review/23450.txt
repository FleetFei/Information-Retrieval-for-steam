This is another difference to Anno 1404's postcard view. The way the city comes alive with perfectly animated little figures is also very well done. I think the game could have benefitted from having camera controls that are similar to the classic Total War series.<br><br><b>Research<\/b> <br>One thing I think was too easy was carrying out research.
