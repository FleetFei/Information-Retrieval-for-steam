They are like additional moves you can gain depending on which one you choose, and can alter your character quite a bit. Some of them can give you projectiles, some can act as assists, some can even alter your character's movement! They really bring a lot of depth to this game and can create all kinds of matchups. Competitively this could be an issue, because there's so much to try to remember. But casually it's freaking awesome.
