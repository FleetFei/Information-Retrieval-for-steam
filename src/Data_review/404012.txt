I have had many battles where I sent my Gorebull straight into the enemy lord, had the entire enemy army surround him, and he still survives whilst routing the entire army (with a herd of minotaurs behind him no less). Other re-hashed units include Chaos Hounds in both regular and poison, as well as Chaos Spawns (once you go minotaur you never go back). For other regular infantry they have a decent variety of Gors, Ungors, Beastigors, as well as a Razorgor and Centigor cavalry line. Marching is no longer an option, but every regular action besides raiding puts you in a hiding state. Basically what this means is that after you sack a huge settlement after an epic battle, you can go into a hidden encampment and rebuild your troops with little fear of being completely wiped from the battle attrition.
