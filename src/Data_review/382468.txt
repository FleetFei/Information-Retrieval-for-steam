DLC!<br><br>You get Chihaya Kisaragi for Alisha, Haruka Amami for Rose, Miki Hoshii for Lailah, and Yayoi Takatsuki for Edna. The battle music for these costumes are &quot;Me ga Au Toki&quot; for Chihaya, &quot;GO MY WAY!!&quot; for Haruka, &quot;relations&quot; for Miki, and &quot;Kiramekirari&quot; for Yayoi. They are all off vocal versions, so feel free to sing along! I know I will... I can't help myself.<br><br>As always, Alisha and Rose costumes are interchangeable, and unlike the Classic Tales costumes, you actually can use the hair for both costumes on either character! It doesn't actually give the other character's hairstyle, but you do get matching ribbons for the respective costumes. So Rose still has Haruka's hairstyle, but blue ribbons to match Chihaya's costume, for example. I haven't actually used the costumes on Alisha yet, but I assume it's the same for her, Chihaya's hairstyle, but with red ribbons to match Haruka's costume. This was definitely an unexpected feature for me, and definitely a plus. They didn't have to do this, but I'm glad that they did.<br><br>In addition, you get a full set of 13 chibi idols to use as attachments! Unfortunately, you can only use two, instead of three, because each one takes up 1.5 units of attachment space. If you've seen Puchimas!, then these attachments are very reminiscent of the puchidols from that show. Tiny, pudgy, and adorable. They're a bit silly, but it's not like the vast majority of the attachments in this game weren't already silly to begin with!<br><br>My only real complaint with this DLC is the way that Tales of Zestiria handles music. It prioritizes Sorey, and then Sorey's partner, and then Alisha\/Rose, and then Alisha\/Rose's partner. It would be much better if the music selection was random. But that's not really an issue with the DLC, moreso an issue with the game itself. If you want Alisha\/Rose music, then you either have to not use the costume for Edna\/Lailah or not use Edna\/Lailah for Sorey's partner. If you want music for Alisha\/Rose's partner, then you have to not use the costume for Alisha\/Rose and also not use the costume for Edna\/Lailah or not use Edna\/Lailah for Sorey's partner.<br><br>Overall, it's definitely worth the price tag if you're a fan of THE iDOLM@STER. If you're not familiar with the source material, though, I do imagine that some of the appeal will be lost. But even if you're not, you still might enjoy it. The costumes are pretty cute, and the battle music works much better than expected. Different hairstyles are nice, also. Especially if you can't stand the lightning bolt that Alisha's got going on.
