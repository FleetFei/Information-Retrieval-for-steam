In this game, the lack of voice acting, I think, enhances the atmosphere and enjoyment.<br><br>I'll re-iterate the character portraits and art style again. Also the sound design complaint as above.<br><br>To finish this review, and if the developer bothers to read this far down, I would like to say I love The Deed and this game, but I would LOOVVEEE to
