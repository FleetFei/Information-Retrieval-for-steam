BAF follows the events of Operation Arrowhead when you successfully overthrow the Takistani regime. In this DLC, the British forces commence Operation Crimson Lance to control the rising insurgent activity in post-OA Takistan. You play as Brian Frost, who coordinates with various British squads to accomplish objectives.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\"> <b> Campaign <\/b> <\/div>In Operation Crimson Lance, you play as Brian Frost. Furthermore, the game successfully attempts to incorporate some personality into Frost's teammates through their heavy British accents. For instance, Frost surprisingly meets an old friend 'Tanny' at the start of a mission. Random events also occur that help or hurt you further into the game. If you do not help him, following missions become more difficult. In other words, you are presented with choice and it can affect the game. Regarding AI, the AI feels more reactive towards your commands. However, while better than Czechia DLC, AI driving  still is horrible.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\"> <b> Conclusion <\/b> <\/div>Arma 2: British Armed Forces is a very good DLC. It features a strong four mission campaign that shines in presenting Brian Frost and his personality and background. Missions also vary with optional side missions and missions depend on cooperation and communication. There is also a good soundtrack that complements the environment and operation. My only issue was the AI driving behaviour. I recommend this DLC because it expands on the overall Arma 2 story in Takistan while adding some fresh content to the game.
