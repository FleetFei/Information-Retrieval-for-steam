These modes are Career and Free Play.  For each mode you create 4 profiles.   For each profile you get a club card.  The career club card is broken down into Career Points, Bank Roll and Time Played. In other words does IGT Slots Paradise Garden  immerse you enough that you can believe that you are at the casino.  Unfortunately this is where the package falls short.  <br><br>There are two casino themes that are available but each looks to be rendered as low resolution FMV and this detracts from the immersiveness.
