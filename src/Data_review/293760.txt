From there you can configure engines I4, I6, V6, V8, Flat plan V8 and V12.  From there you can adjust the bore and stroke*, head configuration, aspiration type*, crude cam profile*, compression, fuel ratio and timing.  From there you can configure fuel delivery system and exhaust. I expect stuff like that to be worked out down the road.<br>As a game, it\u2019s too crude at this point to give a fair review of.  I will say from what they have now my expectations are very low for this aspect of the game.  I contrast this with some high expectation for the car and engine building aspects.
