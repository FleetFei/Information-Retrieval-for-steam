Other objectives are more in recovery of data though for you as the player it boils down to just arriving somewhere still intact. <br><br>The game does a good job of introducing mechanics as well as the types of units available and what uses they might have. There aren't THAT many but enough to create some pretty decent strategies as well as make some fairly frantic experiences in real time. Tanks, Shield units, APC's and missile launchers. You're in charge of their order and the path they take through the city. <br><br>The main mechanic you'll be dealing with is planning a path (that can be changed at any point) and placing power ups down to aid your convoy in completing the mission. All are placed with a specific diameter and duration and for the most part are useful in dealing with a specific type of tower. There are also situations where you must travel inside and air support is not available or timers are placed for example to keep a bridge down.
