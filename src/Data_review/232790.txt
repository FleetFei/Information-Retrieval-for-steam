The environments are varied and interesting to look at while the characters are all quite memorable.<br><br>Sporting an all-star cast, the voice acting is top notch which brings each of the characters to life with standout performances from Elijah Wood as Shay and Jennifer Hale best known for her work on Mass Effect (Femshep) as the megalomaniacal computer. For example: choosing to lie to certain characters won\u2019t change the way they perceive you and will have the same effect as telling the truth.<br><br>Act one is also fairly short, lasting around 4 hours and it ends with a bit of a cliffhanger which will leave you anxiously waiting for Act two.
