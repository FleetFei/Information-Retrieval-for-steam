My mic and webcam were instantly recognized and the quality was fine.<br><br>So i searched for a game and got into my first lobby. Round was alot of fun and enjoyable.<br><br>You choose your cards and once chosen your cards are on opposing sides in a standoff. You have two minutes to plead your case and then the other players choose who would win. If it happens to be a tie, a random spin decides who wins. And this is wheree twitch comes in. You can stream your game on Twitch and your viewers are able to vote on the argument winner alongside the players. It's delightful to see the loser of the argument\/his cards get burned, ripped to shreds or trampled, since the animations look really nice.<br><br>I have a few little issues with Superfight though at this time:<br><br>1.
