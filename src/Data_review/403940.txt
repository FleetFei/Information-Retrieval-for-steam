You must click on those tiles to find the exit to proceed to the next level. Underneath the tiles can be magic, healing, traps, chests, keys and of course, monsters. You can only clear tiles that are not protected; so you need to work out which tiles you can uncover by killing the monsters with your hero, whilst taking the least amount of damage possible. If you take too much damage, it's game over and you'll have to start a new game.
