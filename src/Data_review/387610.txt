The vast majority of the film takes place in the titular basement, in which five teenagers take refuge after they are attacked by a person unknown, for reasons unknown. The emphasis is on a character driven narrative and psychological horror.<br><br>At the start of the film the viewer is presented with very little information about what is going on: who these people are, how they know each other, how they came to be attacked, or where exactly they are, other than in a nondescript basement.
