It's like &quot;what if the helicopters in Battlefield 4 were a joy to fly?&quot;<br><br>The developer has found the &quot;sweet spot&quot; between a fun and accessible light sim, and a fairly difficult simulator. You don't have to memorize 100 keys and learn how to operate a radar system. It's really that good.  <br><br>Coop missions:  Currently you can play any of the missions with a friend or two.  Multiplayer &quot;just works&quot; and you host a game, your friends find it, and you take off together.
