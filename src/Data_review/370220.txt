There definitely is a lack of variation in gameplay.<br><br>The story is a little too light, and told in a disjointed style that makes it unclear what it is even about. It's more of a mood piece. I found the writing probably the weakest part of the game.<br><br>The standout here is the art. It's a very different style, that plays with shapes, lines, layers, and negative space.
