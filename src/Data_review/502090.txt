I approached this game with an open mind, and I really enjoyed it. Yes, it's pretty rough around the edges. But it also provides several deliciously surreal environments to explore. The story, even though it was told in a very clumsy way, with text in broken English that sometimes wasn't even fully visible, touched interesting subjects.
