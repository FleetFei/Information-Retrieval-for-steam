Sadly there is no online multiplayer, making Miner Warfare exclusively a party game. There is no single player experience. That isn't really the point.<br><br>Now if you break down the game, is it the prettiest? No, but it's fun. Is it the most innovative? No, but it's fun. Does the game have an awesome soundtrack?
