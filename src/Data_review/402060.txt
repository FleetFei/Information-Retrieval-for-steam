In essence, Californium is a walking simulator where the sole gameplay element revolves around finding glitches in your reality: Items flickering in and out of existence, TV sets sending out secret, subversive messages only you can hear.<br><br>In \u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665's fiction, reality is always malleable, and such is the case also in Californium. Californium is a very Dickian experience in the way that for most of the time, you have absolutely no idea what is going on. Diversity would have been more than welcome.<br><br>Aesthetically, Californium sits at a happy medium. I did also run into an unfortunate bug that forced me to replay an entire section, which is a shame.<br><br>Overall, Californium is a hard game to recommend, but after weighing all of the aspects of the game I'm going to do that anyway.
