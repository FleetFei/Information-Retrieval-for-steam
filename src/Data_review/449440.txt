Anatoliy Loginovskikh. In the summer Anatoliy is a sheep herder and during the winter when the weather is too cold to do that job he becomes a game designer. That might sound a little funny to some of you but to me that sounds like a cool life. Although this is his first game on Steam he has created other games in the past. He claims to have a very old computer that doesn\u2019t allow him to make videos for his games and this would explain the simplicity of them.<br><br>Simplicity at its best can generate fascinating games that are open to a broad audience. It\u2019s a clever take on the genre that contains a number of elements, varied enemy types and a number of different tools that you can utilize to your benefit in taking down the enemy.<br><br>Each area has a number of different game zones that you need to successfully complete in order to progress through to the next one. Although it is the telltale sign of how simple the game is at its core it is immaculately presented and is pleasing to the eyes. The music while not quite as good as the graphics is more than suitable for the game. The biggest problem with the game at the moment is that there may be too little ammunition offered in the game \u2013 I run through the ammo that I have very quickly and\/or things take longer to kill than what you have ammo for, whichever way you choose to look at it). The other issue that I have with the game is that you cannot change any options (e.g. turn volume up or down, etc.) within the game.
