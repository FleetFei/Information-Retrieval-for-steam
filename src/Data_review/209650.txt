But to be honest, I found Advanced Warfare to be a pleasant surprise once I got into it. <br><br>So first, multiplayer: The game takes Black Ops 2\u2019s create a class system, but adds Score Streaks into it (so 13 points instead of the 10 from Black Ops 2). Since you can take out Streaks, it\u2019s possible to nab a few extra points for your normal equipment, or if you find yourself with extra points, you could even take a Wildcard and get a 4th Streak. And speaking of Streaks, you can now modify them in a really interesting way. Take UAV for example; normally you need a score of 400 to call it in. What you can do however is modify it so that it costs extra points, but will get a cool secondary effect (for example, for 100 points it lasts longer, 200 it jams the enemy mini-map, 200 shows the direction an enemy is facing, 300 moves into orbit and cannot be shot down, or 300 to make it so any score you get towards it isn\u2019t wiped when you die). All Streaks have these types of upgrades and it\u2019s an amazing addition.<br><br>Also on the topic of multiplayer, the movement feels fast and very, very smooth. Guns handle well, and it really does feel like the maps have been properly designed around everyone being able to double jump and dash, not just they built the maps and then tacked those things on at the end. Finally, those loot bags that drop sometimes in MP are really cool \u2013 they let you customise the look of your character, as well as potentially dropping weapons that have unusual stats (For example, -1 damage, +1 fire rate is one I\u2019ve gotten).<br><br>I\u2019ve given the new Survival mode, Exo Survival, a few runs (as a solo) and it is pretty fun. It isn\u2019t quite zombies since you are just on the 1 map \u2013 there\u2019s no unlocking\/moving between rooms or anything, but the objectives aren\u2019t just simply to survive. There are however objectives, for example hold a specific area on the map for a wave, collect items or defuse bombs that give you bonuses. Honestly, I\u2019m not sure whether I like this or zombies more, since there are fairly different.<br>I\u2019m about 4 missions (1.5-2 hours) into the campaign at the time of writing this, and thus far, I\u2019m enjoying it. <br><br>There are a ton of gadgets, and unlike some previous games where you may get to use a cool gadget once in a 30 second segment and then forget about it forever, I find the game using them multiple times which is great. To be honest, with all the cool technology available, it really makes it feel like the whole \u201csquad takes out an army\u201d deal CoD has going seem like it could actually be plausible. Also, the game does a little less hand holding \u2013 if only a little. You can run ahead and won\u2019t have to wait for teammates, they\u2019ll catch up (it\u2019s only happened to me a few times, but a good example is in the single player video, somewhere in the first 8 mins).<br><br>The graphics are actually pretty good, particularly for a multi-platform release, and the game isn\u2019t incredibly taxing on your system. There are a ton, and I mean a ton of options. Honestly, Sledgehammer deserves to be commended for those video options. (As a side note, if you are struggling to run, you can turn down Screen Space Ambient Occlusion to anything but HBAO+ and Anti-aliasing: Supersampling to a lower number to get an easy performance boost. (This may not be the most efficient way but it certainly worked a lot for me when I was testing out performance).<br><br>So multiplayer has an fps cap, which caps the game out at 91 fps (at least it\u2019s not 60, or god forbid, 30\u2026). Single player on the other hand has no cap, so you can push your computer to the limits if you want to.<br><br>The single major negative I have thus far about the game is how the games are hosted. I had thought they were going with dedicated servers, which they apparently don\u2019t because almost everyone game I\u2019m in people are spiking like crazy for the duration of it. To be honest, with dedicated servers this would be at least the second best CoD in my opinion, perhaps even challenging CoD4 for the title in the future.
