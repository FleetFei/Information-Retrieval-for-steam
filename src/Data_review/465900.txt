This game is based off of Edgar Allan Poe's Gold Bug, though it does take its liberties, understandable since the original story is pretty short. LeGrand is a character in the original story; there is a Newfoundland dog in the original story; in the original story, the protaganist finds Captain Kidd's treasure buried under a tree with the help of an encrypted parchment - the message of which is the exact same as the one in the game. Also, Poe fans like myself know that Dupin, the detective who is your partner in the game, is the legendary French detective Poe created for Murders in the Rue Morgue, so very cleverly done by the creator of this game to combine two Poe classics into one.
