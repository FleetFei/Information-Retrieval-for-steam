You just run through rooms until you shoot an energy-core-thing, and then you leave. You even got a way-point on the top of the screen so that you don't get lost, so the whole affair is incredibly stream-lined. Enemies are constantly spawning in from all directions and it gets hard to manage if you don't slow down. You do have some secrets thrown in the mix but it always involves shooting a cracked wall that hides a secret room, or looking behind some boxes for ammo and health.<br><br>The guns you get feel suprisingly good though, but they have the odd issue of not doing as much damage as you expect them to do. That being said, you can rack up extra damage by getting kill-combos, which helps alliviate that problem. However your gun selection feels limited and half of the weapons can and will damage you if you get too close to their damage radius. This is unfortunate since close-quarters combat makes up most of the game, so it often feels a bit risky to use these weapons unless you hang back and pick off enemies from a safe distance.<br><br>As for the multiplayer: its uh... there I guess.
