It's brilliant if that's the kind of thing you're looking for, though there are some that may be put off by the simplistic gameplay. If I had one complaint about the visual style its that sometimes the visual effects make it very hard to tell what's going on in the game sometimes. If you have trouble with them as I do, you can just turn them off.<br><br>Unfortunately, for all it's polish it's still a very simplistic game.
