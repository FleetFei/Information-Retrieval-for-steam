It's from Troma.<br><br>&quot;WHO THE HELL IS TROMA?&quot; you might ask yourself.<br><br>TROMA Entertainment.<br>The purveryors of crazy, zany, sick, twisted, hardcore, grindhouse, action\/scifi\/horror\/romance cult movies in the universe of B-movies. That's who. They even had a Saturday morning cartoon show at one point. Their fame stems mostly from the movie: &quot;The Toxic Avenger&quot;, a fugly, but &quot;lovable good guy superhero mutant&quot;, and an assort of other movies, with other characters that have developed large cult followings. They've been around for about 40 years now.<br><br>SO now these guys have a PC game. ACTUALLY, about 15 years ago; said they didn't release it then, figuring the game would get totally banned. <br><br>*Update: &quot;DrSpikee&quot; shared this was originally a 1999 Polish game called &quot;Rezerwowe Psy&quot;*.<br>Thanks dude! :D<br>It appears Troma was able to use it to make their own game. (seems minus the original sex\/nudity!)<br><br>THE GAME:<br>Starts with a disclaimer; and Lloyd from Troma pointing out you don't need it: you already bought the effing game! LOL, NICE! :D<br>Then, a &quot;Troma&quot; intro, a collection of clips from their many old movies: gross, gory, hardcore grindhouse, with boobs in one scene (more on boobs later). There's the menu, pretty straightforward. Start a New Game.<br><br>The game is an isometric, tactical, turn-based, squad-based shooter.<br>You start out with 3 dudes: The Toxic Avenger (Toxie), Sgt.Kabukiman, and The Stranger.<br>Your goal: to wipe out the Surf Nazis (see the movie &quot;Surf Nazis Must Die&quot;).<br><br>The game is very similar in handling to the original Fallout game, but with complete emphasis on COMBAT only. Even the inventory menu looks inspired, so fans of Fallout 1\/2, will find some familiarity. When you bag someone, they die, leave a bloody mess, and their loot in a nice pile for you to pick-up through the inventory screen.<br><br>Everything costs action points. Action points to walk, action points to shoot, action points to reload, to move things in your inventory even. <br><br>The game is divided into &quot;missions&quot;. The level areas aren't big, this is not an RPG, this is a pretty straightforward clean and sweep game: GO AND KILL ALL OF THE BAD GUYS.<br><br>From this point, that is all you do. There isn't anything to interact with, but you can blow away all the different pedestrians with names with the likes of:  &quot;Hooker&quot;, &quot;Freak&quot;, &quot;Fat Guy&quot;, &quot;Crazy With A Shotgun&quot;, etc. Some will randomly draw weapons and shoot you. Shooting a &quot;overworked office worker&quot; might yield you some loot, like booze, drugs, money, a mobile phone, a porn mag, or whatever. Most of the loot appears to be just &quot;novelty&quot; items. The only things of value are any weapons\/ammo they have. Like Fallout 1\/2, you can waste ANYONE,  but there is no &quot;karma&quot; penalty in this game.<br><br>You can also target individual parts: legs, torso, or head. Just right cilck on the target to get a small menu with attack options. Works well.<br>However, it doesn't seem to really do anything. For example, you can't blow off legs or heads.<br>Haven't seen any animations other than the character falling to the ground, and then a pool of blood appearing. I noticed when I shot someone with alot of strength still, in the head, they died immediately.Other times, I aimed for the head, and they were just lightly wounded.<br><br>I suspect this is probably something they never had time (or budget) to finish or properly implement.<br><br>To complete the missions, you only have to snuff out the BAD GUYS (with various weapons). <br>Once that is done, there is a &quot;Thumbs Up&quot; button that appears. <br>Click it, and you are done.<br><br>Between missions, you are treated to the narrator telling the story. <br>LOL. Man this is so bad. Sometimes, you will see a random, non-sequiter video clip. One, shows the creators of South Park in a fake commercial about hermaphrodites. Another, of Lemmy from Motorhead doing a weird PSA type thing. Another is a metal music video. Another is a montage of super-gory moments from Troma movies. <br><br>(**NOTE, you will miss these if you &quot;left-click&quot;. I tried &quot;right-clicking&quot; and then they played. However, the game glitched-out when the next level started.)<br><br>Graphics:<br>Not bad for a game 15 years old. There isn't anything to interact with, there isn't a load of detail in the hand-painted backgrounds\/levels, but the areas are diverse and seem to look better as you progress. The animations were good.<br><br>Sound:<br>The sound FX are alright, a couple times, they didn't play, but was rare and only in an instant. <br>A couple of sounds, seem to be right out of Fallout, like when reloading or messing with the menu. Hmmm. When you kill someone, they let out a really long, over-the-top blood-curdling scream. The characters\/NPCs will make funny comments or noises at times. Like, Toxie might run out of action points while trying to go somewhere you told him to, and will stop and say something like:&quot;Yeah, I'll get to it as soon as I finish taking my toxic waste dump!&quot;<br><br>Music: Varies, from PORNO music, to alot of  SKA and METAL, with R or X rated lyrics. It's like they couldn't afford GWAR and got some other bands who weren't as extreme. The narrator might pop-in once in a while to give a game hint. Whatever.<br><br>PROS:<br>-Straight-forward, old-school Turn-based Squad Action<br>-Good bloody, dark humor<br>-Interface design is simple and efficient at the same time<br>-Novelty of playing classic cult characters from the TROMA movies and fighting other characters from the movies<br>-If you like games like POSTAL, you'll feel right at home<br>-Great music<br>-the game proudly doesn't take itself seriously<br>-Tons of bonus features, including old CD-ROM style Macromedia player interface (will be in your Steam folder), where you get a bunch of &quot;behind-the-scenes&quot; stuff. A lot of blood, boobs, and booty. There is a whole section of the &quot;Tromettes&quot;, hot, naked\/scantly-dressed chicks in sexy or PORN-Y poses. <br><br>CONS:<br>-Seems like you never get enough action points to do things<br>-Some lite-RPG elements would have been nice<br>-Inventory system is glitchy as heck!!!! I recommend when clicking an inventory\/ground object, to click REAL FAST (tap) and do this in the upper-left corner of the object. Glitchy inventory system can cause you to easily pick-up\/put-down an object costing you valuable Action points<br>-How managing your inventory is never explained well. For example, took awhile to figure out character's &quot;backpacks&quot; can hold seemingly infinite amount of items!!<br>-For a &quot;Troma&quot; game, it greatly lacks the gore; it feels dare I say: unfinished? Fallout, Arcanum, Postal, and just many others games even from that time, were bloodier, gorier. So this was a disappointment.<br>-Sometimes, you'll spend TOO MUCH time hunting for that ONE LAST ENEMY on the map<br>-Game mouse-scroll is way too fast and there is no way to use the keys or to slow it down<br>-Wished the world was more detailed and fleshed out<br>-Rough around some edges<br><br>MAYBE:<br>-Permadeath! If one of your first 3 guys gets wasted, they're OUT.<br>You advance into the next missions with whoever survived. OR, if you can reload from your last saved (you can save whenever)<br>Some people will like this, some will not. So far with my game, I got a hot chick added in one mission.<br>-Doesn't seem to be anyway to restore health during a mission, so might be too challenging for some.<br>-Sick, Troma style humor isn't for everyone (gosh, maybe anyone, LOL)<br><br>OVERALL:<br>Remember, it's not an RPG or even some big epic game. <br>AND DON'T TAKE IT SERIOUSLY!<br>You go out and kill the bad guys (and whoever else). That's it.<br>It would be interesting if they make a new game where they implement more things to do, more gore, more interaction, etc.<br>Some will FAP to the (mostly partial) nudity in the game (or
