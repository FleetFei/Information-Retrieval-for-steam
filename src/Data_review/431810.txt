Rencounter tries to apply the same mechanic, where you are shown an overview of the map to see which rooms are connected to which place and where you need to go next. The other room might be filled with supplies you drastically needed or may contain enemies you need to fight.<br><br> If there are enemies in a room, you are then shifted to a 3D overview from where the gameplay is turn based. Hunger levels go down quite rapidly and you might run through a few rooms without getting any sort of valuable loot or supplies.
