But you don\u2019t control the surfer himself. Your job is to change the placement of the waves (up and down) to get a good ride and score as many points as you can. You have to bypass different objects like ships, rocks and bones and try not to hit any of these.<br><br>But there are not only usual waves to surf on. This way we managed to get the highest score.<br><br>Surfingers also offers local and online multiplayer. You play splitscreen and the one getting farther than the others wins.
