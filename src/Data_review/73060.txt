Sound is nothing special. <br><br>The campaign is a series of &quot;missions&quot; where the new King of Ardania must gather his closest allies and sail forth and solve the mystery of the rising tide of undead. There are also limited resource mode and survival mode if you need more of a challenge. <br><br>All in all, DA is a bit of also-ran in the RTS \/ Tower offense-defense game.
