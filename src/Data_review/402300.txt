As time passes new classes arrive and higher levelled and better equipped adventurers too.  Eventually organized parties will attempt to reach the depths of your dungeon.<br><br>You constantly balance between increasing the strength of your dungeon and monsters while also allowing adventurers to experience thrill and sucess, encouraging them to return.
