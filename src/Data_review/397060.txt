The puzzles are mostly easy and intended to teach certain rules, but some of the AI duels can be pretty difficult unless you build a deck to counter them. However, unlike Hearthstone's adventure mode, they are actually balanced and you don't constantly feel steamrolled.<br><br>You'll get a good amount of cards and currency from playing through the free components of the game, by the way.
