These are the kind of structures that Recursed requires you to construct in order to solve platforming puzzles.<br><br>Rooms are chests that you can pick up, carry around, and enter in. By nesting them in other chests, you can shape the world by creating intricate entangled configurations that allow you to reach places otherwise unattainable.<br><br>The fun aspect of the game is to make mental maps of such knotted networks of chests, and to keep track of the relationships between rooms as you change them. It is an abstract mental maze of endless loops and infinite regressions.
