shapes and colours. Switch adjacent stones around to match three or more in a vertical or horizontal line to remove them and make all the stones above drop down. In each square of the grid (underneath every stone) is a tile. For every match that's made over a tile, that tile is then removed. Remove all tiles on the board to complete the stage. As far as I know, that's the bare minimum for every match-3 game? But this has some extra features.<br><br>As you clear the tiles, you reveal a maze-like track hiding beneath the board.
