In the end, it actually does what it says on the tin, provided you can get it working.<br><br>First, my hardware setup is almost custom-tailored for good results with this program. On my system, as with most I assume, I had to manually select my audio output device and make sure it was in WASAPI mode. Anyhow... this fixed my garbled sound issue.  Next, I chose a preset...
