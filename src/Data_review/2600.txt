It has an interesting story and world, interesting lore and interesting characters with also very surprisingly great voice acting. I absolutely loved exploring the open world hub areas without the burden of objective markers, soaking in the world and atmosphere from just talking to npcs and discovering side missions which by the way are very well done and tell great stories on their own and end up being very memorable. The ideologies of the different vampire factions I found very intriguing in particular. The main missions offer up nice variety with the visuals and locations you are visiting and you can tell the developers tried to make an effort to make each mission feel different from each other so the game doesn\u2019t get repetitive. However you can tell the developers ran out of either time or money because the level design in the later stages become really generic and repetitive especially compared to the early missions like the amazing ocean house hotel. The rpg mechanics are implemented to the game very well, I appreciated the amount of options in the character creation and the leveling up system. I really liked the morality system with the humanity and being faithful to the masquerade, it\u2019s well integrated with the main and side missions in particular. I appreciated all the different ways you can tackle an objective even though I always ended up just going in guns blazing.
