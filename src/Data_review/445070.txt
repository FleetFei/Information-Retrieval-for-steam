I like the theme. The &quot;roll&quot; box looks a tad out of place as just a green box, perhaps a little bit of style on that and the dice, but not a complaint just a suggestion.<br>The music is good, decent variety.<br><br>The game itself:<br>I'd never heard of this boardgame but it looked pretty fun, so I got this. The &quot;how to play&quot; tells you what you need to know; how to move, what does what, how to win (or lose :P), and it all became clear after a game or two.<br>Pass and play works fine, haven't tried online multiplayer but I suspect there's a small community at the moment (whatever could tell you that, oh single english review?)<br>The AI is, from what I can tell, good.
