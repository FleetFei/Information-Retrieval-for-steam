In total, you have 5 leagues and 2 cups to play in and the aim is to reach and win the Premier League and the Cups, of course.<br><br>The management aspect of the game has good depth but it is easy to use and understand. If you manage to finish the season in the top 3 teams, you will be rewarded with additional prestige points. <br><br>Match day \u2013 Each half will consist of 24 action moves to be used between each team, with a total of 48 moves per match. Remember, you and your opponent will use 3 action moves per turn. They are plenty of moves to make your mark and score goals.
