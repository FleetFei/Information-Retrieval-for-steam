While you have access to every unit and building in the latter you have to complete Quests in order to gain technology points. Unlike campaign mode you manage your production and research on a turn-based global map. If this sounds already good you will like the second part as well since the combat takes place on a terrain you are used to from other RTS games: maps in real time. Besides that there are also Quests for you to complete. Sounds good doesn't it?<br>It's just a different approach which I really happened to like.<br><br>Also there's another thing that separates Wyrmsun from your usual fantasy setting. Mods.<br>Wyrmsun is a game with high mod potential and features built in Steam Workshop integration.<br>So what does that mean for the game and the players?
