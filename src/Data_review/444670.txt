Tracks are incredibly twisty, turny and bumpy. They remind me of audiosurf tracks, or rainbow road from Mario kart. Racing down these is a blast.<br><br>Even cooler though, is that you have the ability to roll off of these tracks and skip ahead. Since the tracks are constantly going downwards, you can simply fly off of the edge and land somewhere below. You have a set amount of time to do this, or else you'll respawn back at the last gate you past through. This adds a great risk, great reward mechanic in the game, where skipping ahead could gain you the lead, but not timing the jump properly could cause you to fall behind.<br><br>There are multiple gates throughout the levels, that once you go through serve as a checkpoint, and will fill your boost meter. I'm hoping the simple stuff like that will be fixed, because I don't really have any problems with the game itself.<br><br>It's a great idea for a game, well executed, and I honestly can't wait to see the online mode available.
