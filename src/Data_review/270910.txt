re-release of the 2001 smash hit, Worms World Party. This game was released in July 2015.  The remastered edition has proven to not be as popular as the predecessors with only 6000 people picking this game up from Steam. <br><br>The gameplay of Worms World Party Remastered is essentially the same as the original game. Absolutely everything is the same. The huge selection of weapons in your worm\u2019s arsenal makes a return; the classics are still here such as bazookas and grenades, along with some of the newer weapons such as exploding sheep, homing pigeons and napalm strikes. The game has a single player mode, online multiplayer, and local multiplayer. I only played a couple of matches in the single player mode against the AI, but this proved to be a difficult matter, simply because the AI in this game totally knows how to control their weapons more than me, and can take the wind and other elements into consideration, meaning you'll probably get beaten. <br><br>That being said, the multiplayer is where you'll get the most fun out of Worms as you should well know if you played the other games in the series. The online play is broken though, I tried playing and it is a convoluted mess. You have to put in IP addresses of servers, ports, everything that shouldn't be needed to be done in a remastered version of the game - when I finally managed to get it all set up, there was two people playing...two people in the multiplayer side of this game. There was no point me trying to get into the game as they were just sat in their own two separate lobbies themselves.  Where I had the most enjoyment and gameplay was in the local multiplayer playing with my partner.  <br><br>Before you start a match, you get to create your own team of worms, giving your team a name, then naming all of your individual worms - next you can give your team a style of voice, with a large selection of accents to just from, customize your worms grave stones, and your teams flag. Finally you also get to choose your teams &quot;victory&quot; music which plays when you win a match.  The hot potatoes style gameplay of passing the controller between each other works just fine, as we were both laughing at each other's failings on their turns. One matched slowly turned into eight or nine matches, it became very competitive at times too. <br><br>The graphics in Worms World Party Remastered didn't really get a massive revamp over the previous games; the resolution did get upped to 1080p though, which is a nice plus to see. The UI is clean and easy to navigate around. The audio in Worms\u2019s games is always fascinating to me. The cute little worm voices which act as a sort of commentator to your turns all have a comical value attached to their lines. All of the weapons have an over the top sound effect with them too. The holy hand grenade is just beautiful<br><br><a class=\"bb_link\" href=\"https:\/\/www.youtube.com\/watch?v=lPryqNLXV7E\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noreferrer\"  id=\"dynamiclink_0\">https:\/\/www.youtube.com\/watch?v=lPryqNLXV7E<\/a><br><br>Worms is always a pick and play sort of game, I had played the previous game so I knew what to do, but my partner who had never played a worms game before was easily able to pick up the controls after a turn or two of playing, don't tell her I said this, but she actually got quite good and managed to beat me more times than I am happy to admit! If you feel that you want a little extra challenge in the game though, you can give one team more worms than the other one, giving you a handicap situation. <br><br>Every single match in worms has a randomly generated world. You'll never fight on the same map more than once. The worlds which the game creates can sometimes give one team an unfair advantage, such as hiding a couple of worms in a remote location with no hope of them either doing anything remotely for a few turns, but this also means they can't get hurt for a few turns.  Along with the randomly generated terrain, there are also a number of weapon crate drops which happen at random points too; these crates hold the more rarer and powerful weapons such as nuclear tests and air strikes for example. On top of these weapon crates, you'll also find utility crates which hold more useful tools such as blowtorches, parachutes, teleports and many more of the more random items in the arsenal. <br><br>Worms World Party Remastered does not have an end to the amount of content you can get out of it. You could literally play this game for hours on end and not get bored of it. We have only been playing the game for three hours now and see no reason for us wanting to stop anytime soon. This is definitely a game to keep installed for when you want to have a couple of games with your friends on the local multiplayer mode. <br><br>Even though Worms World Party Remastered is supposedly the better version of the World Party game, it still had technical problems. Firstly, the game will refuse to run if your screens resolution is anything higher than 1080p. I had to lower my settings to this from 4k in Windows before I could even get past the main menu screen. The technical jargon that you need to do for any sort of online multiplayer should not be present in a remastered game. The game is on Steam so it should just run off the Steams network multiplayer options. Be warned, when playing online too; your external IP address is clearly shown for everyone to see... The only other small issue is that the menus also don't show in 1080, they are in 640 by 480. Whilst this isn\u2019t a massive problem, the game shouldn\u2019t really be advertising itself for being completely 1080. The game also works very well with the Steam controller as well as the regular Xbox pad. <br><br>Worms World Party Remastered is definitely a game I will recommend, purely because it works on newer operating systems without you having to download patches and mods. I will say that you should only be looking at picking this game up if you plan on playing it locally with friends. The single player is not worth it, and the online multiplayer is non-existent now once you manage to get past the strange setup. Playing this game with friends will bring plenty of exciting times and tons of fun for a very little price of \u00a310, 99. <br><br><b><u> Tom's Score Card<\/u><\/b><br>1) Stay away<br>2) Not Recommended<br>3) Only recommended when on sale<br><b><u>4) Recommended<\/u><\/b><br>5) Highly recommended<br>6) This is a must play<br><br>If you found this review helpful, please consider giving it a thumbs up.
