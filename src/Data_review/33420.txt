The Cartel is the &quot;odd one out&quot; in this series as it is the only one taking place in a modern day setting instead of the good old Wild West. Interestingly, the concept works and it makes for a good - albeit somewhat mediocre - game that fans can feel free to pick up as it becomes, after all, an important part of the franchise.<br><br>The game is best played cooperatively. For some reason the developer decided to pick not two (remember Bound In Blood?) but 3 protagonists. At the time (2011) the Chrome engine already looked outdated and while there is a handful of cinematics the game's story is not exactly very original.
