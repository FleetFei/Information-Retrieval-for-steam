7\/10<\/b><\/div><br>UFHO2 Is a very nice (now) free2play puzzle game developed by a small italian indie software house. How can I describe it? It kind of reminds me of a chess game, but ignoring the entire setting consisting of funny aliens going around in space and meeting other species to confront, you will have to move your tiny character along the &quot;game field&quot; with a limited number of moves before your turn ends, and the purpose is to reach and grab more crystal than the adversary. Most of the times you will play 1v1, but in some missions you will play 1v2, 2v2 or worse! The peculiar part of this game, besides the funny alien voices, is the game field made of hexagon checks which link to each other by limited sides, hence you can move from check to check only if they are facing by the riht sides. If they're not you can rotate them, but this will decrease your available moves per turn. <br>This sound simple and almost boring but the real challenge lies in the fact that You can turn every check in the field but your adversary can do this as well, and considering that the AI is quite clever the game will quickly turn into a prediction game, where you must value all the possible moves from the adversary and make his path longer or more difficult so he has to waste moves to make a quick path while you reach your goals.<br><br>That's all. Along the game you will find other powerups and things like that, but the game is actually this and nothing more, yet it entertains you with AIs more and more challenging.<br><br>Oh, I almost forgot.
