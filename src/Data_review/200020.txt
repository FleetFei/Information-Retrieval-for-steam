In some sections you must avoid lethal fire from tanks, helicopters or hvg. machine gun emplacements [that you cannot destroy,] or the like, while picking up ammo and grenades from fallen enemies, until you find conveniently placed weapons at the end of a level section. I found myself smiling as my AI buddy and me took on hundreds of cliche enemies ala Rambo style.  I guess if you played games twenty years ago this may remind you of the titles of the same franchise and bring a smile of nostalgia to your face.<br><br>Overall though I would only recommend getting this in a sale.
