Music is OK and it fits well with the game.<br><br>STORY<br><br>You're some random dude who's learning some mad skills. That's about it. Thanks, Reload!<br><br>CONTENT<br><br>Nothing to say here. It's a clean, classy game in which you're preparing to toast bad guys 4 realz by toasting them in their simulated forms. One other minor negative is that start-up is just a tad slow - staring at &quot;Copyright 2014&quot; (paraphrasing) gets old fast.
