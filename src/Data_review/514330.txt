Even her \u300aCombo Challenge\u300b is probably the easiest in the game now as it is far easier than Hitomi's. Nevertheless, linking combos together during fights themselves does require either some experience or practice as players will have to learn cancelling as she otherwise will perform short pauses in-between moves that might be disadvantageous to oneself.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Design<\/div><br>Mai sticks out from other fighters as many of her combos end in tinder, fire, and explosions.  Even on PC there will be some lesser particle effects to admire. If you like a spectacle in your fighting games, this definitely is the DLC for you.
