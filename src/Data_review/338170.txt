Finally, player count could be a real issue at some point. For months before the Free Weekend that's occurring as I write this, the game had less than 20 players. It has no single-player content, so unless you have a friend to 1v1 against, you're boned if no one else is online. The game has a 1v1 mode vs a random player, but a tiny pool of players means the ranking system can't really do it's job well and you'll be matched with the same insane player endlessly. I'd more than recommend it on a very cheap sale or bundle because that's sure to bring in some players for a while and the low price means even if you can only play for that week, it's not as much of a let-down as it could be at a more substantial price point.
