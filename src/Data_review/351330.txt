RPG outside of Steam for a few years now, I thought I'd describe exactly what playing this game is like and why I enjoy it. Please note that the gameplay is not like your typical RPG, nor is it all that similar to the other DROD games, although it is still a turn based game that requires a lot of thinking.<br><br>DROD RPG is a resource management optimisation game. The optimisation is not just about choosing what resources to spend, but WHEN to spend those resources to ensure you make the most out of them. Fighting monsters is automatic and costs you Hit Points based on your Attack and Defense stats, the higher they are, the less HP you lose. You also have keys and money that can open doors. It's one big manually crafted puzzle.<br><br>Along the way you will reach numerous scorepoints that award you a score based on the resources you have. The offical level set, Tendry's Tale, is split into two entirely separate halves, and the second half is quite difficult.<br><br>Ultimately DROD RPG is a game that you'll either love or you'll hate but you should definitely give it a try.
