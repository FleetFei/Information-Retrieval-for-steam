Once you are in the game, seated at your captain\u2019s cockpit and piloting the battleship through an asteroid field in space, the action becomes frantic! I myself, sometimes like to just hold down the triggers for auto fire and having the controllers rumble and rumble endlessly made me think twice about their quality as it was getting pretty loud.
