(A few bucks and even fewer experience points.) Before you could fly up and go straight... now you either have to wind back and forth through the maze of trails, OR as i did, put them off until after you get the horse. (You pretty much have to revisit most of the islands several times anyway.)<br>The third is the difficulity in finding enough of any one (well 5 actually) troop. This is even worse in F&amp;I than WOTN, because they have added a leveling up system for troops which gives a BIG incentive to keeping the same ones throughout.
