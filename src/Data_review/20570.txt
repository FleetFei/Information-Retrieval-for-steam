of War II Chaos Rising, is the first expansion pack to the Dawn of War II RTS game by Relic Entertainment. Although it uses the basic principles laid out by Dawn of War II, it adds new and interesting mechanics to the formula which makes this expansion unique in itself.<br><br>The player takes the role of the 'Force Commander' from the original Dawn of War II along with his band of Space Marines. Here, one year after the Tyranid Invasion, the  capital planet of Aurelia, long lost in the Warp, has once again emerged. It is now up to the Blood Ravens to find out what vile things it has brought from the depths of the Chaos infested Warp.<br><br>PROS:<br>+ Old Formula with new Mechanics: - The expansion adds a 'Corruption' bar that measures how much your characters and soldiers are tainted by Chaos. The player will now have to be more careful on their actions since certain events carry consequences that may drastically alter the story.<br><br>+ Carry Over Progress: - Rarely do I encounter a game that will allow a carry over of your save files with all (most) of your progress. Lucky for us, Chaos Rising is one such game.<br><br>+ Fun new Toys: - Extended Skill Tree, New Wargear, new Squad Member(s), some new Maps (also a CON, see below), and an extended story to the Lore of the Blood Ravens will keep the players entertained as they develop new tactics and builds for their Space Marines.<br><br>+ Squadmates Does not Die as easily: - This is just an observation on my part but it seems that the Squadmates of your Sergeants doesn\u2019t die as often as they did on the original game. This is a huge bonus FOR ME, since I thought it was kinda 'wrong' that Space Marines died way to easily back in Dawn of War II.<br><br>CONS:<br>- Shameless Map Recycling: - Most of the maps from the original Dawn of War II game were heavily reused in this expansion where the devs just rotated the default camera angle 90 degrees and tried to pass  them off as new maps.<br><br>- Uneven Voice \/ Sound Levels: - The OST was great as was the Voice Acting, but the speech sound levels were not properly leveled. Some voices play very loudly which hurts the ears, others play softly that it is drowned by the background music.<br><br>- Too Short: - I found the campaign to short with fewer missions than the original game. Optional quest are a rarity which limits the possible enjoyment of Space Marine builds.<br><br>- Heretics: - Filthy Chaos worshiping Heretics.<br><br>CONCLUSION:<br>GOOD EXPANSION, I like this expansion and enjoyed playing its campaign. It has expanded the lore that surrounds the Blood Ravens Chapter by adding more mystery and intrigue to an already secretive Space Marine Chapter. The new Corruption Mechanic as well as the multitude of new Wargears and expanded skill tree shall keep the players entertained with new build possibilities and tactics.<br><br>It may be worth the full price for some people but seeing how short the Campaign was, I would rather have people buy it during a Sale.
