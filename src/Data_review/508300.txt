For a game with &quot;Tactics&quot; in the title, I was really hoping for the ability to put those tactical skills to use in game. There is some strategy play as the title also comments on, but not a great deal. The game is not complete yet, so I am hoping for changes that will let the player use more tactical choices to better accomplish his or her goals. <br><br> I also do not like the scenario system at all. This is merely a personal thing, as I know there are a lot of people that do like the scenario or individual mission system. I prefer a large world, like a sandbox world where you can choose from different countries, something like Crusader Kings series. This game is more akin to a system like Warcraft or something, where you play each mission, and after it is done, you move on, but what you have done in that previous mission does not matter much at all. There are some things that do carry over, in the form of pantheon, but otherwise not much. This can be a pro or a con, depending on the person, but I wouldn't hold back a recommendation because of this.<br><br> If you are looking for a complete game ready to be played start to finish, don't buy this game yet.
