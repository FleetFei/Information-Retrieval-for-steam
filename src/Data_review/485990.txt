To answer that, do you know Fairy Tail? If yes, you might want to buy this. If not, I can't think of a reason why you'd want to, unless it's to complete your DLC collection for this game (or just happen to like the preview screenshots of the skins).<br><br>So, what you get are sixteen Fairy Tail themed character skins. Sounds like a pretty good deal for any fan of the show or manga. But it's not as great as it sounds. Unfortunately, not all of the skins are as accurate as they could have been. Generally, the outfits themselves look fine and what you'd expect them to look like. But some of the characters' hair choices don't match those of the original content:<br>Why is Gray's hair brown when it should be black?<br>Why is Lucy's hair pink-ish when it should be blonde?<br>Mirajane's, Jellal's and Aquarius's hair are also not what they're supposed to be like. I don't understand how they can get all the characters' outfits down perfectly, but then mess up something as simple as not giving them their correct hair colours.<br>Furthermore, I'm disappointed by not seeing the Fairy Tail character names used in the UI during the actual gameplay, but instead we still get shown their Dead or Alive character names. Also, they keep their default voices, when I'd have loved to hear the official anime voice actors for them.<br><br>Sadly, as Fairy Tail fans, this is the best we can get, and the closest we can get to seeing our beloved characters fight it out against each other in a videogame published in the west.
