There's a lot to unpack so lets just dive right in.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Aesthetics<\/div>Sublevel Zero looks like what my memory of Descent is. As I've been playing the game I keep thinking to myself &quot;wow, this looks so much like Descent just a little better&quot; and then I went and played some Descent and it's amazing how much older it feels. Everything important has a color. Weapons and their ammo are color coordinated, enemies are colored to match their type, and while to start there sometimes feels like an overwhelming amount of color, you quickly learn to appreciate it.<br><br>While it is an excellent upgrade to the Descent aesthetic in most ways, one significant downside is the enemies.
