Rather than shuffle off to the job centre to seek new employment, he decides the best way to fix his circumstances is to oust the ruler of Austria-Hungary.<br><br>Perhaps he sees it as the next logical step on his career path, which makes at least as much sense as the rest of the game. Some of this is traditional item-based puzzle solving, where you use whatever crap you find lying around Viktor\u2019s world to mash together peculiar solutions to peculiar situations, but just as much is dialogue-based.
