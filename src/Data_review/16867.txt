The special tile improvement for the Inca civilization is \u201cTerrace farm\u201d which allows you to build a farm on hills, this helps to increase how fast Inca cities grow because of increased food supplies. The special unit for the Inca or the Slingers (which replace the archer unit), and the final note worthy perks are that Units ignore terrain costs when moving into any tile with hills, and no maintenance cost for improvement to hills.<br><i>(just a personal side-note here, Isabella is a pompous noble and we shouldn't be kept in the same room because one of us will be provoked into invading because of insults to ones sense of honor.
