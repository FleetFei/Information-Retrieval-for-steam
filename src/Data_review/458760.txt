A slightly spiffier way of presenting a short story or novella, in other words. Obviously this will be fine for some, and a deal-breaker for others. <br><br>   As long as you're not in the latter category, the story of this VN is, I'm pleased to say...actually pretty darned good (if a tad on the sappy side)! I suspect it's not too much of a spoiler to say it has something to do with the &quot;nature&quot; of Artificial Intelligence, and the curious effects of the cold, vast loneliness of space...so if that sounds like your cup of tea (or, er, tube of space-paste), there really isn't much to hold you back. Especially when the frigger is FREE.<br><br>   All told, not a bad little effort at all (I even found myself getting the teeniest bit teary by the end)!
