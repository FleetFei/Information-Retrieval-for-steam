The marine specialists don't administer the medication, even when the animals are placed in an isolation tank. It's for these reasons that I normally don't bother with species that are 100% aquatic. I'm going to experiment a bit and see if there's something I can do to compensate for this issue, and I'll update if I find anything that helps. <br>______________________________________<br><br>For
