The objective is to keep this enormous orange a** (I guess a TYRANTasauras Rump, or a T-Rump)  from getting to the White House.  And let me tell you, you do NOT want this a** getting anywhere near the White House.  The last thing you want is for T-Rump to get its hands on nuclear weapons or the world is doomed.  Dooooommed!   In other words...  Game over man. <br><br>You have Big Beautiful Walls which are preventing the giant a**  from getting to the White House.   To complicate things the a** keeps flatuating out its devoted minions called Trumplings who will attack the walls.    They are pretty efficient at getting through the walls.  Let me tell you the walls aren't all that Yuuuge, believe me, but I don't think it would matter if they were 10 feet higher and someone else was paying for them,  the Trumplings aren't human so they chew right through them.   Trumplings are mean nasty vicious little creatures, deplorable even,  though generally pretty mindless. <br><br>You can place big American Guns in various locations to take out the Trumplings.  The guns will fire automatically at the Trumplings, though at a slow rate.  You can manually take control of any gun to fire much faster.  Though it seems Trumplings are most vulnerable when attacked from the front.    Unfortunately the T-Rump is invulnerable to the guns.   So it will only be stopped by the walls, which will cause it to turn around and go the other direction.  <br><br>To help you out, the Trumplings when killed by your ample supply of guns on every street corner, will sometimes drop kernals of truth.   You can pick these up and when you've gotten 5 of them you can launch the Fist of Truth.  This will temporarily stun the T-Rump, buying you some time,  and also  help to deflate the T-Rump's massive ego (its health bar).   If you can get its ego down to zero you can save the day!  Sometimes you can get the T-Rump to go in the opposite direction when hitting it with the Fist of Truth, though I've not figured out if there is a way to reliably do that.    If there is a trick to getting the T-Rump to do that, it would help out a lot.   <br><br>All-in-all it's a simple fun little game, with a pretty basic story line.   Granted it's not very realistic. A giant orange a** trying to get to the White House to destroy the world!    Seriously, where do people come up with these crazy ideas?   Still it is a game and a halloween themed horror game at that, so I guess being the halloween season explains the crazy concept  of this game. <br><br>Good thing something like this could never happen
