It has a nice way of keeping the game too easy or too hard with the threat level that you can choose from, Low, Medium or High. Starting off you will probably want to start with low just to get used to the game. But where this game falls apart is the controls. You start off without any indication of what each button does or what. I would suggest playing this game with friends who already own it or if you're really desperate then buy a 4-pack so that you can play this game with your other friends. I was confused as I hadn't even had time to adjust myself to what the controls were. I would've liked a explanation to what you had to do to kill the ghost at the start when it explains how to win (keep generator on until the helicopter arrives) which would've made it a little less stressful. The game can also get a little repetitive as this isn't much variation to each of the levels which there are only 6 of. The ranking system whoever is nicely presented and well made. You unlock guns by levelling up then use the money you have got form the missions to buy them. It works really well with this sort of game. It's a great way to pass the time either if you're playing alone or with some of your friends. Although I would've liked the choice of First-Person mode instead of over the should Third-Person mode.
