Bad porting aside, the game revolves around just killing and destroying stuff all the time. It lacks variety in it's main missions which showed real promise. For argument's sake one can say the game is old and it's not fair be hard on it but there were games way before SR2's time that offered much more variety in missions. The story is not that compelling too. The story is all about you being a prick, going on a killing spree, eliminating other gangs that had taken over the city which the Saints once ruled. May be the charcter was meant to be that way - an arrogant a-hole, who knows?, I found the cockiness annoying. Be forewarned- Port issues like fps drops, crashes, loud cutscenes can get you \u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665 off. Fps drops not so much but crashes are a little frequent, atleast once in 2 hours or so. To fix loud cutscene audio i had to fiddle around lowering windows volume level considerably and adjusting in-game sound sliders until i found a good balance between cutscene volume level and in-game volume level.<br><br>Where the game makes you forget all of this is the Activities and Side Missions part. Its pure fun and can add to atleast half of your playtime. There are tons of activities like brawling, racing, pimping, trailblazing with an ATV, Heli-assault, Drug-trafficking, Insurance Fraud-my personal favourite much like goat simulator, Hitman targets etc. These side activities really make a difference and makes up for the lack of variety in the main missions. If they could've just put in a taste of these during the main missions then it could've been a much better experience overall. May be the core idea here was to offer a direct approach in the main storyline ie, eliminate your foes with guns blazing and chill out later doing these cool\/varied activities to brush off the hollow approach or vice-versa. It leaves you with a mixed feeling in the end.        <br><br>The graphics are decent considering its an old game. The music and voice acting is good though its a bummer you can't use custom mp3s for the radio. Driving, shooting etc are pretty good (I used a controller).
