It seems a bit random whether or not a line of defense with a goalkeeper can still be passed with mediocre odds, despite a goalkeeper having a 40+ defense value (my strikers usually have like 20 or less). I understand that 40 is simply the max value the goalkeeper can roll, but unless I've missed something I've got no clue when my player will do better than average or when the goalkeeper is likely not to stop a shot.
