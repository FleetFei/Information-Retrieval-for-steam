Boo.<br><br><br>Now I'll list the differences in Clockwork Man: The Hidden World:<br><br>   - The length is longer. Because of the higher difficulty, possibly much longer.<br>   - This game has a journal where Miranda records her adventures and anything of note. The characters and world are pretty nicely designed (it takes place in a sort of Victorian Steampunk-ish setting) and the voice acting is decent. Gameplay primarily consists of clicking within scenes to find a list of objects that are hidden throughout. Occasionally, there will also be scenes where you have to find, combine and use objects in specific ways to solve puzzles.
