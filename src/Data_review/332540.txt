Don't let this put you off if you don't know how to play Poker. The game has a very nice tutorial and a &quot;cheat sheet' with some combinations you can use. Also, it's possible to use just one card to attack, so the player will always have an attack option.<br><br>Right now there's three classes and four chapters to progress.
