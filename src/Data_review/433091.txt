But you'll likely phase out SPARKs as the game progresses.  They can't benefit from PCS or weapon upgrades.  And they spend a long time being repaired... slowly... one at a time.  I'm convinced all of this is a design choice:  Assist the early game then yield to stronger human heroes later.<br><br>Chief Lily Shen is playable during the new story mission.
