You have many graphic options in the menu that you can mess around, which I feel is very nice for those that do like to change their graphical settings. I feel it is a bit overpriced, you're pretty much paying for the anime name, which I don't see it being justifiable of a 60$ USD price tag, this feels more like a 30-40$ game.
