Another change is that Hercules is no longer sleeping, he is always standing up but other than that most of the assets seem the same from the first title which I was happy about. No voice acting except for the narrator and she does a good job, however her lines were lower in volume than the game\u2019s general music causing me to have to adjust the volume whenever I watched a cut scene. The music of the game is repetitive, sadly. The same few tunes play over and over again, while not bad, the get dull.<br><br>I was happy with the improvements made to <i>12 Labours of Hercules II: The Cretan Bull<\/i>, it makes the game feel more complete than the first title. Collecting them all unlocks a simple jigsaw puzzle to play with and completing it grants a bonus stage, there are four all together.
