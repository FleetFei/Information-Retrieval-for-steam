Options to adjust controller and joystick input are also available. Unfortunately, this is as far as settings go; there are no video options whatsoever.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Performance<\/div><br>While there are no video options mentioned above, the game runs smoothly at 60fps throughout. You always want to be on the move, traversing the map to pick up new weapons and their ammo in order to survive the onslaught. There are 15 weapons and by the last mission, you should have used most, if not all of the weapons. Each weapon is unique and have their own HUD, audio, and damage profiles. Movement is very smooth, which is good news as you\u2019ll be moving a lot to dodge enemy fire. Each enemy type is unique, ranging from simple footsoldiers to large ships that cover the sky.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Game Modes<\/div><br>While completing the story mode will take you 4-5 hours (if you go around collecting everything), there are other game modes to try each with their own rules.<br><br>In the <b>story mode<\/b>, you play through each level to unlock subsequent levels. Once you have enough power cubes (there are 10 per level), you bring them to a satellite dish to receive a message. These messages outline how the rescue mission is going. Each level also has these cute little \u2018robodudes\u2019 (7 to 8 depending on the level) where collecting them will net bonus points in the end.<br><br>In addition to the story mode, the game boasts 4 other game modes: <b>Arcade<\/b>, <b>Deadline<\/b>, <b>Sequence<\/b>, and <b>Survival<\/b>.  <br><br>In <b>Arcade<\/b>, you start with 3 lives and must survive as long as possible. Every 500,000 points you earn will net you a life. <br><br>In <b>Deadline<\/b>, you must score as many points as you can in 5 minutes. <br><br>In <b>Sequence<\/b>, you have 2 minutes to destroy as many enemies as possible in each zone. There are 5 zones total. When you die, you are taken to the next zone without all the weapons you have found and equipped (besides the starting pistol). <br><br>In <b>Survival<\/b>, you only have a single life and must kill an enemy every 30 seconds or else the time hits 0. Once the time hits 0 or you die, the game ends. Each of these modes use the same levels from the story mode.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Audio and Music<\/div><br>Overall, the audio effects in this game is great. Standouts include the sound of your character as he moves, which makes a nice mechanical sound.
