The girl with the bear as an example can throw a knife with one button and teleport to and\/or trade places with it with another button. It is a very intriguing usage of the formula with enough differences to stand on its own two feet. There are of course powerups like in Bomberman, but interestingly the more pickups the character gets, the more they charge up to ultimately use their Ultra ability.
