Exzeal is a vertical shmup and a beautiful direct sequel of the classic Trizeal. However, Exzeal differs from Trizeal in essence, because while Trizeal had 3 different transformations of the ship at any time during the game (at the touch of a button), Exzeal doesn't allow this, however there are 4 different types of ships settings that can be selected before the start of the game, they are: [Type 1] with a normal shot and also a much more powerful shot which is charged holding down the appropriate button (R-Type style) so, when fired, this charged shot eliminates many enemies at once and is important to say that you can  charge the charged shot faster making a  360-degree rotation movement with the analog stick; [Type 2] similar to type 1, but the charged shot travels beyond the ship's front and back as well on the upper and lower diagonals; [Type 3] allows only front normal shots, but causes more damage than regular shots of the other types and finally [Type 4] has normal front shot and also a kind  of short-range generated by the movement of the ship in the directions rear, left and right. These 4 different types of configurations bring good variety to gameplay  The game's graphics were very good for the 2007 standards and remain good even today. The songs are great, contributing directly to the immersion of the game. The controls respond well to commands  (I used the official wireless Xbox 360 controller through the analog stick). The player can save replays of matches and the game also has an online leaderboard very good where you can watch the replay of other players  inside the leaderboard, something always useful for those who want to know and learn the patterns and techniques used by the best players. Exzeal is a beautiful sequence that honors the classic Trizeal, with good graphics, nice music and a very good and fun gameplay.
