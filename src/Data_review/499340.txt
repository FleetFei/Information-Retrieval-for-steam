The game is set in an an asylum with the player being inserted into the role of Turner. Turner  has led a troubled past and an unstable mind. Why else would he be in an asylum? There are two movement mechanics in the game. The first is your basic set of left, right and jump. The second is a rotating\/turning system. This second system rotates or turns the entire level in either clockwise or anti-clockwise direction - it's your call. You will need to quickly learn and become skillful with both mechanics as they are equally essential to complete the game. They have been well designed and work as expected.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Over 400 Levels<\/div><br>Even the toughest of levels are generally completed within a matter of seconds. This would normally be a problem because players like to have a lot of content within their games. However, to encapsulate the 'bargain' feeling of the game there are over 400 of them within the game. The games range in difficulty from very easy - those that introduce new gameplay mechanics (such as collapsing platforms, activation pads, etc.) and ridiculously challenging. This is a fantastic mix that will have you rage quitting often. But the key thing to note is that the game is very fair. For every level that I've felt was designed poorly (unfairly) I've returned to it later to realize that it is fair and that it was me.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">More Than Just Puzzles<\/div><br>I've played my fair share of puzzle games this year. Many of them offer nothing more than that, a puzzle game. Turner is different. The comic book style cutscenes tie together a well structured story. This was one of my favorite aspects of the game. It shows that the developers pay attention to details in their game development. This is something that I can appreciate as I continue to study a tertiary level game development course (on top of my multiple degrees). I wish this was something that I could see from more developers. However, I understand that if you fail at this aspect of your game that the response is &quot;Why bother?&quot; or &quot;That was pointless.&quot;<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Free Soundtrack<\/div><br>As an added bonus the game's soundtrack has been added as a free downloadable DLC.
