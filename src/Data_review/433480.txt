The Goblin King will rise again!<br><br>Rayon Riddles \u2013 Rise of the Goblins King is an adventure\/puzzle game where you control up to three different goblins to clear each level. The first level is more or less a tutorial to give you the feel of the game and show you how the craft system works. In a nutshell, you have to find blueprints within the level which will show you which item you will need to create a specific object. When you have these items, head up to a workbench to craft that piece. <br><br>The puzzles in Rayon Riddles \u2013 Rise of the Goblin are not very obvious, which is good but can also be a bit frustrating; more than once I was looking all around a level to figure out how to clear a stage. In that regards, make sure to fully explore the each map to discover blueprints. If you don\u2019t find them, you won\u2019t be able to move on, and that\u2019s where the frustration can creep in. Another point - you will die a fair bit in this game and your blue soul will fly back to a goblin crate in the sky and will literally be dropped back to the level. Plenty of humour in this title, which will give you a few laughs here and there. <br><br>The first couple of levels are fairly simple but from there onwards the difficulty will increase dramatically. There are plenty of objects to interact with, but depending on which goblin you control, you might not be able to use specific ones. For example, the Tech is the only one who can craft items, but he is not strong enough to lift substantial articles the likes of huge rocks or barrels; this is the speciality of the heavy goblin. However, neither of them can fit in small places like the tiny goblin can. Depending on the level and which goblins you have at your disposal, it can be a bit tricky. Nonetheless, you will have to make sure the Goblin King can move throughout the level freely to win the stage. <br><br>You do have enemies to avoid but realistically this game is all about solving the puzzle to overcome obstacles. <br>Graphically it looks very good and has plenty of humour. Control-wise, you can use the gamepad or the mouse and keyboard. You might find it difficult from time to time to drop an item on the workbench to craft. If you are not on a specific spot, your goblin might drop the article behind the table which can be a bit annoying.
