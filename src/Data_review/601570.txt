of life VN. This is true; no doubt about it, but the truth is that statement hardly really covers what it\u2019s about. It\u2019s about depression, loneliness, hope, friendship, love and a whole multitude of emotions. It\u2019s about finding yourself, about the entertainment industry and dealing with the cards life has presented you. Imagine a really good \u201clifetime channel\u201d or \u201cHallmark channel\u201d movie where your decisions help decide the final outcome of the story. Before I spoil anything let me run through the categories:<br><br>Story<br><br>Excellent. Witty and deep writing that can be tender and hilarious at times. Lots of different topics covered. Loved the frequent philosophical debates between characters. Think \u201cNotting Hill\u201d meets \u201cGood Will Hunting\u201d.<br><br>Acting<br><br>This VN has voice acting! And it\u2019s really good, not your typical cheezy ones that are often hired for VN\u2019s. Kind of different feel as well as half the characters are British and they seem to have really gone to the trouble of getting real Brits for the roles. Thank God for the subtitles as some of them have accents that might be hard for non-Brits to understand. The acting in itself is generally very good, especially the actresses that play Jacqueline and Natalie.<br><br>Art<br><br>I liked the sprites; nice to see sprites that have the proportions of normal people instead of the Boob Monsters we see so much in VN\u2019s. They\u2019re not the fanciest sprites in the world but their subtlety works well with the feel of the story. I think it would have been nice if they had costume changes since they were aiming for reality but I guess I\u2019m picking at straws. The backgrounds look like oil paintings, a nice little stylistic decision, it think. The sprite and background combination worked well.<br><br>Audio<br><br>I like thed music, nice little relaxing instrumental tracks that help flavor the story. I had some volume issues between scenes and at times with the music vs the VO. Granted it might be because of my laptop speakers as it was a lot better when I put on my headphones. Nevertheless I recommend the dev\u2019s bear this in mind in their next VO as a lot of people do play these games on their laptop speakers\/using basic speakers and it should still sound ok despite it.<br><br>Gameplay<br><br>It\u2019s a VN so obviously not a lot of gameplay to be had. This one had no minigame, which I don\u2019t mind that much as the story is really good and a minigame might have tried my patience too much. There are choices that lead to different endings. Only had one run of the game myself so don\u2019t know how the other endings pan out. Please ignore the amount of time it said I played as I accidentally left the game on after I finished playing it for the night. The game is about 2-3 hours depending on your own reading speed and whether you let the characters finish their lines of if you click ahead as the prompter finishes the lines. I believe there are only 3 endings so not a lot of replay value either.<br><br>Conclusion<br><br>All in all this is a VN that is atypical and I\u2019m happy that VN\u2019s like this one exist. This is a deep story and I do recommend it for those looking for a new take on how VN\u2019s can be used as an art. Not the lightest read in the world so if you\u2019re looking for a light bubblegum story there are other places to look.
