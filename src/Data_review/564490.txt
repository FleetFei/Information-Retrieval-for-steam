Unfortunately, a fair amount of the list appears behind the mini-map that resides in the upper right corner of the screen.  The mini-map cannot be moved, so it does not appear possible to see what is behind the map.<br><br>Regarding the mini-map, using tab you can click on the two buttons on the map, however, at this point, the buttons both seem to zoom the map in and there does not seem to be a way to zoom back out once it has zoomed in.  <br><br>As for interacting with characters, pressing E while looking at an NPC allows you to interact with the NPC, the NPC will make a statement after which you can pick one or two responses to the NPC's questions.  <br><br>When moving around, the character gets stuck on objects and areas of uneven ground.
