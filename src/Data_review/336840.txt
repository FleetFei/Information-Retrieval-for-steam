The mechanic creates fast paced gameplay requiring you to very quickly change colours in order to successfully progress through the level.<br><br>You also have other obstacles to avoid. This can keep people entertained trying to 100% every level for a long time. <br><br>The game has a very stand out graphics style. The cartoony look is represented throughout the game and is consistent. What I found really unique is the way in which the world reacts to how you are doing in the game. If you aren't doing very well then the levels turn very grey because you aren't colllecting many colours. As you collect more colours the world becomes more and more colourful. This is a beatiful addition that makes the gameplay more fun for people.<br><br>The game has multiple control methods. You can use classic mouse and keyboard. However I wouldn't recommend it. A controller is key to being able to play this game properly. The colours of your character is optimized to the colours of the buttons on your controller. Therefore it is very hard to remember the keys on the keyboard wheras it is very easy with a controller.<br><br>The game features 2 controller setups for this. So I had to go in the options menu each time to reset this back again. This was an uneccessary hassle and was an annoyance. <br><br>Color guardians options menu is also very lacking in the needed options. There are also seperate audio sliders for different settings. However this is all that is in the options menu.
