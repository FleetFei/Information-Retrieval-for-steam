Here, it's about painting walls and droids different colours with your paint gun. I couldn't work out if it was me or the game at fault.<br><br>But all the puzzles are contained enough to be manageable. I found the early stuff a bit gentle and I rattled through a lot of the game before hitting the real challenge, but it always changed things up before becoming boring.
