A description of each is provided below, including their respective rules.<br><br><ul class=\"bb_ul\"><li>Classic Sudoku - fill the entire grid with numbers from 1-9 so that no numbers repeat themselves in any of the 9 rows, columns, or 3x3 houses.<br><\/li><li>Overlapped Classics - portions of a classic board are overlapped with additional boards.<br><\/li><li>Nonomino Sudoku - 3x3 houses are replaced with colored irregular blocks.<\/li><\/ul><br><u><b>Gold &amp; Lives<\/b><\/u><br>In adventure mode, the player starts with 10 hearts (life). Each time a player incorrectly inputs a number in a cell (excluding pencil notes), one life will be lost. There is also no method of replenishing hearts\/life in a playthrough. Once all hearts are gone, the adventure is over.<br><br>Gold is the currency system in Quest, and it is randomly awarded from correctly inputting numbers into cells. Gold is used to purchase\/activate power-ups (described below).<br><br><u><b>Power-ups<\/b><\/u><br>The power-up system is a unique mechanic in Sudoku Quest that introduces a little bit of freshness when the gameplay gets stale. It also makes Sudoku solving very friendly to newcomers. The list of available power-ups are provided below (all are unlocked from the beginning).<br><br><ul class=\"bb_ul\"><li>Reveal Cell.<br><\/li><li>Remaining Values: how many unrevealed cells contain a specific value.<br><\/li><li>Shield.<br><\/li><li>Possible Values: choose a cell and pencil in all possible values.<br><\/li><li>Shuffle: shuffle revealed\/unrevealed cells.<br><\/li><li>Highlight: highlight whole row &amp; column of hovered cell.<\/li><\/ul><br><u><b>Adventure Mode<\/b><\/u><br>The goal of adventure mode is to reveal specifically marked cells - the player is not required to reveal an entire board to progress. The player can either continue to the next board upon completing it, or stay on the same board to finish revealing all cells (and earn gold). The board types vary each level, and the difficulty begins to increase as the starting number of givens is slowly diminished in each subsequent level.<br><br><u><b>Custom Games<\/b><\/u><br>An infinite Sudoku generator is provided so that the fun never ends! The player is able to configure the: board type, number of givens, starting life, and starting gold.  <br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Conclusion<\/div><u><b>Advised Suggestions<\/b><\/u><br>There are a few suggestions that may improve gameplay listed below.<br><br><ul class=\"bb_ul\"><li>Improve aesthetics\/add themes.<br><\/li><li>Implement personalization options.<br><\/li><li>Provide a description of a power-up's ability upon mouseover.<\/li><\/ul><br><u><b>Verdict<\/b><\/u><br>Sudoku Quest is a casual Sudoku generator with a twist.
