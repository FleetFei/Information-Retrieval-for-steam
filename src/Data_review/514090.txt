Yes please!<br><br>So, what does this \u201canimated\u201d in the title mean? Is the whole puzzle a video instead of an image? Technically yes, but the game manages to achieve the perfect balance between movement and stillness in its themes, at least for the biggest part. It feels like you are dealing with both a classical static puzzle and an animated picture simultaneously! This is happening because while in most of the themes there is a static thing e.g. a big building, at the same time this is surrounded by moving things like clouds, water with reflections or even ducks moving in its surface! On a side note, it seems that this is the biggest difference with the other game of the series, Wild Animals, because due to its theme there is a significant higher amount of movement involved.<br><br>Thankfully there is no rotation involved with the pieces. While this might be a desirable function for some people, for me it would make most pc jigsaw puzzle games unplayable, as it would take all the relaxation part of the experience away. <br><br>There is a \u2018group-all-pieces\u2019 button that is extremely useful - especially at the very beginning - as it is shorting out together all the border pieces. Also you can close the game at any time and the progress for the current puzzle is left intact, which is nice.<br><br>The only negative thing that I can say about the game is the very repetitive music. You can choose between 3 short tracks and while the tracks are not bad, they will shortly become tedious to listen for the whole puzzle, let alone for hours (I am giving some extra points to the third track). Actually these 350 pieces puzzles can take more than an hour to complete, so it would be better if you are equiped with your own background music! Also I would like to suggest to the developers to save more space on the left part of the screen by allowing more pieces to be put there and turn some of these \u2018constantly on the screen\u2019 options a pop up button maybe. <br><br>Although every theme can be played with 3 different quantities of pieces, 60, 240 and 350 (with the 60 being obviously asymmetrically easy compared to the other 2), some people can be sceptical about the price VS the limited amount of different themes in the game - only 10. But let me tell you, if you plan on finishing all the themes in all the settings the game is guaranteed to offer you many hours of puzzle solving and while on discount you cannot go wrong with it if
