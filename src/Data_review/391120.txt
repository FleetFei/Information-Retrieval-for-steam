Giving them a bear hug is fine but walking away from them hurts their feelings so much, they want to kill you. I believe this will be patched but I found this glitch hilarious. <br><br>There is a scavenger hunt in the first chapter, in a form of gathering ingredients for a ritual to destroy a clingy apparition and I needed some time to gather them all. That\u2019s just me though.<br><br>I have no problems in turns of framerate drops and crashes but I\u2019ve witness several non-gamebreaking bugs that ruins the atmosphere at times.
