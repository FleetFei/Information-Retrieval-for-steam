Sydney has a rather slow walking speed and it would be nice if you could make him run by double clicking around an area. You can also examine objects in your inventory and is a good idea to do so when you get something new. Another minor nitpick, and this is not a problem in most areas but the position of your inventory at the bottom of the screen is slightly annoying in two areas where you are moving from north to south as you'll expand the inventory by accident instead of moving Sydney.
