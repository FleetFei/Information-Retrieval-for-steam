Haven't gone in myself as I've heard it's abysmal but I can well imagine. I've had a couple of their previous titles and multiplayer reliability is not too hot. But for what amounts to PS3 gen graphics it's weak.<br><br>Controls- As I said nothing short of a full 360 degree strapped on moving platform with projection could get close so that is kind of moot.
