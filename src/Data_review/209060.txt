I can see why they were possibly changed since th spheres could possibly hide bullets that you wanted to dodge, but it wasn't so problematic that it warrented a change. Maybe a small drop in opacity and thats it.<br><br>Thirdly: The graphics and sound. OH MY GOD ITS SO SHINY! also the sounds are wonderful. Music is great too. If a soundtrack was released I'd put it on my iPod.<br><br>Again, this game is still a must buy. The addition of extra missions and the sheer insanity of this game is great.
