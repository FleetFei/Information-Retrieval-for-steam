Also while this game has a heavy focus on telling the story through text which is perfectly fine, I find myself with no option to speed up the dialogue, only the options to wait through the very slow pacing of the dialogue or to skip all of the narrative completely. <br><br>Neither of these options are desireable as I would like to know more about the story, but sometimes lack the patience to deal with the slow pacing, so the ability to control the pacing would have been welcomed.<br><br><br><b>What is your verdict?<\/b><br><br>Endica is a game that I am enjoying so far and I'm glad that I made the purchase. Endica gives off the atmosphere of a proper adventure game, the aesthetics, level design and narrative all have clear effort and thought put into them, the retro vibe is prevalent throughout the game and adds to the charm. <br><br>With that all being said, I should mention that I'm a little sad that I will most likely not be able to play the co-op which comes across as one of the games big selling points.
