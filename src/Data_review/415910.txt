These power ups give you the ability to block the pawns for a few turns, makes one of your chess pieces move in the sense that it was a queen for a turn and a mine that you get to place down and blow up a pawn. So overall there is a nice variety of powerups that you can use to help you play through endless mode.<br><br>Puzzle mode is what you would expect it to be. You are given a set of pieces and have to try to take out the pawns before they get to the other side. This time you are only given a single life, so each move is crucial in order to succeed. There are around 48 puzzles for you to complete. But they only unlock 8 at a time after you have completed the previous 8 puzzles.
