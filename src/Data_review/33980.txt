Not a bad feature, but all of them are so similar on the basic level, that it is absolutely no fun. Stubbs the Zombie was different, one-of-a-kind gem... With great fusion soundtrack, which I really enjoyed.<br><br>My mark is 8\/10. I usually play serious long-winded games, like epic RPGs and strategies...
