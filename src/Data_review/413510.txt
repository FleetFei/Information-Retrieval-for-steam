It is still hard to tell whether an object is on screen or in the background. It looks good, seems like it will have plenty of content, and the ship upgrades look promising, as long as they are balanced and implemented well.<br><br>While it is fun, I have only captured one planet due some problems I have encountered with the control scheme. This leads to problems with moving around the map. For example, attempting to move in a left-up diagonal direction while shooting right will cause the ship to remain stationary, as it is attempting to move left but also in the direction of the cursor, so no movement occurs. I thought that a controller would remedy the control problem, but it was even worse. Movement is made easier, but my cursor remained on screen and the ship would only point in the direction of the cursor, no matter where I tried to point the ship with the controller. Therefore, my killing abilities were greatly hampered. If the control schemes are fixed then the game will be vastly improved. <br>A few other issues:<br>It is hard to tell when objects are part of the background and when they are actual impediments that can cause damage, so making obstacles more obvious would be helpful. The edges of the map are also hard to see on some maps. Spawning was good for the most part but on the second planet\u2019s invasion 2 ships spawned on top of me and damaged my ship, resulting in my death and a failed planetary takeover. I stopped playing at that point. <br><br>While I have not played long, I still enjoyed taking over that first planet and hope that many more conquests will come.
