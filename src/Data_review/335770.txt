It is easily one of the most innovative systems that I have seen created on the RPGMaker engine.<br><br>Cons:<br><br>Subpar English Translation - The text and content is fairly unintelligible in a lot of dialogues. It would be nice if the description of a spell also included what reagents it required, but as for now, just buy 10 of every reagent and cast a spell; to figure out what reagent that spell requires. (Then buy more, if you plan on using that spell).<br><br>Lack of Explanation on Skills - This may just be a symptom of the poor english translation, but it would be nice if skills were explained a bit better. Most are easy enough to figure out, but I would want to know (for instance) how much raising my skills influences my strength and HP growth. That is to say, what secondary effects skills influence, other than the obvious 'You have a higher to-hit with attacks.'<br><br>Lack of Content \/ Storyline - There is already a lot to do within the game, places to explore and chests to plunder. While I havent accomplished everything, or killed every monster (Dragons, Giants, and Demons are rough) it definitely feels like it needs more content. I enjoy the open-world-free-roam aspect, it would be nice if there was more of a quest system or loose storyline to explore. <br><br><br><br>Conclusion:<br><br>For the price, I might say to wait on it a little bit longer, for some of the systems to be fleshed out and more content to be introduced, but it is definitely playable, and I have not ran into any game-breaking bugs personally.
