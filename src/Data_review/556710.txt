The game is already difficult enough without it.<br><br>I recommend this game to it's target audience: horror fans. This game is incredibly atmospheric and creepy. Danger lurks around every corner, and you can bet that it's stuff made of nightmare fuel. Playing this game does scratch that horror itch that horror fans crave.<br><br>Old school horror games were punishingly difficult (think Ghosts and Goblins). I do have some gripes as I have listed above.
