Although there are some small flaws in the game, it doesn't stop it from being as great as it is.<br><br>Jumping right into the game, you are welcomed by a simplistic, yet perfect menu. It shows the title of the game and there are a very small amount of sub-menus. The rest of the screen is covered by a neat animation of one of the rotary blocks. The only problem I encountered with this ability were some of the larger rooms, due to the number of objects and shape of the room, as it rotates you could accidentally find yourself on the ledge of a platform and able to access the exit without really solving it correctly, if that make sense? One last thing I want to mention is the absolute fantastic controls.
