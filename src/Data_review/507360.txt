Turn-Based (Nothing happens until you take a step, or you can skip a step if you need a monster to approach so you get first hit instead of it.  Monsters won't move until you move. Sounds effects, but no background music (so far)<br>9.  Permadeath?  I'm assuming, but have not died yet.<br>10.  Keyboard or Gamepad (I used keyboard, so don't know how good the gamepad controls are)<br>11.  ???  Something I missed or can't remember?
