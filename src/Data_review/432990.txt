Or to play a series of twisted experiments with a cute critter that can fly. Green Game: Time Swapper takes players into a world where they can indirectly control the environment through the fragment of time that allows certain elements to change. This, in turn, allows a flying bird to escape a number of traps and it\u2019s up to you to make sure it flies safely to the exit. <br><br>The flying bird cannot be controlled and moves in a single direction, unless made to do otherwise.
