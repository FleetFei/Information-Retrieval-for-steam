Thank you, it is very much appreciated.<br><br>I wanted to post a brief FAQ, based on questions posed which I really hope will help enhance your enjoyment of this pack. I really want you all to enjoy it. Aviation is a fantastic hobby!<br><br>Missions:- Yes, these are &quot;true&quot; missions, complete with full mission rewards and medals that you can earn, generated with the mission SDK. Please see below for how to earn your mission reward medals.<br><br>ATC:- Unfortunately it is not possible to use ATC in these flights due to a limitation of the core simulation. The ATC, unfortunately, pulls you off your flight plan some 40 or 50 miles from the airport and directs you to a runway. WIth most airports this is fine. However, with these specialist approaches the in-built ATC has a habit of directing you in to the side of a mountain or getting horribly confused. The default ATC is very simplistic and crude and simply does not understand how these approaches have to be flown to be safe. It will happily fly you in to the side of mountains at these airports. An exercise in frustration I didnt want you guys to endure. To get around this, your GPS is loaded with the STAR (the standard terminal arrival route) used by the real airlines. Following the GPS will on nearly all of the approaches line you up with the centreline of the arrival runway, with the exact procedures used by the real airlines. At the very small airports (which have no real life control tower ot ATC too), you will be guided to the airport. The choice of runway is then yours.<br><br>Mission Success Rewards:- ALL the missions will generate an award  - be it a gold, silver or bronze medal which persists in your pilot logbook, based on the complexity of the approach. However, to win that award you must perform a textbook landing on the runway, landing in the first third of the runway, aligned with the centreline, a safe touchdown rate etc. In short, you need to make a perfect landing! You should also slow to below 10kts or a full stop on the runway. The mission will then signal success to you and you will be given your award. Landing long on the runway, landing fast, at a bad angle etc will not give you these awards! It took me a few attempts at many of these airports, but that is half the fun. You have to earn those achievements! If you don't get a success message and an award it is a signal that your landing was less than perfect! That's ok, it happens to us all, Have another go and keep practicing!<br><br>Flight Plans:- Flight plans have been included in the GPS for your convenience. However, if you wish to fly your own route that is fine. Mission rewards will still be triggered and give to you for a great landing. Please bear in mind the ATC limitations above. These are true missions, not a set of flight plans. They are there for your convenience to complete these missions.<br><br>Manual: The manual link can be found on the right side of this page or in the DLC folder of your root FSX folder. Please take the time to read it, it has a lot in there, including descent calculations etc.<br><br>I hope that this helps with enjoyment of Dangerous Approaches. Again, my sincerest thanks to all of you.
