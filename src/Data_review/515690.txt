However, they don't feel weird when they are played next to each other. All in all, the graphic is decent for such a cheap price and it looks great to see some different custom asset once in a while.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Story<\/div>Because this game is based from Indonesian history, i take it that the developers are from Indonesia. However, it can't be used as an excuse for the poor dialogue. The most common mistake that i find is the &quot;better i&quot; word which is better to be swapped. I think that the devs need to hire a translator or proofreader for their work.<br><br>Speaking of translation, some of the dialogues itself are still not translated to English. Although each party member learns similar skills, they also can learn different (although not unique) skills from the rest of the party.<br><br>The game treats each objective as a mission. However, the game doesn't keep track on them. There are also some boss battles in each dungeon and some areas (some of these areas will be visited more than once with different boss battle).
