I don\u2019t think that this is bad in general. Even if you can beat some of them more easy you will still get how cool some of the mechanics were you had to use. Instead I\u2019d like to mention the great dialogues and sidekicks you\u2019ll find along the road of your tragical seeking for revenge. The artstyle is simply amazing, not to mention the amazing soundtrack. Lastly, the developer is actively taking part in steam forums and constantly updates the game to squash remaining bugs I personally didn't get confronted with.<br><br>If you are into platformers,
