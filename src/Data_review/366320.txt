The games main mechanic, changing seasons, makes this all the more impressive as each season is also visually distinct.<br>  While not as technically advanced as some other games, like Dust: An Elysian Tail, the animations look really good in Seasons after Fall, and a lot of care seems to have been put into making the main character, a young fox, feel like a young playful fox. In Seasons after Fall these powers are the abilities to change seasons. In summer certain plants will grow and water levels will be lower, and in autumn mushrooms grow and winds will be stronger. It's a simple concept, but it works well here.
