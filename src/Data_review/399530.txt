Ported from a mobile game, you get three stars (&quot;a perfect level&quot;) if no spheres are destroyed during a level. To me in these cases it usually does not look plausible that they could have hit each other. But in <i>Hyspherical 2<\/i> it does happen, probably because of numerical imprecision in the collision detection. It's frustrating when you are just about to complete a level and then this ruins it.<br><br>Then again the levels are usually rather short.
