As mentioned before, you also have Ariadne\u2019s Thread and its magic is useful to progress in the story, it lets you melt things and also revive people who have been turned to stone and its final power up breaks through illusions. The game offers three difficulty levels as well as a custom option to tweak the game experience to what you were prefer. You have an in game map that allows you to teleport and a useful inventory system with interactive objects that sometimes require extra steps to be taken before you can use them, like a box that must be opened or a corkscrew you need to put together.<br><br>The game has graphics on par with most other titles in this genre. The scenes are well done, colorful backgrounds and well-drawn characters. The animation is smooth, characters walk and talk well in engaging cutscenes. You won\u2019t have to worry about squinting, the items in the hidden object scenes are easy on the eyes and aren\u2019t a chore to search for either. The audio is good, the music fits the story well and the voice acting for the most part is well done. My only issue was with Hephaestus, his audio level was lower than the other characters when he spoke. There are a lot of Steam achievements to hunt after as well as trading cards to collect adding more value to the game. If you\u2019re a fan of hidden object games then you need this in your collection, if you\u2019re not then this might very get you hooked on this genre.
