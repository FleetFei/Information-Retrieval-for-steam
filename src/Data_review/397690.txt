But with this race to increase the number of bullets on screen, comes a problem. HIIIPE severely needs an epilepsy warning, but some gamers will be able to adjust to it's apperance with time. Your gun shoots a straight bullet shot, which can be upgraded by collecting blue drops from every enemy. Killing enemies also increases your power meter and a multiplier. Some enemies drop gold squares that give you a temporary additional wide shot. They drop frequently, so the majority of the game is trying to dodge bullets to survive and and grab these squares to kill a screen filled with dozens of enemies. Some enemies like bigger ships, minibosses, and snake like enemies require alot more hits, which can more easily be dealt with your beam by pressing the fire button, which also slows your movement speed. This beam also seems to provide a shield and can destroy enemy bullets.
