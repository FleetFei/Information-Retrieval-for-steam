It\u2019s highly recommended you play those two games before touching this one.<br><br>FINAL DOOM adds literally no new mechanics or anything to the table. If you\u2019re a stubborn kind of gamer you can still enjoy it, but it\u2019s noticeably more frustrating than TNT.<br><br>FINAL DOOM is sort of like an ultimate mastery of old school DOOM, its levels making the most of DOOM\u2019s mechanics and requiring the most of the skills you developed in the original two games.
