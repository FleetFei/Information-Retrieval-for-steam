Without the time constrain of the TV series, more emphesis is allowed to be placed on certain scenes which may seem like minute passing details in the TV series, but in fact had a lasting impact in the story point, such as spoiler<span class=\"bb_spoiler\"><span>Okabe stopping Daru from going to the offline meet, which causes Suzuha to not meet her father there<\/span><\/span>. There are no H-scenes in this VN if you are uncomfortable with that stuff either.<br><br>All in all, this is one of my favourite VNs to date, and i've really enjoyed it, and needless to say, highly recommend it as well.
