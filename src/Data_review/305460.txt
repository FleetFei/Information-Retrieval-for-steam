and fight off his minons using nothing but your skull. While FrankNJohn may appear to be just another roguelike, it's also very much a twin stick shooter in disguise. <br><a class=\"bb_link\" href=\"https:\/\/www.youtube.com\/watch?v=IReS8H_ovbo\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noreferrer\"  id=\"dynamiclink_0\">https:\/\/www.youtube.com\/watch?v=IReS8H_ovbo<\/a><br>Starting with basic head attacks you progress through the world killing creatures, each level is multilayered and set around a 'tree of life' set in the center. You are able to clear them completely or just make a beeline for the next portal. Clearing levels is far more rewarding as you have the chance for more marbles (currency) and skull caps. Marbles gives you the ability to upgrade skill trees after your characters death which results in future playthroughs becoming much longer as you become more powerful. Skill trees included Damage, Defense and Utility and the  games 'hub world' seemed to contain secret areas to break open once becoming strong enough.<br><br>Skull caps are really the essence of the game, each having pros and cons while some had entirely unique powerups set on timers. During play I discovered all manner of caps, most being reference to great games from the past. Examples include Turtle Cap (TMNT) which provides a full body shield, Bomberman Cap which gave the ability to drop bombs as you moved and the Sonic Cap, which provided a great speed boost of course. <br><br>When first playing you will notice the art direction and level design, it's very distinct and beautiful, quite similar to the Trine series. Couple that with the music which was put together by Ben Prunty (FTL\/Gravity Ghost\/StarCrawlers) and you really do get drawn into a Tim Burton-esque world.<br><br>For Early Access this is a solid game that's addictive already (just one more turn!). At this stage it only includes the one area 'The Garden of Denial' and a boss fight with spider Queen Sheela but in future it will add many more zones, bosses, skullcaps and local co-op support. Would love to see online co-op added but I'm not sure if that's possible. Also I'm sure they are planned but achievements would be great. :)<br><br>If you enjoyed games like A Darker Purpose or The Binding of Isaac, FrankNJohn is basically a 3d third person version with similar dark twisted humour. Worth shortlisting and recommended.
