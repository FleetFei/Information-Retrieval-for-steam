Lastly, at times they tended to get bunched up together and stuck to one other during the race...which greatly slowed down the momentum of gameplay. A game like Space Ribbon is crying out to be played frantically and ultra fast-paced, with lighning quick precision moves and that white-knuckled edge-of-your-seat sense of speed and acceleration.
