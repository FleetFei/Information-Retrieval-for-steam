bought this pack alongside the rest of the NJCL collection last year, and now after playing all 10 scenarios, I have to say: It's about as above average as you can get. Just like the New York-New Haven Scenario Pack, some of the scenarios in this pack are good (my favorites are NJ Transit Train 7241, Local Freight SA-31, and Hoboken Deadhead), while some of them are really bad (the worst in my opinion being NJ Transit Train 3373), however I'm still recommending this pack for those who already own the content. I would NOT recommend this pack if you are new to Train Simulator however. All scenarios require NJCL, however most of them (7 to be exact) require the New York-New Haven add-on.<br><br>I'll try to summarize my experience with these scenarios (all scenarios except one require you to turn on the in-cab signal and safety systems):<br><br><b>NJ Transit Train 2607 (Part 1 and Part 2):<\/b>  Relatively okay. You're basically running NJT Train 2607 from Hoboken to Long Branch, before yarding your train at Long Branch. There is a bunch of AI throughout Part 1, but significantly less in Part 2. Ending screens are just the generic &quot;Take a quick break&quot; messages.<br><br><b>NJ Transit Train 7241 (Part 1 and Part 2):<\/b> Thought I enjoyed it. You're basically running NJT Train 7241 (there are some typos in the scenario description, briefing, and manual for both parts as I pointed out in the review video) from New York Penn to Long Branch, making quick station stops along the way. There is a bunch and a variety of AI throughout both parts, such as a sweet chase of an Amtrak AI in Part 1, and a CSAO freight train at Monmouth Park in Part 2. Ending screens are also the generic &quot;Take a break&quot; messages.<br><br><b>NJ Transit Train 3508:<\/b> It was okay. A pretty hour-long scenario, but at least you do get to drive the MultiLevel Cab Car in this pack. You drive Train 3508 from South Amboy to New York Penn while making quick station stops along the way. The amount of AI on the NJCL portion was about a couple, while on the NEC portion there's about 7 (could've been opportunities for more interesting AI, but oh well). It's basically a generic &quot;Stop at station, continue, stop at station, continue&quot; type scenario, and it gets boring after a while.<br><br><b>NJ Transit Train 3373:<\/b> Didn't like it at all. During the first quarter of the scenario, you drive from Red Bank to Long Branch, switch your locomotive over to diesel mode, then make quick stops in-between Long Branch and Bay Head. The amount of AI is lacking with little to no variety. Could've been an opportunity to do the full trip (the real train goes from New York Penn to Bay Head), but unfortunately we don't get it sadly. And to think it doesn't get any worse than this, it ends with another &quot;Take a deserved break&quot; message.<br><br><b>Local Freight SA-31:<\/b> Decent freight scenario, yet sadly the only one in this pack. You start at South Amboy where you'll be held due to another NJT train on the same track. Once you get the clearance, you'll be picking up a gondola consist there and then heading to Red Bank where you'll be held due to track work. Ending message is actually unique (Ends with a reminder to go grab some lunch), however there's not a lot of AI in this scenario. Still decent nonetheless.<br><br><b>Hoboken Deadhead:<\/b> Shortest scenario in this pack, but yet is pretty good nonetheless. You're taking a Comet consist from MMC to Hoboken Terminal where passengers will later embark on their morning journey. Only a couple AI with only one variety: MultiLevels. But the yard exploring at MMC and making best use of the freight cars that comes with the NJCL add-on is what makes it good.<br><br><b>NJ Transit Train 4366:<\/b> Average scenario at best. You're basically taking an NJT shuttle train from Bay Head to Long Branch, stopping at all stations in-between the two. There is a decent amount of AI in this 45 minute scenario, and the ending message is also unique (ends with &quot;Let's find some hot coffee on a cold day&quot;), but it's mostly &quot;Stop at station, continue, stop at station, continue&quot; type scenario, which it gets boring after a while. Still better than the Train 3373 scenario.<br><br><b>NJ Transit &quot;MMC&quot; Extra:<\/b> An alright scenario; idea of doing one like this is cool, but the execution is above average at best. You're finishing up the MMC Extra trip from Long Branch to MMC by starting at Rahway, stopping at Newark Penn to get the MMC crew on board, and then parking your train at the MMC yard. This is the only scenario that requires the most DLC (NJCL, NYNH, ALP-46, F40PH-2CAT). Spotted about 8 AI trains in this 30-minute long scenario. Also, despite it not being a passenger scenario, you still have to make it to checkpoints in time without getting penalized. Also ends with &quot;Take a break&quot; message.<br><br>So that's my summarized review. The full review is on Youtube if you want to check it out. Like I said, I would recommend it for those who already own the content while I don't recommend it if you are new to Train Simulator or if you don't own the required content yet.<br><br>Final Score:
