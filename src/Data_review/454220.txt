Hunting<\/div>The Temporal Invasion is a story-driven, riddle-based game that encourages the player to utilize any available resource needed to solve the provided puzzles. This may involve fine-tuning your Google searching skills, gathering friends, or even renting books at the library if you\u2019re that desperate. Be prepared to ALT-Tab a lot in this immersive puzzle hunting game that does an eerily great job at making you feel like a conspiracy theorist.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Story<\/div><u><b>The Temporal Invasion\u2019s Narrative<\/b><\/u><br>The story involves you, the puzzle hunter, living a life where your memories differ from that of reality. For example, the POV protagonist truly believes that \u201cThe Chinese\u201d started WWII. A letter from Dr. Quantum informs you that you are not insane. In fact, you are immune. Seeking the truth will reveal everything.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Gameplay<\/div><u><b>Puzzle Hunting<\/b><\/u><br>There are a total of 10 Puzzle Hunt challenges (Cases) in the game. Each puzzle contains 10 \u201cCheck Points\u201d where the solution of each will be a clue for the next. Everything must be solved in order; no skipping is allowed, nor will &quot;Hints&quot; be provided. A prologue for each Case is provided, detailing the motivation for the upcoming Puzzle Hunt and summarizing the important objectives that need to be achieved. The clues needed to progress may be hidden in the form of: <br><br><ul class=\"bb_ul\"><li>Historical knowledge.<br><\/li><li>Cryptograms.<br><\/li><li>Steganographic images: concealing a file, message, image, or video within another image.<br><\/li><li>Riddles, Metaphors, and Choice Based Decisions.<\/li><\/ul><br><u><b>Tools<\/b><\/u><br>Tools are provided to aid you in your quest to seek the truth, such as:<br><br><ul class=\"bb_ul\"><li>Computer: Software, Limited Browser.<br><\/li><li>Software: Photo Editor, Chat App, Notes App, Sandbox E-mail App.<br><\/li><li>Magnifying Glass.<br><\/li><li>Record Player.<br><\/li><li>UV Light.<\/li><\/ul><br>It may be important to note that the Computer and Sandbox E-mail App do not interact with anything outside of the game. They are tools provided to help solve Cases and increase immersion. They will not require an internet connection, and no E-mails will actually be sent, nor will personal information be stored.<br><br>Many of the clues can be solved using one or two tools, with most of the answers being relatively straightforward and simple. Some will require two or more steps to solve, but with a fine use of out-of-the-box thinking coupled with strong Google searching skills, nothing is ever <i>too<\/i> difficult. If you ever find yourself spiraling into a frenzy of trial and error submissions thinking that maybe the solution is, for example, not precise enough\/in the wrong language\/missing accent marks, etc., you are thinking too deep. The submissions are generally not case-sensitive, unless obviously presented as such.<br><br><u><b>Sample Puzzle &amp; Walkthrough<\/b><\/u><br><a class=\"bb_link\" href=\"https:\/\/youtu.be\/OZNcgkK8BIE\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noreferrer\"  id=\"dynamiclink_0\">https:\/\/youtu.be\/OZNcgkK8BIE<\/a><br>In the above video, an image is provided as a clue that needs solving. Upon closer observation in the Photo Editor, the quote <i>\u201dThe Truth got shifted by Two Pushes, Do you want to shift your body from this Earth??\u201d<\/i> is shown across the center, and a seemingly indecipherable message is written below it: <i>\u201dYJGGNGT KUNCPF.\u201d<\/i> <br><br>It is already somewhat apparent that the jumble of letters is an encoded message. The clue to solving this message lies in the quote, specifically the portion \u201cThe Truth got shifted by Two Pushes\u2026\u201d This provides the hint that the message is encoded with a Caesar Cipher (also known as a Shift Cipher). This means that if letters in the phonetic alphabet are assigned sequential numbers, the encryption process involves replacing the original letter of the message with a new, encoded letter shifted by two numbers. For example, A &gt; C, X &gt; Z. Decoding it will requiring moving it backwards or forwards (shifting) by two letters. A simple Caesar Cipher Decryption Tool on the internet will easily break this for you. Although this may have seemed easy, especially with the solution presented immediately below the image, there are many much more difficult puzzles left to solve.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Conclusion<\/div><u><b>Advised Suggestions<\/b><\/u><br>There are a few suggestions that may improve gameplay; these are listed below.<br><br><ul class=\"bb_ul\"><li>Fix minor grammar mistakes.<br><\/li><li>Fix minor clipping issues - occurs on every puzzle document.<br><\/li><li>Provide a borderless full-screen option to ease window-switching.<br><\/li><li>Allow music switching. <\/li><\/ul><br><u><b>Verdict<\/b><\/u><br>The Temporal Invasion definitely feels cheesy at times, especially when it tries to connect so many different ideas and conspiracy theories; however, after puzzle hunting for a while, it does a great job making you feel like a detective. Even though this game relies on storytelling through an alternate history, it only does so in between nodes of historically accurate content. With a unique approach on player interaction, along with fun little puzzles to solve, The Temporal Invasion is recommended to any fans of puzzle games or conspiracy theories.
