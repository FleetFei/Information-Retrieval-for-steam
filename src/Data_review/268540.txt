I have no problems with using the German VA.<br><br>Broken audio : Some users comment that audio seems to cut off abruptly at the end of some spoken lines. I never had this happen, but you do have to hold down the mouse button to open the small interaction menu.<br><br>Story : Some people just don't like the premise or character personality, or how the game ends.
