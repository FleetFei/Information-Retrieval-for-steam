As our flashy opening cinematic progresses, the train starts to speed and wagons start to teeter dangerously. We, as the most responsible mother, turn to check our nameless little girl, and guess what? She is not there! We are in an alien dimension, there is no clue about the whereabouts of any other passenger, the train keeps moving at an alarming rate... and we have a daughter to look for.<br><br>Welcome to the immersive chaos that hails from any other HOG. Good setting, interesting story, horrible character design, mediocre HOGs and neat puzzles added together gives you <i>Mystery Masters: Psycho Train<\/i>. I'll vote this one up for the lack of a &quot;Meh&quot; button, so take it or leave it on your own choice.
