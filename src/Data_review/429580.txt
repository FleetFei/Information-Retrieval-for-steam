There are some video game references that a reasonably acquainted person might appreciate.<br><br>There are four other main characters beside Ami the protagonist. Many of the typical character stereotypes are there. There's the crazy and adventurous simpleton. There's the stubborn tomboy. There's the silent nerd and the responsible and outgoing one. In this case however it only affects the dialogue immediately after the choice. Unfortunately it doesn't seem to have any other effect afterwards in any other part of the story.<br><br>Except for the end, of course. Since it's a romance novel, depending on your choices throughout the novel Ami will inevitably wind up dating one of the girls. All four endings are fairly different and I recommend to see them all, as they reveal interesting aspects of the characters.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Overall quality and conclusions<\/div><br>Visually <i>AWCA!<\/i> looks good. There was only one graphical glitch during my playthroughs (i.e. once candles on a table were getting overdrawn by the woman in the back) and the transitions work well. It's sometimes off-putting.<br><br>Also, sometimes the speaker gets mixed up with another. There are also some typos present though not too many.<br><br>Soundtrack selection is nice and it works but it's also bland and forgettable to my liking.
