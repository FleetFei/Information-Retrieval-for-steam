This documentary sums up so many of the questions I had about these early pioneers who helped create the very landscape of what has become video game entertainment.  If anything, this documentary helps showcase the pioneering technical and creative spirit that helped make video games what they are today.<br><br>What's wonderful about video games is that they seem so simple and yet are anything but.  Is it entertainment?  Is it art?  Is it just a business?  Is it fun?  Is it dangerous?
