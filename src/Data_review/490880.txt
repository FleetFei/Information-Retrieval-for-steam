The game is set within an arena playing field. There are two primary game modes - campaign and arcade.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Campaign Mode<\/div><br>I spent most of my time with the game playing campaign mode. This is where you have to complete missions and where you have the ability to upgrade and improve your robot. The levels become increasingly difficult. As a result of this increasing difficulty I recommend that you collect as much gold on each level in order to purchase upgrades as they offer a significant advantage. It seems like every time I would die and upgrade I would be unbeatable for the next few levels.<br><br>Upgrades include: <br><br><ul class=\"bb_ul\"><li>Improved armor (essentially hit points)<br><\/li><li>Regeneration (slowly regenerate your armor when you're not taking damage)<br><\/li><li>Improved speed<br><\/li><li>Increased damage<br><\/li><li>Rate of Fire<br><\/li><li>Multishot<br><\/li><li>A Cash Magnet (makes getting the currency that drops off of mobs easier)<br><\/li><li>Cash bonus (increases value of currency dropped)<\/li><\/ul><br>Personally, I found multishot, damage, rate of fire and armor, in this order, to be the most important. However, I played enough to know that it is essentially a score attack mode. However, it is intended to be more difficult than the campaign modes as you can only be hit a few times before it's game over. As a result I would recommend that if you have any interest in the campaign mode that you use that to familiarize yourself with the game. This should net you better scores in the long run.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Presentation<\/div><br>The graphics are more than suitable for a game of this type. I like the thumping soundtrack.
