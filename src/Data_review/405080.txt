A few months later, Evie returns from India, and with the help of Inspector Aberline, begins investigating the murders.<br><br>Much of the investigations involve piecing together the Ripper murders, locating evidence with your eagle vision, and slowly assembling a timeline of the victims' final moments. Naturally, the devs set the final battle in a cramped basement. Even if the camera itself were not an issue, you'll spend several minutes running round and round in circles, trying to get the jump on someone who can see you... because it's a small basement! It's an exercise in frustration that I eventually walked away from.<br><br>Without spoiling the encounter, I'll say that as a story piece it made little sense beyond its location. If it weren't for the awful final encounter, I doubt I'd have anything bad to say at all, other than some quibbles about the combat.
