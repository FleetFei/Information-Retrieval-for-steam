As you progress through the stages, you will encounter objects which are not always so kind to Nyan Cat, such as bombs, dogs and UFOs which attack you! Whilst in total I have only played Nyan Cat for just under an hour, it is certainly a game I will keep installed as it always brings a smile to my face when I load it up. If you are really into your endless runner type of games, this is obviously not going to be better than the likes of BitTrip, but it is a really nice little competition in that genre. With the addition of leader boards for friends and globally, it adds a little edge to the game which may be overlooked in others of this type. <br><br>I didn\u2019t have any technical problems whilst playing.
