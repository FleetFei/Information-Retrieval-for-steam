Free them from cells, point them at armouries, and watch your mini-rebellion take hold of the base. I mean for christ sake, you can't even fire the gun until you've obeyed your mech's AI's very specific demands on how to deal with what are already very restricted situations - in what is supposed to be a game about freeform chaos. <br><br>To anyone who does not already know this: you do NOT put tutorials in a Just Cause game. Come on, Avalanche. This is DRM levels of bad business. <br><br><br>Apart from that atrocity, the DLC is pretty solid fun.
