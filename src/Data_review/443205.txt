I very much prefer Steams &quot;per-user + no \u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665\u2665t&quot; solution to licensing - which by the way, for the first time allows hassle free installation on multiple devices.<br><br>Cakewalk Support is the weak link.
