engulf you in this fast paced music and rhythm game. Pulsen\u2019s high BPM tracks will have you moving along to the beat as you scurry to match the corresponding controller prompts. With full support for keyboards, gamepads, dance pads, and Rock Band drums, be prepared to let the music flow and put your reflexes to the test. Local coop is supported.<br><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Gameplay<\/div><u><b>Song Selection<\/b><\/u><br>There are approximately 80 songs that are fully playable to choose from at the moment, with more being added throughout the duration of Early Access. Upon selecting a song, modifications can be activated and manually changed for personalization. Continually missing a note will cause a meter to degrade, and by missing enough notes, eventually leading to a fail. This meter can be replenished by consistently hitting more notes.<br><br><u><b>Difficulty Levels<\/b><\/u><br>There are 5 difficulty levels you can choose to play for each song: Beginner, Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert. These effects manifest themselves to the beat by: scrunching and releasing multiple notes, swerving the note board, vibrating the receptors, drastically increasing or decreasing the note space\/layout, etc. This feature makes it insanely fun to explore each song and find a favorite one to master.<br><br><u><b>Video Gameplay<\/b><\/u><br><a class=\"bb_link\" href=\"https:\/\/youtu.be\/NkM_emS_W14\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noreferrer\"  id=\"dynamiclink_1\">https:\/\/youtu.be\/NkM_emS_W14<\/a><br><div class=\"bb_h1\">Game Options<\/div><u><b>Audio-visual Calibration<\/b><\/u><br>There are visual and audio sync calibrations to help if anything seems to be a little off on your setup.
