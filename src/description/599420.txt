
							About This Game
							Ride the portal stone and jump over the dimension and space to attack the enemy.It consists of the sky and the desert, the fantasy forest and the stage of the universe planet.You can get new weapons from each planet.And some upgrades are available.You must collect all the weapons to defeat the very strong boss.If you are not ready, you will have a very tough fight.Are you ready?This game currently only supports HTC Vive.
