
							About This Game
							Hack_me is a hacker simulator game. The main objective in game – is to hack big companies's servers to show people that there is nothing in this world that protected on 100%. You’ll need to use your cmd, bruteforce, SQL-injectors and other programs to hack servers. 

FEATURES 
• References to Mr. Robot and other films and games 
• Dozens of hacker’s stuffs to achieve your goals 
• Dynamic gameplay 
• Real hacking - you'll use real ways and programms to achieve your goals (all programms were renaimed for for general security ;D )
• Pleasant atmosphere – music, graphics, and your partner – Elena, prepare you to have a good time
