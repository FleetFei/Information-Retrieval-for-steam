
							About This Game
							A fast paced multiplayer FPS game that focuses on creative weapons and game mechanics that reward skillful killing!Polywar is a quick and skillful online multiplayer game. Tons of weapons that enable crazy weapon combos giving the player varied matches. Polywar provides interesting and creative ways for the player to destroy their enemies. Instead of just damage, weapons have many different attributes they can afflict on their targets. Its simple controls yet hard to master skill ceiling makes it perfect for both casual and hardcore gamers.Features Online multiplayer matches Unlock new ability Unlock new weapons Unlock new decorations, different viewfinders and character A total of 62 unlockable things 12 unique weapons
