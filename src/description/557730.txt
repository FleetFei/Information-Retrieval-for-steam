
							About This Game
							Christmas is coming, Santa is on his way. However, not everyone likes his visiting,  play this game to find out why.

It's snowball fighting in VR! 

Move your head to dodge incoming attacks, and counter their snowballs with yours to show them your excellent snowball fighting skills. 
Compete your high score with other players and rank yourself as high as possible on the leader board!

This is a game designed for VR from the ground up, No keyboard or controllers are required, very easy to pick up.
A fun and entertaining game for you and your family!
