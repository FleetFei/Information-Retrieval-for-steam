
							About This Game
							Travel the globe in search of flavorful and exotic ingredients to customize your own chocolate creations. You’ll use post-World War II economic and technological advances to customize your very own chocolate creations, assist the Baumiesters, and strive to become CEO of your very own chocolate empire!					For the first time ever, you have complete control over the ingredients, look, name and description of your customized recipes					How you customize your chocolates has an economic impact on your success					Travel to ports around the globe to discover ingredients, build your empire, and follow the continuing saga of the Baumeister family					Economic sim focused on mouth-watering chocolate					Latest installment of the award-winning Chocolatier® franchise
