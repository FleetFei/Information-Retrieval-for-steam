
							About This Game
							Puppy Dog: Jigsaw Puzzles is the perfect game to dog lovers and jigsaw puzzle game enthusiasts. The game includes 84 challenging jigsaw puzzles with beautiful pictures of irresistible cute puppies. There are 4 levels of difficulty adding to the fun. Puzzles will increase in size with additional pieces to place, and the quicker you solve each puzzle, the better star rating you will get. Are you up for the challenge?
