
							About This Content
							Space Harrier is an all-time SEGA classic and pioneer in pseudo-3D graphics. With Harrier, you can set up fixed bursts and shoot all around.

This DLC will add 1 ship, playable in DLC Mode.
You will receive access to 3 special stages designed specially for this ship. (Only scores using this ship will be registered in the special stages.)
You will also get access to the SEGA pack: 10 newly arranged stages including 3 boss fights, as well as CS PACK 2: 10 stages from CS mode.
Buying any DLC also lets you use all the DARIUSBURST ships from the base game in the DLC Mode stages.
