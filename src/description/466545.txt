
							About This Content
							The Clone Wars Character Pack brings exciting characters from the acclaimed animated series to LEGO Star Wars: The Force Awakens. This character pack includes the Jedi Barriss Offee, dark side warriors Asajj Ventress and Savage Opress, Clone Army officers Captain Rex and Commander Cody, Weequay pirate Hondo Ohnaka, and the bounty hunters Aurra Sing and Cad Bane.
