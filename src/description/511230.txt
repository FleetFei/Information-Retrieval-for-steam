
							About This Game
							The Last is a survival game with a twist, Your main objective is to stay alive and making your camp bigger. The game focuses on the player camp and player made buildings, as a player you will be able to build plenty of different structures to suit your needs on this lonely island. There are plenty more to the game than just this though, You need to worry about Hunger, Thirst and fatigue to be able to stay alive.
