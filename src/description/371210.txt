
							About This Game
							Each house holds many terrible secrets. They are not easy to solve, and the answers sometimes only lead to more questions. But what can you do when your life depends on these answers?The game GRIDBERD is an action horror game in the first person, in which you have to solve the terrible mystery of the ancestral Johnson Mansion. Who are you? How did you get here? What really happened? And who is this mysterious ghost that longs to kill you, and is already hard at work sharpening his axе?  Take a walk through the old ancestral mansion, take a look at the most secluded and most mysterious corners of it, and discover the secrets that it holds. Uncover crumbs of information from the past, and the history of this place. Open secret passages, solve puzzles, and skillfully bypass the traps that have been prepared for you in the old castle. Run away and hide from its ruthless creatures, which cannot wait to get at you. And remember – behind every corner horror and terror await you, and that which you will find, could change your life forever. So just running away will not work! The story is just beginning, so see it through to the end!Features: - Stunning graphics, created on one of the most advanced graphics engines – the CryEngine 3- Riddles and puzzles, as well as hidden paths that can reward you in the most diverse ways- A fascinating story with unexpected plot twists- The ability to achieve the goal in several ways- High-quality and well-thought-out sound accompaniment- Detailed and atmospheric environmentsConctact Us: wareberd@gmail.com
