
							About This Game
							Dodge Master is a high speed test of reflexes that will challenge your dodging ability throughout the experience. You play as a white block that you must guide through a series of levels that will really test your hand-eye coordination skills. Dodge Master features a lot of variety, you could be going through a maze one level while ducking into alcoves to avoid being crushed in the next.Key Features50+ Handcrafted LevelsAlternate GravityUnique BossesBouncy BlocksLocks and KeysRandomly Generated Levels
