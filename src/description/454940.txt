
							About This Game
							Slogan: "Amnesia is no excuse"The main character Olga has lost her memory but 17 years later someone decided to make her remember who she really is. Olga woke up in an abandoned place, which turned out to have many mysteries and secrets which must be uncovered. A mysterious stranger will be accompanying us on our way and giving us advice and riddles to help us reach the exit. But is it really the exit?
