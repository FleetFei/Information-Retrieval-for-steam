
							About This Content
							The official Art Book for Demetrios - The BIG Cynical Adventure!In this 88 page PDF, you will find lineart and artwork from the game, and also exclusive pictures from the original version of Demetrios created in 1999, but never released! Also included are comments from the game creator comparing the two versions.IMPORTANT : The PDF will be downloaded to your Demetrios Steam folder. You will need to open the game directory and find the "artbook" folder there.
