
							About This Game
							Epic Battle Fantasy 4 is a light-hearted turn-based RPG, heavily inspired by old classics like Final Fantasy and Golden Sun. You will battle through waves of adorable enemies, grow your characters, explore a diverse world, solve puzzles, and of course, save the world.Key features Over 140 different enemies to slaughter, from fluffy animals to gods. Over 170 different items of equipment, and 150 different usable skills, allowing for lots of character customization. Inspired by 16-bit era RPGs, minus the annoying features like random battles or save points.  Contains lots of video game references, immature humor, and anime boobs. A mix of orchestral and electronic background music by HalcyonicFalconX. Full Soundtrack included! 25 hours of gameplay, plus Newgame+, survival and boss rush modes. Suitable for both casual and hardcore RPG players.
