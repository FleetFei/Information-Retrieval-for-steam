
							About This Content
							The fully unlocked “Sepulchral Strength” black-green deck. The city-world of Ravnica relies on the black- and green-aligned Golgari Swarm to oversee the messier aspects of life and death. The “Sepulchral Strength” deck harnesses the power of death for its own purposes. Sap vitality from enemy creatures and add it to your own en route to crushing all opposition! Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013. Requires Magic 2013 Expansion.
