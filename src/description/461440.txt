
							About This Content
							VHS Glitch teams up with MindShaft Games to compose an exclusive original soundtrack for the brand new local multiplayer game "Demons with Shotguns" 

A 6 songs album composed by the official "Demons with Shotguns" theme, 4 songs for each individual arena in the game and the "End Match" interlude.

1. Demons With Shotguns Theme 04:15
2. Cemetery Arena 05:26
3. End Match 01:28
4. Hell Arena 04:31
5. Metro Arena 04:38
6. Church Arena 04:00

Also included as a bonus is the OST release trailer video.
