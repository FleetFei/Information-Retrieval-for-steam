
							About This Video
							Directed by: Nicole ElmerWritten by: Nicole Elmer, Jorge Sermini, Jason TermblayStarrring: Jorge Sermini, Michelle Keffer, Danny Trejo Exhausted from the demands of his gift as a healer, Diego lives in isolation in Puerto Rico. However, his life is disrupted when Hilary, a filmmaker, arrives. Intrigued by Diego's mystery, she tries to get closer. As the two become attracted to each other, Hilary discovers Diego’s “gift” comes with a violent curse that changes their lives.
