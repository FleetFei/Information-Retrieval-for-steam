
							About This Content
							Crusader Kings II: Songs of IndiaCrusader Kings II: Songs of India contains three new songs, totaling over 10 minutes, created by the talented composer Andreas Waldetoft.These grand songs run while playing as Indian characters to enhance the players experience and to enrichen the atmosphere of the game. Andreas Waldetoft has been working on Paradox Development Studio games since Hearts of Iron II and Europa Universalis III and has composed the music for the award winning titles Crusader Kings II and Europa Universalis IV.
