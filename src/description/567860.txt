
							About This Game
							Place tiles on the board to match the color or symbol of adjacent tiles to create 'passages' from edge to edge. Completed rows and columns ('passages') disappear from the board. Passage 4 is an easy to learn but challenging game that incorporates the best elements of several classic games. It will keep you fascinated for hours. Give it a try!More than 220 levels 	3 game modes with 3 levels of difficulty 	6 different graphic themes 	Spoken tutorial	Great sound effects and background music
