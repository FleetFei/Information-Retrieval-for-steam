
							About This Content
							This full-color art book featuring Yomawari: Night Alone contains many of the key illustrations, story scenes, and character designs that went into the creation of the game. From narrow alleyways to threatening spirits to the little girl herself, Director and Lead Designer Yu Mizokami guides you through the darkness and explains some of the inspirations that came about to bring everyone this haunting tale. Stylized to look like the little girl's diary, you may even find some cute sketches inside!
