
							About This Content
							We're proud to announce the release of Frank Klepacki's third album in the 8-Bit Series - Invaders!   

Klepacki is the world renown writer/musician/composer behind other game soundtracks including the legendary Command & Conquer series.  This epic album includes all of the following tracks:

1. Alien Schemes
2. Mobilize
3. Machines vs. Brains
4. Galactic Overview
5. Not of this Earth
6. Cranioid Attack
7. Armored Support
8. Protect The Planet
9. Unusual Activity
10. Soldier Formation
