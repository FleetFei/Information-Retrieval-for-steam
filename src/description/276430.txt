
							About This Game
							A lot of exciting tasks and different pets are waiting for you! You will be in charge of caring for each little fosterling personally. The playful little pets will need a lot of attention when running around lumbering. Teach your animals tricks and pamper them with nice treats. When having achieved a task very well you will get “extras” to upgrade your pet hotel to lead your pet hotel to worldclass success!Features15 new and individual missions to accomplish Various opportunities for individual interaction with animals Highly detailed graphics of pets, objects, setting and movements
