
							About This Game
							Bombernauts is an explosive online multiplayer battle game with fully destructible voxel terrain. Throw stupid amounts of bombs to blow your robot friends off the world into the deadly lava below where they will surely die a fiery death! If that doesn't work, whack open a few piñatas with your trusty baseball bat, grab some overpowered powerups (like jetpacks, black holes, missiles, luchador masks, and more), and win that way instead!
