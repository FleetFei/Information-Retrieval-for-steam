
							About This Game
							[the Sequence] is a logic construction puzzler. Let your mind be your guide: Lay down seven different kinds of modules as your roadblocks to get you (portrayed as a binary data point) to a sweet destination.Apply an out-of-the-box thinking to get you to the next level! Every module is customizable in various ways; be free to create mind-blowing sequences that no one else could build. [the Sequence] features- Variety of different levels to beat - Several kinds of modules - Sandbox mode - Stylish minimalistic graphics - Futuristic sound - Smooth ambient music - Provides a great challenging experience for players
