
							About This Content
							This set consists of a complete VT11.5 train set, including the power cars and all passenger coaches

Popularly named the TEE (for Trans Europ Express), the VT11.5 presented a milestone in Train design in it's heydays. With it's destinct and representative design the TEE turned heads everywhere. The first trains entered service in 1957. The last one in West Germany was decomissioned in 1988 whereas East Germany put the last one out of service in 1991.

The models in this set are spectucaular replicas of the original with fully modelled and highly detailed interior design.
