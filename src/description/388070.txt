
							About This Content
							This is a brand new game mode that allows you to engage your friends and acquaintances. Immerse yourself into the tactical turn-based battles together!

What new features are awaiting you?
- Finally, you could take control of enemy troops (who have been crushed in a single company). You are waiting for an unforgettable clashes of Mexican gangs with Somali pirates.
- New entertaining maps for fighting. Look for new ways to interact with the environment, think about tricky maneuvers that will lead you to victory.
- Your troops are no longer limited to only three characters - collect full combat teams of different experts, equip them and direct them right into battle.
- Take care of your soldiers - the more they are fighting, the more cool they become!

Compete, increase your skills and open up new tactical opportunities!
