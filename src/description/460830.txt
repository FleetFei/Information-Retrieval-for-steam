
							About This Content
							The Orange Deck is an expansion to SUPERFIGHT that adds over 80 geek based cards to the core deck.  This expansion references video games, television and movies, comic books, and things from the weird part of the internet.The Orange Deck comes complete with new Achievements, Titles, and Art to unlock!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Orange Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.
