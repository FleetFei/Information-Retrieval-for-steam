
							About This Game
							Meet the continuation of the hardcore platformer Project: R.E.B.O.O.T!
You have to play for the intelligent robot, created by the best scientists for the annual tournament "Project: R.E.B.O.O.T".
The best minds from all the planets are programming their robots each year, which must pass 30 challenging levels, equipped with deadly traps and various obstacles.
In the game you expect sea hardcore and fun, as well as new, unique locations and a variety of levels.
Also do not forget that your main goal to reach the end and prove to everyone that you are the best!
