
							About This Game
							Always dreamed of becoming a forklift operator? Now you can!You get to drive a forklift, move pallets around and do crazy stunts.Features: The forklifting adventure you've always been dreaming of! Experience the glamorous life of a warehouse worker. Realistic, accurate and highly responsive controls. Support for most steering wheels. All the pallets you can wish for! Pick pallets up! Put pallets down!The game you never knew you wanted!Force feedback only works on Windows, and may not work on racing wheels.The Steam controller is emulated as an Xbox360 controller.
