
							About This Game
							A game played entirely along a single line. Platform and fight across a variety of levels that will put your determination, patience, skill, accuracy and reactions to the test. No spoken words, and no written instructions. This is a game for platform lovers and is a great challenge for speedrunners. Go in expecting a pure platforming experience where the focus is on changing the gameplay on a fundamental level.Fight through a variety of levels that take advantage of the unique perspective culminating in a reaction-testing boss fight.
