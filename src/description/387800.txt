What Is Microsoft Outlook?
Microsoft Outlook is an extremely popular e-mail program used by over 500 million people worldwide. Outlook for Office 2013 & Office 365 is the latest version of the program and belongs to the Microsoft Office Suite of products. Used by professionals and students all over the world, this e-mail program promises greater e-mail organization, search, communication and social networking features.
