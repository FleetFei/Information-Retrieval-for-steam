
							About This Content
							The digital artbook of Cursed Sight. Featured inside are the sprites, backgrounds and CGs used in the game. The creator's thoughts are included next to each image.

This artbook is presented in PDF format and can be opened using Adobe Acrobat Reader, which can be downloaded for free online. Once purchased and installed, the artbook can be found in the game's root directory. To access this folder, right click on Cursed Sight in your Steam library and select properties. Inside the pop up, pick the local files tab and click the browse local files button. You should see the PDF in the folder that appears.
