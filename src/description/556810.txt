
							About This Content
							The “Last Days of Spring 2” Official Soundtrack features music composed and produced by Antonio Lorenzo Carino. Reunite with the feelings and memories through this empathetic story through the vivacious and charming music that served as the backdrop of this visual novel. Also included is the behind the scenes Director's Commentary taking you though the Last Days of Spring journey.
