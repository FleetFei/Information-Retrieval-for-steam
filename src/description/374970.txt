
							About This Game
							Experience some of the worlds most beautiful real and fantasy golf courses in relaxing 360 degree outdoor VR 3D rendered environments.Designed and created especially with virtual reality in mind, Golf Masters, plays just like real life golf complete with realistic ball and wind physics that determine your results on the fly. Play through 18 fully 3D modeled holes (additional DLC content coming soon) as you try to shot the courses lowest score and have your name etched into the Steam leaderboard of true golf masters. Shoot for the hole and pull in one of the low scores for the course. Fully optimized for the Virtual Reality experience Golf Masters has full support for the HTC / Valve Vive motion tracked joysticks, as well optional control using the Oculus Rift and Microsoft 360 PC controller.
