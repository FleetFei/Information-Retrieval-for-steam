
							About This Game
							QUBIC is a retro-like turn-based puzzle game in which your task is to solve the labyrinth, collect the stars to unlock next level, avoid enemies/traps and... SAVE YOUR QUBIC GREEN ASS!Key features:    Different levels that will blow your brain!    Mad retro-like 16bit pixel graphics!    Fast turn-based game-play!    Simple controls and cubic-like visual style.    Prepare for DOUBLE-PENE... TROUBLE!    Awesome soundtracks included.    Amazing backgrounds & emoticons that will allow you to make GODLIKE Steam Profile page by using pixels!
