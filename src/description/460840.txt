
							About This Content
							The Digital Deck is an all-new expansion deck, exclusive to the digital version of SUPERFIGHT, that adds 100 new Character and Attributes to the core deck. This expansion adds even more ridiculous current pop-culture characters such as A Guy Who Knows A Guy, Donald Trump, and Master Chef, as well as new over-the-top Attributes to create new ultimate fighters...or losers!The Digital Deck comes complete with new Achievements, Titles, and Art to unlock!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Digital Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.
