
							About This Game
							Detective Hunt - Crownston City PD is a noir visual novel!You are Damien Hunt, a junior detective of the Crownston City Police Department. Together with your partner, you are to investigate criminal activities around the city. You grew up hearing that the city is so safe, so beautiful... but is it?FeaturesStory driven gameplay presented as a film noir-esque comic book / visual novel feel Three different yet intertwining chapters Multiple endings to each chapter. You choose who to apprehend, who to pursue and watch it unravel. Interview witnesses / Interrogate suspects. Depending on your method, you may uncover hidden clues and information
