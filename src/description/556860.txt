Can you discover the truth about the supreme emperors betrayal and get your revenge or will you get beaten again by the empires finest?FEATURES Gladiator management sim with RPG and tycoon elements Build and manage your own school for gladiators. Send out scouts on missions to explore the far corners of the world. Gladiatorial ranking - top 10 best gladiators only fight in the Colosseum Rise to the top as gladiator trainer and ascend through a hierarchy of grades.
