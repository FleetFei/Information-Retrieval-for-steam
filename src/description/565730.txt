
							About This Game
							Overview:"Sanctuary VR" is a first-person puzzle solving and exploration game designed for Oculus Rift, it has a strong narrative that is fully voice acted. The game is a seated VR experience played with a controller. Story:A group of refugees fled the war on earth for a new home aboard a creaking old star-ship named the "Korolev", but they never arrived at their destination. You venture into deep space alone searching for the "Korolev" and the loved one who never arrived. Features:Believable and interesting puzzles (no key hunting, or page finding) Unique setting, the "Korolev" is a unique ship worth exploring. Strong narrative that is fully voice acted - Replay audio logs anytime while exploring, no need to stare at a menu while you listen.
