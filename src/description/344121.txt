
							About This Content
							An additional playable scenario for the title "Nobunaga's Ambition: Souzou with Power Up Kit". Kagetora Nagao, born the fourth son of Tamekage Nagao took control of the Nagao Clan after successfully removing his uninspiring brother leader. Officers against the young Kagetora taking reign turned their warriors against him but Kagetora turns them away within his skillful tactics during battle. An new era of Kagetora opens when he changes his name to Kenshin who is to be known as the God of War throughout the country later on. Relive the historical drama of the ambitious expedition the young hero sets out to.
