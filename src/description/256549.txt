							*NOTE* This pack is available in the in-game store under the Marketplace tab.  You are strongly recommended to find the product there and make your purchase from within the game.In order to be able to use this add-on livery product, you MUST first own the Great Northern F7 ‘Empire Builder’ Loco Add-on.  If you check in the in-game store it will verify if you own it before you purchase.The EMD F7 was a 1,500 Horsepower locomotive produced in the late forties and early fifties and originally intended as a freight hauling loco.The locomotive was produced in both A units and B units.  The A units had a cab for the engineer, the B units were intended entirely as slave power.This pack represents the F7 in Southern Pacific's splendid grey and red "Bloody Nose" livery, ready for your freight hauling needs in Train Simulator!You must own the Great Northern F7 ‘Empire Builder’ Loco Add-on before you will be able to use this add-on
