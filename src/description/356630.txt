
							About This Content
							Initially Chip's Challenge 2 seems to be about playing through a series of puzzles. But it’s also about learning the mechanics of game play, so as the Player you can become the Creator, making the game more than just passive experience, but a creative one too.The paint style interface allows you to make complex logic puzzle, skill dodging levels or just simple mazes, so that you can get a taste quickly of what it’s like to be a game designer, by seeing your ideas coming to life. Chip's Challenge 2: Editor includes:All the game elements, abilities & monsters from Chip's Challenge 1 & 2Two player co-op split screen optionAbility to swap .c2m files with friends You've been a Player of Chip's Challenge 2, now you can also be a Creator too.
