
							About This Game
							What is AoF World Online: AoF World Online is a single/multiplayer fantasy online experience where you unravel the mystery behind an illusive force threatening Fasaria and her 3 moons.
 NO MONTHLY or ADDITIONAL FEES. No Need for Item Shop. All Platinum Tokens and Extras can be earned fairly in game. New in game Leaderboard added. Climb the ranks and reach greatness.  Explore Fasaria and her 3 moons.  Journey through the Planetarium.  Populate and build your space station.  Level up your skills and character.  Collect tribute from your fleet.  Play slots to earn Platinum Tokens.  Gain karma and earn rewards.
