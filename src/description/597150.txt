
							About This Game
							Raise your army, learn powerful combos and take back your land!Slash and Dash your way through hundreds of enemies in the fast-paced skill-based action of Hyper Knights!Become stronger and learn new powerful combos to help you on your journey.Raise your army and recruit allied Knights to join your cause, command their armies, siege enemy castles, raid their outposts, ambush their convoys and use the loot to build your lands.    Slash and Dash through your enemies    Command hundreds of minions    Recruit allied Knights to your cause    Upgrade your skills and learn powerful combos    Defend your lands from enemy attacks    Duel enemy Knights    Siege their castles and take back your land!
