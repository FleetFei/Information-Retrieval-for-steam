
							About This Content
							Jace Beleren’s fully unlocked “Mind Maze” deck. As a specialist in mind-magic, Jace Beleren uses his abilities to gaze into the minds of others, discovering even closely guarded secrets. See what it takes to master the powers of a mind mage as you control your opponent’s every move and warp their perceptions of time. Jace has powerful illusionary magic to throw at any problem that comes up on the battlefield. Your ability to control Jace’s powers of tempo and card advantage shows a master’s touch.
