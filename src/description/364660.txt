
							About This Game
							Luminoso is a puzzle game with laser beams. The goal of each puzzle is to make all laser beams hit the receiver pieces of their respective color.Features Many unique piece types, each with different effects. Lasers may have different beam colors. Colors can be mixed to produce new colors, or split into their component colors. 60 hand-crafted levels with varying difficulty. A level editor to create your own levels. Chilling music that doesn't get on your nerves.
