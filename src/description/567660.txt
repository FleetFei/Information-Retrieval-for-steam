
							About This Game
							Baseball Riot is a fun physics based puzzle game packed with slapstick comedy and mayhem!Take control of retired star batter Gabe Carpaccio as he goes on a crusade to free his former team from the grasp of Explodz Inc.Smash baseballs at various Explodz fueled goons such as obnoxious fans, biased umpires, leery catchers and more. Bounce the ball of walls and platforms to knock out many targets with one hit.Detonate Explodz crates to launch the baddies sky high! See the enemies tumble as hapless ragdolls and try to master each level by collecting three stars.Conquer more than 100 levels and save the day!FEATURESEasy to learn physics based puzzle gameplayFunny knockouts with ragdoll charactersMore than 100 levelsExplosive chain reactions and destructible items
