
							About This Game
							A truly unique puzzle experience that embraces simplicity, elegance, and challenge. Strata paints a simple yet challenging puzzle game onto a sophisticated canvas of color and sound. Test the limits of your brain by completing complex weaves of color to solve the puzzles. The goal of Strata is to strategically layer colored ribbons to match a pattern. Using forethought and strategy you must sequence your moves correctly. Over time patterns emerge and your tactics may evolve, but Strata will keep you on your toes with increasingly difficult puzzles. Special thanks to those who supported Strata on Greenlight!Key FeaturesHundreds of increasingly bigger and more challenging puzzles. Option to Perfect each grid by completing it without pulling a ribbon back. A simple, yet addicting game for any age. Beautifully recorded sounds accompany your actions.
