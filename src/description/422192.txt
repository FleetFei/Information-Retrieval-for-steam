
							About This Content
							Smitemaster's Selections are level expansion packs for DROD.  These Caravel-produced level collections add to the official story of DROD with quality writing and voice acting.  You will have about a hundred new and unique puzzles to solve in each Smitemaster's Selection bundle.Expansions 5+6 contain these episodes:Beethro's TeacherMaster Locks + Master Locks Expert
