
							About This Content
							This package contains the Artbook and Original Soundtrack of Soul Gambler. The Artbook has 76 pages and it features the making of Soul Gambler. Learn about the insigths and decisions made by the game developers during the creation process. See unreleased concept art, sketches, scenarios and many illustrations.In addition, you will receive the Original Soundtrack of the game with all the songs produced on the MP3 format.
