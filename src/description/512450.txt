
							About This Game
							BitRay 2 -  is a simple puzzle game in which your task is to destroy all the black cubes, and do so all the white appeared on the white platform. 

Features: 
- Beautiful minimalistic graphics.
- 2 Types of graphics - (Default and Outline graphics)
- More than + 160 different levels.
- Good TimeKiller .
- Many cubes and spheres with their own unique abilities 
- 3 Soundtracks
- Low price.
