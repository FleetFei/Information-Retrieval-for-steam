
							About This Game
							The Crazy Chicken first saw the light of day 17 years ago, and has been on lots of adventures since then. Now it's back to face its biggest challenge yet: In Crazy Chicken: Tiger & Chicken, you play the hero in a fast-paced story filled with terrifying enemies and dramatic combat. As a Shaolin fighter, you must liberate your oppressed people and save the love of your life.FeaturesAction RPG for the whole familyFight with a variety of weaponsSophisticated skill systemBeautiful 3D graphicsSupports controllers
