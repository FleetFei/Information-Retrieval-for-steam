
							About This Game
							Hunt and eat other fish - simply, grow into larger beasts!Animal survival game based in the fish world! You start as Bibos the fish and straight away you are ready to dive in to the waters of the mesmerizing world of Feed and Grow.Growing into more mods and contentWith the first release we want to test the basics of multiplayer. Further development will include new fish abilities and passives, new fun content for both single and multiplayer with game mods. Also, we want to focus on the technical stuff and add new animations, new features, make gameplay smoother and more fun.Show your love and support in reviews! We're grateful for all your feedback and tips, it helps us make the game more awesome.
