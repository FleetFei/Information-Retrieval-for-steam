
							About This Content
							EDM producer Encanti (Ben Cantil) is back with nearly 200 new Mutant presets... AND THIS TIME IT'S PERSONAL! Cakewalk Mutant R3LOAD is a suite of 197 all new sounds for Z3TA+ 2 from Encanti's personal arsenal of sounds crafted while teaching synthesis at Berklee Valencia and during production of his upcoming record. If you like the sounds from the previous Mutant packs, Mutant R3LOAD is a must buy!
