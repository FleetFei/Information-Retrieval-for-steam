
							About This Content
							Created by Riley D. Owens (aka Darkwoulfe), this product consists of 25 top-down uniquely designed, 3d rendered tokens, depicting heroes, villains and monsters. You can use these to replace the letter tokens or built-in tokens in many popular adventure modules. Just unlock the encounters and drag these over top or drag them over top of the listing in your combat tracker.This Token Pack includes:    Ankheg    Anxious Player    Axe Fighter    Bat in Flight    Beltram    Centipede    Dual Wield Ranger    Elven Archer    Fighter with Scimitar    Ghost Spider    Hardened Fighter 1    Kenku with khopesh    Kenku with staff    Kru-Knid Ambusher    Kru-Knid Attacker    Manticoid Archer    Manticoid Martial Artist    Manticoid Mystic    Manticoid Spearman    Manticoid Warrior    Master Drayven Stahl    Maverick Player    Necromancer    Southern Fighter    Tierfling PlayerRequires: A Fantasy Grounds full or ultimate license or an active subscription and any Ruleset.
