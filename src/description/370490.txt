
							About This Game
							A Cold War super computer has gone rogue and is printing launch codes for a first strike.  There is only one way to prevent nuclear Armageddon; enter the dot matrix, collect the codes, and defeat the ASCII army.ASCII Attack is a retro style indie action title inspired by arcade games of yesteryear.  It is a skill based game with a unique set of controls and a new take on classic arcade action.Take the fight to the enemy and battle on 25 levels of 80s era dot matrix printer paper.Multiple modes and enemies provide extended gameplay challenges.Peace out and live through waves of invincible enemies in Survival mode.Embrace the fight and unleash the power of the ASCII @om bomb.Face the ultimate challenge in the ASCII Kicker mode.Full Steam Controller and 360 Gamepad support; perfect for Big Picture.
