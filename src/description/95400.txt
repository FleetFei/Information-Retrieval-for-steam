
							About This Game
							ibb & obb is a two player cooperative game set in a puzzle filled world where gravity goes both up and down. You can only succeed by working closely together.Find a friend for some true local co-op couch fun or match up online.Fall up and jump down through 15 levels filled with double gravity puzzles and discover 8 hidden worlds that will test your new non-Newtonian skills to the maximum.All levels have their own unique music, composed by Kettel, known for his warm melodic electronica.Key FeaturesLocal and online co-opWarm electronic soundtrack by KettelSteam achievements, leaderboards, multiplayer, cloud saving and trading cardsSecret levels that will test your skills to the max
