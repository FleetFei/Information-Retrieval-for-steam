
							About This Content
							A complete set of Runestone cards, which give your Talisman characters extra abilities, for use in online and offline game modes.Important note - These Runestone cards can be unlocked through normal game progression.Designed to speed up a game of Talisman, and also make your characters more powerful, Runestones are cards which you can attach to your characters to give them the advantage over their opponents.Each character can have 3 Runestone cards equipped, so you can mix and match which abilities are suitable for each character.Will you give the Troll a higher starting Craft? Or maybe make your Wizard immune to being Toaded!
