
							About This Game
							Lume: An illuminating puzzle adventure!						With a set built entirely out of paper and cardboard, and sumptuously filmed, Lume is a game with a style unlike any other.						Power to your grandad’s house has failed. What’s more, he’s nowhere to be seen.  Immerse yourself in Lume’s photoreal world, solve perplexing paper puzzles to help restore the power, and uncover a deeper mystery behind the blackout.  						This game forms Part 1 of a larger, ongoing story.
