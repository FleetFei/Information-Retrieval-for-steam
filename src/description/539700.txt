
							About This Game
							SWEATER? OK! is a game that tuning the traditional Snake gameplay concept upside down! This time You will mantle the role of the food, running away from the pursuing Snakes.You take on the role of an apple, collecting stars and running away from snakes! You will be presented with a large randomly-generated level with stars and snake eggs scattered all over.Your goal is to get as much points on the level as possible and to mark yourself as the bravest apple of all apples who ever stepped on snake's eggs!Key features:    Dynamic game process that will keep you entertained.    Level generator that makes every try unique!    Leaderboards that display the results of players worldwide. Be the best!    First sweater-like Steam game ever.    Simple and intuitive control scheme.
