
							About This Game
							Western Adventure - classic minimalistic arcade game of the genre Endless Runner!Wild West. Bold and beautiful girl Jenny is running towards adventure! On her way she meets dangerous robbers, burglars and various obstacles. But Jenny is not afraid of difficulties on her way. She jump over obstacles and run further on the endless arid deserts of the Wild West! Run, jump and run again!Features:Nice graphics;Good music and soundtrack by Jason Shaw;Easy control;Score;Trading cards;Sheriff Badge!
