
							About This Game
							Top-down shooter and tower defense hybrid with Mechs and space zombies. Defend your home base, wipe out hordes of undead creatures, upgrade your towers and protect power cores. There is 4 unique campaigns with more than 50 missions, several types of towers and upgradable mechs. Choose between different type of robots: Thunder, Fury, Revenant and Punisher. Build labyrinths, upgrade your skills, buy new weapons to destroy them all and save your base.
