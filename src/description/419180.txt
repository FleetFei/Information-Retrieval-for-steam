
							About This Video
							High school freshman Jason (Bobb'e J. Thompson) just found the girl of his dreams. But to win her heart, he'll have to prove he's got what it takes to make the school's hottest dance crew in this comedy directed by Nick Cannon. Co-starring Mike Epps, George Lopez, Luenell, Wilmer Valderrama and Katt Williams.
