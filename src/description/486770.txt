
							About This Content
							Electronic music trailblazer Giorgio Moroder and co-composer/producer Raney Shockne teamed up to create a next-generation soundtrack for TRON RUN/r. Focusing on the edginess of the game design and marrying sonic notions of speed and space with data streaming and AI, Moroder and Shockne created a memorable soundscape that ties perfectly to the onscreen action in the game. A handpicked team of cutting-edge remixers was then asked to reinterpret and extend this work. The result is a breathtakingly sweeping soundtrack that stands on its own as a wothy successor to the iconic soundtracks for the TRON films. Featured remixers are Autechre, Rusko, Plaid, Darkstar, patten and Bibio.
