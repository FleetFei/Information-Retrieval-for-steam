
							About This Content
							The Master of Crazy is back and ready to put you through the test of time!
						Fix your time machine then travel back and experience 50 prehistoric levels where Rube Goldberg-style game play relies on Stone Age technology!
						Pamper a dinosaur and create power. Generate electricity with prehistoric tools. Ignite dynamite without a match. Blow up your creations and light up the sky! Set your goals and put your imagination in gear to do all of these things and much more!
