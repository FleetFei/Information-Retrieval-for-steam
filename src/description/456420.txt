
							About This Game
							Gunslinger Trainer is a western VR game where you have to train to become the ultimate gunslinger of the west. Shoot, Explode and Throw items at a variety of targets in different western locales to become the ultimate be it all Gunslinger.

Use your resourcefulness by destroying targets in unique ways with items that are scattered around to be used at your disposal. Light up some sticks of dynamite, throw a knife, throw an axe! Anything to score more points!

Kick back and relax by practicing your marksman skills by shooting objects on the side, as well as discovering secrets that are hidden in each level.
