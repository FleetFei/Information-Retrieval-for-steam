
							About This Game
							In that game you will be given the triangle with 3 sides, also there will be three balls that you should hit. On the course of game the speed of balls will grow, and there will appear different traps on the map. All game time you will enjoy relaxing music. Also as game goes on you will gain the coins that will allow you to buy game background.
The whole game is dynamic and won't let you be bored.
