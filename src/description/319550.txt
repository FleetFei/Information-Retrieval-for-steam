
							About This Game
							Get inside a particle accelerator and experience unbelievable speed previously reserved for only the most advanced laboratories on Earth! The Collider is a fast-paced and compelling arcade game that will put your reflexes to the test. Tune in with the energizing electronic music and beat your friends' high scores.Game Features: •             10 levels full of twisted obstacles•             Simple, intuitive controls that lets you accelerate to unbelievable speed•             Leaderboards – compete with your friends and beat their high scores•             Elegant futuristic style•             Energetic electronic soundtrack•             Steam Achievements to track your progress•             Support for mouse and game pad•             Complete experience without limitations of a mobile game
