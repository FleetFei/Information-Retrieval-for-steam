
							About This Content
							The NASCAR Foundation is a national charity that impacts children’s lives through the Speediatrics Children’s Fund and the Betty Jane France Humanitarian Award. Since its founding in 2006, The NASCAR Foundation has positively impacted more than 1 million children. In honor and memory of Founder, Betty Jane France, Dusenberry Martin Racing will be donating $1 of the sales price of each pack to The NASCAR Foundation.
