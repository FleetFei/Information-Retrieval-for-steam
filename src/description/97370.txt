
							About This Content
							Liliana Vess’s fully unlocked “Obedient Dead” black deck. Liliana Vess is a necromancer, specializing in dark magic that unlocks power from death. The risen dead fight for Liliana, and their ranks swell every time she claims another victory. If her endless swarms aren’t enough to overwhelm the enemy, Liliana’s pinpoint removal spells will kill off the biggest obstacles—and reanimate them over to your side. Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013.
