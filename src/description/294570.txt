
							About This Game
							There's more to this case than meets the eye! Join Freddi Fish and her best friend, Luther, for the grand opening of beautiful Coral Cove Park - But wait! A mysterious sea creature has been seen lurking inside. If the park is to open on time you have to help Freddi and Luther find the clues, solve the puzzles and get to the bottom of this watery predicament.New locations and puzzles each time you play! Packed with puzzles, games and activities! Find the clues and collect the tools to solve the mystery!
