
							About This Game
							Sharpen your axe, string your bow, brew your potions!  Ancaria needs heroes like you. Grab your friends to join the resistance!The once peaceful land of Ancaria is in grave danger. The evil Ashen empire has enslaved its population and is using the orc-like Grimmoc to wipe out the Seraphim, who swore to protect it. In order to defeat the  empire you will have to prove yourself as a warrior by riding terrifying mounts, acquiring earth shattering skills and looting  powerful weapons and valuable treasures. Grab your friends and join the resistance!Key Features:Battle it out with up to 3 player co-opTake charge of 1 of 4 unique playable character classes with their own set of skillsExplore the vast world of AncariaRide thumping mounts and use devastating war-machinesTons of loot!Define your own fighting style with different weapon combinations
