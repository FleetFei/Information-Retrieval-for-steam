
							About This Game
							SUPER IMPOSSIBLE ROAD is a futuristic racing game about cheating.Get ahead of the pack by taking epic shortcuts across the procedurally generated speedway. These risky overtaking manoeuvres can take you straight into the lead, or drop you mercilessly into the void.Local races against human and AI opponents using 4 player splitscreenTime Gate game mode with global leaderboardsSurvival game mode with global leaderboardsProcedurally generated tracksVehicle customization for boosting statsPhysically modeled material system with real time reflections, object-based motion blur and HDR supportFull surround sound system supportTechno soundtrackComing soon during Early Access:Online multiplayer Single player career More environments, vessel components, improvements and polish!
