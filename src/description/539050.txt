
							About This Game
							Jump into Super Island God and take on the role of a lost island tribe's deity in this exciting new God Simulator. Harness your divine power in this built-for-VR game and reward (or punish) your civilization as they journey through dozens of handcrafted challenges.Early Access Launch Features:
Immerse yourself in Virtual Reality and watch your villagers and environment respond in new and novel ways.  Direct your villagers across six unique and challenging islands with hidden objectives and time attack modes. Construct and grow your village using unique buildings and an expanding number of villager jobs.   Wield your divine powers and cast miracles to reward (or punish) your villagers.  Discover hidden toys and easter eggs such as the hungry volcano (feed me...)!Unlock unique cosmetic hands by unearthing hidden coins.  Super Island God is a VR-Only game!
