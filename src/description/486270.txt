
							About This Content
							In the waters of the Caribbean there are fortunes to be made and lost...or stolen.In these times it takes courage and daring to carve out a niche and forge a new legend. Will you sail the high seas in search of fame and fortune, lead a band of buccaneers in search of booty, or captain a ship of the line in the service of King and country?Pirates™ is a complete game based on WizKids’ best-selling Constructible Strategy Game™ and the award winning Savage Worlds roleplaying game system. All the rules you need to play this game of fast and furious swashbuckling fun are in this book.So set a course for adventure; fame and fortune await over the horizon.Requires: A Fantasy Grounds full or ultimate license or an active subscription and the Savage Worlds ruleset.
