
							About This Game
							DungeonUp is a dungeon crawler in which you have to make choice in every few steps.Inspired by but different from Tower of the Sorcerer, DungeonUp is based on procedurally generated levels so you will encounter a whole new set of challenges in every re-run. This is a must-have for rogue-like and puzzle game fans.Features: 50+ levels in 5 zones with different monsters and theme. 10+ levels with secrets and plots. 40+ monsters, and some are with special abilities. In each zone there's a boss battle, requires you to fight with wisdom. 40+ unique items, and some are designed with puzzles. Oryx's 16-bit Fantasy Sprite Set. 3 difficulty levels, designed for both casual and hardcore players. Maze Mode for your coffee break. Explore more fun from tons of Player Maps.
