
							About This Game
							In this VR adventure you are the Dragon Mage, who trains dragons and fights enemies while gliding through the air and counter attacking with a giant staff. Reclaim the land that was taken away by the invaders, and practice new moves in the sanctuary. Teratini VR game play is a cross between tennis, dodge ball, and sword fighting. Neat Stuff Many enemy variations, abilities, and attack formations.  Train, fight, and level up with your dragon.  Things to know before you buy or try Not the best game if you are sensitive to heights.
