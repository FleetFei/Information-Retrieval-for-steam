
							About This Game
							3D Point-and-click adventure puzzle game wrapped in cinematic 2D cut-scenes.After a concerning message left on Rob's answer-phone from his panicked-sounding twin, Nick, he rushes to his brother's office to try and find a hidden mask. Not long into the search, things take a very dangerous turn...Who is the dead man in the newspaper article? What is so important about this mask? And where is his brother?Mirrored - Chapter 1 is the first part in this escape-the-room style trilogy.
