
							About This Content
							GameGuru Buildings PackThis pack contains 50 quality building assets all ready to be used in your GameGuru creations. 4 Concrete Bunkers (all with internal spaces) 4 Office Blocks 5 Skyscrapers 4 Warehouses 4 Grunge buildings 4 Sandstone buildings (all with internal spaces) 4 Gothic style 4 Flats 5 Houses 4 Huts (1 with internals) 4 Shanty huts (all with internals) 4 Sheds  (all with internals)These are mostly scenery buildings with just a few that allow the player to enter. We do plan to extend the range of buildings as we develop more for the pack. There are currently 17 buildings in the pack that are modelled both inside and outside. We're working on an update that will allow for more of the buildings to have internal areas and doors that open and close.The pack will effortlessly install direct into your GameGuru library. When you run GameGuru and click on New Entity in the Library you will see a new section called "Buildings Pack". Click on it to start exploring your new library resource of game assets. All the assets in this Mega Pack are royalty free, meaning any game you make with them can be legally sold by you.
