
							About This Content
							The Mecha Ritz: Steel Rondo Soundtrack is a collection of the 36 original tracks from the one-person developer, and all recorded with a FM Synthesizer to perfectly emulate the retro aesthetic. Songs will be places in your Mecha Ritz: Steel Rondo folder in the Steam Directory: ...\Steam\SteamApps\common\Mecha Ritz Steel Rondo\Soundtrack
