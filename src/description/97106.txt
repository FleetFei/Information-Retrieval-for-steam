
							About This Content
							Power up your weapons and suit with the Blitz Pack.  Includes the Napalm Mortar, Incendiary Rounds for the Shotgun, and Blitz Armor.  Napalm Mortars set the earth ablaze with flammable shells to burn down your enemies long after impact.  Incendiary Rounds for the Shotgun incinerate on impact, damaging enemy armor over time.  Blitz Armor is available for both Section 8 and Arm of Orion forces and displays the emblems of the most bold and fearless soldiers.
