
							About This Game
							In the future there's only death and destruction, so we took all the criminals and put them in cars, now they battle each other for your entertainment, so sit back relax and just hit start to play Iron Madness.  Iron Madness is a fast-paced multiplayer/split-screen car combat game with up to four players, blow your friends away with a wide variety of weapons including rockets, saw-blades, mines, and machine guns and more.
