
							About This Game
							Going Up is a fast-paced science-fiction sword fighting game. Fight endless waves of enemies in a totally doomed quest to reach the end (and a less doomed quest to reach the high score board).Features: Endless waves Multiple kinds of evil robots Wield a bolt of lightning! In VR! Boss battle Probably not going to shoot your dog (but it is Early Access)Planned features: Cosmetic unlocks More enemy types More boss battles
