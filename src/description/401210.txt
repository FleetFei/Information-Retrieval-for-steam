
							About This Game
							Challenge 3 difficulty levels of puzzles, and uncover the true ending of Puzzle's quest!Boulder's, Ice, Patrols, and Spike traps oh my, along with many other types of fiendish traps await you!Puzzle Sages is built on the concept of presenting fun, but difficult challenges. The game's main features are as follows! 30 Puzzle's of various difficulties and types! No luck based challenges! if you beat a puzzle it's purely because of your skill as a player! A fun story! 100% hand crafted challenges and levels! 3 varying difficulty levels with different endings, to push you too overcome the challenges to discover the truth!
