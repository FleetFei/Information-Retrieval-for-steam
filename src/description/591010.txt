
							About This Game
							Space Ripper is a hardcore space shooter. There’s no saves, a little HP and many dangerous enemies…If you can survive for 5 minutes you are arcade God!  You need to destroy the enemies and collect gears to upgrade your ship. Game balance changes periodically, which makes the game unpredictable and difficult to get a high score.Features    more than 50 different waves of enemies and bosses    15 guns    upgrades your ship: weapon, engine, wings, death ray    boosters: firerate, speed, kamikaze, time shift etc.    wicked bosses    great number of explosions, fires and lasers
