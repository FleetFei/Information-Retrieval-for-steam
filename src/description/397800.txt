
							About This Game
							Battle Quiz is a classical multiplechoice trivia quiz game, where you have to answer questions like you know it from school of TV shows. Game uses sterotypes from countries such as: Germany, Russia, USA, France, Italy and many many others. The rules are simple. Try to complete the quiz by answering all of the questions. It may sound easy, but will require good knowledge and also some good luck. Many characters to choose from, large question base, and a lots of great fun! Also an important element of the game are the characters - funny and representative for each nation. But hey, those are only funny stereotypes.
