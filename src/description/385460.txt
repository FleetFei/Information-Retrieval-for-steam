
							About This Game
							ATV GP is a wild racing game featuring an overwhelming sense of speed. Smooth gameplay, outstanding audio and visuals all designed to keep you on the edge of your seat throughout the whole experience.Game modes: motocross GP, career mode (125cc, 250cc and open class) hill-climbing-collision courseFeatures: quad tuning (categories: 125 cc, 250 cc, 450 cc, 600 cc) 12 tracks for GP mode 4 closed tracks for collision course 4 hill climb tracks customizable number of opponents (3-7)
