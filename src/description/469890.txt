
							About This Game
							Space Codex is a casual retro arcade minimal awesome top-down shooter featuring waves of enemy spaceships with different attacks in what is, literally, a bullet hell. To beat them, you will have power-up options available through the map. The game only gets harder. How long can you stay alive?Features:- Multiple and unique power-ups- Beautiful retro, pixelated graphics- Unique chiptune soundtrack- Multiple enemies with different behaviorsMusic by:VisagersawsquarenoiseTeknoAXE
