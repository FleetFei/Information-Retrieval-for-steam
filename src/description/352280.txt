
							About This Software
							Even if you don't have a band, you can realize your musical vision using virtual instruments. Music Creator and SONAR include new Cakewalk Sound Center, which gives you access to at least 150 different instrument sounds. It includes acoustic & electric guitars, basses, drums, pianos, synths, strings, ethnic sounds, and more.Cakewalk Sound Center is bundled with the following Cakewalk products on STEAM. Music Creator 6 Touch Music Creator 7 SONAR Artist/Professional/Platinum SONAR X3 Base/Studio/Professional
