
							About This Game
							You've lost your job, wife and family. After several years of wandering you're appointed as a Chief Editor in the “Our Ural” magazine. You've got 9 weeks to prove your worth and get back to the rich, lucky and fun life. Your team consists of talented journalists with lots of ideas, but don't forget about the investors who're dictating the rules. Become the Chief Editor for this magazine and win the “Magazine of the Year” competition!
