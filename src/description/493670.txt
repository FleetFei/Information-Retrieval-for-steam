
							About This Game
							Fight through countless enemies and massive bosses in this indie shoot-'em-up! Use the unique teleportation mechanic to dodge enemy fire, then counter with an arsenal of powerful weapons. Valkyrius Prime features all new character art and story, plus many more improvements over the original, including refined level design and improved graphics.

In addition to the exciting action that only an arcade style "shmup" can bring, Valkyrius Prime features:
- 5 difficulties for casual and hardcore gamers alike
- 6 stages and boss rush mode
- Online high score tables
- 2 player co-op (at least one gamepad required for second player)
- Gamepad support
