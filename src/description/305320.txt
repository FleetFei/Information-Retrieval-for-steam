
							About This Content
							All content included is royalty-freeFrom funk, folk, pop, jazz and rock, Aaron Comess has done it all in his illustrious career as one of NYC's premier drummers.Widely know for his work as a founding member of the Grammy-nominated, ten million album-selling band Spin Doctors, Comess bring his signature style and sound to this incredible new Loop Loft collection.  Aaron Comess Looped Vol 1 features six unique sessions containing grooves that range from fat back, soulful Stax-esque beats, warm, stirred brush grooves, thunderous rock and songwriter-friendly tracks.  The collection also includes samples of Aaron's entire drum kit, allowing you to program your own grooves using the exact same sounds from the loop sessions.
