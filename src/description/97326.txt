
							About This Content
							Fully-unlocked “March to War” red and white deck. Lead an expansive army of Soldiers onto the battlefield and wage war against all who would oppose you. Take advantage of your superior discipline, superior tactics, and superior weapons. Every card in your deck is fine-tuned toward victory, from pinpoint removal to tame the mightiest opposition to spells that bolster all of your fighting forces. Several cards in this deck create multiple creature tokens, rapidly establishing superior numbers in your favor. Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2012. (Requires the most recent version of Magic 2012 and Magic 2012 Deck Pack 1.)
