To redeem the code for item pack, click on the Astro Shop button (Red button with golden star inside upper right of the screen) and click Buy Astros, then paste in the code. Item pack may be picked up from any Bank's Receive Item option after 15 minutes.Modified Clipper – A type of ship designed to traverse quickly across the open sea. The normal version of this ship requires Adventure level 52, while this special version only requires Adventure level 16. With improved turn and wave resistance, it is well suited for the needs of Adventurers.2 x Modified full rigged sail – Two high quality studding sails that can be easily taken off and equipped to any type of ships to increase the sailing speed. +22 Vertical, +25 Horizontal Sail, -1 Turn.Shared Storage Usage Permit – The permit enables all your characters in one account to access a special shared storage through the Storage Supervisor in most towns. Up to 50 items and 5000 tradable goods can be stored. This permit last for 15 days and additional permits may be purchased from the Astro shop in the game.Strongbox Permit - The permit enables the character to access a special safe box from the personal inventory at any time. Up to 30 items can be stored, and items stored cannot be plundered and does not reduce in durability during normal sailing. This permit lasts 15 days and additional permits may be purchased from the Astro shop in the game.Sea King's Talisman – It will increase the rate experience gain by 30% for 7 days, so players may level up faster.Wind King's Talisman – It will increase the bonus ship speed by 30% for 7 days, so players may sail faster.Flame King's Talisman – It will increase the rate of gaining fame by 30% for 7 days, so players may obtain permits faster to enter ports in different areas of the world.Check (100 million) – A tradable check worth 100 million ducats. Players may cash checks at the bank once reaching a combined fame of 10,000 or ask to trade with other players for ducats once reaching level 11 in one class.
