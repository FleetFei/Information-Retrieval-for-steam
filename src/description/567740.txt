
							About This Content
							This is the first part, the FPS career mode, of the new and improved PGM. You'll need the original game to play but other than that, they're completely separate.This new DLC includes: A complete graphical overhaul. Character creation. A clock that shows the real time in game, but you can also choose to speed things up.   Dynamic league tables/world ranking, which means that NPC-teams will play matches continuously. AI simulation so you can experience the ingame matches.  Logical progression for your character as well as the NPCs.  Better resolution and sound support.
