
							About This Game
							About this game：Sin Castle is a slightly high difficulty adventure game that focus on puzzling， contains various policy elements, rigorous rules. The key of puzzle this game is reasonable attributes assignment, props use, and equipment collocation. As the grids opened, game player will encounter all sorts of strategic monsters and puzzling design.Features:Well designed game content: More than 20 puzzle designs, different kinds of attributes assignment, props use and equipment collocation derives multiple ways of playing. A variety of grids design include monsters, stores, linkage and hidden grids, etc. We spent a large amount of time in balancing game mechanics and numerical system to make the game has high playability. Ukiyoe and glass painting helped us to creat a minimalist style.Colorful props: Various props design include more than 40 normal props, more than 30 special props, 9 pair of equipment(4 items)with specific set bonuses, more than 10 rare props, such as Dragon Tears, Chest of the Fate, Sanctuary of God, Spear of Longinus, Samuel boots of destruction, Beelzebub blade of Gluttony, etc. Conquer evil desires with these strong props.Tough monsters design: Three different level monsters are more than 40, dishonest Myrddin snake spirit, Wine-loving drunkard, jealous lovers, etc. Different monsters require different solutions.Thanks：Thanks for viewing!
