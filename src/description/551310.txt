
							About This Content
							Ultimate NPCs - Skullduggery (PFRPG)This book was written to be a resource to Game Masters and Players alike. It contains a wealth of information for developing plot points, character behaviors, histories, guilds, and many other valuable assets designed to aid in the running and playing of fantasy role playing games.This module includes 30 finely crafted NPCs at levels 1, 4, 8, 12, 16, and 20 making 180 unique characters with:    back stories    personality traits    role playing cues    unique items    unique spells    Including 8 generic NPCsRequires: An active subscription or 1-time standard or Ultimate license of Fantasy Grounds and the included Pathfinder ruleset.Conversion by: Danny Stratton
