
							About This Content
							A Fantasy Grounds II adventure module from Triple Ace GamesThose Magnificent Men In Their Flying Machines!In a bid to raise morale and emphasize the links between the islands, the Trade Council sponsors a competition. Intrepid captains and their crews must navigate their way between the major islands.The ships crew, which completes the race in the shortest time, will win fame and fortune across the known skies. But first they must survive, monsters, pirates, glowmadness, treachery, conspiracy, the tactics of their fellow competitors.The Race is a stand-alone adventure for the acclaimed Sundered Skies setting and the award winning Savage Worlds role-playing game.This product is an installable module for use with the Fantasy Ground II virtual tabletop. The Savage Worlds ruleset and a Full or Ultimate license of Fantasy Grounds II are required to use this product. The Sundered Skies setting module is highly recommended.Author: Markus FinsterFantasy Grounds II Conversion: Kevin Doswell
