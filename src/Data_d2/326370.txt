
							About This Content
							Experience a dual soundtrack by Chris Hülsbeck and Machinae Supremacy!This is the official soundtrack for Giana Sisters: Twisted Dreams and Giana Sisters: Twisted Dreams - Rise of the Owlverlord, a re-imagined, re-invented reincarnation of the classic Giana Sisters.Brand new game innovations include an awesome twist that let's the player switch between 2 personalities of Giana, cute and punk, which have different abilities and game-play mechanics. Not only Giana, but the whole world morphs from fairy tale to nightmare and back and so does the music: Chris Huelsbeck Productions & Fabian Del Priore created the compositions and Machinae Supremacy provided heavy metal renditions of the in-game tracks, that can also fade smoothly between cute and punk - you have to play the game to fully experience this effect. But here's the full soundtrack with all the tracks in both versions for you to enjoy if you're away from the game.You will find mp3 and FLAC files in your Giana Sisters Twisted Dreams folder in the Steam Directory:...Steam\steamapps\common\Giana Sisters Twisted Dreams\SoundtrackSimply go to C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Giana Sisters Twisted Dreams\SoundtrackNote, it may be in C:\Program Files\... or D:\Program Files\... depending on your Steam settings.Giana Sisters Twisted Dreams-------------------------------------------------------------------------1.
