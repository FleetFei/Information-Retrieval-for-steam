
							About This Content
							Composed by industry veteran Howard Mostrom, the Planetary Annihilation Original Soundtrack is a moving, sci-fi influenced orchestral score that juggles serenity, devastation, and exploration with soothing strings, soaring tones, and powerful percussion. 

Brought to life by an 80-piece orchestra and a full choir, the Planetary Annihilation Original Soundtrack weaves an emotional tapestry that speaks to both the creation and destruction seen in Planetary Annihilation.

MP3-format files as well as other media will be placed in your Planetary Annihilation folder in the Steam Directory: ...\Steam\SteamApps\common\Planetary Annihilation\Soundtrack DLC
