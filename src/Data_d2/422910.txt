
							About This Game
							Yes, this is your grandfather's Creeper World.  Now on Steam and with updated unit graphics!Imagine an enemy that is everywhere and moves like a giant, organic mass across the map.Imagine your base and your people surrounded by a blanket of crushing destruction from all directions. Your only hope, well what hope?Then you take the high ground and pound this enemy into oblivion. You've saved the day just so you can do it again and again. Do you have what it takes to save your great city and all of history?The Anniversary Edition includes new and shinier unit graphics, increased resolution background images, and the same awesomely high ration of strategic depth to game complexity.
