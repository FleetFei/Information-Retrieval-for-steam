
							About This Game
							Robot vs. Birds Zumbies depicts the spread of avian influenza virus through the infected dead birds that turned into zombies, a robot is built by survivors to eliminate that threat, your mission is to control the robot to remove the birds and prevent zombies proliferation of the virus. 
You must solve various puzzles to eliminate them. 

Various weapons and mechanics with many puzzles. 

More than 60 levels
