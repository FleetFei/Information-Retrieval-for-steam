
							About This Game
							Please read the Early Access description carefullyTOTM is a multiplayer action game with realtime strategy influences. Kill creeps and opponents, gain power, grab items. Be one step ahead of your enemies and choose the right combination of spells. You fight about strategic points on a large outdoor map.Compared to other MOBAs, TOTM features more action-oriented direct controls (WASD or Gamepad) as well as a third person camera view.Be sure to check the hardware requirements before making a purchase. This game requires 64 bit Windows 7 or 8 as well as a modern gaming GPU. It will not start on 32 bit systems or older graphic cards
