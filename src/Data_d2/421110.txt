
							About This Game
							Welcome to AWAKE, Mother nature is your biggest ennemy ! Your primary goal ? SURVIVE ! In AWAKE you are a shipwreck-survivor which managed to reach an island. Explore the island and tame the wilderness ! Find food and something to drink to stay alive, collect ressources and craft shelters, fireplaces, and many other things ! Please, remember that this game is in EARLY ACCESS, PLEASE BUY THIS GAME ONLY IF YOU WANT TO PARTICIPATE IN THE GAME DEVELOPMENT AND ONLY IF YOU ARE READY TO ENCOUNTER SOME BUGS.
