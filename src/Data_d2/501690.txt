
							About This Game
							Help Mr. Bravado retrieve the ancient artifact from Torch Cave and more importantly - help him get out of there alive!ABOUT TORCH CAVEJoin Derek Bravado as he explores 40 different caves with lots of dangerous traps that might get him killed in every moment! Use hooks to travel faster and jump higher or even save your life in some situations.Use fire weapons to strike down flying robots that were built centuries ago inside the Torch Cave and have awoken by the sound of Derek entering the cave.
