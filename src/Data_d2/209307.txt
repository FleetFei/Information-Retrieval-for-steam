
							About This Content
							Aggressive combination attacks; can explode the corpses of their enemies.

The harsh wastelands are home to the Tribe, a loose federation of fierce warriors led by powerful chieftains. The Tribe lives for battle; when one member falls, his comrades are enraged.

This add-on includes the Tribe team, 17 Tribe avatars, and a unique Tribe hat for Team Fortress 2 players.
