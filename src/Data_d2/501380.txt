
							About This Game
							"Alcatraz Island Lofts", an experimental VR short, is a dark comedy monologue set in 2025 San Franciso that imagines a rather cynical solution to San Francisco's housing crisis and NIMBY ("not in my back yard") dilemma: converting the historic prison on Alcatraz Island into over-priced lofts.  
Run length is 1 minute 30 seconds.
