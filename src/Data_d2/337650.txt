
							About This Game
							Soft Body is a meditative action game where you control two beautiful, gooey snakes at the same time. In the game, you paint the world with your body, and as you do so, the world changes around you and reveals new obstacles and challenges. Although the action is quite tense, the mood and aesthetic is contemplative and calm.100 levelsFour unique modes for all players: from simple bullet dodging to brain-splitting masochismNew Game+ option for both Soft Game and Hard Game: rethought mechanics and revamped levels for those seeking the brain-splitting challenges
