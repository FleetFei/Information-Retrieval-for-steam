
							About This Game
							A Twitch.tv integrated social drawing game. Viewers compete against each other to be the first to correctly guess what the streamer is drawing.As a Twitch streamer are you looking for ways to engage your viewers? Doodle WHAT?! is a fun and engaging way for you and your viewers to directly interact.The Features!Interactive social game-play experience utilizing Twitch chat. Allows viewers to compete against each other via chat. Multiple game play modes.Multiple word categoriesPersistent Leaderboard.Easily connect to your Twitch channel. Twitch.tv broadcasting built in. Works with any number of players.Customizable word database.
