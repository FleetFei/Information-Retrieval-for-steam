
							About This Game
							Reed - is a small creature, the latest creation of the old supercomputer. 
Old supercomputer dies, he has lost all of the cubes and he began to slow down... 
Without a supercomputer, around the game world will come to an end. 
Can you help him? 
Can you collect all of cubes?, scattered around the levels that are dangerous... 

--------------------------Reed-------------------------- 
-Sweet pixel art graphics. 
-Immersive atmosphere. 
-Exellent unobtrusive ambient. (music) 
-The classic platformer gameplay. (like on old consoles) 
-Full support for gamepad. 
-Many different dangers.
