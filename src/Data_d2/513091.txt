
							About This Content
							The ÖBB Sgns Hopper Pack recreates the modern and versatile style of multimodal freight vehicles increasingly used across Europe. Each wagon is capable of accepting hoppers of various types and sizes and which may carry a variety of loose materials. Their multimodal concept results in faster turnaround times and more flexible working arrangements for the hauliers.
