
							About This Game
							Astroderps is a fun puzzle game for all ages. The objective is to solve puzzles that guide your Astroderps to safety. You can move blocks to create a path, extinguish fires, build bridges, and deflect projectiles. Timing and dexterity is key to get your Astroderps safely from one portal to the next. 

There are multiple levels for users to solve, each more challenging than the previous one. Are you up for the challenge?
