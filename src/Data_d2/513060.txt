
							About This Game

Wills and Wonders brings you on an adventure through Earth's mythology as you embark on quests to capture creatures, find skills, and recruit new leaders. Build armies, take land, and assert your dominance over all of Midgard.You will choose one of five nations starting with a powerful ruler in a single territory.
