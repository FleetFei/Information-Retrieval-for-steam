
							About This Video
							Craig Robinson, Anna Kendrick, John Francis Daley, Rob Corddry, and Ken Jeong star in this raucous and outrageous comedy. Following The Rapture, Lindsey (Kendrick) and her boyfriend Ben (Daley) discover they've been left behind, doomed to a life of hell-on-earth. Things get worse when the Antichrist (Robinson) falls for Lindsey and decides to make her his bride. Now the couple must stop the wedding—and save the world—in this star-studded comedy of apocalyptic proportions.
