
							About This Game
							Join the mob and rule the city! Will you become a celebrity mobster, or rule from the shadows? Life of a Mobster is a thrilling interactive novel where your choices control the story. The game is entirely text-based—without graphics or sound effects—and fueled by the vast, unstoppable power of your imagination.Will you betray the five families to win the heart of a good-looking FBI agent? Will you end up as a prisoner, a senator, or FBI director? The choice is yours.
