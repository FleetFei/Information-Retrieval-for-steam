
							About This Content
							The LMS P3 Coaches Pack 01 recreates variants of LMS coaches in use from 1933 (Design Period 3), including the flush-sided coaches created by William Stanier in LMS maroon and Bloood & Custard liveries. It also includes the distinctive 6-Wheeled Full Brake coach in LMS maroon and BR Crimson. The LMS P3 Coaches Pack 01 includes the following variants: LMS P3 FirstLMS P3 Brake ThirdLMS P3 CompositeLMS 6-Wheeled Full Brake
