
							About This Game
							Spheritis is a 3D race & puzzle game where the player controls a ball which properties and special abilities can be changed.Explore the levels and find ways to pass the traps and barriers to reach the exit.Single player campaign:- 17 campaign levels.- 4 difficulties modes.- An unlockable extra game mode.- 8 ball types.- 7 specialball abilities.- 20+ Steam achievements.- Leader boards for all levels and game modes.- Steam cloud support for game saves.
