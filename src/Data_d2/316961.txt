
							About This Content
							The Black Shark: Republic campaign is a hand-crafted campaign that tells the story of you as a Black Shark attack helicopter pilot. The campaign is non-linear and has story arcs that branch based on your mission results.Features of the Black Shark 2: “Republic” Campaign:20 handmade missions with voice-oversHundreds of complex triggers that create an interactive combat environmentFully detailed mission briefings and mission maps Storyline briefings Please note that ownership of DCS: Black Shark 2 is required to play this campaign.
