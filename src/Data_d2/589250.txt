
							About This Game
							Step into the shiny shoes of superheroes and villains in PowersVR. An action game with a comic book aesthetic, which features a diverse cast of costumed vigilantes, each with their own unique superpower. Fly, shield and fireball your way through Metropolis island - a sandbox city where you get to collect and test out your powers, or experience the upcoming story mode, told through comic panels and speech bubbles.
