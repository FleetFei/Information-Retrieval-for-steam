
							About This Game
							Enter the dark space of GridVR and experience classic games in gripping VR style. Bring your reaction and creativity to a new level by shooting at objects in shorter periods of time.
Shoot flashing fields from all directions and experience all-new classic games like "Snake" or "SimonSays".
VR-Grid also stands for a minimalist design to create a certain retro feel in the VR world. This gives the classics the perfect setting.
VR-Grid is therefore perfect for VR beginners and arcade fans!
