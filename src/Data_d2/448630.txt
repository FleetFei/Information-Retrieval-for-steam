
							About This Game

Safe places are hard to come by; danger is everywhere! Do you choose to merely survive, or attempt to stop the epidemic in this territory?Main Features Original combination of tactical elements and Arcade gameplay Realistic survivor AI Wide range of level types and settings Built on the custom, acclaimed engine that powers AI: Rampage!
