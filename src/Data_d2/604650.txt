							Daring Tales of Adventure #05: Sky Pirates of the CaribbeanA Fantasy Grounds Adventure module from Triple Ace GamesDeep in the Bermuda Triangle trouble awaits our heroes!In recent months a large number of cargo planes and ships have gone missing whilst traveling through the mysterious area now known as the Bermuda Triangle.Reports of these disappearances have been kept secret from the wider populace in the hope of avoiding alarm. These missing planes and ships are not caused by aliens or even freak weather. This is simply piracy!Flying high above the cloud layer is a highly advanced flying airfield. It stays there using a variety of advanced engineering and weird science, it hovers undetected allowing the Sky Pirates to swoop on unsuspecting victims!Our heroes are thrust into the middle of this adventure when they are persuaded to take a vacation in Cuba. Once attacked by the Sky Pirates they discover the real reason for the missing ships and planes!Sky Pirates of the Caribbean is a high octane pulp adventure that takes your heroes on a wild ride in the clouds!Author: Paul Wiggy Wade-WilliamsFantasy Grounds Conversion: Clemens MeyerReleased on March 02, 2010. Designed for Fantasy Grounds version 2.9.9 and higher.Requirements: An active subscription or a one time purchase of a Fantasy Grounds Full or Ultimate license and a one time purchase of the Savage Worlds ruleset.
