
							About This Game
							Notice: The Demo is a pre-alpha version of Lighting End VR.Have you ever imagined dominating the light in VR?In the peaceful universe, there are some Seeds of Light. They are wrapped in Dark Flower, waiting for the light coming. “Lighting End VR” is a fun and aesthetic optical path puzzle VR game, with the vast universe as background, walking through the beautiful and cute planet. Players need to use the mirrors to change the light path from Light Planet, bypassing many obstacles to make it point to the Dark Flower, thus liberating the Seed of Light. In each levels, there are unique optical devices, Light Planet, Light Block Planets and Dark Flower. Light Planet is the starting point, and the Dark Flower is the terminal point. You need to drag the light indirectly to rotate the Light Planet and the optical devices, light up the Dark Flower, and liberate the Seed of Light hidden in the Dark Flower.■ Artistic Low Ploy style universe scene■ Tricky level design■ Unique optical device■ Full of Zen aura■ The full version will be based on chapters and levels.■ More colorful lights will be added.■ More optical divice will be added.
