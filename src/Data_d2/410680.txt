
							About This Game
							Angels That Kill is a dark, first person adventure game. It tells the story of two men as they uncover the truth behind a murder that has shaken the city. Play through both sides of the story and discover the truth behind the murder.Play as James Baley, a drunk and drug-ridden retired boxer who has lost everything and moved to Noll City to start again. Go through the first day, interact with the population and make decisions that could affect a number of people. Hide, run, explore, and discover while becoming a part in Noll City in this tragic time.In the demo you will be able to:Start exploring your options.Just because there is a story doesn't mean that you cant help make it, choose between right and wrong, make decisions you didn't mean to, and discover your own beginning to this story.Explore the world.It might not be the biggest game ever made, but it is packed full of secrets and goodies waiting to be discovered.Immerse in a noire inspired world.Every aspect of the game has been carefully detailed to appeal to any noire(or noir) lover.Change the world to your choosing.You have the ability to change the amount of rainfall, the amount of fog, the lighting, and more.
