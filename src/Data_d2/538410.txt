
							About This Game
							Oh no!
The chicken coop is getting too crowded! Chase down those chickens and toss them out of there before the coop gets overrun!

The chickens will run from you and hatch more and more chicks until they are free. If too many chickens are in the coop at once, you lose!
Make sure you put on your deodorant because this one will get ya all sweaty! :D
