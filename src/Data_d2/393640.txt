							Music composed by Dren McDonald The soundtrack features more than 40 minutes of original music composed and recorded exclusively for Gathering Sky. The score was recorded at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music with a small wind and string ensemble and vocalists as part of the Technology and Applied Composition program.
