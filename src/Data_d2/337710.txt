* Contents
Tkl Online (TO) is an MMO-style single player RPG created with RPG Maker VX.

You are a human in the world of Orbis Terrarum, where monsters and magic are real.

Be one who slaughters monsters with an arsenal of weapons.
Be one who can down monsters in one swoop with powerful magic casts.
Be one who develops skills to capture monsters.
Be one who buys and sells goods and supports other adventurers.

Orbis Terrarum is a free-roaming open world where you can become anything.
