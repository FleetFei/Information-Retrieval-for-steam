
							About This Game
							Dare to be scared!  After 30 years, Atari's classic groundbreaking game is back - with all-new scream-worthy chills and thrills!  With spooky enemies lurking around every corner, are you brave enough to enter the Haunted House?					   KEY FEATURES:					   Explore the dark depths of Graves Mansion - find your way through 20 dark and eerie levels - just don't let the ghosts send you running scared!                                  						     Uncover dark secrets - solve the mystery of what happened to your long-lost grandfather!
