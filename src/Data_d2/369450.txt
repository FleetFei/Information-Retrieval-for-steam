
							About This Content
							Guns and Goblins brings two new things to the world of Fortune's Tavern: Guns and Goblins!

Features:

 *Build new rooms in a dank cave so that Goblins will visit your tavern,
 *Build new Goblin specific tavern features,
 *Speak to 14 new Goblin guests
 *Work with the Goblin engineer to create a new weapon: the gun
 *Enjoy a new way of killing your enemies - with long range violent death!
 *Quest for parts to make your Gun even more powerful!
