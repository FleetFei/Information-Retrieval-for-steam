
							About This Game
							Our story takes place during a war of succession. The Empress’ murder has left a realm in flames with many hungry rivals ready to seize the throne by force. The Duke Ivan then calls together a prestigious council of six trusted advisors to restore order and end the conflict that set ablaze Ashan’s lands.Might & Magic® Heroes® VII makes a triumphant return bringing you the essence of turn based strategy gaming. Set in a fantasy universe with RPG progression with a strong story narrative, you will embark on a journey that you will never forget! Explore and conquer the enchanted world of AshanDevelop and manage your economyLead armies of legendary creatures to battleBuild up Heroes of Might & Magic with hundreds of abilities and powerful artifacts
