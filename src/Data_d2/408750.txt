
							About This Game

Incredibly difficult levels, bonuses, achievements, and of course rhythmic Starsphere music will give you a lot of fun (pain) to play.Features Continuous movement. Do not stop at nothing! Dynamic levels, a puzzle on the reaction, time and not only. Simple, but interesting game mechanics and control. No time to think! Jump!  Vertical and horizontal levels. Starsphere upgrades. Rhythmic and uplifting Starsphere music. Caution! The most difficult and boring game in the Universe.
