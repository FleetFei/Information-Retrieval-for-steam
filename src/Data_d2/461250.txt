
							About This Content
							"People are 'people' when they are 'needed.'"A princess that doesn't want to die. A warrior who doesn't want to live. Their lives cross paths amid the war and turmoil of Medieval Europe. The next chapter of the Narcissu 10th Anniversary Anthology further explores the theme of "Life and Death" in a self-contained side-story.A visual novel originally released only in Japan "Narcissu: Die Dritte Welt" by stage-nana, the chapter has finally been translated into English thanks to the Kickstarter support of fans of the series. This latest version now features re-recorded voice acting and upgraded artwork!Bonus material: "1993" Short StoryA continuation of the "1980" short story included with "Himeko's Epilogue" is included as a bonus feature in the Afterward
