
							About This Game
							Init. will test your ability to see through illusion and discover innovative and cerebral challenges that can be solved as simply as changing the way you perceive them.Features* Over 40 unique and challenging constructs* Bring along a friend and overcome special split-screen objective-based missions* Melt into an ethereal soundtrack and succumb to binaural manipulation* Use 'Root Access' to modify constructs and reveal secret areas by reconstructing the environment around you* Delve deeper into the mysterious Invertica system and discover a terrifying global agenda
