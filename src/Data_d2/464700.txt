
							About This Game
							The gods have trapped your sister. Prove yourself worthy to bring her back. Maui is a free action-adventure game based on Hawaiian mythology. The game features an innovative "stat grid" that lets you switch stats on the fly to face any situation. Features: Play the game completely for free--no microtransactions, no DLC, just a free game for you to playExplore a beautiful ancient Hawaiian islandInnovative "stat grid" lets you switch character stats instantly at any timeCollect offerings and face trials from the gods Narrative and mechanics drawn directly from Native Hawaiian culture and mythology
