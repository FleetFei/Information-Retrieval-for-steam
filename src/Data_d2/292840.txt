
							About This Game
							Climb in and help Pajama Sam control the Sock-O-Matic Mark III, the sock-sorting dream machine the makes chores fun!Concentration is the key as Sam tries to control that crazy contraption. Chutes, pushers, slides, and swing arms are just a few of the gadgets you'll need to guide Sam's socks into the baskets. The Sock-O-Matic even has a Custom Level Builder that allows kids to create their own puzzling levels!Challenging, arcade-style games for children of all ages.Brand new action with all the quality you'd expect from Humongous Entertainment.Save your game after each level and track the progress of up to 75 players.Junior Arcade games are jam packed with fast-and-furious game-play that satisfies kids' hunger for action and surprises!
