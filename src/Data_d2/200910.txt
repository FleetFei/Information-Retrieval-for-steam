
							About This Game
							Before the Echo combines elements of both rhythm and role-playing games to create an entirely unique experience. Join Ky on his ascent of a mysterious Tower, guided onward by a mysterious girl named Naia. Encounter deadly monsters and ridiculous characters, and discover the grand scheme behind Ky's mysterious imprisonment...that is, if you can survive the journey.						Key Features: 						Fully voice-acted, 10+ hour story mode						With music by Ronald Jenkees and Michael Wade Hamilton						Deep item synthesis and spell customization mechanics provide a full RPG experience						Full integration of Steam achievements and cloud saves
