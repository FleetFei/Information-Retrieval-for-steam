
							About This Game
							The year 2243. World War 3 has divided the world onto factions run by corporations. 
From now on, every conflict between the factions is resolved on the Arena. 
People fight there, for their faction and for their own life. Those people are the Gladiators of their own time. You are one of them. 

Neon Arena is a dynamic stylish top-down shooter in which you should face the horde of enemies who want to steal your energy spheres. 

Fight on the dynamically changing Arena for the ultimate prize. 
Unleash the power of different weapons onto your enemies. 
Cooperate with your allies to leave the Arena victorious and alive!
