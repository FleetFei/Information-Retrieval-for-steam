
							About This Game
							Colorful retro graphics meet a hilarious modern narrative in this charming and challenging point-and-click adventure! Neighborhood troublemaker Tick wants nothing more than to be a hero and earn the love of the girl of his dreams, Georgia McGorgeous. The only way is to pass the tests of Strength, Courage, and Wisdom to prove his worth and draw the legendary Sword of Blergh from the stone!
