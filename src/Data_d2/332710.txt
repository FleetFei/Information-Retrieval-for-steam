
							About This Game
							1Quest is a turn-based roguelike RPG where you play as a young adventurer who pursues an evil blood cult that has abducted several children, including the princess of the kingdom!You got 7 days before they can start their evil ritual to summon an ancient demon by sacrificing the children. Each region of the realm contains dungeons full of hostile creatures, but you will also find help and get various rewards depending on which path you take towards rescuing the children. But you need to hurry, if you want to save them all...Fight monsters, grab loot, become more powerful and prevent the blood cult from summoning the demon.Key featuresTurn based RPG5 races18 Differents classesMore than 150 class abilities and spells for charactersMore than 100 monsters20 Differents dungeons with specific rewardsMagic system based on spell schools and magic affinities for more diversity and replayability
