
							About This Content
							Play with the fate of Legends in a battle of wits, strategy and combat where lives are gambled on the turn of your cards. Defeat your challengers, expand and master your deck, earn rewards and customize your Legends as you write and re-write your story in the Chronicle.

The Foundation Pack is a great way to get the strongest possible start in Chronicle, with everything you need to get your deck-building and gameplay flying.

The Foundation Pack contains;
10 Booster Packs, each containing 5 cards
500 Platinum Ingots - premium currency allowing you to buy additional Booster Packs and enter Dungeoneering rounds
1 exclusive Foundation card back
500 Gem Shards, allowing you to craft your own cards

Note: Only one purchase of this DLC pack per account.
