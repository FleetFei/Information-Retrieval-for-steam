
							About This Series
							Sometimes coming up with ideas can be a little hard . Here we explore how we can use 3D packages to block out rough scenes in which we paint over them.
You only need a basic understanding of 3D to follow along for this one .


In this Tutorial you will get-

-2 Hour HD Video with commentary
-PSD / JPEG Files
-Maya/OBJ Files
-Bonus Sped Up Process Painting ( no audio )
