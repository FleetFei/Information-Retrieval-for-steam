
							About This Game
							Overgravity is a 2D multi-player space themed shooter.The main feature that distinguishes it from other shooters is the speed of the gameplay, not to mention the dozens of weapons and powerups.There are also six single-player modes which can be highly customized.Single-player:Normal modeUnstable mode (the map change during the match)Coins mode (every damage generate a coins explosion, who has the most coins wins)Vampire modeCapture the flag8 different TournamentsMulti-player:Normal matchTeam deathmatch
