
							About This Game
							"I'm the person you wish you never met."Sepia Tears is a romance visual novel. It's about a memory that a boy named Mark tries very hard to forget, and a girl named Myra who unearths it. Together, they search for a storybook romance and realize that love doesn't always bloom as neatly as it ought to. Perfect for fans of anime and VNs who want a classic story with a twist!Features:Vocal ending song 40,000 word story CG gallery containing game CGs, chibi CGs, and promo art
