
							About This Game
							To survive in the wilderness always seems to be something dangerous and scary. But in this game you'll stay alone with nature, which does not want to kill you. Wild animals are aggressive here, but they just defend their territory and themselves, and they never attack first. Animals will study you and your habits. Will you live in harmony with nature or will you kill it - depends only on you.▪ Achievements▪ Trading Cards▪ Emoticons
