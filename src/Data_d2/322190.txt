
							About This Game
							From the makers of SteamWorld Dig:In SteamWorld Heist, you command a steam-driven pirate crew in a series of epic tactical shootouts. It’s turn-based strategy with a twist: You manually aim the guns of your robots, allowing for insane skill shots and bullet-bouncing action!As the captain you will board, loot and shoot your way through enemy spaceships. Overcome the challenges of the vast frontier by upgrading your recruits with unique abilities, weapons – and even stylish hats!Key featuresStrategy focused on skill rather than chance Procedurally generated levels15-20 hours of playtime, followed by New Game+Kind to newcomers, deep and challenging for veteransSoundtrack by Steam Powered Giraffe
