
							About This Game
							New kind of adventure is a 3D adventure game with RPG elements. Mary hides in a wardrobe after her parents had a fight. She falls asleep. When Mary awakes, she discovers that she is in a beautiful, magical world with giant strawberries and magical druids!With her new friend Aquarius, Mary must find a way back to the real world!Features:Use Mary's new found powers and Aquarius' magical abilities to solve puzzles and complete quests.Collect Artefacts to unlock new magical powers and abilities.A huge vibrant world, with colourful wildlife and environments.Gather items and resources to trade with Druids.Original folk jam soundtrack.
