
							About This Content
							Inside this deck lurk over 90 new cards inspired by the bloodiest movies, novels and games to ever keep you up at night.  Use this deck by itself and pit classic monsters against slasher icons, or shuffle these cards with any SUPERFIGHT deck to add a devilish twist to your absurd arguments.  The Horror Deck even includes its own set of terrifying Location (Blue) and Scenario (Purple) cards!The Horror Deck comes complete with new Achievements!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Horror Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.
