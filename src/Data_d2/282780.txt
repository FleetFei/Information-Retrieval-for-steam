
							About This Game
							Stick 'Em Up 2: Paper Adventures is a run'n'gun/platform game of stick figure characters fighting through beautiful paper and card levels.Fly across city rooftops firing rockets from a helicopter, leap between pirate ships with a sword, race buggies on the moon, and more!Play with between 1 and 4 players with extra controllers or by connecting over your local networkPlay together in campaign mode, or fight against each other in VS and Team VS modesDon't have enough controllers for 4 people? Your friends can join your network game for free using Stick 'Em Up 2: Starter Edition, available for computers, smartphones, and tablets!
