
							About This Game
							Car Car Crash invites you to play with toy cars like you always imagined.  Peak around the corners of building as the cars zip by on the streets of the miniature towns and cities!  Stand tall to get a birds eye view to see it all!   Made for VR this table top experience is both a challenging game with levels and skill progression as well as an open environment where you can just play with the items around you.
