
							About This Content
							The Walking Dead Deck is an expansion to SUPERFIGHT that adds over 90 cards inspired by Robert Kirkman's hit comic book series.  All of your favorite survivors and villains are here, along with our darkest set of attributes yet.  Do not shuffle, dead inside.The Walking Dead Deck comes complete with new Achievements, Titles, and Art to unlock!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Walking Dead Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.
