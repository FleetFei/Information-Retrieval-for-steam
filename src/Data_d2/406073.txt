
							About This Content
							We spend much of our youth in schools. Whether historical, fantasy or modern, many games need that touch of academic setting.Classic School Tiles resource pack is composed of the essential details needed to create classrooms, libraries, study rooms and the dreaded principal's office. From a blackboard to messy student desks and more, this pack will really bring your school setting to life!Features:A smooth, painterly style like the one you'll find in Pirate Ship, Casino, Valentine's, Winter Wonderland, Sci Fi, Halloween, Evil Castle, Royal and Dungeon & Volcanoes tile packs.Environments can be used in both modern schools and medieval fantasy schools.Auto-tiles for floors (A2) and walls (A4), and a variety of objects in tiles B and C.Animated single and double doors, and more!
