
							About This Game
							Be a mage in virtual reality. Learn and cast powerful magic spells to fight off the golems, an army of mindless creatures left behind from the battles of the past. Fight with a variety of control schemes including: Vive Wands, Leap Motion, or Oculus Touch. Features:8 Unique and Customizable SpellsExplorable Overworld Unlockable Talents and AbilitiesChallenging Special Encounters Endless Arcade ModesLeap Motion and Tracked Controller SupportLeap Motion Note!:To play the game with the leap motion you need to switch to the Leap Motion Version. Right click the game in your library, click "Properties" then navigate to the "betas" tab and switch to the Leap Motion build beta.
