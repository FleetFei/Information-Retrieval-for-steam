
							About This Game
							Rail Adventures is a fast paced action/racing game in a minecart on railways, set in epic and beautiful 3D environments.
Navigate and shoot your way through challenges and obstacles in a game which combines elements of racing and shooting in a rollercoaster like environment.
Control your cart, shoot/destroy/avoid obstacles, collect items and power-ups and race your way to the finish line in a new and exciting gaming experience.
