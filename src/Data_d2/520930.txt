
							About This Game
							Pong It! VR is a virtual reality game for HTC Vive where you can enjoy a realistic ping pong simulation in sci-fi setting among the clouds!

Features:
- Improve your skills by playing against challenging AI opponents
- Choose the mode and difficulty that you like best
- Futuristic environment makes for an otherworldly ping pong experience
- 11 tracks of background music to choose from and Burnt Calories Counter for more fun

Four modes available:
- Practice: Improve your skills with no pressure and no limits
- Quick match: Match up with an AI opponent and challenge yourself to get the highest score
- Endless: Swing your racket like there is no tomorrow
- Whack-a-mole: Test your reaction speed and accuracy by hitting the indicated area as fast as possible

Come join us in the VR world, where nothing is impossible! 

We are working hard to release new exciting game modes and to improve look and feel of the game. If you have any ideas or suggestions, please feel free to leave a comment.
