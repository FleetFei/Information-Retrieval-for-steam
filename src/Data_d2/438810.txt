
							About This Content
							Tic-Toc-Tower is getting a visitor! A new challenger comes to take on the twisting tower and its maddening rooms.
From the far away kingdom of Elektropia, Teslakid seeks to traverse the great Tower of the Clock and find it's treasures. 

This DLC features  'Teslakid' from Teslagrad as a playable character.
He will be available to anyone who owns the game Teslagrad. So if you own the game Teslagrad, you won't need to buy the DLC.
