
							About This Game
							You were foolish enough to wander into the cursed ruins of the North. While looking for powerful artifacts and knowledge, you have awaken dark powers and their undead servants. They are coming!In Last Mage Standing you are a young mage, standing alone against a horde of undead. Your goal is to survive as long as possible - use your fireballs and weapons to defeat the attackers!The Last Mage Standing will get new features, spells and weapons with upcoming updates and free DLCs!
