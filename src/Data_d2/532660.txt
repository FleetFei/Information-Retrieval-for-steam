
							About This Game
							An Old School Fantasy FPS     You find yourself on a small floating island. A grumpy old man introduces you to your new gained powers. During the game you learn new spells by defeating increasingly difficult monsters. The ultimate goal is to defeat the dragon to be able to return to reality.      The Survival Mode throws 20 increasingly difficult waves of monsters at you. Fight for a highscore in the online leaderboard by surviving as long as possible!Key Features	5 versatile spells as weapons  	10+ enemy types in two unique environments	Fully dynamic lighting and weather	No time wasted! Short, polished experience
