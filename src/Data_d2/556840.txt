
							About This Game
							“The Dome” is an online open world robotics game with RPG and survival elements.  The game features a variety of attack skills, a very exciting multiplayer driving experience, and large-scale robot combat.Key FeaturesAcquisition of resources, construction, hunting, melee and ranged weapon skills, vehicles and large robots in addition to other means of transport, and two factions to align yourself with and engage in PvP.Thanks to Lelouch for translating.
