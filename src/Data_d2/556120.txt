
							About This Game
							"Reptilians Must Die" - an amazing adventure game with a gripping storyline, based on the right patriotic education and spiritual scrapie fixed!2015. Mikhail Ivanovich carry out his duties of president of Russia, and suddenly at the height of work day aliens call. They say that they are ready to destroy the humanity and only MI can save the planet from destruction. Sure MI accepts the challenge. But not alone - true friends, women that are in love with him and of course people of Russia. Journey to the future, space flight, meeting with Stalin - that's what will wait for you in new original game "Reptilians Must Die"!
