
							About This Game

Let your imagination be your guide! Design, control, and ride more than 10 unique attractions!Virtual Rides III turns you into a showman! Whether you change the color of the components, the components themselves, or the decoration – it's all in your hands!Use lighting, effect equipment, fog machines, and stroboscopes to perfectly stage your fairground attractions!
