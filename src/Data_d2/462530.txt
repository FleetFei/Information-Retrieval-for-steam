
							About This Game
							8i brings virtual experiences to life with the highest fidelity, most realistic volumetric video of humans. A truly immersive experience, volumetric VR gives you a sense of presence and freedom to move through space and time, opening up worlds of possibilities.

8i volumetric VR experience features:

True presence: Step into the story and feel like you’re not just watching someone, you’re with them.
Freedom of movement: Walk completely around the person you’re watching as if you’re in the same room, and choose your own perspective.
Experience it anywhere: View on virtual reality headset, mobile and on the web
