
							About This Game
							INO is a short Adventure/Horror story driven game.It tells the story of Kaede and Hitaki, two classmates that get involved with a mysterious girl called Ino. Follow the story as they try to figure out who she is while they try to escape from being murdered.Features:- Unique maps with original graphic assets- Chase system to keep you on your toes- Puzzles to test your wits- Character driven story
