
							About This Content
							Revisit the two-dimensional battlefields of Swords and Soldiers in a brand-new single-player campaign!
Take control of Chief Meat and command his troops on their journey to the annual Super Saucy Sausage Fest holding a grand prize of 1000 cows.  Bolster your ranks along the way and battle old enemies intent on sabotaging your efforts on winning the grand prize.
Also included with this downloadable content is an improved challenge mode: Boulder XL!
