							S.K.I.L.L. - Special Force 2 - Recruit PackThe high-value Recruit Pack offers these cool (and useful) items for S.K.I.L.L. – Special Force 2:Camo Spray Giganteus FXUse this spray on your weapon to permanently cover it in a spooky yet spectacular animation.G36C EOTech Giganteus FXYou receive the popular G36C assault rifle with the very useful EOTech visor for seven days – already coated in the Giganteus FX camo spray. As with all temporary weapons in S.K.I.L.L. – Special Force 2, this weapon does not need to be repaired over the seven days that it is available: you save a lot of SP!MP5KOne of the most popular submachine guns in S.K.I.L.L. is the MP5K – this pack secures you the permanent variant of the gun. The MP5K is perfectly suited for all close combat situations, for example in team deathmatch on the Hangar map!EXP Booster (100%)For 1 day you will get double the amount of experience points after each match when you activate this booster. Perfect for ascending the ranks quickly!M14 TH3This grenade is your alternative to the standard M67 grenade. The M14 TH3 sets everything within metres alight after detonation, lasting for a few seconds. It's the perfect method for stopping your enemies, especially when they need to defuse the bomb in Blasting!How do I unlock my DLC in the game?After purchase of a DLC pack, you will be presented with a Steam product key. Log in to S.K.I.L.L., head to the shop and go to 'Top up Cash'. This will open a window with a button labelled 'Gameforge Coupon'. Click this and follow the directions to redeem your Steam product key. The contents of the DLC pack will then appear in your inventory in the 'Gifts' section.
