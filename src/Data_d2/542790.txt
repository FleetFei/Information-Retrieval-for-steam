
							About This Game
							RacetronicVR: Hovercar racing, laser shooting, fully motion controlled madness! RacetronicVR is a single player time trail racing game built exclusively for VR. Built for use with motion controls only, it supports the HTC Vive.Steer with one hand and shoot with the other. VR racing as it should be: with motion controllers. 3 galactic tracks with a bevy of obstacles, shortcuts, power ups and secrets keep the good times racing. Local leader boards for track times, become the fastest racer in the galaxy.
