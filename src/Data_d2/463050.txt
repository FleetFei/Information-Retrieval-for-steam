
							About This Game
							This is a quest based on Edgar Allan Poe’s “The pit and the pendulum”. A pit and a pendulum await you…A story about a man who survived suffering and being tortured by the inquisition in a Spanish prison. The main character was convicted for some crime and underwent torture. The court sentenced him to the death penalty. The convict woke up in complete darkness. He thought that he died and was in the afterlife.. The prisoner gathered all his strength and slowly got up, moved forward, and ran into a wall. He remembered horrible tales of the inquisition’s traps. This was on huge stone cell with traps.Special features:
Beautiful professional graphics.The plot is based on a classic novel.Quality sound accompaniment.Historically accurate clothes and architecture.
