
							About This Content
							Chandra Nalaar’s fully unlocked “Born of Flame” red deck. Chandra Nalaar is a hot-tempered pyromancer who wields powerful fire magic against her foes. The tools of Chandra’s quest for freedom include elementals and phoenixes, the embodiments of red mana. Of course, Chandra also has a formidable array of spells at her disposal that you can use to rain fiery destruction down on your foes! Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013.
