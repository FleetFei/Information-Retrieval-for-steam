
							About This Game
							Are you after a new VR experience?Something that tests your skills without leaving you sweating in fear?Then try Hand Eye Cubination, the first third person VR platformer on Steam!You heard that right, third person! Platformer!But how you ask? Why? What even?Simply put, you control two cubes through the direction your hands are pointing.Your goal is to guide your cubes through a maze of challenges!Did I mention you can't see your hands? Because that's a pretty important feature.You have to rely 100% on your human senses to successfully navigate through the world!Come give it a crack and put your hand eye coordination to the test!Hand Eye Cubination!
