
							About This Game
							You are a Dragon Leader able to control dragons. Drax has been captured by an evil wizard and 
only you can aid him in returning to his home!

Collect various power-ups unlocking new abilities by pointing & clicking a dragon. 

Solve puzzles to progress, jump right into the 1:1 scale action using magic wands, bows, explosives to defeat your enemies!

Search the environment for coins or items.

Help Drax by leading his way through a magical fantasy world full of dangers!

GAME KEY FEATURES :

- turn your room into a virtual adventure
- navigate dragons through various levels with point & click movement
- change perspectives from toybox to FPP 1:1 scale
- solve puzzles to progress
- collect coins for points and items in the game world
- take your time to explore each level!
