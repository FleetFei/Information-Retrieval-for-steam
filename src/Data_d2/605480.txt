							Daring Tales of Adventure #04: The Talons of Lo-PengA Fantasy Grounds Adventure module from Triple Ace GamesIn 1935 the heroes uncovered part of an ancient Chinese artefact in the mountains of Tibet, an artefact sought by a Chinese mystic by the name of Lo-Peng.A year later in someone has stolen the relic from Boston Museum. A strange Chinese pictogram left at the crime scene leads the adventurers on rollercoaster ride from Boston to Afghanistan, onto Venice, and finally to China and the tomb of the first Emperor.Martial artists, ancient Chinese warriors with supernatural powers, deadly traps, and fierce Afghan tribesmen stand between the heroes and victory. Can the heroes escape the Talons of Lo-Peng?Author: Paul Wiggy Wade-WilliamsFantasy Grounds Conversion: Clemens MeyerReleased on March 02, 2010. Designed for Fantasy Grounds version 2.9.9 and higher.Requirements: An active subscription or a Fantasy Grounds Full or Ultimate license and a one time purchase of the Savage Worlds ruleset.
