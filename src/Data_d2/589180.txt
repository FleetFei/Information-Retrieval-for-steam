
							About This Game
							Defuser VR is an easy to pickup bomb defusing game set against the clock.1) Choose how many bombs you think you can defuse2) Flip the bombs to their green side to stop their timer3) Watch out, if you let a bomb go off, it could knock over your defused bomb, restarting its timer! Choose how many bombs to challenge yourself with and try to beat your high-score. Height adjustable play surface, just grab the handle to choose your play height.
