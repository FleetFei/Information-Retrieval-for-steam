
							About This Video
							A house with a terrifying secret is the setting for this Japanese horror film. A young nurse arrives at the house to find that two brutal murders have taken place there, and now the house is cursed, set to exact revenge on any and all who set foot inside it. Soon, the nurse may be the next in a long line of bloody deaths at the hands of the vengeful spirits who are out to make things right, once and for all.
