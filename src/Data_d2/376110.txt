
							About This Game
							Black Island is a classic adventure game with cinematic storytelling. Gather items, solve puzzles and throw yourself into the darkest of mysteries. If you are a lover of adventure games - Black Island is for you!

A man finds himself on a mysterious island with a splitting headache and no memories. Last night’s drinking got out of hand – again, or at least that’s what he thinks has happened. But as the day goes on the harsh truth starts to dawn on him.

Black Island is a story of a man and his struggle of survival. It is a story of what happens when man tries to play god. There’s nothing scarier than a man who is driven by his possessive belief in utopia.

Features:

- Classic point and click adventure game with a strong story
- Several puzzles to solve
- Four videos which unfold the story
- A trilling atmosphere of mystery and nightmares  
- Beautiful graphics and real life situations
- Voice over, sound and music which create a surreal and mysterious soundscape

This is the first part of Black Island. We plan to release sequels in the future. Please give us feedback; we would really like to get your opinion:)
