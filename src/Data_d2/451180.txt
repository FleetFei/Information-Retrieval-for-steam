
							About This Game
							Metris Soccer, is an arcade street football(soccer) game inspired by classic games from the early Fifa street series . Take to the streets and play in locations all over the world, as you try to become the best. Score amazing goals, pull off epic tricks,  and taunt with style as you embarrass your opponents. Features: Play 3 v 3 matches in classic street football style10+ Tricks, which will let you build up your skillmeter! Once your skillmeter is full, unleash a devastating SkillBreaker, which depending on how many victims you claim, will end a match!Diverse and fun soundtrack, with each Arena having custom themes.Local Co op Dynamic Tournaments : A singleplayer/local coop game mode with randomly generated objectives, so each playthrough is different.Player Customization: Represent yourself on the streets with your own personal style.Unique Arenas inspired by real world locations!
