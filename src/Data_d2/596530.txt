
							About This Game
							A nostalgic RPG experience, Chaos Drift features a 20+ hour soul-gripping storyline complete with moments that will make you belly-laugh and others that will make you cry. Complete with a customizable stat system, a socket-based skill system, hidden bosses, unique treasures, and unforgettable characters, Chaos Drift is an RPG that will make you think of old favorites such as Final Fantasy 6 or Chrono Trigger. As you trek through the past, present, and future to stop Chaos from overtaking the timeline, you will encounter mysteries that cause your characters to come face to face with dark truths that will shape each of their realities.
