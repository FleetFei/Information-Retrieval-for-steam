
							About This Content
							1918. The First World War is nearing its end - the Allies are preparing for the final assault on German positions. The town of Cambrai becomes an important strategic point for the second time since 1917 - the Germans, entrenched at the town are targeted by a steel armada of hundreds of tanks.
In this multiplayer addition, you can take the command of the British forces, and break through the German defenses, using the power of armored forces combined with infantry attack.  
You can also take the side of Germany and try to hold back the onslaught of the Entente. Despite the lack of heavy weapons, you will have the best infantry at your disposal -  veterans of trench warfare, as well as a powerful defense system.
