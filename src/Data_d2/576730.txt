
							About This Game
							In this game you will control little white spirit which is trying to get back his mask, who abandon him. You will face traps, bosses and another dangerous obstacles in beautiful animated world. Reflex, fast decisions and great mouse control are keys in this game. Slideshow intro videos will get you into story and won't let you leave main character lonely.Features:- Mouse control - 30 levels - 3 bosses - Shop- 6 "slideshow" video - 14 soundtracks - 2 ability of the spirit
