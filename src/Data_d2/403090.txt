
							About This Game
							Rebuild the devastated islands as you take control of the Rescue Team in this fantastic Time Management game! After a terrifying tornado leaves storms through the islands, it's up to the Rescue Team to save the day and save lives. Use strategy to figure out the quickest way to complete your goals, and then race against the clock to remove debris, repair bridges, put out fires, and protect the civilians in Rescue Team 2!Protect the civilians!Race against the clock!Rebuild the devastated islands!
