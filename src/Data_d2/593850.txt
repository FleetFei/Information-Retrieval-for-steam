
							About This Game
							Davyria is an action role-playing game with a well-designed and challenging combat system. You play an Eternal Warrior wandering the barren island of Davyria.Features A skill based and challenging combat system Dark Comic Graphics A variety of different weapons (greatswords, axes, swords, hammers and more) Different npcs with which you can speak, trade or solve quests for them Wander through a barren land divided by the conflict between two incredibly powerful beings Discover an epic story line and get special items by finishing side quests
