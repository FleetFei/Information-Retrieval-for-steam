
							About This Game
							Take a deep breath and dive to live an epic journey through the depths ! 

"UnderWater Adventure" is a 3d action game, with a little part of puzzle. 
Take your little sub,collect the gold ingot and go back to the surface. 
But watch out from all the dangers of the abyss ... 

- Use your grapping hook to help you in your journey. 
- Active mine and use barrels with their own properties to make your way. 
- Resolve puzzle and mechanisms 
- Score bonus for speed 
- Difficulty rises gradually. 
- Replayable levels (when game finish)
