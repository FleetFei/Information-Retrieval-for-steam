
							About This Game
							Lead your civilization of Orc Children, stranded on a planet.
Build infrastructure, explore continents, and instruct your peers.

Children of Orc mixes the point-and-click adventure game with real-time-strategy.
You control one single Orc and will have to instruct your tribe on how to explore and exploit your habitat.

Children of Orc requires an AVX capable processor (2011 or later.)
