
							About This Game
							Chess Knight 2 is the classic board game of Chess reimagined for the casual gamer looking to learn and to grow with the most popular game in history. A medieval world of strategy and tactics await you to engage in epic battles of wits as you master the moves and the strategies in a conquest to claim the kingdom of Chess. Learn to play in this fun, colorful and intuitive download offering multiple levels of difficulty. You can play in top-down 2D or fully animated 3D mode in a challenge against the computer or a good friend or relative.
