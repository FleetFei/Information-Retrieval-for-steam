
							About This Content
							As one of the most powerful beings in the Multiverse, you’re always seeking new magic to expand your mastery and defeat your foes. This Duels of the Planeswalkers 2012 deck pack includes two brand-new decks for Campaign and Multiplayer play. “Dark Heavens” is a white and black deck that combines the supernatural powers of Angels and Demons to punish the opposition. “Forest’s Fury” is a green deck that uses the towering might of Treefolk and the awesome power of nature to stomp on any who stand in your way!
