
							About This Game
							Explore a dangerous planet that's different every time and try to survive! The Galactic Union's job for you is clear: research the alien wildlife -- for science! However, in this case, researching may include lasers, explosions, hungry aliens, space sushi, and other similarly delicious-or-terrifying hazards. In-between missions, use scrap metal you've collected to upgrade your stats.Whether you survive or die, you'll come away better than you started!Features: Completely procedurally generated levels 200+ items, from swords to cookies to grenades Alien monsters to fight or befriendEveryone gets the same Daily Challenge - compare your progress with your friendsClass system - each clone skin has a different advantagePet cloning - maintain your companions' DNA in their own clone vats
