
							About This Game
							Savana is a narrative point and click game where you play as a young girl from a remote village.The narration of the game tells different story lines from a real life experience of growing up in Mozambique. You play as a young girl called Savana and see first-hand what a typical day in her life is like. Helana, project lead, spent her formative years in Africa. During the game she describes her first hand experiences as the game draws out towards its tragic end for Savana.Studio Message/CreatorsHelana Santos Hannah PayneMichael Duwe Ollie ClarkeChris Randle
