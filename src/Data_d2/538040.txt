
							About This Game
							Dante's Forest is an immersive VR action horror game built for the HTC Vive. Alone in a haunted forest you find yourself surrounded by wave after wave of ghostly creatures. By pure chance you discover that the beam of your flashlight will vaporize the sinister specters... for a while.

How many waves of ghosts can you survive?
