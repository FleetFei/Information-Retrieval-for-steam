
							About This Game
							Dragon Rider is a VR Compatible intense multiplayer arena game set in the skies on the back of a Dragon! Find your friends online, or make new enemies, and fight in a 4-player free for all.FeaturesCustomize your dragon's abilities to give you an edge against your opponents.Multiplayer online areas allow for playing against your friends, or anyone you happen to meet.Single player mode to practice your aim against the AI Dragon Riders.Fully supported in Oculus VR. Really feel like you're riding your Dragon!Gain experience from your online combats to improve your skills.
