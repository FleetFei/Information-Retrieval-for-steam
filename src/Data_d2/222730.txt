
							About This Game
							In Reus, you control powerful giants that help you shape the planet to your will. You can create mountains, oceans, forests and more. Enrich your planet with plants, minerals and animal life. There is only one thing on the planet that you do not control: mankind, with all their virtues and and all their vices. You can shape their world, but not their will. Provide for them and they may thrive. Give them too much, and their greed may gain the upper hand.Key FeaturesControl four mighty giants, each with their own unique abilities Terra-form the planet to your will, experiment with different terrain types A complex system of upgrades and synergies allows for endless styles of play Observe humanity, let your giants praise or punish them Enjoy an interesting art style and a strong soundtrack Enrich the planet with over 100 plants, animals and minerals Unlock new content by helping humanity achieve numerous developments
