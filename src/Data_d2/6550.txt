
							About This Game
							Dante's past is now revealed as Devil May Cry returns to its roots.  Master Dante's multiple fighting styles while battling never before seen demons and new characters as you fight your way towards a brutal confrontation with Dante's mysterious twin brother, Vergil.					Choose Dante's fighting style - maximize the impact of close-up blade attacks, or blow enemies away from a distance. 					Huge arsenal of weapons - handguns, shotguns, swords, and more. 					A horde of demonic enemies - more ferocious than ever - with powerful weapons and abilities to transform and fly.
