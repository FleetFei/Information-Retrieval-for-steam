
							About This Game
							Ghost Cleaner is a combination of arcade game and puzzle game. At first glance it is very straightforward, as you use a gun to destroy pink bricks. But the game requires you to use both strategy and tactics to finish every level. Try it once, and you'll be coming back for more! Physics BasedLots of variety across all the levels.100 levels to complete!Low price for fun game that's easy to play.
