
							About This Game
							Race downhill while performing aerial acrobatics in Escargot Kart, the original rolling platformer. Creatively rocket, jump and parachute your way across dozens of virtual physics playgrounds. Land a satisfying quadruple backflip or watch your hero somersault downhill after a crash. Unlock more characters and vehicles as you master each world. Earn coins for each flip and exchange them for power-ups. Mix and match equipment to jump higher, fly farther and crash harder. Complete hundreds of action packed challenges Unlock and combine equipment to increase your abilities Earn achievements landing spectacular stunts
