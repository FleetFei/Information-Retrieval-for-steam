
							About This Game
							Become an elite pilot of special Robot Squad.
Use specialized, remotely controlled robots, which you will control yourself.
Deal with the hardest missions which can't be performed directly by people due to the conditions.
Human life and safety is in your hands!

Game features:
- 4 different robots: 2 terrestrial, aerial and underwater
- 6 training missions
- 16 story missions which take place all over the world, in different settings
- 10 different accessories mounted on robots
- plenty of gameplay elements like: guards, explosives, mechanisms
- upgrading parameters of robots and accessories
- different types of missions: rescue, spy, exploration, military
