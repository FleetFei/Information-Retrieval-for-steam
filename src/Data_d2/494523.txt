
							About This Content
							Intitally designed by Czech Aircraft Works and still in production with Czech Sport Aircraft, the CSA SportCruiser is a low-wing two-seat aircraft with a single engine and tricycle undercarriage. Engines up to 120 horsepower can be fitted, giving this light-sport/ultralight aircraft a possible cruising speed of 133mph (214km/h) and a range up to 630 miles (1,014km). Developed by Alabeo, the CSA SportCruiser is a fast, light aircraft which is incredibly pleasant to fly.
