
							About This Game
							Control the rescue workers who rebuild three islands in the wake of devastating weather! Remove debris to free trapped workers in Rescue Team! Repair damaged restaurants, houses, and sawmills to produce necessary resources. Race against the clock to collect gems, put out fires, and save stranded swimmers! Rescue Team is fun, gripping, and unlike any other game you have played! Key Game Features:  60 challenging levels on three islandsInteractive tutorialHours of catching gameplayAdditional house-repair mini-game
