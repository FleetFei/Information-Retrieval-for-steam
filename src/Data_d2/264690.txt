
							About This Game
							A roguelite deckbuilding adventure game about magical coins. You play as a lootmancer, who can unlock the hidden power inside of coins and use them in magical duels. The loots you take from chests and enemies also become your next moves, so plan carefully!FeaturesRoguelike structure: randomly generated levels and permanent death!19 unlockable character classes with unique gameplay!One of those classes is a monkey!
