
							About This Content
							This addition to multiplayer functionality, includes three new competitive modes and six maps, for those modes.
In the Moba, you operate only vehicles to support allied infantry and, earn points to buy more powerful units. In the Rebellion mode, you need to break through to the flag held by the rebels, in the city, before the enemy reaches it. New maps: Fortress, Streets of Europe, France in the snow, Factory 113, Station.
