
							About This Game
							Exercise to your own music!  Punch, dodge, and dance around the ring. BeatBoxer is a VR boxing game that puts you in the spotlight of your own boxing match. Play any song from Youtube or use Airplay to play a continuous stream of music from Spotify, Pandora, iTunes, or your favorite music app.Train up from a feather weight, to world champ with increasing difficulty.  Dip left and right to avoid dodges while executing upper cuts and jabs to the rhythm of your music.  Immerse yourself and dance around the ring in Room-Scale VR.
