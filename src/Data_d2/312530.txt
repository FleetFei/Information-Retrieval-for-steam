
							About This Game

Win over the crowd and gain a following by blasting your feathered friends with Shotguns, Net Guns, Mind Control Rays, Saxophones, Magnet Guns, and pretty much anything else a duck could use as a weapon. One hit and you're roasted. This is DUCK GAME. Don't blink. 2 to 4 PLAYERS in either Local Multiplayer Couch Combat, or NEW Online Play!
