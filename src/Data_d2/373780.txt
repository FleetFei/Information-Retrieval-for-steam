							Super Chibi Knight is a sweeping classic action adventure game "By Dad and Daughter" with awesome gameplay, memorable characters, and adorable voice acting. The soundtrack has played a critical role in the game since day one! Composer Brian Holmes poured his heart and soul into over 70 tracks with almost 2 HOURS of music! It is our hope that this soundtrack might inspire listeners to create their own music and video games.
