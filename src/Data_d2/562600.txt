
							About This Game
							A slider puzzle is the one where the aim is to sort the given pieces, using a blank slot for movement.

Our features:
★ 8 (3x3), 15 (4x4), 24 (5x5) and 35 (6x6) block boards
★ 9 different theme
★ 180 pictures to solve
★ Guaranteed solution for all puzzles
★ Lightweight & supports SD Card install
★ Minimal device permissions
★ Numbers at the tile's corner to help

Slider puzzles help to improve the thinking power, agility, hand-eye coordination and patience of children.
