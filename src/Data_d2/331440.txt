
							About This Game
							bit Dungeon II is fast action-adventure game with a giant overworld to explore. You are a spirit in an undead world of demons. Your "loved one's " grave has been desecrated. Fight through these corrupted lands, and bring peace to her soul. The dungeons are randomly placed in the overworld, changing your experience each play through. Features: Permadeath You get one soul, and only once chance to retrieve it when you die. Steam Play Support. Each weapon type has a unique power attack including ranged, and magic weapons. Level up based on which weapon you use. Randomly generated items. Giant difficult dungeon bosses. Enemies with disgusting faces. Original Chip Bit music by Stress_tn. Controller Support. Once you beat the game it starts over, play forever, become stronger then... a god.
