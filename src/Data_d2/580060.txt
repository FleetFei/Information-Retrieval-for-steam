
							About This Game
							Cut Cut Buffet is a slicing simulator for the ages, cut your way through mounds of food and multiple game modes. Can you stand the heat of the kitchen?


Features:

- 3 game modes with more on the way
- 2 difficulties per mode
- Realistic cutting mechanic
- Cut all kinds of foods
- Fish
- Immersive restaurant environment
- Multiple Weapons
- Developer updates

Game Modes: 

1. Survival Mode - Cut as long as possible and avoid the bombs
2. Time Mode - Cut as much as possible while avoiding bombs
3. Practice Mode - Hone your skills in a relaxed infinite setting
