
							About This Game
							Golf Pro VR is a fun and challenging golf game designed from the ground up for virtual reality. Grab your club and step on to the greens in a one of a kind golf experience!

Practice your game at the state of the art practice facility which features 50+ acres of grounds including 5 practice greens, over 15 targets and various hazards to help you hone your game. The initial course, Hallerbos, takes golfers on a journey over rolling hills and through tree lined fairways before finishing at it's signature 18th hole.

Start your virtually real pro golf career today!
