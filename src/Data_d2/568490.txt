
							About This Game
							A cute and quirky classic adventure platformer about a Frog named Hoppy searching for his frog friend Jumpy.

Features a large variety of gimmicks, enemies, obstacles.

Unique and challenging boss fights.

Equip charms to alter your play style.

It has hats.

For the classic hardcore platforming gamer, created in a little over 4 years by a single developer.
