
							About This Game
							Protect the universe from the evil Alien robots terror-bent on eliminating the Earth and all of Humanity! Take control of the ship and blast the hell out of anything that moves and if it doesn’t move – blast it anyway to be sure. Collect coins to get points and use those points to get power-ups.  Choose wisely, the wrong power-up at the wrong time is the difference between a high-score and having to press ‘Play’ again. You were warned! Key featuresEvil Alien Robots terror-bent on destroying you and everyone else.Semi-procedural game generation. Never play exactly the same game twice.Super hard gameplay.Super addictive gameplay.Great training in mastering your blink reflex.Source code available to buy so you can see how it was made.
