However, difficult times are approaching in lands not too far away... Weeks after disappearing Scarllet and Wyatt are found in Dark Azulel, a land forgotten by the government, separated islands governed by no one. A mysterious and dangerous sect called Black Scepters are pursuing Scarllet for unknown purposes... could it be related to Scarllet's new found gift?The heroes are back!
