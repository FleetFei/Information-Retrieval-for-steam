
							About This Game
							Battle it out on the proving grounds and get ready for Future Wars.					Similar to classic titles such as Advance Wars and Battle Isle, the game takes strategy gaming away from the micro-management overkill that it has become and using simple, proven rock-paper-scissors principles, reopens the genre to everyone.					With a modern 3D engine providing the graphics, a proven, easy to learn, but difficult to master set of rules surrounded by an engaging plot and challenging gameplay, the game will appeal to newcomers and hard-core gamers alike.					Also available is unlimited access and infinite possibilities to mod the game through open data structure, open scripts and an easy-to-use editor.FeaturesModern 3D graphics & proven gameplay embedded in an engaging and sometimes, humorous plot					Infinite modding possibilities through open data structure, open scripts and editor					Novel and unique game modes such as AI vs. AI or play-by-email					Rock-paper-scissors principle makes Future Wars easy to learn but difficult to master					In-game achievements allows for hours of replay to acquire top honors
