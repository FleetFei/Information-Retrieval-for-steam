
							About This Content
							Whether you're on a lookout for futuristic humanoids, or in need of some android characters, Futuristic Characters Pack has a character or two for you.Combining the widely-spread and popular RTP style for character sprites, this pack can find a place in many environments. The inclusion of custom facesets keeps this pack fresh and exciting. A collaboration between Harmonic and Hirei, this fun pack is a must-have!Features:18 humanoid characters, with matching character sprites and facesets, as well as a base for child characters4 android characters, with matching sprites and facesets2 service robots, with 4-direction walking animationsFamiliar RTP style for characters and a modern anime style for facesets
