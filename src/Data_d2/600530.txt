
							About This Game
							Kautic is an action platform game where villagers from a little Town have been petrified and now Kautic, the Bald Wizard, has the hard mission of saving them, but he will need to venture into a world controlled by evil creatures.Features     High difficulty like in the classic games of Super Nintendo     Boss battles     27 Different enemies     Permadeath     Pixel art     Easter eggs     Choose your way through the game     Without dialogue and texts, anyone can play     Different biomes     Full controller support
