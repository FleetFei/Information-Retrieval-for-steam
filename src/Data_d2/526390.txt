
							About This Game
							The year is 2067Planet Earth is no longer suitable for life due to radiation and numerous ecological disasters. The latest catastrophe forces the remaining people to escape in a rush. Due to computer malfunction, the last cryocapsule was left on Earth. You wake up after 2 years only to discover you are the last remaining human: no friends, no family and a very hostile environment... Your only hope is to get a spaceship and escapeInevitable Path is a puzzle-adventure platformer game. Collect the parts of the spaceship, keep an eye on vital parameters and try to survive on a dying planet, which was once your home.
