
							About This Game

With combined forces you and your team will be able to activate the ultimate team ability: The ultra heavy-duty polygon bomb. Fine-tune your strategies or just let hell loose and ascend into the poly-olympus.You can control your heroes with your gamepads. Haven’t got any/enough?
