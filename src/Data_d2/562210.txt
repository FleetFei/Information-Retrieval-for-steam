
							About This Game
							From the creator of Pythagoria & Aurora NightsDo you want to learn fascinating facts about the history of Ancient Russia? Take a quiz to test your knowledge, and learn some interesting facts along the way. The modern people of Russia, Ukraine, and Belarus all claim Ancient Rus' as their cultural ancestors. So preserving history is important, and this helps to make learning that history as enjoyable as possible.This game is split into missions, which consists of sequences of questions. Most of the questions include additional information and in-depth explanations. Each mission includes a brief, unique description to help players get prepared.The content is carefully crafted by professional historians to ensure it represents a comprehensive up-to-date overview of events, culture and influential personalities from early Russian history.
