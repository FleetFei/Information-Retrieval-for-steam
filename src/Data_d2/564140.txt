
							About This Game
							The time is now.

You are a newly appointed defense network engineer in charge of keeping the company of your choosing from harmful outside DDoS attacks. 
​Rival companies want to take your servers offline and its your job to make sure they don't. 
Expand your network, build defenses, and attack different territory to dominate your opponents.

The player will begin with a single network nest to manage in a single region and attempt to expand their network by strategically building different node types to expand to other Networks. Points are added to the player’s total number of points based on multiple successful actions.(Attacking opposing players, Company Perks associated with the player’s chosen company, Expanding to other regions, and Defending against incoming attacks).
