
							About This Content
							Return to FreeportFreeport is known for its adventures, from Death in Freeport (the one that started it all!) to the mega-adventure Black Sails Over Freeport. Now the City of Adventure goes back to its roots with Return to Freeport! This six-part adventure series for the Pathfinder RPG is a new way to begin your Freeport adventures.Part Three: Storming the Razor CavesIn Return to Freeport, Part Three, the protagonists follow clues to a secret pirate port and uncover a plot by a mostly-forgotten foe.For Characters Level 5-7Fantasy Grounds Conversion: Matthew RuddellReleased on March 02, 2017. Designed for Fantasy Grounds version 3.2.2 and higher.Requirements: An active subscription or a one time purchase of a Fantasy Grounds Full or Ultimate license and the included Pathfinder (3.5E Compatible) ruleset.
