
							About This Game
							Krinkle Krusher is an action castle defense game where you cast elemental spells to defend your kingdom from twisted and hungry creatures called Krinkles.  After several generations of peace, they happen to come back exactly when the Ancient Tree bears its first fruits in centuries, and of course a cake was made from it. SPELLCASTING STRATEGIC ACTION!  Freeze, burn, and zap them all! Cast spells to defend your castle! Use the power of fire wall, ice crown, mud, energy mine, thunder and tornado! Upgrade and combine your spells to face the Krinkle horde and their bosses!  HIGHLIGHTS  - Face more than 12 Krinkle types and 3 different bosses, each one with unique abilities and weaknesses!  - Learn 6 powerful spells as you progress and upgrade them to full devastating power!  - Battle in different environments that will change your strategy!
