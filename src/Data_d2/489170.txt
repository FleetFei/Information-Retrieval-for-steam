
							About This Game
							“Galaxy Reavers” is a cutting edge full 3-dimensional space war RTS game developed by Good GamesThis is a fun yet challenging RTS game. In order to survive in the enormous galaxy, players have to build up their own fleet and equip every warship. Also, players will have to control the warships in every battle to defeat your enemies, conquer different planets and thus expand your territory!KEY FEATURES“Galaxy Reavers” is bound to turn over your gaming experience with its exquisite graphics, stunning battle effects, well designed warships and full 3-dimensional space battle! It’s the next generation RTS!This game allows players to control warships to complete precise actions. With keyboard/mouse, you can command a warship to rotate, accelerate or even flank the enemies! As a commander, you need to be accurate and visionary to lead your fleet to win the battle!The growth of your fleet can be free and diverse. We offer warships and devices of different styles. Along with the modular device system, you can equip your warship with various devices. It’s high time to build your fleet of the galaxy!
