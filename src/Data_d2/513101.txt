
							About This Content
							Two-axle sliding wall wagon used for the transport of moisture sensitive goods. The package contains the Hbillns 303 in brown livery and DB Cargo colors. Included too is a special version of the Hbillns 303, the Hbillss-y 307. This is a freight wagon with a special bogie for the transport of shock sensitive goods.The Hbillns 303 Wagon Pack for TS Marketplace features the standard brown, DB Cargo and DB Intetrans liverys and includes the RandomSkin feature which gives realistic variety to your consists when placing the wagons in a scenario.Features:3 different liveries Brown Colour, DB Cargo, DB IntertransAutomatic end plates and dynamic numberingAll liveries have the RandomSkin feature installed.
