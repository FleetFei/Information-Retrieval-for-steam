
							About This Game
							Shoot to the rhythm!Musical Range is a VR rhythm game where you use firearms to play along to your favorite music!Real SongsThe game features stellar tracks from some great indie artists, as well as music from some of your favorite games! From "The Blood of Kingu" by Therion to "Still Alive" from Portal, there is something for everyone!Full 61 Note RangeIf you can play it on a piano, you can play it in Musical Range! Each target represents a note within the 5 octaves, making the possibilities limitless! Familiar, but ChallengingIf you have played a lot of modern rhythm games you will feel familiar with the gameplay, but it now has the twist of laser-focused precision! An experience never really possible until VR!
