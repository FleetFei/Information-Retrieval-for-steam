
							About This Game
							Filthy, Stinking, Orcs! is a VR tower defense game set in a fantasy world besieged by monsters. Plan your strategy and join in the action by taking control of the weapons of war with your own hands! Defend your citadel and villages in beaches, plains, swamps, and mountains across 16 levels. Fight orcs, goblins, ogres, and dragons! Fire bows, crossbows, cannons, machine guns and more!
