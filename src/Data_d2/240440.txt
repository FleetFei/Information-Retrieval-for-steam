
							About This Game
							Hacking. Dial tone. Cassette decks. Sabotage. Suitcase decks. Brainbox injectors.Quadrilateral Cowboy is a single-player adventure in a cyberpunk world. Tread lightly through security systems with your hacking deck and grey-market equipment. With top-of-the-line hardware like this, it means just one thing: you answer only to the highest bidder.The Standard Edition includes:Quadrilateral Cowboy.Developer commentary.Reference manual.The Deluxe Edition includes:Everything in the Standard Edition.Quadrilateral Cowboy interactive art book.PDFs for box art, poster, and papercraft characters.
