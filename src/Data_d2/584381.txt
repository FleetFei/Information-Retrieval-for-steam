
							About This Content
							This is the remix soundtrack for Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED, created by FamilyJules and A_Rival!  It contains 4 new tracks from each remixer, including 5-1, 5-2, 5-3, and FortissiMole!

NOTE: This DLC does *NOT* include the Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED soundtrack songs from Virt, Girlfriend Records, OCRemix, or Danny Baranowsky.

ALSO NOTE: Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED is still in Early Access, so this OST may be updated in the future!
