
							About This Game
							When there are no good superpowers and super-names left, comes our hero - SuperMegaDude!The first part of an old school 2D "run and gun" game drawing inspiration from such games as Contra and Metal Slug.Eight directions to shoot in;Seven weapon types (including the Flickinator, the Three-barreled Shotgun, the Atomic Toaster and others).18 enemy types (a giant anime-style head, bikers, australians, cyborgs and samurai, just to name a few).A feiry soundtrack by the Beat Devils.P.S.
