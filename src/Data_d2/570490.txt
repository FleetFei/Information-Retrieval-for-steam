
							About This Game
							NO ENGINEERING, ALL PROCRASTINATION! SHENZHEN SOLITAIRE is a brand-new solitaire variant originally developed for SHENZHEN I/O, and is now available as a standalone game! It's simple but challenging, and oddly addictive.  A brand-new solitaire variant inspired by Mahjong and FreeCell! Simple to learn, but persistently challenging! Over 98% of deals are solvable! Win 100 games and become immortal! (with a Steam Achievement)NOTE: If you own SHENZHEN I/O, you don't need to purchase SHENZHEN SOLITAIRE! After solving a few puzzles in SHENZHEN I/O, a link to a full version of SHENZHEN SOLITAIRE will be placed on your in-game desktop for you to play.
