
							About This Game
							Anmynor Puzzles is set in a world of magic where challenges await in the 100 levels that make up this adventure. Use and combine the abilities of 17 unique and charming characters, the Golems on a journey to gather all the Elemental Runes. Anmynor Puzzles is set in a world of magic and adventure. You will guide a group of Golems through 100 levels. The adventure takes place over 17 stages, each with new obstacles and new Golems. The Golems will need to combine their different abilities to overcome the challenges ahead. 

Feature List:

17 different types of Golem, each with unique personalities and abilities.

100 beautifully detailed, full 3D levels with challenging puzzles.

Puzzle Game with real time interaction.

Use catapults and cannons to launch your Golems, fly across the scenery with air balloons, travel through portals and many more crazy game mechanics.

The fusion of two Elemental Golems results in Combined Golems that inherit the abilities of both Golems and gain a new unique ability.
