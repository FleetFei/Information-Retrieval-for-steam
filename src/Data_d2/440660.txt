
							About This Game
							Void Vikings is an action packed 2.5D top down space shooter where survival is the focus. Pilot a ship equipped with special abilities to destroy the hordes of enemies that are thrown against you. Pillage any loot their wreckage leaves behind to outfit your ship to become more powerful. Warp from system to system to take on larger and stronger waves of enemies. Death is the end but the glory from your battles will carry on.2.5D, top down, fast paced, action packed roguelike space shooter.Endless procedurally generated systems and enemy fleets.Seven unique ships to unlock and upgrade, each with their own special abilities.Pillage randomly generated loot to increase your fighting power.No extra lives or second chances.
