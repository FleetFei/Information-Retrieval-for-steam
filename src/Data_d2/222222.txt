
							About This Content
							Many Dwarven clans disappeared during the Magic War. One clan even went where no Dwarf has ever gone before; space. Now they have returned and brought with them new and strange technology for their Dwarven brethren.
Dig deep and prosper! 
Star Dwarves is an item pack that contains:

  Two new Dwarven fighter classes: the ranged Redshirts and the melee Knight
  One new enemy: the Beardprober
  24 new space themed props for your dungeons: Capsule Beds, Space Doors, Space Chairs, Holographic Training Dummies, The Space Bridge and many more items prefixed with the word “Space”
