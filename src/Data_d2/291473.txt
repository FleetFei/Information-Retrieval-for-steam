
							About This Content
							No fantasy game is complete without a grand castle for its kings and queens!Royal Tiles Resource pack is a tile add-on that will help you create the perfect castle. Besides the essential throne room, this set also contains tiles for a royal garden as well as an elaborate dining room. Elaborate textures and fine details will help you achieve the look of perfect opulence and greatness. This resource pack includes:Auto-tiles A1-A5Animated character sets: door, gate and fountainTile B with decorations for a castle hall, throne room and waiting roomTile C with decorations for a royal banquet, kitchen and bedroomsTile D with outside decorations for a royal garden, royal wedding or a complex maze
