
							About This Game
							Grey Cubes is a modern variation on the brick-breaker-type game, based on real-time physics and three-dimensional gameplay (not just 3d look).Minimalist in style, with clean graphics and deep, original soundtrack.The game contains 60 levels, each one is unique, each one with new challenges.Features:- addicting mechanics based on classic idea mixed with real-time physics and three-dimensional gameplay,- 60 highly diversified levels,- a traditional system of power-ups incorporated into modern gameplay mechanics,- multi-layered levels.
