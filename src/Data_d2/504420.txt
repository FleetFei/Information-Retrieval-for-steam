
							About This Game
							Malazard: The Master of Magic is an action-filled tower defense game, where you are able to step into the role of a wizard.With the help of Virtual Reality, the game's main purpose is to give you the feel of wielding destructive sorcery in pair with a fast-paced combat system. Besides your magical abilities it is still important to be ready to dodge an attack any time in order to prepare your own.In the time of need, demonic creatures are sieging the last resort of the planet. You are the only hope to keep this world from getting torn apart!We need a powerful mage immediately, more than ever!(HTC Vive required)
