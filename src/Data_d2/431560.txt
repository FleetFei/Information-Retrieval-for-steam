
							About This Game
							You are an artificial self-developing intelligence programmed by unknown scientist for the show "Hard Room". The greatest minds of the world create robots which have to pass through three dynamically changing locations. And you are the one of these robots. Glorify your creator and prove that you are the best.New vision of a hardcore. Games have never been so cruel to players before. Are you brave enough to experience it ? Then move on, 45 levels of hell and remember, every mistake is very expensive
