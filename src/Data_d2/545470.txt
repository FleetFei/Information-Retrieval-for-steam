
							About This Game
							1 - 4 player/s
Navigate your spaceship inside a deadly arena. Dodge bullets, saws and lasers, while avoiding and using other obstacles in your way. 
See if you can survive in Singleplayer or challenge your friends in Shared Screen Multiplayer (2 - 4 players) for ultimate carnage!

Tip:  Hold Left Shift while launching the game to access controls and resolution menu.
