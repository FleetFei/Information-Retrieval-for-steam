
							About This Game
							Take care of your dogs, manage your village and survive incoming storms in a frozen landscape!You must lead your village, hunt, explore and build in order to survive. Storms will hit your village every now and then, its your duty as the chief to gather the required resources and food in order to see the light of day after each storm.some features:-Village management :use your villagers to craft, build and gather resources.-Dog sled: 4 loyals dogs that will provide swift transportation, you just have to take care of their basic needs.-Storms: Storms that get more demanding as days go by, they will test your strategic skills as the leader of your village.-Diverse wildlife: from raccoons to huge bears!-online co-cop (still very buggy and unstable)
