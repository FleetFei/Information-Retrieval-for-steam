
							About This Game
							Sword and Shield in hand, fight your way along this sword-fighting experience, exclusively for VR.Please note that the Act 1 serves as a proof of concept for the fighting system.We need your help to develop an amazing game.Try it with your friends and family and send us your feedback for the upcoming episodes (which will be bigger in terms of content and play time).The experience should take around 10 to 15 minutes to complete... that is if you reach the end alive!This game was developed by Cyberith GmbH, under the Lead Developer Pierre Mercy.
