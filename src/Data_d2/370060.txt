
							About This Game
							Coffee Shop Tycoon is a management game in which you take control of your own coffee shop. Will you thrive and become a popular coffee shop or die trying? Everything lies in your hands. The game is NOT another 'click and wait three hours for your employees to cook' kind of game you will have to take choices constantly that will bring more costumers or make you lose money. We are building a game in which customization and planning is as important as having fun. Coffee Shop Tycoon features:Brew your coffeeHiring and Training StaffMarketing Campaigns Seasons and Trends Several coffee shops with different stylesBuild reputation with Gourmet beverages and special coffee beans Choose your coffee shop logo and colorBuy furniture and decoration for your coffee shop Add services to your coffee shop (Wifi, Rewards, and more) (Coming soon)Special EventsResupply food and drinks
