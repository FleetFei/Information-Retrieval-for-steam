
							About This Game
							8-Bit Armies: Arena is the multiplayer-only version of Petroglyph's popular 8-Bit Series of games!  Get online and play one of six different armies across dozens of multiplayer maps versus players around the world.Arena gives you the full PvP multiplayer functionality of 8-Bit Armies, Hordes, and Invaders including all of the following...All 6 Armies (Renegades, Guardians, Lightbringers, Deathsworn, Colonial Marines, and Cranioids)Over 30 Official Multiplayer Maps with support for 2 - 8 players and additional free maps created by the communityThe original game tutorial to get you up and running quicklySelect music tracks from our composer Frank Klepacki across all three original titlesReplay mode to watch and learn new strategies from the top players Observer mode to spectate games in progress and stream onlineCustom Game options to choose your own win conditions, random crates, cash, and other parametersQuickmatch mode - just click and get into the actionDedicated 24/7 monitored servers
