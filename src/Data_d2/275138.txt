							BNSF Wagon Pack

Formed in 1996 from two already huge Class I railroads, Burlington Northern and ATSF, BNSF is a giant of US freight transport. BNSF trains run a network of more than 32,000 track miles, moving all kinds of freight across America. 
The BNSF Wagon Pack includes the following rail vehicles in BNSF livery:
BNSF Boxcar 50ft (two versions)
BNSF Flatcar green (with/without log load)
BNSF Flatcar red (with/without log load)
BNSF Hopper 100ft (two versions)
BNSF Hopper Air slide
