
							About This Game
							The Great EscapeThe Great Escape is a Infinity Runner, where the player guides a spaceship through a narrow tunnel filled with incoming obstacles. Maneuverability is pretty nice, but it will be necessary great reflexes and a lot of patient for this game, considered very hard for it own devs. Great Escape is procedural, the more you advance harder the hazards become. Badges :      The main goal is achieve high score and record your name in our world leaderboards.The Great Escape is another game from brazilian indie game developer XiNFiNiTY Games.
