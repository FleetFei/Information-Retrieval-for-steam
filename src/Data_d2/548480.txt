
							About This Game
							Events occur several months after the global epidemic. Infected turned into aggressive creatures. All survivors are infected with the virus. In order to survive you need to use different medicines. Also you need to avoid predators.

You can play alone and in co-op with other players. The choice of a lot of characters with different characteristics.

The player can build a base, to grow food, hunt, cook, collect resources and craft. The game has a lot of weapons, transport from cars to tanks.
