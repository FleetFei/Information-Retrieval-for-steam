
							About This Content
							A set of Special Costumes. These unique and colorful costumes will give a fresh look to every battlefield!

How to use: Choose the appropriate character under "Select Character," then choose "Change Costume."

This set includes the costumes for;
Yukimura Sanada, Oichi (x2), Okuni, Kunoichi, No,  Ina (x2), Mitsunari Ishida, Ginchiyo Tachibana, Nene (x2), Gracia (x2), Kai, Aya, Naotora Ii, Koshosho, Lady Hayakawa,  Mitsunari Ishida and Toyohisa Shimazu.
