
							About This Game
							Causality is a puzzle game about manipulating time, altering the sequence of events and changing the outcome of each level.Set across strange and alien landscapes, help a group of stranded astronauts find a route to safety. Travel through time, work with your past selves and solve paradoxes as you take on this uniquely challenging puzzler.Each level represents a brief but hazardous moment in time. Levels are completed by guiding each astronaut to an exit that matches their colour, within a limited time frame. When all astronauts reach an exit the level is complete.Control time and alter the outcome of each levelWork with your past selves to solve puzzles60 Levels to complete13 achievements to unlock
