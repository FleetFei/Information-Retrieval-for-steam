
							About This Game
							Car racing but different. Enjoy the realistic RC car physics engine of these mini cars on different routes, in various lighting conditions, indoor and outdoor!

Prove your driving skills early, so that you have soon enough money to buy new faster RC models and other routes. Train in single races while the clock is ticking or compete against your friends in the multiplayer mode via LAN or Internet. Then chances are good to leave your opponents behind you during the competition races!

Features:
- Single player races against intelligent computer opponents
- Multiplayer with up to four players via LAN or Internet
- Large selection of indoor and outdoor tracks
- Many RC model cars
- Realistic RC car physics engine
- Various game modes: Single race, time trial and competitions
- Earn money by winning races and thus unlock more tracks, cars and access to the competitions
- Moddable RC cars ( purchasable engines, car skins and tires )
- Damage simulation of the cars ( repairmode )
- Extensive configuration options
