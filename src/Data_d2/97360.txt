
							About This Content
							Ajani Goldmane’s fully unlocked “Celestial Light” white deck. Ajani Goldmane works his magic through others, purifying body and soul. A fierce guardian, Ajani refuses to let anyone under his watch fall. Ajani’s cat warriors form the core of his soldiers, but he also has powerful celestial beings at his command. Use powerful life gain effects to outlast your opponent in your march to victory! Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013.
