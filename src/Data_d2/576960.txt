
							About This Game
							Out of the Box allows you to experience the feeling of being the bouncer in the most exclusive and also dangerous club in town.In this demo, we offer the chance to get to know a little bit about this story which takes action mostly at night. Here you can meet some of the most important characters, and also become familiar with the game's mechanics.Throughout 4 different days,you can get in Warren's shoes, and find out what it feels like when you ask some disoriented bum for his ID, when you just take the bribe and let someone you shouldn't in, or when you try to reason with helpless drunk people.Do you need more reasons to sign your contract with "The Box"?
