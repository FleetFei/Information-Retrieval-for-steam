
							About This Game
							Claw your way through the abyss of the mind and survive the horrors that stalk you as you attempt to survive seven days as... an Indie Developer.
Make It Indie! is an indie game about making an indie game. It is up to you to make tough choices about your indie game while also managing your schedule. Will you crunch to oblivion, ignoring the world around you? Or will you risk it all for a much needed break?

Make It Indie features:
• Over 30 minutes of HD videos that immerses the player in the life of an indie developers.
• An intuitive gameplay experience.
• Multiple unique endings.
• Truly diverse music tracks.

P.S. Don't take this game too seriously!
