
							About This Game
							Polywings is an indie flying game with short and challenge missions, atmospheric music and low poly graphics.
The game is currently is on the first development phase. I'm a single developer behind, so your feedback and support is really important for me :)
Hope you could help me in making this game bigger and better!

IMPORTANT:
It only support Keyboard and xBox 360 controller for now. (full controller support will be ready soon)

Current content:
-7 missions.
-1 map.
-Atmospheric music
-1 Plane.
-Low poly graphics.

I'll improve a lot of aspect of the game: Water, Skybox, Shaders, Maps, Gameplay.

The final version will have:
-5 maps.
-History mode.
-Challenges mode.
-Exploration mode.
-Achievements.
-Trading cards.
-Highscores.
