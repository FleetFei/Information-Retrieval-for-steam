
							About This Content
							For Rayman and his band of friends, the adventure continues in the UNO Rayman Theme!  Enjoy your matches in a lush and lively environment as you and your opponents find creative, new ways to mess with each other’s chances for victory. With four brand-new and original Theme Cards, you’ll be able to hide your lead from others, swat cards back at their players (yes, even Wild Draw Fours), or mix up everyone’s hands if you’re feeling particularly daring at the time. Excitement, surprises, and more await you in the UNO Rayman Theme!
