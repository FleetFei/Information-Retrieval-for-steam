
							About This Game
							Because Single Is Too LittleThe Game In A NutshellDouble Death is a crazy arcade brawler & shoot'em up with the heart of soul of those over the top 90's beat'em up games combined with the flair of modern survival and squad-based games. With 9 different guns and over 2300 different weapon attachment combinations along with multiplayer and a fully integrated modding system, we've packed in enough to keep the fun coming. The Premise It's the year 20XX. Zombies exist. They're killing people. Terrible stories are now acceptable by the general public, and you've found yourself smack in the middle of one. Scared of becoming just another background character, you've decided to survive. Core Features     4 Different Game Modes: Classic Survival, Endless Survival, Arcade/Story Mode, Infection PVP    Singleplayer & Multiplayer, including Co-op and PVP    9 Different Weapons and Over 2300 Different Weapon Modifications, including Chain Lightning, Fire, Explosions, Poison Gas, and More    Weapon Mod Synergy; Different weapon effects will inherit and synergize with other effects, including ones modded in.     A Variety of different maps with different terrains, including Jungles, Cities, Powerplants, and more!    Fully integrated Modding & Custom Map system, with the ability to make custom gamemodes and weapons as well!    Player Customization, pick your gender and from a wide variety of crazy unlockable hats to show off in Multiplayer!    A zany cast of wild zombies and bosses to mix up gameplay
