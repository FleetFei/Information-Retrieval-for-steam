
							About This Content
							Drive the NS SD60E - 6920 Veterans Unit in this detailed session that will have you making your daily run of local freight. You’ll need to make sure you couple the correct train cars in the right order that are waiting to be processed in the rail yard. Once you’ve got your train ready you’ll need to deliver each of the cars to locations around the map.

Experience the power of the NS SD60E - 6920 Veterans Unit as you shunt the yard and make your freight run!

Features of the NS SD60E - 6920 Veterans Unit include:

Brand new Custom Cab and in-cab Signals!
Fan Animation
Ditch Lights
Night Lighting

Surveyor Tip:
To create your own custom session in Surveyor using your new locomotive. Locate the NS SD60E - 6920 Veterans Unit by selecting the 'Train' Tab and searching for 'NS SD60E'.
