
							About This Content
							The vast world of Last Dream features over two-hundred brilliant compositions. Each track was carefully curated to power the emotional force of your adventure, immersing you into dynamic gameplay. From flashbacks to side quests to secret locations and more, talented artists generously lent their passion for great music to Last Dream.
