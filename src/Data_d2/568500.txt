
							About This Game
							Atomic Reconstruction is a atmospheric Puzzle game, where you have to use the power of fusion and fission to create and modify specific atoms or groups of atoms.Combine and split atoms on varying playgrounds by mass and work your way up to the desired Atom, the fewer steps you need, the better.Experience 33 unique challenges in the atmospheric world of chemistry.Compare your skill with other players on the online highscore.
