
							About This Game
							Dragon Skies VR is a story-based RPG/shooter that puts you right in the saddle as your kingdom's sole dragon rider. Mount up and fly your dragon through treacherous valleys and caves, battling with rebel airship pilots that want nothing more than to take you down a notch. With a rich story, living characters, a powerful spell crafting system, and decisions to make that affect the future of the world, you are certain to find yourself immersed with Dragon Skies VR.Note: This game is still in development, and any features listed here are subject to change by time of release.FeaturesDragon Riding - Fly freely through the skies atop your dragon's back, with your spell-casting gun in one hand, and your steerpathic wand in the other. Note: This game uses extreme locomotion for flight. You have full control to perform aerial maneuvers such as intense dives, back flips, and barrel rolls. The feeling is akin to riding a roller coaster. Highly advanced features for combating VR sickness have been implemented, but it will be up to you to fly your dragon smoothly.10+ Missions - From taking down a rebel force in the western canyons, to flying through narrow caves with dangerous traps to save a friend from wrongful imprisonment. Each mission was crafted to follow the story and provide a new, exciting gameplay element.World & Lore - Learn about the history of the dragon riders, and discover your role in your kingdom's turbulent past.Characters with Personality - Wander the outskirts of your floating city of Cidholm, meeting and making connections with its citizens whose lives depend on your choices. All characters are fully voice acted and custom animated.Crafting - Use collected energy orbs called Soul Seeds to craft new spells to use with your spell casting revolver, or to improve your dragon's abilities.
