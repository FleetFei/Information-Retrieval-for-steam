
							About This Game
							Simple rules.Complex variations.Elegant solutions.Hade is an ambient logic puzzle game made in minimalist style.Game mechanics is based on the manipulation of the movement direction by placing objects on the field. With trials and errors player must find right order of positioning to solve the puzzle.    Features:150+ levels with various difficulty.Different mechanics, drasticly changing game rules, such as portals, destroyable obstacles, temporary intangibility, etc.Relaxing ambient music.Trading cards and achievements.
