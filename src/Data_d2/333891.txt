
							About This Content
							With the Going Batty Bundle you will adventure through EverQuest II as a ferocious Freeblood Player Race.  In addition to playing this amazing race, you will also get your claws on the Power of the Freeblood appearance weapon.  This allows you to use a claw-attack animation in place of equipped weapons.  Last but not least, with the Going Batty Bundle you will also receive a unique flying mount where you travel through Norrath as a cloud of bats!
