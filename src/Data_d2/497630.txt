
							About This Game
							"When the interstellar silence is broken, that humanity is in danger, no one wants to go there, they call the H.U.N.T!"H.U.N.T is a sci-fi FPS with co-op mode. On the G-Wares frozen planet, a team of HUNT have been sent to exterminate the aliens and protect the colonies. These soldiers have not been trained to conduct regular wars, no, they are there to act where nobody wants to go. When the enemy is unknown, when in excess, when inordinately stronger, the HUNT are sent.So get out your Dart-SMA 8: 216, the favorite HUNT's weapon, and exterminate the enemy!Choose among three HUNT soldiers based on different skills and weapons. Djimon, Wladimir and Jeanne are teammates with complementary skills.Eradicate monsters with amazing capabilities: cloaking faculty, flame jet or acid soil, sudden appearance... The monsters on G-Wares doesn't lack of ways to attack you by surprise.Cooperate with two other players online through the cooperative mode and keep grouped to release your teammates caught by monsters.
