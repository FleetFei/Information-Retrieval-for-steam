
							About This Game
							The Room of Black & White is a story about one girl entering a mysterious room. The player will take the role of a girl named 'Diane' and will have to clear the Keepers' quest in order to escape  Why has she come here and why does she have to clear quests in ? The player's task is to unravel her memories and find the truth. Quests are going hard as story goes on.What will face Diane as she attempts to escape ? key Features This Game Is Inspired By Chess. The visual design consists solely of black & white. The player will explore four different themes during their quest. Find scattered memories to uncover the mysteries of the game's perplexing story.
