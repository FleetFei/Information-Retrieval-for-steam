
							About This Video
							It's ANGRY VIDEO GAME NERD - THE MOVIE! Based on the hit web series, AVGN: The Movie follows the Nerd (James Rolfe), a disgruntled gamer and online celebrity who must overcome his fear of the worst video game of all time in order to save his fans. Hilarity ensues as a simple road trip becomes an extravagant pursuit of the unexpected.
