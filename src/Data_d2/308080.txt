
							About This Game
							Altitude0: Lower & Faster is a skill based competitive racing game where extremely low and risky flying is a must. Reflexes and quick thinking are required to fully enjoy dangerous edgy flying. Flying low over rugged terrains and obstacles like blades and fire rings will challenge you to the max. As you build your skills, you will be rewarded with new planes and plane add-ons. Performance parts and plane decorations will help you upgrade your in-game presence and status. And if you manage to excel in Altitude0, your achievements and results will be immortalized through numerous Hall of Fame rankings. Flying becomes contact sport with plane collisions and crashing is just part of fun. Join official events and team up with others or create your own events and competitions.Fun crazy plane crashingGame features advanced and sometimes silly soft body deformation system.
