
							About This Content
							Europa Universalis IV – Sounds from the Community –Kairis SoundtrackTaking inspiration from the rich tapestry of the Europa Universalis universe, this music pack breaks new ground by being the first ever community produced piece of music for the award winning franchise.Composed by avid Europa Universalis fan - Kairi Sawler, this collection of five songs is inspired by East-Asian melodies that expand the Europa Universalis gaming experience. From traditional instruments and calming melodies to up-beat percussion this music pack enhances the Europa Universalis listening experience with more beautiful music.The track list includes:Takeda SunriseJade AmbitionsEmperor's RoadSilken PathForest Shade
