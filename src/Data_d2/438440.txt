
							About This Game
							Star Rogue is a sci-fi roguelike platformer. In this challenging pixel indie game you will take control over a brave space marine to investigate an abandoned space station full of traps, robots and aliens. In each playthrough you will encounter a different environment full of randomly arranged rooms. With the choice between several weapons, abilites and different items, your playing experience will be unique each time you play.!!! ATTENTION !!!This game is still in development!
