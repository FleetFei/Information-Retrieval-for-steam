
							About This Game

DX is a complete re-imagining of Turbo Pug 1, with all new artwork, music and exciting mini games. Fast paced, addictive, casual Pug-runner style game play with more variety than ever!Features:Explore an all new Pug World - Run and jump through a variety of passionately crafted environmentsMultiple paths - Take different paths/routes, some may be more difficult, some may reward you with collectables!Characters galore! - More characters than ever, more variety and choiceMini games - Enjoy secret mini games with new game play mechanicsLeaderboards - Leave your friends in the dust, rise to the top of the leaderboards and go down in history as a Pug Master!Custom skins - Create your own custom level skins, or enjoy skins created by other users. Submit your skins and if proven worthy, we'll make them available for other players to enjoyJukebox - Chill out and enjoy the new soundtrack on the Jukebox.
