This pack contains image tokens suitable for ALL epic tier monsters from the SECOND core 4E monster book. Upon activating the module, the tokens will appear in your Token box for use throughout your game. They have been organized by genre and product name to make it easy to use multiple token pack modules within your game. Tokens are scaled and organized in the following sizes for medium creatures: 32x32, 50x50, 75x75 and 100x100 pixels.
