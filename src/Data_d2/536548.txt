
							About This Content
							Toposim North Africa for FSX: Steam Edition provides multi-LOD 7-11, 19m maximum terrain mesh for the whole North African region, improving the accuracy of the default terrain mesh and offering a marked improvement to the landscape profile. VFR flying over North Africa has never been more realistic with lakes, mountains and ground undulations all where they should be. Please note this pack corrects the default terrain mesh and does not include any textures.This pack includes terrain mesh for the following countries: 	Western Sahara	Morocco	Algeria	Tunisia	Libya	Egypt	Sudan
