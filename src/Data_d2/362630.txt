
							About This Game
							- Is there any other reality than the existing one? Is there any other independent world from our world?...Yes there is. And if this world is as cold and unbearable as the winter's wind, what happens if we get in there...get in the Alter World...The Game is inspired by Stephen King’s book - “The Dark Tower”. The game is a 2D platformer with a interesting gameplay mechanic. Player can pass on an alternative universe anytime and use an alternative universe reality to move to another level. Levels are unique, with puzzles, and the player will have to react quickly and assess when to move between the Worlds. The game is made in a unique, art style, like a painting. 80 unique and challenging levels, Do you think, that you are a Pro Gamer? Try Alter World and you will challenge yourself!  10 different locations, these places will conquer your heart!  2 Different game ending, listen to your ego, and finish game  unique Sound System , each Level has different sounds. Strange and quiet melodies would soon conquer a player's heart and feel themselves in the tale . Keep Going, going in Alter, find your friend, and watch out of sudden death...
