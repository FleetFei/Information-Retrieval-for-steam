
							About This Game
							"Get Over here" puts you in the shoes of a Reality Show contestant!
This Reality Show is a bloody competition where you gotta pull your enemies using your hook Gun and finish them with your special move. If you're skillful enough to survive the tournament, beat all your enemies and dodge all the traps, you will be crowned the winner!

AIM, PULL and FINISH THEM!

Features:
Up to 4 players battle, Local and Online!
12 over the top, stylized characters!
7 different studios filled with dangerous Traps.
Spice things up with SPECIAL ITEMS that may make or break your strategy!
Stunning finishing moves!
