
							About This Game
							Tower Ascent is a Virtual Reality climbing game, where the player is challenged to reach the top of each tower as fast as possible! Compete with other's times, or take your time finding just the right hand hold to reach the top. Race against your own best time in each level or team up with a friendly archer in the asymmetrical Helper mode! Master all the levels and reach the top as fast as you can! Many updates to come!
