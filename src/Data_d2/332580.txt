
							About This Game
							Explore strange new worlds and enjoy unique adventures in Mimpi!Mimpi is fantastic new adventure, puzzle, and platformer game.  The game is like a psychedelic illustration come alive. Mimpi the dog is searching for his master in eight diverse worlds.  The environment is seldom repetitive, the puzzles are always new.  The story is told without words by the game world itself.
