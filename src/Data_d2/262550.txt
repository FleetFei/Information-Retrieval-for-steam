
							About This Game
							In the year of 18XX, the west is overrun by thugs and outlaws. A group of bandits have kidnapped Mayor Johnsons daughter and are spreading havok across the land. Only Gunman Clive can bring order to the west and save the girl!Jump and shoot your way across 16 diverse and challenging levels and fight massive bosses. Gunman Clive is an old-school sidescrolling platform game set in the old west with full 3D graphics and a unique artstyle.Features:Old school sidescrolling actionFull 3D graphicsUnique sketch shaded artstyle3 different playable charactersDozens of different enemiesEpic boss battlesSupports Xbox 360 controllers
