
							About This Series
							Setting up Lights in a painting, is a demonstration on how to simplify your light set ups and understand how to approach lights in your painting. 

The topics covered are 
- Understanding basic lighting.
- How lighting can be used for drama.
- Add subtle lights to push your art.
- Set up sketch, and what to consider.
- Understanding your forms above all else.
- When to add more lighting.
