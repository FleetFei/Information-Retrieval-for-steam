
							About This Video
							Always remember the cardinal rule of dining out: Never mess with the people who handle your food! Ryan Reynolds (GREEN LANTERN), Anna Ferris (TV's "Mom"), and Justin Long (LIVE FREE OR DIE HARD) star in this hilarious comedy about a group of young waiters and waitresses searching for the meaning of life, love, and friendship—while waiting for their next big tip— in this outrageous laugh-fest.
