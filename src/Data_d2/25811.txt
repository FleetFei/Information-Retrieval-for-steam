
							About This Content
							This spritepack includes 16 new sprites for the Western countries in the Enlightenment era of 1700 to 1795. There are uniforms for:
						
						Dutch
						Spanish
						French
						British
						Portuguese
						Danish
						Prussian
						Scottish
						Austrian
						Swedish
						American
						Scandinavian
						Venetian
						Revolutionary French
						English
						Generic Western
						 
						Sixteen historically authentic modeled units for the “Age of Enlightenment” with new animations, for a more realistic and beautiful game atmosphere. Units are equipped with muskets, sabres, tricorn and wig. The generic western units are embellished for this period, too.
