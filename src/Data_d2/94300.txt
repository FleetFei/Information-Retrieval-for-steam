
							About This Game
							The Dream Machine is an award-winning adventure game about dreams and voyeurism. It's built by hand using materials such as clay, cardboard and broccoli. Five out of six chapters have been released so far.In Chapter 1 you play as Victor Neff, the husband in a young couple who've just moved into a new apartment.  While trying to get settled in you discover that all is not as it seems in the quiet, unassuming apartment building...In Chapter 2, Victor goes to confront the landlord, Mr. Morton, only to find his apartment empty. Can Victor find the elusive landlord in time and is there more to Mr. Morton than meets the eye?Key FeaturesWorld: Explore a creepy apartment complex and uncover the mysteries within.
