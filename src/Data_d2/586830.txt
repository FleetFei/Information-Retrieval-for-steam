
							About This Game
							Have you ever dreamed of creating total mayhem? Perhaps your goal in life is to destroy cities. Maybe, just maybe, you thought becoming a giant monster of incredible strength pounding through a city, feasts on people and creating havoc is what motivates you. Then welcome to MIGHTY MONSTER MAYHEM! Mighty Monster Mayhem gives you the power to become the ultimate destructive monster. You move through cities, climbing to great heights, leaping immense distances, knocking down buildings, and creating as much destruction as you can before the time runs out. Eat civilians or military men to restore strength! Grab friends and join together to destroy cities as a team of monsters, or compete against each other for the most chaos!This is your chance to travel, see new cities (and destroy them), meet new people (and eat them), make new friends (and smash them)! So much mighty fun!Smash buildings to the groundEat civilians AND military!Climb the buildings!Throw vehicles! Destroy entire cities!Leap to incredible heights!Compete with friends for complete chaos!Choose your favorite mighty monster!Mighty Monster Mayhem gives you the power to transform into the monster that everyone says you are.
