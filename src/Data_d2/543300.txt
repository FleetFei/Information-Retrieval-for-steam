
							About This Game
							Horizon of History is an educational VR experience that will take you on a journey through the ages. It's a virtual time machine that will teleport you between key periods of the history of Poland's capital city - Warsaw. You will see Warsaw's panorama as it was in 11th, 14th, 16th centuries and after it's total destruction during the Second World War up until today. Each time period has various items and artifacts available for you to play with:Hit the Target With A BowHack Wood With A Slavic AxeCut Vegetables With A Polish SaberShoot Targets With Deadly "blyskawica" Submachine Gun and More!Feel the history in your hands. Experience everyday tools and items from 1000 years of Warsaw's history. The viewing point is located at a virtual recreation of the Warsaw's landmark building - the 44 story Palace of Culture and Science and it's famous observation deck.
