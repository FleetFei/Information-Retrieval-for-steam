
							About This Game
							A dog's journey to reunite with his best friend. Lolly Joe is a 3D-platforming adventure that takes you through the surreal worlds inside a boy's subconscious mind. With his magic lollipop, Joe must overcome a variety of challenges crafted from his friend's dreams and memories.Explore vibrant, cartoon worlds inhabited by funny and strange creaturesChallenging gameplay that put your platforming skills to the testAn emotional story inspired by real world eventsUnlock 27 costumes and accessories to customize Joe's appearance in amusing waysUnique experience every time you play, with platforming challenges randomly varying on every runFully playable with any Xinput-based game controller
