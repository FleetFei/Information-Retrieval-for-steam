
							About This Game
							The Sheeple are in trouble - they've been enslaved by the Drones - and it's all Patchman's fault!  Now it's up to you to redeem Patchman, rescue the Sheeple, and uncover the awful secret behind the Drone army.  Stay out of sight, wear clever disguises, search the forest for seeds, grow fruits and plants, tear down propaganda, awaken the Sheeple to break the control scheme, and power down those nasty robots!All that and more awaits in Don't Be Patchman, a sneak-and-grow action/adventure game.Premiering soon on SteamOS / Linux, followed by Windows and MacOSX releases - along with Steam Achievements, Trading Cards, and Cloud Saves.
