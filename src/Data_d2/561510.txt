
							About This Game
							DragoDino is a 2D rogue platformer on PC/Mac/Linux currently in Beta.To retrieve Bob's egg, the player will have to climb different levels, each randomly generated, and reach the top of the giant tree. To succeed, he'll need to gather all Super Jumps by fighting the enemies whom have it.EPICFace multitude enemies to haul yourself to the summit of the world and save your egg.CARTOONExplore a fun colorful world where the appearances are misleading.ROGUEDiscover a world created randomly to every party and adapt yourself to survive.
