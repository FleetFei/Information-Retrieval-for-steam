
							About This Content
							Raising Volume documents the creation of Volume, Mike Bithell's follow-up to the award-winning videogame, Thomas Was Alone. Enlisting the help of Lord of the Rings star Andy Serkis; YouTube celebrity Charlie McDonnell; the writer, comedian and BAFTA-winning voice actor Danny Wallace, and one of the most talented teams in videogames, Mike took two years to bring Volume to life. Raising Volume is the definitive account of the game's development
