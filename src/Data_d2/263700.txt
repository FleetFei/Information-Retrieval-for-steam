
							About This Game
							Fertilize the forest with the unicorn, cover the sky with black holes, flood the world with candy, but just get the muffins back!Muffin Knight is an arena based action-packed platformer with stunning visuals and a myriad of fairytale characters, each with their own unique abilities, which gain strength as you advance.This is the story of a little boy, on his journey to return the old fairy’s magical muffins. A strange curse was set on him: with each muffin he touches, he turns into a different creature.
