
							About This Game
							"Inside Me" is a top-down shooter in which you play as one of nanorobots embedded in the human body to fight infection.While clearing the infected cells you will fight with various enemies and dangerous bosses.The general gameplay consists in controlling more smaller bots in fighting with parasites.During the game you will be able to reveal unique abilities which you can combine with each other.FeaturesHardcore gameplayColorful hand-drawn 2d art styleThe use of unique abilitiesCombining of abilitiesBattles with dangerous bosses
