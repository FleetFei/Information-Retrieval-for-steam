
							About This Content
							A new spell-caster weapon type that rewards mobility and clever positioning. The Tome can unleash devastating forays of magical attacks, teleport the caster and hamper his enemies with crowd-control effects.

This DLC unlocks the Tome weapon type in the game. This includes several legendary Tomes such as the Nekonomicon and Fifty Shades of Slay, as well as a new legendary destiny card for Tome-users.
