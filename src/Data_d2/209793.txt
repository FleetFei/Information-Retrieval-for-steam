
							About This Content
							Algorithms and Angelology is the second soundtrack album to be released for Splice featuring music tracks from the 4 Epilogue sequences, plus a bonus track!
Tracklist:

Ariadne
Ganymede
Phylonoe
Daedalus
Reflection

Songs will be placed in the Splice folder of the Steam install directory. To view, right-click on Splice in your Library and select Properties. Then select the Local Files tab and the Browse Local Files button. In the system file browser, open the folder named "soundtrack" to view and listen to your music.
