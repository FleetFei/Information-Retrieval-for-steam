
							About This Game
							During the cleanup work Abrix robot got stuck in a room full of containers, crates and other objects. Help him find a way out of the room.Abrix the robot is an addictive puzzle game for everyone. Travelling on over 70 different rooms run into various obstacles whose removal will require to use your head.Game features: 70+ different rooms. 5 levels of difficulty, from beginner's guide to extremely difficult. Editor of the rooms allows you to create your own puzzles. The opportunity to share your own rooms with your friends via the Steam Workshop. Saves in the Cloud Steam, achievements and Steam trading cards. The unique soundtrack and available DLC bonus tracks of electronic music composed by Ree-D.The game provides over 40 hours of intellectual entertainment ranging from fairly simple puzzles, to the extremely difficult, forcing the player to maximum effort. You can also create your own rooms with the editor, make them available in the Steam workshop and invite your friends to play. Challenge them and see if they solve the puzzles designed by you!
