
							About This Game
							Battle Trucks it is a race on huge and furious trucks. Your aim is to destroy anyone who stands on your way to victory. 
In Battle Trucks you can find a great amount of trucks and racing tracks for all taste. Car's physics will please you with its lightness and arcade, damage system will surprise you with its realism. Enemies will not give you the opportunity to relax.
Race! Fight! Win!
P.S. So how many combat races can you survive?
