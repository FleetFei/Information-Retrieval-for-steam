
							About This Content
							The fully unlocked “Mindstorms” blue and red deck. On the city-world of Ravnica, magical research is the chief domain of the Izzet League, often with explosive results. The “Mindstorms” deck is one such result. Dangerously unstable elementals and weirds take the fight to the enemy, while lightning strikes rain destruction from above. Use the Izzet’s deadliest discoveries against your enemies! Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013. Requires Magic 2013 Expansion.
