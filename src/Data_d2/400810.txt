
							About This Content
							Adds 6 new units to the game, 3 melee Golem units & 3 ranged gunpowder units. The gunpowder units make pew-pew sounds! Smokey and noisy! The first two units are recruited at Tier 2 (one golem and gunpowder turtle), two more unlock at Tier 3 (again one of each), and the last two at Tier 4 (one more golem and also bombards!).
