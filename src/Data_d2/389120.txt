
							About This Game
							10 Minute Barbarian is a unique strategy game that combines fast-paced exploration and even faster-paced action combat. Choose your battles carefully, then watch as armies of hundreds crash castle walls, cast spells, and send fireballs into each other's ranks. Features:High-speed strategy gameplay for the busy gamerGiant battles with hundreds of soldiers supported by special units like dragons, demons, and necromancersControls easy enough for casual playersNo micropayments---pay once, get everything10 Minute Barbarian is available for Windows and Mac OS X.
