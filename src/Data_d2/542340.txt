
							About This Game
							Have you ever wanted to shoot a slingshot at people?
This game can give you such an experience!
But be careful,because you have a slingshot
and people for which you intend to shoot
there is a whole Arsenal of weapons,which they will apply,
to kill you.
Rely only on your slingshot and agility,
find enough stones,
shoot all the enemies
and rest from the battle with a sense of superiority
over the heavily armed commandos.
All this awaits you in this interesnoi and exciting game.
