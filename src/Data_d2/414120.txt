
							About This Game
							Modbox is a VR sandbox where you can use mods to create game experiences and share them online. Go online to play multiplayer creations together in a variety of game modes - or create your own game worlds by piecing parts together.Select from a large selection of mods to choose content for your creationsCheck out tutorials and our wiki at http://www.modboxgame.com/ on how to buildShare your work on Steam workshop ingame, or try out others onlineConstruct with primitives by just touching them together. Add in hinge joints, ropes, propellers, rockets, piston, balloons. Then control them by wiring them together with throttles and levers.Pick up and play with toys to interact with the world - including baseball bats, basketballs, crossbows, pistols, chainsaws...
