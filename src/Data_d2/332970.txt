
							About This Game
							Storm United is a class-based multiplayer First Person Shooter bringing top tier competitive FPS fun through 6 different game modes, 4 distinct classes, 30+ weapons and a variety of available maps.Storm United comes with a built-in development tool named the "United Developer Hub" (UDH), allowing players to:1. Submit ideas, suggestions and bug reports.2. Select and influence development priorities based on the various submissions.3. Participate in fundamental gameplay decisions through a voting system.UDH is easy to use and tells us when and what our team should work on, giving the players the ultimate platform to be heard. More details about it can be found here: http://www.stormunited.com/discover-our-community-driven-development-tool/With an ex Counter-Strike pro-gamer at the head of the project, a talented indie team, and UDH at your fingertips, we encourage you to join us on our journey and help us build the next great FPS!
