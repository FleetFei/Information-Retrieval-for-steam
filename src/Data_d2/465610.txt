
							About This Content
							The City Z soundtrack provides all of the game's music in cleanly-edited MP3 format. It contains 8 music tracks with a clear old-fashioned Zombies influence but also with rock and western inspirations.

Music composed by Philippe Capelle.

Track list:
1 - Dark thoughts
2 - Bad friday
3 - Around me
4 - You're one of us
5 - City of zombies
6 - On the other side
7 - Strange day
8 - No escape
