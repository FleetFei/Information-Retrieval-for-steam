
							About This Game
							Beeswing is a game set in a small village in rural Scotland, the village I grew up in, home. Visit the places and people who shaped a life and discover their stories. Represented in hand painted, water colour graphics with a unique, acoustic soundtrack. FeaturesNo puzzles or fighting or anything like that. Under 3 hours of content! Stuff you find in your regular day like people who leave their TV on when they have visitors and friends feeling a bit glum! My mum, she's there.  Quite a slow movement speed.  Old People! Sad bits.and not much more!
