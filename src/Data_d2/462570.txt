
							About This Game
							When a bolt of lightning sends you back to the days of King Arthur, you trade in your farmer's hat for a suit of armor and set off on a journey to find the Fountain of Youth - or at least make it back to the present by supper! To complete your mission, you'll need to gather resources, build thriving settlements and fight off goblins and skeletons! New Yankee in King Arthur's Court combines nail-biting time management thrills with the ease-of-play casual gamers enjoy.
