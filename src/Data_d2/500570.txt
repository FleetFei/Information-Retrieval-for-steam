
							About This Game
							Mahjong Deluxe 2: Astral Planes is an out-of-this-world Mahjong game produced by award-winning game developer EnsenaSoft, challenging you to complete increasingly complex mahjong puzzles rendered in glorious 3D. Match and rotate 3D tiles and test yourself to solve 84 mind-bending puzzles. Four levels of difficulty are included adding to the fun. Puzzles are laid out using random tile orders allowing you to replay each mahjong layout many times with a new experience each session. An exciting scoring system, great sound effects and relaxing music adds to the fun. Suitable to players of all ages and experience levels, Mahjong Deluxe 2: Astral Planes offers an extraterrestrial rendition of the popular Chinese classic, and is a must-have game in your collection today.Key Features84 Mahjong Puzzles To Solve4 Levels Of DifficultyRichly Rendered 3D EnvironmentsRandom Tile LayoutGreat Graphics, Audio & Score
