
							About This Content

But, are they truly to be trusted, or is she just walking into a trap from which there is no escape? And, could there be an even more dangerous force lurking in the shadows?Features* Similar in length to Episode 1* More investigations and interrogations of crime scenes and suspects* New characters and music
