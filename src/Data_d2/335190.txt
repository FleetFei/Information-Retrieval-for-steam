
							About This Game
							200% Mixed Juice! is the 10th Anniversary game of OrangeJuice, an old school RPG featuring a 200% mix of OrangeJuice characters.At certain points in time and space, many fun worlds exist. Each world was created by a smart little fairy named Navi for the sake of the sleeping princess with nothing to do. A world where you're able to experience a lively school life.A world where you can see the skies crowded with airplanes.A world that has fallen to ruin from endless wars...It's time for the sleeping princess to wake up and start her adventures in those worlds.Features:Over 80 characters to do battle withCutting-edge graphics to amaze your friends and neighbors withA stunning digital soundtrackOnline multiplayer
