
							About This Game
							Amino Corp have been suspected of committing some suspicious activity that has led the Bureau Investigation agency to send some of their agents to investigate the company. Some Weeks have passed however there has been no response from those agents sent. The agency has requested help from P.A.S.K.A.L to go into the Amino Corp building to arrest the CEO, Mr. Betas and to rescue those agents. A member of the P.A.S.K.A.L unit, Rokiahi has been sent on the mission, however some unexpected things have begun to happen.SettingsControl Rokiahi in an Action + Rail Shooter + Third Person game. Graphics fully 3D with Anime themes plus Moeness.WARNINGThis game is hard!
