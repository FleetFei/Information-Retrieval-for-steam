
							About This Game
							Philia is a myst like "point & click" adventure game, the sequel to the game Elansar.
You play Elina and your husband is now in danger!
Help Elina to save the love of her life through the mystery of her subconscious !
Explore three worlds and solve over 15 puzzles by trying to understand how they work.
This game is available in French and English.
