
							About This Game
							Welcome to HVRGUN, the worlds best Assault Pilot Training program. Where you'll be placed directly into a virtual battlefield and test your ability to dodge, shoot, and destroy an endless onslaught of enemies! This program will place you in a variety of environments with an even wider arsenal of weaponry at your disposal, ensuring you're ready for any situation. Of course, you'll have to first prove yourself before you're able to obtain our top tier weaponry.
