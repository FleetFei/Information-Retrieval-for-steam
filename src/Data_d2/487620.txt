
							About This Game

Find the perfect angle by rotating the camera to clean up the chaos VIRTUAL REALITYTo enjoy this game you need a compatible VR device: OCULUS or HTC vive.REAL OPTICAL ILLUSIONSNothing is what it seems! Test your visual acuity by accepting a greater challenge each time.STUNNING GRAPHICS AND SETTINGPenetrate the mind of the protagonist immersed in a seductive atmosphere. Original soundtrack Translated into 11 languages: English, Spanish, French, Italian, German, Portuguese, Chinese, Russian, Korean, Japanese and Catalan. Unique graphics and setting.
