
							About This Content
							You can play up to two players, play with your friends, 
turn it on at a party. Each time the mini-game gets faster.

Bit Defense:
Defend the biggest barrier against gunfire.

Space Travel:
Dodge the space objects and shoot the meteorites.

Balloon Pof:
Aim and shoot the balloons.

Bug on Machine:
Prevent insects invade the machine.

Bounce Ball:
Defend all sides of the area, do not let the ball escape.
