
							About This Game
							Meet Chaos Theory! A balanced mix of arcade gameplay and challenging physics-based puzzle solving.The main objective in Chaos Theory is to fill "Collectors" with magnetically charged particles. Those particles move freely in the environment and are influenced by other objects and their magnetic forces. Attract free particles, bind them to objects, and shoot them onto the Collectors to finish a level.Physics-based puzzlerMore than 50 LevelsIncludes Steam AchievementsLevel editor: Build your own levels and share them with your friendsTutorials that introduce every new game element
