
							About This Game
							Experience platforming like never before as you leap through surreal environments and discover all new challenges in the newest VR game, TO THE TOP!

Our main goal is to make a fun and engaging experience that will make game players come back for more and more. Our control mechanics are simple and intuitive but includes depth to get better with each game session. 

To The Top is an independent project created by Dan Dunham and Richard Matey.
