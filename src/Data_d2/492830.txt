
							About This Game
							Throne Rushers is an action/platform 2D game where the goal is to reach the throne on the top of the map in first. If you die, you begin again at the beginning of the map ! - Solo and Online. - Fast and fun gameplay. - Big worlds generated randomly. - Two online modes : Conquest and Flags. - Until 30 players per servers. - Character personalisation. - Leveling.
