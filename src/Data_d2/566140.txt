
							About This Game
							Holo-Graham is a strategic puzzle game based in a 4D World!Record your holo-grahams to perform actions in real time and work along side them to solve complex puzzles.The game consists of 20 hand made, and thoroughly tested levels.Figure out the series of switches and time their activations to compete the level within the alotted time of your power bar. If your power bar depletes, your holo-grahams malfunction.
