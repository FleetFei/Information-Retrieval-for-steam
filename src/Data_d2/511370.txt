
							About This Game
							Go down into the depths of the Fire God to retrieve back the stolen fire gems!Wrath of the Fire God is an arcadey VR rail shooter.  The Fire God's army is trying to stop you from retrieving the 4 magical gems stolen from your village.  Unleash your magical arrows on the enemies, dodge enemy projectiles and recover the lost gems!Features
  Highly immersive VR experience.  No need for teleporting or joystick to move through the level.  Burn calories trying to destroy the barrage of enemies spawned by the Fire God.  Get on the leaderboards with the highest rank by killing and collecting the most enemies!  Use special arrows (ice, fire, poison, lightning, etc.) to kill multiple enemies with one shot.  4 levels with 4 relentless bosses.
