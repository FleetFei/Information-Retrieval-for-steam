
							About This Game
							The desolate moon of Lorian V is a place of curiosity, history, and terror. Awaken on the surface from a long slumber and explore the winding caverns below to uncover secrets, acquire new abilities and face powerful foes. Meet the sleepy, stranded inhabitants of Lorian V and hear their tragic stories. Only by doing all of this will you learn-- remember-- the truth. Challenging and rewarding "Metroidvania" gameplay Explore a large, connected, lonely world of secrets and discoveries Precise, responsive controls Lonely, isolated theme Fully voiced NPCs Acquire new powers and weapons to discover completely optional hidden areas Feed and raise your pet into mysterious evolutions with varying effects Touching story of family, courage, redemption and closure
