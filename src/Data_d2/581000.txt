
							About This Game
							Remember childhood? When I am sitting at the TV playing in Battle City on NES? It is time to return this time!Armored Gear - a spiritual successor Batlle City. The game takes the best of the classic game and extending new modern features!In Armored Gear you'll just kill enemies. You will face different problems. For example - to capture the enemy headquarters, security settlements, the destruction of enemy warehouses. In addition, passing the mission, you'll earn not only points, but also money. With the aid of which you will be able to improve your tank or headquarter.Also in the game has a co-op mode for up to two players.The plot in the game is not convoluted. Your tank - Armored Gear. The latest military development. Its key feature is the AI, which itself controls the equipment and is able itself to modernize itself. You will face different challenges that you need to awake to solve and prove the power of Armored Gear.Game features:Old school graphics;Music style: chiptune, industrial metal and Nintendokore;Ability to improve the tank to your taste, adjusting it to the situation;Ability to co-operative play for up to two players;Fully destructible environments;Simple and fun gameplay;
