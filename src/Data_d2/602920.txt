
							About This Game
							Once upon a time a school-boy got a copy-book. But it was not a usual one! Anything you draw in this copy-book becomes alive. And so did Liny! The romantic character, who wants to learn more about the world and become free! 

But it is not so easy. He has to overcome numerous obstacles. Water or an eraser can destroy him. So, sharpen your pencil and draw! Do not let Liny stop on his way towards a dream! 


A hundred of wonderful levels is waiting for you: you will be able to relax and enjoy!
