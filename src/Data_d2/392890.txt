
							About This Game
							King of the Road is back! Trucker 2 lets you sit once again behind the wheel of a truck and race on American highways.Player can choose order of his missions. There are nearly 60 of them. You can chose one of 5 different trucks which can be upgraded and modified in your garage. Road's surroundings are destructible, and the damage is visible on cars. Damage affects car handling.Features:60 levels5 different types of trucks3 difficulty levelsrealistic physic based on Nvidia PhysX enginevisible car damageLarge, beautiful locationsTuningVarying day time and weather conditionsPower-upsTraffic and policeability to use custom textures and HUDsteering wheels and game pad support
