
							About This Content
							This is the remix soundtrack for Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED, created by OverClocked ReMix!  It contains 30 tracks, covering all zones 1 through 5, in both MP3 and FLAC format!

NOTE: This DLC does *NOT* include the Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED soundtrack songs from A_Rival, FamilyJules7X, Virt, Girlfriend Records, or Danny Baranowsky.

ALSO NOTE: Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED is still in Early Access, so this OST may be updated in the future!
