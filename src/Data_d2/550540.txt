
							About This Content
							In the Ottoman Empire campaign  you will take part in the famous battle of Gallipoli on the Turkish army side . 
Under the command of Mustafa Kemal, you'll defend the peninsula from a  large-scale naval landing of Entente forces in various parts of the frontline - from the sea coast to the heights of Gallipoli. 
The campaign includes a diverse set of operations - both large-scale infantry battles and quiet reconnaissance missions.
