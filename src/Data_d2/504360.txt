
							About This Game
							Rage of the Battlemage is a dynamic hack'n'slash game, where you take control over Lorthan, the last living battlemage in the world covered in darkness, evil and brutality. Your goal is to defend those taken by demons vast lands and send their generals straight to hell. You will be forced to learn how demons fight, how to take terrain to your adventage and upgrade your skills. Save all the lands - forests, ice lands and even volcanic terrain and more, where evil grows strong. Remember to watch 
out for deadly traps. Face many kind of enemies, deadly bosses and dive into the rage of the Battlemage! 

Lorthan is a last living battlemag, as well as the most talented of all. He can use all elements magic - fire, water, earth and air. But as he is too much proud he doesn't use air element magic. It serves him only to clean his lawn from leaves. Through gaining experience and knowlage, Lorthan has discoverd, not known to anyone else, the fifth element "Ethernal Ether" which allows him to heal his wounds. As a first mage, he used fifth element in the battle. The fate of the world rests on his 
shoulders. Bravely he stands to fight with demons of hell and monsters coming out of the ground! 

Support him with your own abilities, tactical approach to the fight and desire to victory! Do not give up when you fall! Grab the sword and wand moving forward with rage on the enemy.
