
							About This Content
							These additional resources will open up even more options for your story-telling and are fully compatible with the High Fantasy Resource Bundle. Just add the resources to your game-making engine and you are ready to go!The focus of The Deep expansion pack is on Dwarves and Orcs. This expansion supplies a number of Dwarves, including female Dwarves and soldiers. There are a variety of Orcs as well, from the lowly peon to the Stone, Flame, and Frost tribes. This pack contains:80 Battlers (40 front-view, 40 side-view)64 Dwarf and Orc Character Sprite Sheets!40 Faces (5 full sets!)3 Tile sheets to add on to the High Fantasy Resource Bundle5 Additional Battle BackgroundsBonus script for RPG Maker VX Ace!
