
							About This Game
							An action/adventure game taking ques from both film-noir and samurai-drama's of old, "samurai_jazz" takes players on an adventure through an enigmatic city, engaging in  sword-fights, encounters with mythological beings, and quirky dialog with the local inhabitants presented with a pseudo 8-bit style. While action remains the central focus, a bit of detective work and puzzle-solving will become necessary while adventuring in this pixelated nightmare. Features:Do battle with enemy swordsmen and demonsAdventure through an enigmatic city and interact with it's citizensPresented with 8-bit graphics and electro-jazz soundtrackSupports Xbox 360 Controller
