
							About This Game
							Asylopole is a satirical old-school adventure game set in a dystopian future. You play the role of Adam, a psychiatrist working in a gigantic asylum.Get inside your patients’ heads to find out what’s gone wrong. Experience totally new universes and fight your patients’ worst nightmares...Features:Classic style Adventure with alternative gameplay elementsNon-linear2D hand drawn graphics, rotoscoping, stop-motion80's feelingVaried gameplay: exploration, puzzles, fights,...Quests, mini-games, network (Ultranet),...Created mostly by one personAdjustable difficultyControllers supportGain EXP and increase your statsEat nice Fastbouf burgers ( look at that sauce dripping down ! )
