
							About This Content
							Logan had lived a long and violent life when he was kidnapped for a secret government program - Weapon X. Scientists bonded unbreakable adamantium to his bones, relying on his mutant healing factor to keep the experiment from killing him. After escaping, Professor X recruited him to the X-Men as Wolverine.
