
							About This Content
							Mary Shelley's Frankenstein™ (1995): is a deep and atmospheric table designed by John Borg. This Sega produced table, released in 1995, features interesting modal gameplay, spectacular music and a fully animated Frankenstein Monster that actually throws the balls across the playfield during multiball. Mary Shelley's Frankenstein™ was also one of the only pinball tables to use an oversized 192x64 pixel DMD. 3000 units of this table were produced.
