
							About This Game
							Estranged: Act II follows the story of a fisherman, stranded on a mysterious island during a violent storm.The game continues after the events of Estranged: Act I, which followed our hero's adventure in a search for a way to get home.Experience what the island has in store as you uncover the secretive Arque Corporation, burrowing ever deeper into their dangerous and haphazard operations.Key FeaturesAdventure game with puzzle elementsImmersive story driven environmentsUnique characters and narrativeFamiliar first person shooter gameplay
