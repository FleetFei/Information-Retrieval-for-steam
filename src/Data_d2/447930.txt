
							About This Game
							LYDIA: SWEET DREAMS is a fast paced, intense reflex game, which challenges players to survive and defeat waves of enemies in its vibrantly colorful world. 
In Lydia: Sweet Dreams, player takes control of Lydia, whose life has been taken by the witches, and is now trapped in their realm. Attempting to prevent the witches from taking more unaware souls of The Living, Lydia goes on her dangerous journey.
