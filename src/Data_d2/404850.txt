
							About This Game
							DARTHY wakes up in the hospital and soon finds out he must release the souls of the robots imprisoned in the Gold Doubloons (Gold Coins) by the Giant Space Pirates. Help DARTHY Roll, Bounce, Crash, Fly, and Die to free the unfortunate souls.DARTHY is a Simple Pixel Platformer based on old school mechanics. Made with my kids Kaleb & Jeya play testing.FEATURES◉ 10+ Buildings w/ Old School Checkpoints ◉ Pixel Art ◉ Double Jump & Fireball◉ Collect Coins to help the Robots ◉ Real-time physics◉ Prepare to die.
