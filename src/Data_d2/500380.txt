
							About This Game
							"Fluffy!", an experimental VR short, is pure camp horror with a script based loosely on the iconic 80's horror film "An American Werewolf in London". A surrealistic motel is imagined in the world of the original film, starring a peculiar bellmen (played by Michael Donaldson)who's obsessed with "pets'. Run length is 1 minute 30 seconds.
