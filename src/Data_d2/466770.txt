
							About This Game
							In Ghost Croquet one player controls the hero and tries to beat the level. Meanwhile, the other players take control of the monsters and possess the traps, trying to stop him. As the hero dies, his most successful adversary takes his role - trying to get to the end of the level.Features:Local (split-screen) and online multiplayer.Exploding drones and lasers, with some screenshake.You can pretend you're just a dumb AI, then perform a surprising attack!Hero upgrades - ranging from a dazzlingly stylish top hat to the iconic double jump.Level editor integrated with Steam Workshop.
