
							About This Content
							The amazing pulse pounding soundtrack for Horizon Shift, produced by Jason Heine of Heine House Entertainment. 
These original compositions are a fusion of House, Electronica and Chiptune.

Tracklist:

1. Plasma Engine
2. Ascending Node
3. Solar Flare
4. Destiny Wave
5. Flight Departure
6. Meteor Shower
7. Boss Battle
8. Bonus Stage
9. Credits

The Horizon Shift soundtrack will be downloaded to your computer as MP3 files. You must have Horizon Shift installed on your computer as well to receive the DLC content.
