
							About This Content
							The Green Deck is an expansion to SUPERFIGHT that adds over 80 G-Rated cards to the core deck.  The Green Deck contains some of the most powerful and whimsical cards in the game and challenges players' imaginations.The Green Deck comes complete with new Achievements, Titles, and Art to unlock!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Green Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.
