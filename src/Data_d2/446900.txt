
							About This Content
							As if zombies, mutants, and aliens weren't trouble enough, everyday humans are now causing trouble on the streets.

Help the re-established Swansea Police Force deal with this troublesome humans by repairing and renovating their police station, and by supplying them with a Policeman's Lounge at your hotel for them to relax after work.

Features:

 - A new bar at your hotel just for police officers!
 - A new world map area with Swansea Police Station.
 - Build beds, cells and supply closets in the station to help boost the security of the city.
 - Be gifted the 'Police Issue Desert Eagle' the strongest pistol in the city!
 - As the city becomes more secure watch out for police stashes on the world map containing all sorts of goodies.
