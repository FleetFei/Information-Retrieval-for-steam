
							About This Content
							This is Crypt of the NecroDancer's Extended Soundtrack 2, which includes 40 epic retro and synthwave remixes by Virt and Girlfriend Records!  Both MP3 and FLAC files are included.

NOTE: This DLC does *NOT* include the original soundtrack songs from Danny Baranowsky, nor the original Extended Soundtrack by FamilyJules7x and A_Rival.
