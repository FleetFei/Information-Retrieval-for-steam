
							About This Game
							With an internet crisis looming and a viral video game holding its players hostage, Sam & Max will need to slip into a new reality to set things right. But can the Freelance Police crack this virtual case in time to avoid worldwide system failure?One of six self-contained cases with an overarching story arc.Simple point & click interface with a low learning curve.Catchy jazz soundtrack featuring live performers and over three hours of original music.Intuitive gameplay that appeals to new players as well as adventure-game diehards.And it's funny!
