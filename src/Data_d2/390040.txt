
							About This Game
							Descend into abandoned factories in search of lost cassette tapes that hold boundless virtual worlds. Explore, manipulate, and distort the tapes to make them reveal their secrets. Solve mechanical puzzles and map the winding halls of these empty monoliths. Will you be able to find the way out, or what has happened?Key FeaturesCollect: More than 30 different tapes and distorted versions to explore.Listen: Distinctive soundtrack that changes along with each tape that is warped.Search: Find items within the tapes to help you continue on your journey.
