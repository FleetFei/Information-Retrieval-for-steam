
							About This Game
							If you like total freedom in the way you conquer the enemy this is the ultimate war game for you. Build freeform constructions as a medieval Builder, fight in sword duels as a Knight or snipe with your bow as an Archer. KAG blends the cooperative aspects of Lost Vikings, mashes them with the full destructibility of Worms and the visual style and action of Metal Slug, brought to you by the creators of Soldat. Build Defenses With Freeform Building Destroy the enemy castles with physics and explosives Freedom to move by land, water, dig tunnels or fly in the air Use the environment to burn or drown enemies Interact with a living animal and plant world Drive siege machines with your teammates Fight with 32 unpredictable humans in multiplayer Freely change to one of 3 distinct classes Singleplayer, cooperative and competitive game modes Customize with 60+ unique heads Create your own adventure with community created mods & scripts
