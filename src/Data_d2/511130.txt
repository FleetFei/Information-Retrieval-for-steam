
							About This Content
							To allow commanders build up a more diverse and powerful fleet, this DLC will release a special giant warship–the Titan!

Content
1.Unlocks a giant warship, “Titan”.
2.Unlocks a device, “Torpedo IV”.
3.Gains 200 Khorium.

Titan is a giant warship in “Galaxy Reavers”. Like a great castle, it possesses great defense as well as highest CPU value in game. Along with its most enormous space for devices, the full power Titian is nightmare to the enemy fleet!

In addition, there is 200 Gold in this DLC as well, allowing commanders to purchase more powerful warships and devices!
