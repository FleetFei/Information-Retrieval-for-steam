
							About This Game
							Designed solely for virtual reality (VR), Fractal brings first-person real-time combat to beautiful stylized VR vistas. Enter a dazzling dimension where color is the lifeblood of all beings. Wielding a mysterious brush, you are granted the power to reshape the world and fate of its denizens. Experience intuitive fast-paced combat inspired by the simple motion of painting. Master the rhythmic flows of battles and vanquish foes to evolve your powers based on your playstyle. The world of Fractal awaits, how will you forge its destiny?
