
							About This Game
							Treehouse Basketball is a basketball simulator designed for the HTC Vive. Shoot free throws or play a quick 30-second timed challenge and see how many baskets you can sink. Play on a regulation size court with realistic sounds and physics. This is just the beginning; More features and polish will be added over time!Key Features   Designed for room-scale VR with motion controllers   Free throw and timed game modes   Indoor court with accurate scale using regulation measurements   Realistic sound and physics simulationsAbout TreehouseTreehouse brings affordable technology education to people everywhere in order to help them achieve their dreams and change the world. In our Beginner VR Track you'll learn how to create this game, Treehouse Basketball, and develop the skills you need to get in on the ground floor of the booming VR industry.
