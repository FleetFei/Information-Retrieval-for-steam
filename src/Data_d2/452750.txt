
							About This Video
							A stylish and action-packed adaptation of the landmark Marvel comic. Undercover FBI agent Frank Castle (Thomas Jane) is emotionally devastated when his wife and children are killed after witnessing a mafia hit. Vowing vengeance, Castle becomes "The Punisher," a one-man judge, jury, and executioner who declares open season on the criminal underworld in this taut and heart-pounding thriller in the DARK KNIGHT tradition, co-starring John Travolta, Samantha Mathis, and Rebecca Romjin.
