
							About This Game
							Arkham Nightmares is an adventure game with rogue-like elements. You play Prof. Liebwerk the head physician of the Lovecraftian Arkham Sanatorium. Your task is to complete your journal with all the stories from the crazy inmates. But before they will tell you anything you have to win their trust.During the night you will be haunted by terrible nightmares. Try to avoid those horrors or they will drive you insane. On the other hand... a little insanity might help to convince the patients to tell their stories.Feautres:Innovative genre-mix of adventure, rogue-like and stealth gameplay1920s setting with eerie atmosphere in the Lovecraftian universe    A big island to explore    Multiple-choice dialogs    Replayability    Voice-over    Jazz soundtrack    Day-Night cycle    Adjustable difficulty    Gamepad support
