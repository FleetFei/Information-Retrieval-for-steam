
							About This Content
							This is the soundtrack for Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED, created by the award winning composer, Danny Baranowsky!  It contains 4 new tracks, including 5-1, 5-2, 5-3, and FortissiMole!

NOTE: This DLC does *NOT* include the Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED soundtrack songs from A_Rival, FamilyJules7X, Virt, Girlfriend Records, or OCRemix.

ALSO NOTE: Crypt of the NecroDancer: AMPLIFIED is still in Early Access, so this OST may be updated in the future!
