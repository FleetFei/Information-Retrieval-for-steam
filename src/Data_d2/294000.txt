
							About This Game
							Knight Squad is an insane 1 to 8 players chaotic arena friendship destroyer. Take on current friends and future frenemies in any of the incredibly fun game modes, ranging from Last Man Standing to Medieval soccer. All of that using only weapons from the middle age… and laser guns… and miniguns.Features1-8 Players game packed with crazy party action A bunch of levels are available for each game mode. You're not enough players to fill in the spots? No worries. Bots will jump in. Select their difficulty depending on the amount of challenge you'd like and you're ready to go! Online MultiplayerPlay against other players around the world and compare your skills! A Bunch of Versus Game Modes Team Capture the Flag, Last Man Standing, Capture the Grail, Soccer, and more! Fetch deadly new weapons and power ups on the battlefield  Grab your weapon of choice : sword, bow and arrows, crossbow, bombs, laser gun and more!ChallengesTake on the very difficult challenge levels and make your way to the top of the leaderboard!
