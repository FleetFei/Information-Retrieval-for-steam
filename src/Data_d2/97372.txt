
							About This Content
							Odric’s fully unlocked “Peacekeepers” white deck. Odric is a mighty warrior from Innistrad, where humanity is under constant assault from the terrors of the night. As formidable as he is with a sword, Odric’s real strength is his army. His soldiers are seasoned veterans, ready for battle against werewolves, vampires, zombies, or anything else your opponent might marshal up. Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013.
