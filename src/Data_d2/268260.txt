
							About This Game
							Let’s Sing: The Karaoke for PC. Let’s Sing PC is your chance to bring out your inner-star and pose as Lady Gaga, Maroon 5, Lana Del Rey, Flo Rida, Bruno Mars, Avril Lavigne, LMFAO and many others…Pump up the volume and party in your living-room with friends and family!  Plug in two mikes at the same time and give your best performance singing duos. And surprise everyone when you share your performances on Facebook! The built-in voice recognition feature will allow everyone to have fun and improve their singing skills, whatever their level... So, what are you waiting for?
