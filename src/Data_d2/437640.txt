
							About This Content
							The Starter pack contains a variety of useful items and a special currency for purchases in the in-game store (GXP). With this kit you will be able to extend your potential and get a new interesting experience, with the high variety of the items from the store. Buying the pack you will get a set of items at discount! The GXP will be added to your account immediately.
