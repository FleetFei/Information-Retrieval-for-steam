
							About This Game
							Chicken Assassin - Master of Humiliation - Epic Action RPG! Full of sarcastic and hilarious characters, rich artwork and beautifully structured class/inventory/wardrobe/upgrade/achievement systems to make it a joy to play.Features:*Epic missions and boss raids (Follow the story of Mean McAllister through various missions, plus jump in some of the dangerous boss raids while on the path) *Large inventory system (Over 450+ unique items & 9 combat stances)*Combat and resource upgrades (Are you all out berserker who only cares about fights? or you also like to upgrade your resources? -it's your choice)*Wardrobe (dress Mean McAllister the you want! Various possibilities for different builds)*Achievements (Get rewarded with souls by performing various actions)*Black Market (Don't feel like selling your junk items straight away? Then keep them for black market!)
