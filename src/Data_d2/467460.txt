
							About This Game
							You are a hungry zombie who flies on the dead pterodactyl! Eat brains, bones, liver and heart as much as you can. Relax is impossible because the fat dead guy on the flying carpet with the bio-cannons will haunt you until death. However you will visit the Mushroom Kingdom, the city of the future and even other planets. There you'll meet the dead homeless people, mosquitoes with napalm, rabid squirrels and you will be able fly into black holes. Crazy fun arcade game will give you the impressions at 16 different levels.
