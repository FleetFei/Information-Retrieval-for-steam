
							About This Game
							For the game'You have to escape from this insane place.' You've lost your memory and are trapped in a dark hospital. You don't even know why.. Look around carefully with your imagination and reasoning to find clues that will help you to escape this place.Find useful stuffs through exploration to stay alive and to escape from the hospital, or to solve a problem and puzzle.You will see why you are trapped here and be aware of the hidden secrets of this terrible hospital.Oculus Rift DK2 required to playAbandoned hospital requires an Oculus Rift DK2 HMD (SDK 0.8) to run.FeaturesSensible fear - full of fear and tension in VRInteraction with any environmental objects – Unique and challenging puzzles that might be interacted with environmental objects.VR Support HTC Vive and Oculus Rift consumer version will be supported by updates.Full Steam platform support – Includes Steam Achievements, Steam Cloud Save, Trading Cards, Leaderboards, Big Picture Mode support.Expanded controller support – In addition to supporting the traditional keyboard and mouse control scheme, the game natively supports Xbox 360, Xbox One, Steam Controller.
