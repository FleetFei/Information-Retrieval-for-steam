
							About This Game
							SPLIT BULLET is a game all about 'splitting up' a massive crowd of enemies—yes, those polygons—crawling towards you. Don't let 'em get you and survive as long as you can, by using your instincts and weapons.Features:Minimalistic game designInsane amount of enemies coming out from all sidesMassive amount of bullets splitting around everywhereVarious weapons to choose from, and multiple stages for you to tackleTest your limit in Endless Mode!
