
							About This Video
							Upon reaching the train station to death, a dejected soul is given another chance at life. Placed in the body of a 14-year-old boy named Makoto, who has just killed himself and watched over by a neutral spirit, the soul must identify his greatest mistake in his former life before time in Makoto's body expires. He also has other duties he must complete, such as understanding Makoto's suicide and enjoying his second chance at life. Give second chances a try in Colorful: The Movie.
