
							About This Content
							Own the landscape and keep your foes at a distance with three of the greatest sniper rifles ever made. All three rifles can be used in any multiplayer, singleplayer or co-operative game mode in Sniper Elite 3.This DLC pack includes:Karabiner 98K rifle: one of the last rifles produced by German arms giant, Mauser, and highly sought after by both Axis and Allied forces for its all round high performance.M1917 Enfield rifle: known as the “American Enfield” this crucial sharpshooter was shipped across Europe and North Africa to assist Allied troops, boasting a higher zoom and lower recoil than other rifles.Springfield rifle (tuned): this variation of the American classic has been tuned to increase both its damage and muzzle velocity, making it one the most powerful rifles available.
