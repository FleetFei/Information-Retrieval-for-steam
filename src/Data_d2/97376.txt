
							About This Content
							Yeva’s fully unlocked “Ancient Wilds” green deck. Yeva is a nature shaman from Ravnica, a world covered in city. Yeva’s mystical connection to the wild runs deep. All creatures in Ravnica’s forests heed her call. Elves and elementals alike fight for Yeva in her struggle against the ever-expanding city. Harness Yeva’s powerful army to defeat your opponents!
