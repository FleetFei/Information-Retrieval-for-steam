
							About This Game
							Historium, the historical visitor attraction on the market square of Bruges, recreated Bruges in the Middle Ages through Virtual Reality. The VR experiences takes the user on an immersive journey along the trading routes in the 15the century. You can sail, row, fly, explore and interact to learn about the history of Bruges! This experience is now available on digital VR distribution platforms and lasts around 20 to 40 minutes.
