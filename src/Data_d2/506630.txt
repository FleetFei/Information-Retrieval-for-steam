
							About This Game
							Unfazed is a fascinating 3D FPS Game that allows maximum control of a competent combatant in a hostile enviromment. The gamer can fully utilize a great variety of up to 25 different weapons including rifles, semi-automatic guns, grenades, knives, revolvers and DMRs. He fights his way through zombies and giant spiders among others to emerge victorious. As the game offers unlimited levels there is both endless thrill adventure for both the experienced gamer and the first time rookie!
