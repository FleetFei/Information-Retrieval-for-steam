
							About This Game
							AirMech Command is the definitive Action/RTS experience in VR. With all new tactical room-space controls AND ground-level flight controls, you can command troops and units from orbit, or grab the stick and pilot your AirMech directly. Play Solo, Co-Op, or PVP with dozens of 'Mechs, customizable armies, and unlockable vanity items. Every map is a new challenge, whether running missions to unlock new 'Mechs, or battling your friends. Capture bases to take more ground. Build tanks, turrets, and soldiers then grab and deploy them with cutting edge hand controls that make RTS gameplay smoother than ever. With Vive room-space, you're right in the middle of the action.Want to pull the trigger yourself? Instantly enter your AirMech and pilot it directly.
