
							About This Content
							Endless version randomly choosed levels.

Stay on the platform as long as you can, on your way there will be many obstacles and rogue ninja's. Dodge these obstacles to increase your score. 

Game controls: 
-Keyboard and Mouse- 
left mouse button: Jump 
or 
up arrow key: Jump 
backspace: Throw Kunai 

-Joystick- 
A= Jump 
Y= Throw Kunai
