
							About This Game
							BackgroundLieutenant Ivan Sakharov at the head of the "Dark Project". "Dark Project" has been the murder of the last German soldiers in the USSR.StoryMay 2, 1945.The whole squad killed, eaten, torn to pieces, but you, you survived. You are the only living witness to the horror of what happened. You single-handedly break through the forest and stumble upon the allied bunker. In the hope of a warm welcome, you're not thinking long, come in. The door closes and you become another victim of the terror that awaits you ahead. Will you survive? Will you find out? Will you manage to solve the mystery of this nightmare?FeaturesRealistic GraphicsPerfect Background MusicScary Atmosphere
