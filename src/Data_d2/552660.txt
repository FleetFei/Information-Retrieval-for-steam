
							About This Game
							Alert! Alert! -- Captain, Moonbase Six is being attacked again. Get outside and kill as many as you can before time runs out.

8-Bit Arena is a fast paced gallery shooter where you try to get the highest score in the given time limit. 


Controls:

Left mouse button - Shoot/Hold to Accept
W,S - Move Forwards and Backwards
A,D - Strafe Left and Right
ESC - Exit Game


Music created by Pascal Tatipata
