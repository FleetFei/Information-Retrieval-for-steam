
							About This Game
							Wake is the story of an engineer trapped on a sinking ship. Navigate through the ship however you want to find your way to the surface above, but avoid the ship's corrupted security system!Key Features Run and swim to escape a sinking ship Dodge bullets, fire, electricity and LASERS - but most of all avoid drowning! Randomised areas change the ship every time Tense, close-quarters gameplay Collect keys, torches and other items to stay alive Unravel the truth behind the sinking of the ship Multiple difficulty modes and end-game challenges Compete against others with online scoreboards Xbox 360 and standard controller support Includes full MP3 and FLAC soundtrack
