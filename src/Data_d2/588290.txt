
							About This Game
							Feel the thrill of being a goalie - catch, stop and block slap shots in this fun VR hockey game short for the HTC Vive. Built by a hockey goalie for other goalies that are either too far away from ice or can't get enough of the action.Fully immersive with surround sound and haptic vibration for realistic gameplay.Multiple levels of difficulty, sit or stand, lefties and righties welcome.HTC Vive compatible.FREE TO PLAY!
