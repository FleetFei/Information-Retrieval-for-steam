
							About This Game
							Become the self-proclaimed best detective in the world in “Detective Hank and the Golden Sneeze”, a steampunk themed, interactive whodunnit visual-novel with a different culprit on each playthrough! You play as Hank, tasked with finding the culprit who stole the Golden Sneeze.Features of the gameHigh replay value; multiple people who could have stolen the Golden Sneeze.Multiple ways for the story to runAlternative paths for Detective Hank to take, all with different cluesA total of 9 different endings, depending on your witsA steampunk world captured in cartoon art by Studio EnalyaAtmosphere brought to life with a unique soundtrack by upcoming music talent Storytime SymphonyMake your brain work in this puzzle adventure and solve the mystery of who stole the Golden Sneeze!
