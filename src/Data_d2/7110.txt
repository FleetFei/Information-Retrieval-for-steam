
							About This Game
							Step into the role of an aspiring martial-arts master and follow the path of the open palm or the closed fist.  In this multi-award-winning action-RPG, your choices and actions will determine the fate of the entire Jade Empire.  Will you prevent the destruction of this beautiful land, or will you crush it beneath your heel?  Are you a warrior who uses his strength and fighting mastery to bring peace, or will you instead use your power to bring pain and ruin?  					The choice is yours.
