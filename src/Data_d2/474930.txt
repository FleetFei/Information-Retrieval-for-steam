
							About This Content
							The Mindcrack Deck is an expansion to SUPERFIGHT that adds over 90 new cards created by your favorite Mindcrack streamers for all their fans. Now you can finally know who wins: Aureylian with a glorious beard, or Guude wearing a dinosaur costume.The Mindcrack Deck comes complete with new Achievements to unlock!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Mindcrack Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.
