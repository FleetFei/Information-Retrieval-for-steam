
							About This Content
							Journey to a galaxy far, far away to team up with the Ghost's crew, Hera, Kanan, Ezra, Zeb, Sabine and Chopper, as they join forces to take on the evil Empire in this table based on the Star Wars Rebels™ TV show! 

Table features:
- A fully animated Ghost starship and TIE fighter models
- A flaming engine thruster spindisk
- Awesome mini playfield dedicated to Chopper
- Unique Force™-operated ball locker mechanism 
- Ezra Bridger, Kanan Jarrus, Chopper, The Inquisitor and a stormtrooper appear as fully animated 3D characters
- Seven exciting main modes inspired by the show plus three different multiball modes
