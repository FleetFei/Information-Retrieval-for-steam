
							About This Game
							Elems is an arcade logic game and its goal is to collect the Orb of Life Shards, scattered across 32 levels in 4 zones: Hellish, Icy, Stone and Water zone. While playing, Imp Guardian (the main hero) has to collect all Orb Shards on the level to proceed to the next one.Features: 4 zones32 levelsAbout thousand Orb of Life Shards to collectGravity, Teleports, Energo BarriersRockfalls!Play and think!
