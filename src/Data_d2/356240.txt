
							About This Content
							There is no denying that horror and survival are two themes deeply ingrained in modern games.Frontier Works: Horror Interior Tiles is an incredible new pack containing modern tiles that will find the perfect place in many horror games. One of the most appealing things about this pack is the inclusion of Japanese-inspired material - perfect for all Japanese horror movie enthusiasts! With this pack, you can create inner maps that range from slightly creepy to truly horrifying.Features:A smooth, painterly style with a variety of fine details.Tiles for creating decrepit/abandoned rooms, hospitals and torture chambers.Possessed objects, forbidden experiments, blood, gore and more!Partial Tile A4, partial tile A5 and full Tiles B and C.
