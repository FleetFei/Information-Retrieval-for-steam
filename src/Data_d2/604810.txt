
							About This Game
							In the far-away cold country there lives a Yeti. Every day he is trapped by dangers, whether it be an eagle or penguins. All prevent it to live quietly. Find a shelter and survive in the cold world. In a game there are two modes, one for children, and the second with logical riddles. Solve all riddles and please Yeti. It is a lot of snow and beautiful levels added with interesting music.
