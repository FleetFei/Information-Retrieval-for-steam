
							About This Game
							The house of a rabbit is attacked by reptiles from another dimension, and they prepare occupation of the planet by means of portals. The rabbit in panic decides to gather all the carrots, and further to exchange for the weapon for revenge. Whether the rabbit will be able to fight back evil reptiles and to return the house?Features: You should avoid reptiles and to gather carrots. To pass through enemy portals reducing a way. To win the house using the weapon of a rabbit.
