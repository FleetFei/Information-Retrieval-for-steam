
							About This Game
							Hikikomori No Chuunibyou is a pseudo 8-bit platform-adventure game with beat-em-up, puzzle, and stealth elements. With its storytelling directing the player through open-ended levels, progression through each stage requires usage of parkour-like abilities and martial-arts techniques.Features:    Parkour-inspired platforming    Open-ended levels with story-focused progression    Beat-em-up, puzzle, and stealth elements    Unlimited continues and frequent checkpoints    8-bit graphics and soundtrack    Supports Xbox 360 Controller (recommended)
