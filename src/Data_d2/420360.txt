
							About This Game
							Spectrum is a platform game set in an abstract world of stylized shapes and bright colors. Control a dark three-eyed entity attempting to reach portals while avoiding unforgiving obstacles and elements. Escape from 80 levels spread in 8 mysterious environments each with their own unique identities.

Master gravity by acing exclusive endless jump and dive mechanics to make it to the end. 

Be swift, agile and smart for a chance to complete all three objectives on each levels. 

FEATURES 

- Colorful minimalist aesthetic
- 80 challenging levels with 3 objectives to complete on each
- 8 appealing environments
- Ambient IDM Soundtrack
- Achievements & Leaderboards
