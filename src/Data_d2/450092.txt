
							About This Content
							Created by Riley D. Owens (aka Darkwoulfe), this product consist of 1524 240 X 240 pixel top-down character tokens, 24 uniquely designed and 1500 suitable for fighters, clerics or paladins. Also included is a 480 X 480 pixel Feral Ogre. They are suitable for use with any Fantasy roleplaying gameVolume 1 includes:Cleric with 100 different head choices, each with a different selection of weapon, or weapon and shield (15 choices each)Large Feral Ogre24 Heroes and Villains that are uniqueRequires: An active subscription or license of Fantasy Grounds. It will work with any ruleset.
