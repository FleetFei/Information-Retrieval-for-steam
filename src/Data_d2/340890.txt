
							About This Game
							Unhack is a visual novel x puzzle game that explores the nature of greed. Destroy the 5K Worm alongside fully voiced AI companions! Features the voices of Xanthe Huynh, Skyler Davenport and Aimee Smith. Xanthe's voice can also be heard in series such as Sword Art Online, K-On! and many more.

Illustrations: Hybridmink
Music: Matt Hamm
