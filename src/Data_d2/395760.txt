
							About This Software
							Decent Icons is a Game Launcher for Windows that gives you complete creative freedom. Create icons for games and software from any screenshot, wallpaper or box art image, no photo editing required! Features: Create icons of any size or shape Use any screenshot, wallpaper, box or fan art image (.JPG or .PNG with Transparency support) Easily scale and crop your image with Edit ModeCustomizable options include: auto startup (without steam), highlight, titles, overlays, vignette, bevel, reflections, etc Add a few of your favorite games, or scroll through a larger library with scroll modeContinuous development and community involvementPlanned Future Updates: Multiple RowsTabsMany more based on feedback from the communityMy hope is that we can all build something together that lets people get creative and have fun with their desktop.
