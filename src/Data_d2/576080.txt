
							About This Game
							ABOUT THE GAME

Tracks of Triumph: Good Old Times is a third game in the series with a completely new setting, tons of new enemies and exciting levels. The game is set almost 100 years ago and your tracks must clear the city streets from enemy tanks.

YOUR GOAL

Your goal in this game is to eliminate all the enemies before they eliminate you. You have a big choice of weapons and a lot of different enemies to test those weapons.

WHAT'S NEW?

By destroying enemies you collect experience which you use to level up. That gives you more health and makes it easier for you to pass the levels.
