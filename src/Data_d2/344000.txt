
							About This Game
							This demo allows you to play the whole of the first 'depth' of the game.Play as Doug McGrave, famous demon hunter for hire. Highest level, bestest gear, ruggedly handsome, and unrepentant jerk. For refusing to aid one stinking demon-infested town, you are immediately – and deservedly – cursed. Your powers, your wealth, and your weapons… Poof! Gone. To overcome the curse, you’ll need to make use of whatever weapons you can find.Swords, chainsaws, machine guns, pogo sticks… Use them while you can.Because they’ll all fall apart in your hands.
