
							About This Game
							Sam & Max: Episode 2 - Situation: Comedy - Talk show host Myra Stump has gone berzerk! Sam & Max head down to the WARP TV studio to find out why she's holding her audience hostage. But getting onto Myra's stage isn't going to be easy. First the Freelance Police will have to prove they're worthy of fifteen minutes of fame.One of six self-contained cases with an overarching story arc.Simple point & click interface with a low learning curve.Catchy jazz soundtrack featuring live performers and over three hours of original music.Intuitive gameplay that appeals to new players as well as adventure-game diehards.And it's funny!
