
							About This Game
							THE APOCALYPSE IS HERE …           … SO IT’S TIME TO PARTY LIKE THERE IS NO TOMORROW!Rise of the Chubtan is a chaotic and fast paced fighting game with constant looming death for all the players. Knock your enemies into pools of acid, spinning saw blades or even under a speeding train.You are a Mayan god, fighting your brothers for the souls of mankind. Battle around the world in different arenas, containing deadly traps. The gods, immortal and equal in strength, are not able to directly harm each other. Utilize the traps of the arenas to obliterate your foes…for a brief while.Features: Chaotic combat where everyone loses but one is still a winnerArenas, each with unique set of traps to outlast your foeThe pleasure of hearing the lamentation of your enemy.
