
							About This Content
							The Red Deck is an expansion to SUPERFIGHT that adds over 80 R-Rated cards to the core deck.  This expansion contains the most inappropriate and terrible cards in the game.  We'll be honest: there is some nasty [CENSORED] in this deck.The Red Deck comes complete with new Achievements, Titles, and Art to unlock!Once added to SUPERFIGHT, The Red Deck will be added to your Collection and can be selected when joining fights.  Please note that due to the mature content of this deck, you will need to enable its use within the SUPERFIGHT settings menu.
