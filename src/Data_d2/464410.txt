
							About This Video
							In a land where a hundred-year-old war rages, he travels alone. A fearsome mercenary who trusts only his instincts and his sword. Without a place to call home, he bands with the leader of a group of mercenaries called, "The Band of the Hawk." The charismatic leader and the mercenary bond and an army is formed. Despite their success, the lone warrior questions why he fights for another man's dreams. Could a horrible fate await them both?
