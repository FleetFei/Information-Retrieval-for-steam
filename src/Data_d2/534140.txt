
							About This Video
							After visiting his father in Arkham Sanitarium, young Howard Lovecraft accidentally uses the legendary Necronomicon to open a portal to a strange frozen world filled with horrifying creatures and a great adventure. Unwittingly, the boy reads a few words written in a book his father has demanded be destroyed - and is sucked through a hole in the fabric of the universe.
