
							About This Game
							Bleeding Blocks is a 2D arena shooter. Two players face off in a single-screen deathmatch. Winning requires more than quick reflexes. Players must use cover and tactics to outsmart the enemy.Choose from six unique classes. Will you rush in as the Rogue? Snipe from afar as the Reaper? Or deploy turrets as the Engineer? Play against a deviously smart AI or head to head in local multiplayer mode.Features6 different classes.Bullet-hell reflex shooting combined with tactical gameplay employing cover and flanking.A devious AI capable of flanking and outsmarting human players.Single and local multiplayer modes.Catchy Synthwave music.
