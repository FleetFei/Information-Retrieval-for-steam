
							About This Content
							Magnetic: Cage Closed is a first person puzzle game where the player manipulates magnetic forces to accomplish their goals. In this free DLC,  the focus lies on one thing and one thing alone: Testing the limits of the Magnet Gun and the skill-level of the player. We have unleashed all the evil creative geniuses at the studio to bring you the most difficult maps you could wish for. Pull yourself ever upwards through the hazardous Twister, wrack your brain for a solution in Sequence or, if you really like to torture yourself, face of against a deadly series of traps in The Gauntlet. Five new and unique challenge maps that test your skills to the limit.
