
							About This Game
							Action of Flame of Memory takes place in the 20th century forest, where two guerillas are trying to survive, being surrounded by enemy units. To survive they must ensure themselves food, warmth and security. They have to watch out for hostile patrols and other threats. During the game you will meet several types of threats. Flame of Memory is a combination of climatic strategy and survival game. Try to survive... if you can. beautiful 2D graphics! climatic gameplay! inventory system! crafting system! soundtrack created by Polish band - Contra Mundum!
