
							About This Content
							The Masquerada: Songs and Shadows Original Soundtrack by Josh Whelchel is a musical tour de force that features 40 tracks of original compositions, including the ethereal vocal piece "Broken Clay" featuring singer-songwriter iNCH.

The full set of 320 kbps MP3-format files will be placed in your Masquerada folder in the Steam Directory: ...Steam\steamapps\common\Masquerada Songs and Shadows\Soundtrack
