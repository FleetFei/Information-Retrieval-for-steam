
							About This Game
							Have fun with this amazing tribute to the best SNES and MEGADRIVE games!Welcome to Orange Adventure! This game takes you on an incredible journey with Orange, the Box of Orange Juice, who must recover theOranges stolen by the evil Dr. Juicer's Robots and rescue his friends.Travel with Orange through multiple worlds -- from the green meadows of Orange Land to the desert wastelands of the pyramids, facing multiple enemies and game modes.In Orange Adventure you will find:*40 amazing and complex levels.*8 worlds with diferent enemies and obstacles.*3 Gameplay Modes.*More than 10 enemies type.*Microsoft Controller Support.
