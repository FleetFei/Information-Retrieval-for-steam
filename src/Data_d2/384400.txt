
							About This Game
							Get ready to rock! Hordelicious is a semi-hardcore wave based arena shooter. Survive against endless hordes of enemies with over 45 randomly looted weapons and a plethora of items and upgrades to choose from!Your original mission was to explore the new planet we found. We knew about the life forms of the planet, but what we didn't know was that these creatures actually worship the planet itself.  Now we have disgraced their holy ground with our human feet and angered the creatures of this planet. You have to forget our original plans and fight your way out of there!Features:- Over 45 unique weapons - A plethora of items of upgrades  - Epic boss battles- Achievements to unlock- Levels with multiple variations
