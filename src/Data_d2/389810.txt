
							About This Game
							Rubber & Lead is a homage to AutoDuel, a fantastic game from 1985. Action and RPG elements are blended together to create an exciting combination! You must drive and shoot your way through post-apocalyptic Arizona. Choose your mission, upgrade your car, equip your weapons, and blast away your enemies! Warning: Driving without shooting can be fatal. Game Features:Fight In Arenas Run Missions Level Your Skills Buy A New Car Upgrade Your Car Buy Perks Blow Raiders Up Have A Lovely Day
