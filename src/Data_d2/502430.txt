
							About This Game
							This simulation transports you to the varied work in road construction. Re-enact typical assignments and scenarios on country roads and motorways. Get to work with the asphalt mill, steamroller, marking machine and many more special vehicles.Build a new section of motorway, widen lanes and renew the road surface. Use tools like the jack hammer and secure the site with barriers. Includes detailed vehicles and a range of camera angles.FEATURESDiverse assignments like building a section of motorway, renewing the road surface and widening lanesLovingly detailed vehicles and different camera anglesChallenging assignments ranging from standard road repairs to building an airport roadChoose from lots of special vehicles: marking machine, steamroller, excavator
