
							About This Game
							Booming turbine noise, kerosene smells in the air and constant change and new challenges: The daily life of a large airport is perfectly presented  and is a particular challenge in this professional and economic  airport simulation.Whether refueling  super jumbos, or coordinating  air traffic controllers and technical staff, or even organizing perfect catering: The Management of this airport will always have new challenges for you!FeaturesThe most detailed and comprehensive simulation of the events at an international airportMany management and business options for the expansion of terminals and extension of runwaysCountless missions and tasks that the player has to fulfill in order to guarantee a successful airport
