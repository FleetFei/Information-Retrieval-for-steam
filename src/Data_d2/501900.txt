
							About This Game
							Earthlings wants you! If you are from those who are willing to defend the universe, you need to became a space fighter pilot, enlist to the STAR PROJECT!STAR PROJECT- it's a great opportunity to meet new species and demonstrate superiority of the earthlings over all other populated worlds. Meet with unprecedented aggressive-minded space races and crush their best pilots! Your task is to destroy any military flotilla, which only meet in the deep space. Well, if you fulfilled your duty to humanity with dignity, if you stay alive after fulfillment of its most important task, the honor and glory will cover you and your home at all times.... But enough talk. Are you ready to fly?12 spacecraft; 9 planets; 3 hostile alien race; 12 types of enemies.Ship customization. Spend earned points on the change in the external hull of the ship and upgrade your power, armor strength and speed increase.Battles with small fighters and giant aircraft carriers, and that's not all.
