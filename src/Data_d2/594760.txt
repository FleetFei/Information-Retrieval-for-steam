
							About This Game
							This game is developed from the ground up for HTC Vive and it's not game AAA grade.
Step in the midst of a low poly zombie apocalypse. Kill them all and save the town where you growing up.


Real-life weapon handling: Using VR motion controllers handle over 42 different weapons.
Move where you want: using touchpad you can smoothly go there where you wish.

Few different game mode:
- Throw missions
- Street missions
- Arena missions
(more in next updates)
