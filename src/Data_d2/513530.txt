
							About This Game
							Anykey Simulator allows you to feel the hardship of computer designing.In this game you are faced with solving simple puzzles - you have to move available computer module to appointed place in the field of 5x5 squares, in the way that modules won't suffer, preferably all of them, but your aim is to complete the plan. From the start you have three instruments for solving the tasks - for module building, for it's destroying and for it's improving. You will be given more and more modules to charge, each module has its own unique characteristics that must be taken into account before you set the module, because many of them ignite when they contact with dust and others can't stand the dirty.
