
							About This Content
							A set of 60 additional facial graphics for "NOBUNAGA'S AMBITION: Tenshouki with Power-up Kit HD Version."
This set includes 30 face graphics from  "NOBUNAGA'S AMBITION: Tendou,"  including those for Nobunaga Oda, Kenshin Uesugi, and Yukimura Sanada. 
In addition to these and to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the Romance of the Three Kingdoms series, we will include 30 graphics from "Romance of the Three Kingdoms 12," featuring Cao Cao, Liu Bei, Sun Quan, and more.
The in-game Edit function will allow players to set these graphics to both historical and player-original officers.
