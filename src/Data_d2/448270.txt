
							About This Content
							This Tiny Guardians bundle includes 8 alternative appearances: Knight - she wields a spear and shines in battle. Archer - also a Noxie, but of sheep descent. Magician - an diligent scholar of spells. Rogue - she is known as the Crimson Flash on the battlefield. Berserker - she hacks and slashes her way through foes. Hunter - adept at hunting, he is particularly fond of butterflies. Cleric - a tenacious persona with a soft side. Assassin - one of the five notorious Fenrir assassins.Alternative appearances have the same skills and stats as their original class counterparts.
