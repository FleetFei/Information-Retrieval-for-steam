
							About This Game
							Become a boss of a construction company. Demolish old houses, flatten the ground, fill foundations with concrete and build new buildings - this is what you do. Sit behind the wheel of different construction machines - from trucks and diggers to huge cranes. Win contracts and gather money to expand your company and buy or upgrade your machines.Game Features: advanced physics and interaction with environment 8 different construction machine types realistic handling 20 contracts to fullfill additional challenges and free play mode
