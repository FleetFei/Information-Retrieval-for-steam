
							About This Game
							Sundered is a horrifying fight for survival and sanity, a hand­-drawn epic from the ​creators of ​​Jotun. You play Eshe, a wanderer in a ruined world, trapped in ever­changing caverns teeming with eldritch horrors. Harness the power of corrupted relics to defeat gigantic bosses, at the cost of your humanity. Resist or embrace. Features :● Beautiful hand­-drawn art ● Massive boss fights ● Dynamic encounters against hordes of enemies ● A mix of hand-crafted & procedural levels ● Corruptible abilities ● Multiple endings
