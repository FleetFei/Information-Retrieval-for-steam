
							About This Content
							The Official Soundtrack to the indie hit Guild of Dungeoneering.  This little gem is full of all the theme music with lyrics, all the background music in the game, the bonus song with lyrics from the official launch trailer and of course all of the Bard's in game tunes that have taunted you, made you laugh or sung your praises.  

This is a total of 23 tracks for a total playtime of 1 hour 47 minutes.  (Not including the Bard's songs)

Composed by: Steve Gregan
Vocals 'This is the Guild of Dungeoneering': Barry Keatley
Vocals 'Dungeoneer Mania' and The Bard: Frankie Lane
