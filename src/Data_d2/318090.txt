
							About This Game
							Set in a fantasy world, Middle Board, DICETINY is a parody turn-based card and board game mash-up where you as one of the heroes must take on powerful monsters using minions and spell cards, and ultimately defeat an evil sage to save the world from an apocalypse.featuresOffers unique gameplay mechanic by combining card and board game elements.Various dungeons guarded by powerful monsters. with different game rules.Meet new class-based heroes, each with a unique skill, during your adventure.Build your deck and apply a different strategy depending on the dungeon.Puns and parodies hopefully everyone will enjoy.
