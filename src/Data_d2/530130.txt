
							About This Game
							Akuto: Mad World is an award-nominated fast-paced arena action game with swords and guns. Death is common but by using your weapons and the environment, you can survive to fight another day. Fast-paced arcade action Fight in over 10 dynamic levels Use one of 6 different game-changing bullets to win matches Battle friends or the AI in local multiplayer Survive endless waves of ninjas Mini-games, singleplayer mode, and level editor are coming in future updates
