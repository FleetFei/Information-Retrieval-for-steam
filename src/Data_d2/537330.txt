
							About This Content
							Acceleration Pack includes 12 new game cards (4 rare, 4 uncommon, 4 common) designed by Junpyon and Hono of Orange Juice. Includes 2 cards of a new card type: Gift, granting exciting boosts or penalties to players.

The cards of the set can be purchased from the in-game shop and used in both single and multiplayer. All cards in a multiplayer game can be used by all players via the center deck, even if they do not own the DLC.
