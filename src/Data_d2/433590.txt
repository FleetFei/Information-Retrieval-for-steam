
							About This Game
							"An awakening wrapped in self-discovery" GAMEPLAYThe Song of Seven is a single player third-person adventure game. NARRATIVE- This game definitely has sheep- Also has at least one old man- Adventure around the world of Pria, solve some tricky puzzles.COMPANYEnlightened Games is dedicated to creating a thoughtful, enriching, positive interactive experience for our players. Like a novel or film - we truly believe games can add value to our lives by overcoming obstacles, using intellect, and creating emotional connections. We respect our players intelligence and know everyone is capable of learning organically through the experience.
