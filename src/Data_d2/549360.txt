
							About This Software
							ObserVR is a viewing application for watching multiple live videos directly from Twitch. Join a room with your friends and watch your favorite streamers, or join a public room and meet new people!Key Features: Watch Your Favorite ContentWith Twitch integration, you can follow up to four of your favorite streamers simultaneously. Customize Your EnvironmentSize and place your screens to your liking for the ultimate viewing experience. Chat with FriendsVoice chat functionality lets you talk to your friends, and head-tracking avatars give you a presence in the virtual world. Interact with Objects from Your Favorite GamesWeapons and more from major titles are populated into your streaming environment.
