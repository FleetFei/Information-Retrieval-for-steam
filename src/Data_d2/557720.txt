
							About This Game
							A young prince has been having the same dream and is summoned far from his home. Help him remove an evil curse that has caused a whole kingdom to be trapped in their dreams! Save a beautiful princess before it’s too late in this fun and exciting Hidden Object Adventure game. Dive into the world of Love Chronicles – The Spell Collector’s Edition, and defeat the evil witch that has cursed the entire population.

Experience all the exclusive Collector’s Edition extras!

Built-in Strategy Guide

Gorgeous graphics
Perplexing puzzles
Save an entire kingdom!
