
							About This Game
							Pictopix is a puzzle game where you use logic to color squares on grids in order to reveal pictures. Easy to learn and very addictive, the game starts with small grids and ends with much bigger grids. With over 150 colored puzzles, a shuffle mode and a puzzle editor, Pictopix will bring you hours of fun! The game is similar to picross, nonograms, hanjie, pic-a-pix and griddlers.Main FeaturesPlay over 150 beautiful colored puzzles (from 5x5 to 25x25)Learn the rules with a fun tutorialPlay endlessly with the Shuffle modeEarn awards and achievements for more challengesCreate your own puzzlesAnd share them with others players!
