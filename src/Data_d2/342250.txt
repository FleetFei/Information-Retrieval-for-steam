
							About This Game
							Netherlands, 1520. The Destroyers of the Art come out of paintings and materialize from statues, they come to infect the Art and wreck the city. To trap them, help Mr. Bruesen create the Aspectus, the Perfect Painting, before the time runs out. Find the sect of the amorphous creatures and destroy it. Explore the city after dark and unveil hideous secrets Play the workshops of locks, alchemy, and reparation to get new items Combine up to 4 items to build tricky tools and find a way out Talk to people to get new missions Watch the in-game cut scenes to grasp new details and save the city Dialogues dubbed in English and subtitles available in English, German, French, Spanish, Swedish, Italian, Dutch
