
							About This Game
							DESCRIPTIONExperience the Simple Pinball in Virtual Reality. Play with a real-life pinball machine in a virtual space.Supported controllers are HTC Vive, Oculus Touch, Xbox controller and Keyboard.Controls HTC Vive – Trigger: Flipper / Trackpad: Plunger / Menu Button (Shoulder): Reset Position Oculus Touch – Trigger: Flipper / A Button: Plunger / B or Y Button: Reset Position Xbox Controller – LT or LB: Left Flipper / RT or RB: Right Flipper / A Button: Plunger / B or Y Button: Reset Position Keyboard – Left Ctrl: Left Flipper / Right Ctrl: Right Flipper / Space: Plunger / R Key: Reset Position
