
							About This Game
							Gebub finds himself in a large peaceful world, filled with colorful orbs and many creatures who need his help. Explore your way through over 12 unique environments, collect hidden items and use them to help other creatures, who will in turn aid you in uncovering more of Gebub's mysterious world.Game LengthExperienced players could finish the game in about 1 hour, but a full exploration of the game world will take 2 hours or more.
