
							About This Game
							FATE is the penultimate chapter in the six-part BIT.TRIP series and will leave you wondering what’s next.Key Features:The arrow keys control CommanderVideo while the mouse allows you to fire anywhere on screen! Or if you prefer, plug in a controller for dual-stick shooting action!Collect power-ups to call on CommanderVideo’s friends for powerful shot upgrades!A chiptune-inspired dubstep soundtrack helps bring the action to a whole new level!Video game worlds collide with appearances from Super Meat Boy and Mr. Robotube!Chipmusic artist Minusbaby helps set the tone with a special guest appearance!Enjoy brand new Easy and Hard Difficulty Modes!Ride the vibe in BIT.TRIP FATE to discover where CommanderVideo’s true mission will take him.
