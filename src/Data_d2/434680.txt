
							About This Game
							Lamia's game room is a Gothic style horror mystery card game. You are brought into a room by a mysterious woman named Lamia and forced to play games with people from different backgrounds. You will learn the story as the game progresses. Find out your opponents' weakness during conversations, in order to defeat them.Winning is the only way to leave the room... Game features: suspenseful and mysterious story line. RPG-style dialogues allows you to be immersed in the story. Stylish game design. Easy and simple game play.
