
							About This Content
							Embark on a tour of Ian Vincent's India and discover one of the most densely populated and colorful countries of the world. The year is 1911, and the world is changing fast in the British Raj. Will you complete your Grand Tour in time, or lose to more cunning – or simply better connected – opponents?Ticket to Ride now supports vertical maps! The map India is the first vertical map made available. This vertical map brings a new way of playing by introducing loops called mandalas. On this map players who decide to reach two cities through mandalas i.e. connecting two cities following loops will get more points.
