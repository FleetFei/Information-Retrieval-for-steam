
							About This Game
							zTime (Danger Noodles!) is a game about seeing the future.Knowing the future, you can see the paths of yourself and enemies showing where they will be in the next few seconds, visualized in the third dimension beneath the floor.  Where your path collides with the path of an enemy, the future danger is highlighted while you still have time to counteract.Classic top down style gameplay becomes a symphony of interweaving 3d paths where future events are highlighted, and players have time to change the outcomes before it's too late.zTime (Danger Noodles!) is in early access for Windows. Play it with Oculus Rift or HTC Vive if you have one.
