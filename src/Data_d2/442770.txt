
							About This Game
							An adventure game in the vein of point and click games, about a personal journey of an endearing creature of the Minim species.In the game, the player must use logic, collect important items, and solve puzzles in order to guide Mii in his search to find his life partner Mo. Tracing her steps he embarks on a journey which becomes a self-seeking exploration regarding life choices and personal values. imaginative artworkcompelling storyrelaxing tempoquirky and serene universe
