
							About This Video
							In 1985, a guard at the county jail deals with a troublesome inmate and a surprise visit from his family. In 1996, that inmate finds a teenage girl stranded on the side of the road, her car beyond repair. As both stories unfold, the inmate, the guard, and his family all discover how the decisions you make today can greatly change your future.  Directed by: Mary RatliffWritten by: Mary RatliffStarring: Thomas Keegan, Bo Keister
