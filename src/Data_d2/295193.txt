
							About This Content
							The Torpedopfannenwagen Pack, or Torpedo Ladle Wagon, recreates the special wagons used for the transportation of molten Steel. A special heavy-duty design is required for this challenging and dangerous work. Thanks to their construction, the steel load remains molten at the required temperature of 14000 degrees Celsius for more than 30 hours.Includes: Normal and weathered variants Loaded ( 300 t. ) and unloaded ( 150 t. ) versions Loading and unloading animations
