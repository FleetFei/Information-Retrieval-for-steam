
							About This Game
							Super Flippin' Phones is a game about flipping phones out of people’s hands in an attempt to warn them about an impending disaster (meteor storms, Kevin Spacey appearing in Call of Duty). Unfortunately, no one in the mall is listening to you because they're too busy with their phones. While slapping away phones you evade cops, save humanity & unlock new officers, floors and outfits!The game comes with three difficulty tiers with distinctive cops that have to be evaded in different ways, several floors with their own dangers and opportunities, and lots of outfits for our hero!FeaturesCrazy Phone-Flippin’ Action - Quick gameplay with a tactical layer of risk and reward.A Whole Supermall to Save - Multiple shops with different props and more advanced mall cops.Tiers For Everyone - Unlock the hardest challenges to play new difficulties that amp up the cop variety!SweatFXWorks 2.1® brings post-next generation liquid sweat drop decals.
