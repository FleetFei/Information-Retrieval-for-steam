
							About This Game
							1 box, 6 transformations, 120 levels of increasing difficulty for skilled players who love challenges.

Help Boxy escape its factory and survive the outside world by mastering its 6 transformations: Rocket, Steel, Bubble, Spikes, Vapor and Fire.

More than a hundred levels of increasing difficulty will put your skills, reflexes and cunning to the test. Die, retry, perfect your run and dominate the competitive leaderboards.

- 6 transformations meaning 6 different types of gameplay
- 3 game modes (Classic, Time Attack and Survival)
- 10 worlds to explore, from a cold factory to a hot volcano or a watery jungle
- 120 levels, skill-based or puzzle-based
