
							About This Content
							Talrand’s fully unlocked “Crosswinds” blue deck. Talrand rose from the depths of Shandalar’s seas to rule the skies above them. A powerful sorcerer, Talrand summons fearsome drakes to fight his foes. They are joined by angry sphinxes ready to bring their ancient power and strength against any opponent. Harness the power of wind and wave to achieve victory! Additional content for Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013.
