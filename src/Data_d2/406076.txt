
							About This Content
							The Cyberpunk Music Pack by Scythuz is made to recapture the feel and essence of the popular 90’s sci-fi sub-genre and inject it with modern production values. It’s perfect for games that feature dystopian futures, cybernetic oriented societies and oppressive corporations.Included in this pack are battle themes, peace themes, dungeon themes and various meta effects that are all designed to enhance your games unique choice of setting and build the appropriate mood for this setting. The music in this pack is also appropriate for other futuristic/sci-fi settings! This pack contains:10 synth drenched tracks and 3 meta effects.Looped OGG and MP3 formats for use in RPG Maker VX/ACE and IG Maker.Royalty free sounds to use in your commercial and non-commercial RPG/IG Maker projects.
