
							About This Content
							Third part of the cyberpunk visual novel series Invisible Apartment.

Kacey's story comes to an end. The Invisible Apartment cyberpunk visual novel series began in 2014 and has seen three parts of the series so far.

Kacey & Alex find themselves in a complicated situation. They're both on the run, Alex no longer works at the Central Intelligence and they don't know the whereabouts of Alex's mom who is detained in a sleeper cell.
