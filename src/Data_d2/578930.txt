
							About This Game
							kuso is a sequel to LOVE, and is a reductive platforming game with a retro aesthetic and a focus on a challenging difficulty. You find yourself in a minimalist world and with only the ability to run and jump, as well as the option to leave a checkpoint on any solid ground. Make your way through acid, long jumps, disappearing platforms, bouncers, and swinging axes.No challenge is impossible in kuso. Proper timing and careful jumps will carry you to safety in any situation.The free debut demo version of kuso includes:• 3 new levels from kuso • 6 remade levels from LOVE• New music from the game’s original composer, James Bennett• xinput Controller Support• Steam Achievements• Steam Leaderboards for kuso Speedruns• Steam Leaderboards for LOVE Speedruns• Controller remapping in Steam’s Overlay
