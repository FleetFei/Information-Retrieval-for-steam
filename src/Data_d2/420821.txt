
							About This Content
							The FaceRig Winter Holidays Avatars 2015 DLC brings you five cute and joyful avatars to add to FaceRig. 
It includes a fluffy Penguin, a huggable Teddy-bear, a perky Reindeer and a tasty Gingerbread-man.
The evil elf also joined the gang after being kicked out from Santa's workshop for spying for the Grinch.
