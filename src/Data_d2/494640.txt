
							About This Content
							This DLC contains 5 new ship to play with. Each one with a different mix of weapons optimized to hunt down ships form the same class.The Dreadnought is the new capital ship, it has the widest arsenal and a unique Mass driver Cannon, it can wrestle with any ship and come out on top.The Missile Destroyer can unleash devastating salvo of missiles, or single out weak ship.The Assault Frigate more leaner silhouette is paired with more turrets than what should fit on a ship this size.The Missile Corvette, with it's deadly accurate missiles, is perfect to defend bigger ships.The Interceptor forward beam cannon can cut most fighters in half under 20s.
