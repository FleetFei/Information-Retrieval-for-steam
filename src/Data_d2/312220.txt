
							About This Game
							Tales of Adventure 2 is a 2D shoot’em up with over 30 different stylistic levels and exciting mechanics. Defeat the enemy and gain power-up crystals to advance your stats and abilities. Loot the most exotic items to defeat the enemy!Features30+ levels each with its unique mechanics.40+ types of monsters with unique behaviors and attack animation.All monsters tell you a little about this new world; together, these pieces of information define this world.12 tracks of BGM.Consuming power-up crystals will randomly boost your attributes.8 types of spells. Upgrading spells also changes animation. When you upgrade your low level spells, there’s a chance you will discover a new spell!Items will periodically spawn in your screen, don’t miss them!Special effects from spells and items can stack. Find out the best combination to annihilate the enemy!
